SUBJECT TO CHANGE

By George Gondron

SUBJECT TO CHANGE Would that our lives were, 'Subject To Change' Alas, most are, "Business As Usual" For many, change does not come easy Sameness being the preferred path Most of us unaware of the forks in the road Miss opportunities many times a day The sign on one path, 'Subject To Change' The other points to 'Business As Usual' Even when 'Business As Usual' is a path of pain I'm blind to the pointer that can lead to the new The path of 'Subject To Change' The familiar, the path of least resistance The path that leads to what I expect, yet fear Is always a dead end path Repeated over and again, endlessly The thoughts, the feelings, unmeasured repetitions The path turns in on itself Like a maze with no solution, and no escape A merry-go-round of 'Business As Usual' Yesterday, today, tomorrow, all the same It will not change without a choice A choice to take the other path The path of 'Subject To Change' The path of the possible, yet even unknown The path of the different, yet somehow familiar The internal path, hidden in plain sight A path seldom traveled in a world of distraction We all have an innate, inner path There are no exceptions Seek yours, it waits Find 'Subject To Change'

George G
