SACRED

By George Gondron

SACRED What is sacred, and when does it become so? Who decides? Is there a catalogue of sacred objects, sacred forms? Is there a governmental or religious office one can call for sacred questions and answers? Are family, friends, relatives, all humans sacred? If so, when did they become so, before or after birth? Does condemnation or judgment have anything to do with sacredness? Look into the eyes of an infant, the eyes of a dying elderly Is there sacredness there, in the beginning, at the end? What about in between? The difficult, demanding, struggling, years Meister Eckhart: "The eye through which I see God is the same eye through which God sees me." Maybe I'm seeing with the wrong eyes Maybe I'm asking the wrong question Maybe the question is, what is not sacred? If everything is sacred, perhaps my condemnations and judgments are wrong Maybe I turn the sacred into the profane Maybe

George G
