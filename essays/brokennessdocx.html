BROKENNESS

By George Gondron

BROKENNESS There will be moments throughout your life when you will be offered A gift, beautifully wrapped and waiting to be opened And if you dare, you will discover the following realizations You are tired of wearing a mask, tired of pretending, tired of living In the past or fearing the future, tired of empty promises, tired of Endless desires and unhealthy attachments, tired of wanting more When enough will suffice, tired of hiding from yourself, tired of Ignoring your own creativity and potential, tired of following everyone Else’s rules and demands, tired of not becoming your authentic self You have taken the risk of unwrapping your own brokenness And if you are lucky, it will happen more than once For it is out of our acknowledged brokenness that we begin to heal And it is that healing, where we have been broken, that begins To become stronger than before, more aware and conscious We will have a deeper understanding of our own journey We will be more open to possibility and more cautious of Empty promises, more conscious of hyperbole and ‘gaslighting’ We will be less likely to accept what is as it is and more likely To challenge the status quo with courage and resilience We will rely more on personal experience and less on unfounded Beliefs, less on ‘third party’ information and propaganda We will take the gifts of our five senses of thought and feeling And apply them appropriately without hesitation We will educate ourselves and not be led around by our ‘noses’ Through our brokenness we will begin to perceive clearly That we may respond accurately, trusting our experience Never fear your brokenness, it is your greatest ally, your teacher We are both shadow and light and together we become whole In our society, we lack the necessary connection between inner And outer, we are all external, our inner guides languish helplessly Adopt a meditative/contemplative practice for balance Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Mystery We each contain the Divine DNA of Universal Consciousness We are all connected in our sameness, we grow in our differentness Focus on your breath or a short mantra, breathe in, breathe out Listen to the whispers of your inner guides, heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, they wait

George G
