PART TIME LOVE

By George Gondron

PART TIME LOVE Guess what real love is It's not part time, it's not qualified, it's not limited It's unconditional, it's genuine and absolute And yet, and also, and as well Being unconditional doesn't mean we may not agree Doesn't mean we won't be angry at one another Doesn't even mean that we may not always be together Love is not a feeling, it is a way of being, an action An utter and unrestricted positive regard for all that exists An understanding that what is not me Is a subject, not an object That what is not me is not a means to an end A selfish end But it is that which enables and allows me to become An authentic fully human being, a collaborative effort A growth partnership, not an ego conquest Everything in our current culture, our society Is oppositional to unconditional love It's like an alien idea or reality We hear the phrase 'unconditional love' And give it credence only though lip service Only because, at our core, we don't believe We are worthy of unconditional love And yet it's only because of unconditional love that we exist For no other reason A love that asks of us only one thing To be in relationship, not worship, not adoration Only relationship And in our quieter moments we know the importance Of relationship When we can get out of our heads and into our hearts When like a glass of muddy water, we can be still Until through new awareness and consciousness Though clarity, we see, we know, what innately Our heart, soul, and spirit have always known That we come from love, we live through love, We are love, meant to be connected, in relationship

George G
