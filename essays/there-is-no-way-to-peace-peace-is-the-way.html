THERE IS NO WAY TO PEACE, PEACE IS THE WAY

By George Gondron

THERE IS NO WAY TO PEACE, PEACE IS THE

When we are restricted from becoming our full potential As human beings, fully alive in freedom We, through our personal choices, are our own downfall And we seldom consider it has been our choices That brings us to this present moment Physically, intellectually, emotionally, relationally, and spiritually We are the enemies of our own peace within, Our peace between, and our peace among, Compassion, kindness, love, and connection Are all elements we each possess naturally, internally Waiting to be put into daily practice when and only when We can allow our true selves to thrive And to allow our egoic false selves to wan Our egos are an important function of our human nature When in partnership with our heart, soul, and spirit Always better together, that is where we find peace No thing survives alone, only in conjunction with In our current culture with all its advances It seems our characteristics, our personalities Are more against than for, more con than pro More concerned about ‘things’ than relationships In our rare quiet moments, we are not at peace, peace within We do violence to ourselves through our thoughts and feelings Which then leads to outward violence if only through complicity Nonaction is a silent agreement with the status quo For ourselves and for our context, our environment The enemy of openness and learning is judgment It’s so much easier, more convenient, more reactive Our path to personal peace is an inward process A partnership between mind, heart, soul, and spirit Our true self as opposed to the egoic false self Spend a few minutes each day in silence, with stillness, In the Presence of your image or experience of God God is not, an intellectual concept, it is a lived experience Numinous moments to self-evident to deny Too profound to put into words, listen to the whispers

George G
