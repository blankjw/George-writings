THE DEATH OF MORALITY

By George Gondron

THE DEATH OF MORALITY Morality, like most of life’s learnings, evolves through Stages and states It is the development of moral reasoning And the basis for all ethical behavior The famous psychologist, Lawrence Kohlberg Professor at Harvard University spent five years Developing his six-stage theory of moral development The first stage of obedience and punishment begins in infancy Most adults in our culture are at stage three, the legal level We break the law, and we violate current moral principles The highest level, level six, has to do with human rights, justice, And equality for all Matters of compassion, kindness, and inclusion If what we witness in our society today is any indication Of our moral dilemma, it is below the legal level, stage three When we see daily the corruption in our politics Corporate abuses in the world of business and economics And there seems to be little reaction, little justice For the many offenses, the lying, the deceit, the injustice Clear signs that we are in moral decay Our own Constitution, founded on the principles of law Equality and justice for all is in peril, great peril And if we wear ‘blinders’, stick our heads in the sand And deny what’s so very evident, we are complicit Our lack of outcry, our inability to put people before ‘things’ All things Will be the eventual downfall of this democratic Republic The only winners will be the wealthy and powerful All others will simply be ‘pawns’ to their desires What can be done, and what’s the answer? Spent a few minutes each day in silence, with stillness, with Presence Awaken to the abuses that abound in our society Stop looking through ‘rose-colored glasses’ See reality as it is, not through your defenses Experience the whispers of your own heart, soul, and spirit That hold the truth beyond the empty promises of the privileged Discover your ‘true self’, your courage, your compassion

George G
