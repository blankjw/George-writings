POEM

LOVE ALONE

By George Gondron | Source: LOVE ALONE.docx

LOVE ALONE LOVE HEALS ALL THINGS LOVE NEVER LEAVES LOVE UNDERSTANDS ALL PAIN LOVE WILL ALWAYS BE, NEVER BEGINNING, NEVER ENDING LOVE ALWAYS INCLUDES AND NEVER SEPARATES LOVE DOES NOT DEMAND OR CONTROL, ALLOWING TOTAL FREEDOM LOVE NEVER GIVES UP LOVE CONQUERS ALL FEAR LOVE IS THE NEVER ENDING SANCTUARY TO KNOW LOVE; THE ONLY POSSIBLE RESPONSE IS TO LOVE AND LOVE IS ALWAYS PRESENT; MORE PRESENT TO ME THAN I AM TO MYSELF

GEORGE G
