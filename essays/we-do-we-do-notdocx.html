WE DO - WE DO NOT

By George Gondron

WE DO - WE DO NOT What we do, we are What we do not do, we also are One cannot live in neutrality, with impartiality Our actions speak loudly, whether seen or hidden Our inaction screams of what doesn't matter Yesterday, today, tomorrow, we are how we live What's important always reveals itself What's unimportant betrays itself as well Caring for people, is caring for life Caring for things, is caring not at all Our complaints tell us where our cares dwell Our blame points to what we cherish Our chosen ignorance speaks volumes Our willful neglect belies our excuses We confer with those that are like us We deliberate with those that think like us Yet, that cannot hide the truth of our actions That cannot conceal the false pretenses of our inaction "I can only do so much", an excuse to not do We can always do more More to increase our own awareness More to evolve our own consciousness Resulting in doing more to touch and help others We are, always, what we do We are, forever, what we fail to do Let us do what matters For ourselves, for others, for creation Let us

George G
