DON'T BE A FOLLOWER BE A LEADER

By George Gondron

DON’T BE A FOLLOWER BE A LEADER Be a leader of your own life Don’t assume that what you hear from the loudest voices Is for you, is true, and has merit Remember, those that shout the loudest often Have the least to offer, smoke and mirrors The direction of your life, the most promising path Is always a matter of an informed personal choice Not the voices that proclaim, ‘this is what you need to do’ All those that attempt to direct your choices Whatever they may be, in whatever arena Are primarily for their benefit first and maybe yours second No one has a right to direct your life, absolutely no one When we accept, without question, without doubt What is being offered in the form of a future promise In a narrative that proclaims, ‘I have all the answers’ We play the fool, and we will suffer the consequences A choice made on the basis of an emotional attachment Is almost always a choice made wearing blinders A choice made emotionally and not intellectually Those that shout from their ‘bully pulpits’ only want To control you, manipulate you, dominate you Trust the person that demonstrates through their behavior Their capacity for kindness, compassion, inclusion, and love Beware the person that demonstrates a lack of empathy And who’s narrative is totally self-referential, all about themselves All ego or as Ann Richards the once Texas governor said ‘All hat and no cattle’ Only you are responsible for your own journey Only you have the right to make the important choices The best teachers in our lives are personal experience Mistakes made are nothing more than teachers, not failures Include a meditative/contemplative practice in your life Spend a few minutes each day in silence, be still, embrace Isness Be in love with Love, with Universal Consciousness Breath in, breath out a brief mantra for focus Discover the Divine DNA within your psyche Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit, they wait

George G
