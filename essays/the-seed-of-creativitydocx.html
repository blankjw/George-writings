THE SEED OF CREATIVITY

By George Gondron

THE SEED OF CREATIVITY Within each human psyche Each human soul Each human spirit There exists a creative energy A yearning to express what is unique to the individual To be fortunate enough to be in an environment Which nurtures and fosters that creative energy Is the exception and sadly not the rule Standardized methods of education are not conducive To awaken the creative gifts within each child At best those gifts will be accidentally discovered At worst, they will be stifled as square pegs Forced into round holes If examined honestly, it could be said that our Formalized education, as it exists today Could be called child abuse Creative energy is more about intuition and imagination Concepts almost totally ignored in our methods Of education, on all levels Words like compare, contrast, and compete When used in the context of child development Are in fact antithetical to their innate creativity If you have the ability to encourage a child to dream To imagine, to explore, to wonder, to ask questions Please do so, you become a candle in their darkness We could change the manner in which children are educated But we don’t Our way is too familiar, too predictable, too understated And too formalized with little challenge for creativity Take a little time to reflect on your own creativity Choose to spend a few minutes each day In silence, in stillness, with Presence Empty your ever-busy ‘monkey mind’ of its incessant And often repetitive thoughts that go nowhere and accomplish No-thing Listen to the whispers of your own heart, soul, and spirit Awaken your inner core, your true self, your connection, please

George G
