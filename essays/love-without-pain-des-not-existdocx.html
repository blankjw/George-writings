LOVE WITHOUT PAIN DES NOT EXIST

By George Gondron

LOVE WITHOUT PAIN DOES NOT EXIST Genuine love, unconditional love, will experience pain If for no other reason than the pain of witnessing Those we love suffer, for human suffering is unavoidable Love cannot prevent suffering, it can only be present to the same That presence is called empathy and compassion Something we desperately need now more than ever
