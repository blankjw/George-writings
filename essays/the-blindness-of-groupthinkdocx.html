THE BLINDNESS OF GROUPTHINK

By George Gondron

THE BLINDNESS OF GROUPTHINK It is almost a given Those who proclaim their virtuousness Are more often than not, the least virtuous When did we morph into people who are more than willing To be swayed and influenced by the loudest voices We feed on the lies until we believe they are the truth The phrase ‘alternative facts’ did not exist less than a decade ago Now it has become more important than proven facts As the saying goes, ‘You gonna believe me are those lying eyes?’ Rather than looking at the content of a person’s character We are more influenced by their words, words proven to be false Be careful of the ‘pied piper’ you may follow They could care less about your interests For their interests are only about themselves, not you You are merely a means to an end, their end And if you don’t follow, then you are an obstacle To their selfish desires, you have no value if you don’t follow If you attempt to have an open discussion with a groupthink Individual, usually they will become defensive and angry They live in denial but on some level their conscience Knows that what they cling to exists on shaky ground They reject facts in favor of half-truths and lies Groupthink like corporate thinking, has no conscience Only human beings have a conscience And yet if they are disconnected from their internal core Or in psychological parlance exhibit the traits of a sociopath They cannot acknowledge the existence of their conscience They have no concept of love, except narcissistic self-love It’s easier to say what love is not than what love is One thing is for sure, love is not fear, and they live in fear secretly I hope you are not caught in groupthink I hope you listen to your own conscience and are a discerning person We all have internal references to aid us in discernment To become fully human choose a meditative/contemplative practice Spend a few minutes each day in silence, be still, embrace Isness Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit Live a balanced life, discover your true self, you are love

George G
