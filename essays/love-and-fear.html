LOVE AND FEAR

By George Gondron

LOVE AND FEAR All of our emotions fall under the general headings of Love and/or fear Our preference is manifested through our behavior Through our communication style Through our internalized thoughts and feelings Through every aspect of our lives An outsider observing our culture Would have little difficulty realizing We are a fear-based culture as demonstrated Through all our various media forms Witnessed in our personal communication And our preference to finger-point and blame We judge before we listen We start with a closed versus an open mind Worry, anxiety, apprehension, and a sense of unease All fear-based, all stressors in our lives Each day holding an underlying sense of dread There is nothing positive or affirming When life is fear-based Virtually every addiction and impulse disorder Has as its beginning, in and with some form of fear Low self-esteem, a poor self-image Fear-based When life is ruled by fear, conscious or unconscious There is no hope no potential for the positive For everything is seen through a negative lens Love on the other hand Always sees the possible, the potential, the hope Hope for a better tomorrow, a brighter future Love gives us the power to change Fear’s power is to keep us stuck Clinging to that which is of no use to us, to anyone Our existence is a loan to future generations Make that loan one of love, not fear Through your example break the chain of fear Love sustains all of life, fear defeats life Promote love, reject fear

George G
