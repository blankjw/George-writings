MAKE IT COUNT - Copy

By George Gondron

MAKE IT COUNT Life We only have one, make it count There is no valid reason to not become your authentic self We are each unique with special gifts No two of us alike We are only drawn together in our sameness In order to grow together in our differentness Most will never discover their true creative nature Following the dictates and influences of a society Not focused on creativity, relationships, and connectivity But on consumption and production Focused on the process of ‘human doing’ Exclusive of our true nature, ‘human being’ A ‘throw away’ culture where things have more value Than people What we do becomes more important than who we are When we first encounter the stranger Our mind begins to form an opinion, absent of facts Opinions influenced by race, color, ethnicity, profession, Education, place of residence, marital status, religion Etc., etc., etc. Assumptions precede facts, imagination becomes the truth Avoid that which gets in the way of knowing another No need to ask ‘where are you from, what do you do, and What’s your last name?’ Not until we fist know ourselves, fully, deeply, intimately Will these questions never matter What matters is our human connection, our commonality For in truth our hopes, dreams, and wishes are similar On that level we are more alike than unalike Value yourself enough to take the journey of self-actualization Spend a few minutes each and every day in silence With stillness in the presence of your image of a Creator Your inner core, your internal reference is as important As your intellect, your egoic sense of self Learn to listen to the whispers and yearnings of your Heart, soul, and spirit – they patiently await your attention

George G
