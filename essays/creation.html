ESSAY

CREATION

By George Gondron

CREATION What possible reason for creation? Or is this all accidental, just happenstance? A seed cast into the void, for no apparent purpose Creating beauty beyond imagining Mysteries outside the realm of comprehension The creative drive, the inventive impulse Arises from a love to express Thus does the human soul To simply express itself Love does that And in Love's creation it does not distinguish It includes all, nothing is excluded Unconditional in every facet, all a precious gem The parts exist for and within the whole The whole an expression of the Creator An ever-evolving creation on all dimensions A paradox, a mystery, a riddle This universe, emerging from Love How could Love not love what Love has created? It is impossible for Love not to love It is impossible for creation to cease For love, like life, never ends It only begins Death does not end life, it transitions life A mystery still yet to be learned But a trust revealed by Love The glue of creation, nothing less than love An impossible accident, an intentional choice Designed by Love, sustained by Love Creation is, Love Love is, relational

George G
