ESSAY

PERFECT

By George Gondron

PERFECT By what measure is perfection determined? Paradoxically by an imperfect mind Much like being the judge for my own misdeeds Perfection as a mental construct Is always determined by imperfection Perfection of the heart is pre-existent How could love, mercy, peace, and justice Not be perfect? Ah, you say, justice is not always perfect Peace is not always lasting If so, then it was not justice nor true peace It was merely an intellectual agreement Arrived at through an imperfect mind To be love To be mercy To be peace And, to be justice Are not bargained negotiations Nor agreed upon compromises They are innate qualities of the heart The inherent nature of the spirit and soul They are divine in origin Instilled in the human Known through being loved Accepting mercy Being at peace And, experiencing justice We are not humans seeking to become perfect We are divine in origin seeking to become humans Discover what is already given

George G
