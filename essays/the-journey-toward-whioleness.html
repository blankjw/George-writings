THE JOURNEY TOWARD WHIOLENESS

By George Gondron

THE JOURNEY TOWARD WHOLENESS If we focus on the destination, we miss the journey And it is in the journey where life happens A path of growth and maturity That incorporates our physical, intellectual, emotional And spiritual nature – all must be included for wholeness In our journey, our past is only a reference, the future our hope And the now, the present moment is our teacher But unfortunately, most miss the now Living in the past from regret, anger, or hatred Living in the future from insecurity and fear Life is not a problem to be solved It is an experience to be embraced with open arms Life is best described as relationship, we are relational beings The most important of those relationships Is with other human beings, but there is a problem Since we live in a society that focuses on the economic I.E. production and consumption, in that scenario People beyond our immediate circle are secondary There is little understanding that we are connected To all humanity, without exception As well, being focused on consumption Most of our understanding is a matter of ego, of the mind And a dualistic mind at that, dealing with polar opposites Right/wrong, good/bad, either/or, never both/and Everything becomes literal as opposed to situational What is missing in a consumption society is self-awareness With respect to who we are as individuals beyond our thoughts One path that is beneficial to a process of self-discovery Is a meditative/contemplative practice A few minutes each day of silence, stillness, and Presence Presence being your image of God, of Universal Consciousness Choose a short mantra, no more than three or four words I.E. breathe in wellness to yourself, breathe out wellness to others With consistent and committed practice your mind will quieten You will become aware of the whispers of your heart, soul, And spirit – they reveal your true self beyond the egoic self They hold your truth, have your answers, your journey’s guides

George G
