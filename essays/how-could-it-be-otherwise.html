HOW COULD IT BE OTHERWISE

By George Gondron

HOW COULD IT BE OTHERWISE? At times, this gift of free will seems like a curse I'd rather not chose Or, I'd rather not own my choices I can easily be angry at God for this gift Surely it would be more to God's liking if I only choose what is best God could do that, you know? But then, that's not what Love does Love only gives, freely How could it be otherwise? Yes, but if we didn't have free will, life would be so much better No poverty, no hostility, no disease, no discord Then again, love would not be what love is either Somehow, wanting to love, rather than having to love seems the better choice How could it be otherwise? I imagine God realized the risk of giving us free will Evidence of unconditional love The possibility of great joy, deep pain The agony and the ecstasy For what we feel, God feels as well How could it be otherwise?

George G
