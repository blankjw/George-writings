THE VEST EVIDENCE

By George Gondron

THE BEST EVIDENCE Evidence of genuine love can only be manifested through Behavior, through action, words are only labels Who we are in relationship with one another is always The result of our behavioral interactions, our connection What we do or do not do for each other Especially beyond our too many words, is proof of our love Being present to one another has much more meaning And significance than words alone, always And therein lies one of the major problems in relationships How to be present to each other, how to be good listeners How to remove our armor and be vulnerable, to be open And not defensive, how to be willing to learn from our partners Our significant others, our voice is not the only voice Healthy relationships are hard, they are messy, and If we lose ourself in another, they can be deadly For when you lose yourself, you have lost everything In Hamlet there is the famous line, ‘to thine own self be true’ The importance of that short sentence cannot be over stated For we must be true to ourselves to be true to others But far too many put themselves second And that will never end well In our desire to love others, we must love ourselves as well With the same intensity and determination For in truth we can only know another to the depth we Know ourselves, our strengths and weaknesses To be healthy ourselves we must have balance We must be more than just producers and consumers We must have a connection to our inner core, our internal reference We are not just our thoughts and feelings, we are so much more We are the energy and stardust of the universe, everlasting Do this, adopt a meditative/contemplative practice Spend a few minutes each day in silence, be still, be present A new context for new experiences Empty your ‘monkey mind’, focus on your breath, or a short mantra Breathe in wellness to yourself, breathe out wellness to the universe Listen for the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, they wait

George G
