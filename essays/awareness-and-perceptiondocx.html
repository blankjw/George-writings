AWARENESS AND PERCEPTION

By George Gondron

AWARENESS AND PERCEPTION A famous quote from Thomas Aquinas ‘Whatever is received into something is received according to the condition of the receiver’ In other words, if a person is open to new information Information beyond their current level of consciousness Or awareness Then there is potential for change, for new awareness If their mind is closed the new information is rejected A mind that is closed suffers from a lack of perception And consequently can only respond in a defensive manner The potential for growth, physically, psychologically, and Spirituality happens from an open and curious mind There is never a time, an age, a circumstance When human potential, human growth is not possible It’s always a matter of the mind The word ignorance is the condition of being unaware Or uninformed, having a lack of knowledge New knowledge, new awareness, increased consciousness Only happens when one has an open mind The greatest enemy in our modern culture is ignorance There has been a trade-off between intellect and emotions And those with power and control have learned It’s easy to control by stirring up the emotions They don’t suggest we think, reflect, question, or doubt They point to the next ‘shiny object’ to distract And in most cases that ‘shiny object’ is nothing other than Criticism of their opponents, without valid proof Only their words, words of blame, and alternative facts This path will never accomplish anything promising Just a repetition of the same dysfunction, the same chaos The same illusions and misinformation that separate and divide Add this to your path, a daily meditative/contemplative period A few minutes of silence, stillness, in the presence of Presence A time to empty your mind of its incessant thoughts A time to listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit To your true self, your innate truth given at birth Become aware, be perceptive, you hold the truth inside

George G
