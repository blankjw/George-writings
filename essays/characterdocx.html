CHARACTER

By George Gondron

CHARACTER In the realm of human traits demonstrated through behavior We witness the character of the individual We see and hear through their words and actions who they are Especially when they are under pressure When their ideas, beliefs, and self-image are threatened Any argument against, reveals tell-tell signs of their morality Their honesty, their integrity, their virtue, their reputation Character also exposes one’s ability to be open or closed-minded One who is willing to set aside their own ego for understanding Or to deny obvious truth counter to their desires If we are willing to see through the smoke and mirrors If we can acknowledge the distortion of truth through their lies We will discover the true character of the individual Selfishness is always disguised as selflessness Their self-interest always precedes the interest of others, always And in deceptive ways they will attempt to destroy anyone Or anything, that they perceive to be a threat Or that stands in their way, nothing they say or do is too cruel To prevent them from gaining or accomplishing their goals They seem to relish in personal attacks towards others Demeaning, humiliating, and degrading their targets No one is protected from their abuse If you are not in lockstep with their desires, their lies You are the enemy, an object to be eliminated and put down Perhaps the more important question is this If we support a character type mentioned above What does it say about us, about our own character About our ability to question, doubt, and critically think To seek and search for the truth, rather than simply accepting What’s being offered because it stirs our emotions Not realizing we are nothing more than a means to an end And that end is to someone else’s benefit and not our own Please, connect your mind, heart, soul, and spirit, seek balance Spend a few minutes each day in silence, be still, embrace Presence Empty your mind of its incessant and repetitive thoughts Focus on your breath or a short mantra, breathe in, breathe out Listen to the whispers of your inner guide, your true self, it waits

George G
