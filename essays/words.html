ESSAY

WORDS

By George Gondron

WORDS Words in themselves have no power It is only the mind that makes that determination Consequently, what disturbs one person has no effect on another It is the person with the ‘bull horn’ The one in the ‘bully pulpit’ who knows how to disturb with words They know the most direct method to control the masses Is not through their intellect, but through their emotions Not through their ability to think, reflect, or discern But to distract, deflect, blame, and accuse Facts are irrelevant, they don’t matter once your emotions Take over and you become a ‘reactor’ and not a ‘responder’ Emotional entrapment results in a narrow-closed mind A mind that is limited in its perceptions for lack of clarity Emotional turmoil does not allow for transparency It’s like a glass full of muddy water As long as you keep agitating the water, it will never clear To accept a narrative through emotional entanglement Is to be convinced the lie is the truth, and the truth a lie Consequently, it is no wonder the current national turmoil Almost everything on social media, TV, internet, etc. Is about distraction, consumption, or a form of propaganda Truth, facts, decency, justice, and equality seem to not matter Blame, defensiveness, finger-pointing, ‘alternative facts’ They take center stage, all else dies in the wings I doubt this ‘slippery slope’, this ‘run-a-way train’ can be slowed Much less stopped or turned around, ignorance reigns For the most part we see and hear without objectivity or clarity Only because our minds are never ‘stilled’, always busy Add a meditative/contemplative practice to your daily agenda Empty your ever busy ‘monkey mind’ of its incessant thoughts Thoughts that are mostly repetitive, go nowhere, do nothing Spend a few minutes in silence, be still, be present to Presence Focus on your breath or a short mantra Your mind will wander, come back to your breath Breathe in, breathe out, rinse and repeat Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, have your answers, they wait

George G
