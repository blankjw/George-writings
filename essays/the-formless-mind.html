THE FORMLESS MIND

By George Gondron

THE FORMLESS MIND In all of creation, it is the mind that is formless The mind alone has limitless potential For the mind has the capacity to change It can be positive one moment And negative the next It can be selfless as well as selfish It can be static, stuck, and uncompromising Or dynamic, flexible, and openminded The imagination has no equal What leads us to a path of giving As opposed to a path of receiving Or a mindset of me first And you second It has to do with the severing of the short distance Between the head and the heart Of the inability to see sameness but only difference All of existence is connected But the mind only perceives separation The ‘true self’ uses the heart as its guide The ‘false self’ uses the ego The former does for others The latter does for itself Perhaps if we were mute The formless mind would be more inclusive And less limited and divided A formless mind has the ability to be creative Its unlimited potential knows no boundaries But it also can create its own prison Unable to free itself while holding the key For kindness and compassion to thrive For there to be peace, mercy, and freedom for all There must be a connection between the Formless mind and the loving heart As humans, we all have ‘hang-ups’ But we each have the power and opportunity to change Embrace that capability, change what you don’t like Use your formless mind for good

George G
