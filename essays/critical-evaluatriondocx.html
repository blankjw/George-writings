CRITICAL EVALUATRION

By George Gondron

CRITICAL EVALUATION Any belief system, sacred or secular that does not allow For critical appraisal, examination or analysis is a system Against freedom, a closed system whose requirements For membership, for inclusion, for belonging are narrow And defined, allowing for little flexibility and limited freedom The same can be said for those in positions of power Elected, appointed, or seized Fealty and loyalty at all costs – never criticize the leader Nor the leadership, the hierarchy, the ‘good ole boys’ The punishment for doing so varies Loss of job, alienation, abandonment, exiled, even death And if you’re unwilling to compromise your values, your Ethics, your integrity, you will suffer one of the above Their thirst for power has no conscience Their egocentricity has no limits, enough is never enough Their agenda is selfish, not selfless, and failure to comply Results in swift consequences, none of which are pleasant Maybe that’s why so few speak up, speak out Why so few have the courage to point out that the King Has no clothes, easier to go with the flow, don’t rock the boat When we compromise the essence of who we are At the behest of another or others We become less free, the perpetrators of our own discomfort We are relational beings, life is relationship, we are connected But if those connections are not grounded in equality and freedom For every individual, then in some way, none are equal or free Beware of attaching yourself to a person, group, or belief That does not allow for critical evaluation or analysis In a world that has more than enough resources to care for all Suffering continues, significant suffering, why? Ego, selfishness, desire, a disconnect between head and heart Take time to be, not just do Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Empty your mind, focus on your breath Listen for the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They are your true self, your teachers, they wait Bring balance into your life, touch Mystery

George G
