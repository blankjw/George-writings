BEYOND IGNORANCE

By George Gondron

BEYOND IGNORANCE There is a level of awareness beyond ignorance But not yet stupid where the obvious lies are believed Ignorance is lacking education or knowledge Stupidity is the result of being a slow learner or understanding The former is more of a conscious decision The latter is marked by a lack of intelligence I’m reminded of a poster on my wall that reads ‘The truth will set you free But first it will make you miserable’ The truth requires critical thinking, research, an open mind The necessary requirements of questioning, doubting, challenging One of the hallmarks of an ignorant mind is a closed mind A mind that is easily swayed emotionally Cares little about factual data or proven science It follows the ‘pied piper’, the carrot on the stick mentality A closed mind is motivated more by fear than love A lazy mind that doesn’t want to do the work To discover the truth, just feed it and it will believe It is a defensive mind that has a rebuttal for every challenge A passive listener, listening only to itself A mind that dwells in the past and fears the future They are legion in number and walk among us Proclaiming their righteousness and condemning all others Their greatest fear is change, they cling to their familiar Change is their enemy, or so they believe But their greatest enemy in truth is their very self The process of existence for thirteen billion years is change In all of nature, it’s we humans who resist change We have yet to learn the importance of balance Inner and outer, we are not just random thoughts We have a deep yearning for connection Please, embrace a meditative/contemplative practice Spend a few minutes each day in silence, with stillness, In the presence of Presence, your image of a Creative Source Breathe in and breathe out a short mantra, as the mind quietens Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, they wait

George G
