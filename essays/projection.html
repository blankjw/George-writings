PROJECTION

By George Gondron

PROJECTION IS The psychological phenomenon whereby we project Upon another or others, universal aspects of the human Personality we consciously or unconsciously refuse to see Within ourselves – both negative and positive aspects An emotional overreaction manifested in criticism or Judgment of another is almost always a projection I see it everywhere except within myself Projection is particularly subtle when we fail to acknowledge Our own positive characteristics, our innate gifts It reveals itself when we put others on a pedestal Admiring them for a particular talent or skill Supplanting our own talents and skills To compensate we may inflate our own abilities As if life is a competitive journey of who’s best Here’s a guarantee A conscious choice to individuate, to grow Psychologically, intellectually, and spirituality Will eventually fail without the conscious integration Of our projections, our shadow, and our angels Social media is a cornucopia of projections It is a source where we can vicariously live-out What we reject within ourselves It’s ‘out there’ not in me or us – us being group projection Like politics, religion, economic status, gender inequality, etc. When like the cartoon character Pogo, we can say I have met the enemy, and it is me We are beginning to withdraw our projections and discover They live within ourselves, they are not the other They are you and I, we and us Every thought, feeling, and action we experience is a Matter of choice – we are choosing our lives moment by moment Choose to change – embrace a meditative/contemplative path For just a few minutes each day be silent, be still, Be in the presence of Presence, Isness, Source, God A relational experience with Mystery is always transforming Connect with the innate knowing of your heart, soul, and spirit Discover your ‘true self’, your inner truth, they wait

George G
