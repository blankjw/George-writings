NORMAL

By George Gondron

NORMAL What was once an atrocity has now become normal Mass shootings followed by thoughts, prayers, and mourning Rinse and repeat, over and over and over again It seems we have become desensitized to violence Including the massacre of innocent children There is no sound argument against common sense gun legislation Especially when 90% of our citizens support universal Background checks for the purchase of a weapon As well as the banning of assault-type weapons And large capacity magazines by 70% of citizens And we all know why nothing changes, money It’s not the fear of ‘oh, the government’s gonna take our guns’ Or, we have mental health problems in this nation Hmm, doesn’t seem to be a problem elsewhere in the world Total BS, fabricated lies made up by the NRA’s lobbyists Echoed by the elected politicians receiving contributions From that same NRA, as horrible as it may sound Perhaps taking pictures of the slaughtered innocents And showing them to our Congressmen and Senators And the world, something might just change We have a gun fetish in this society, a male gun fetish We have 4% of the world’s population And staggeringly we have as well 46% of the world’s guns Arm the teachers, open carry, that’s the solution, really? It is astounding, common sense is no longer common Fear reigns, love has died, violence leads to more violence Yes, our new normal is frightening What a message for our children Read this again, reflect on the message, be objective Put aside your emotions, think critically, it’s desperately needed Bring balance into your life, connect inner and outer Embrace a meditative/contemplative practice A few minutes each day be silent, be still, be with Isness Through contemplation, we learn to pray without words We awaken a spiritual relationship with our image of God Our heart, soul, and spirit hold our truth and have our answers They contain our true self, they yearn for our attention

George G
