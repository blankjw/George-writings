Too Late

By George Gondron | Source: Too Late.docx

Too Late As I approach the corner, I see him with his cardboard “I’m hungry” sign. I turn away. I see her with her shopping cart digging through the dumpster. I turn away. The child keeps asking annoying questions. I shout at him/her. I turn away. On the TV an image appears of a starving child. I change channels. I turn away. She/he really is hurting emotionally but I don’t have the time to listen. I turn away.

Is it too late. Is it too late to turn back and say I’m sorry, please forgive me. No. It’s never too late.

George G
