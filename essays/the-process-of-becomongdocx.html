THE PROCESS OF BECOMONG

By George Gondron

THE PROCESS OF BECOMING The Swiss psychiatrist, Carl Jung, coined the term, individuation The gradual integration and unification of the self through the resolution of psychological conflicts And as human beings, none of us escape psychological conflict Our becoming, our individuation is a conscious choice if we are to become fully human and fully alive The more likely choice in our culture toward either is rejection We are confronted with far too many distractions and alternatives that on the surface appear to be more appealing than the work of personal growth and our own further development Familiarity has a way of becoming a self-made prison It takes courage to be committed to the more difficult We prefer the easy way, that in the end is the empty way If you decide to take ‘the road less traveled’, I suggest the following publications to aid in your journey Modern Man in Search of a Soul, author Carl Jung Man’s Search for Meaning, author Victor E Frankl Jesus’ Alternative Plan, author Richard Rohr, OFM New Seeds of Contemplation, author Thomas Merton, OCSC And the last, a very recent publication dealing with evolution, quantum physics, psychology, consciousness, spirituality, and theology by Ilia Delio, OSF, an amazing book The Not-Yet God – Carl Jung, Teilhard de Chardin, and the Relational Whole If you choose the path of becoming, of individuation, of connection to all that is, you will be a positive, compassionate, kind, charitable, inclusive, and deeply loving force for all humanity, I hope you do To further bring balance to your life, to connect inner and outer adopt a meditative/contemplative practice Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Presence, Mystery, Universal Consciousness, Isness, or God The name does not matter, it has always been, is now, and will always be about an experienced relationship, not an intellectual concept, between believing and knowing lies an abyss Focus on your breath or a short mantra, breathe in, breathe out When the ego resists, the mind wanders, simply rinse and repeat Listen to the whispers of your inner guides, they hold your truth

George G
