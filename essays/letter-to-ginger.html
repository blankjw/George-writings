LETTER TO GINGER

By George Gondron

Dear Ginger So sorry to hear about your health issues. I remember you in my daily prayers and wish you a speedy recovery. I love you and realize that genuine love is not just an emotional feeling but an action and and often a sacrifice when we see an obvious or even perceived need. A few years ago, probably about the time you decided not to reopen your studio, it was brought to my attention by more than one trusted source, that you were telling others that my only reason for helping you was to ‘manipulate and control’ you. That you never asked for my help over the years. I experienced a feeling akin to someone reaching inside my chest and jerking my heart out, followed by a deep sadness. Secondly there was a shattering of trust between us which I doubt, even if we had the opportunity could ever be recovered. Just to be in each other’s presence would be awkward. I never felt angry, only sad. Again, I wish you the best. I hope you have a long and healthy life and you experience peace and happiness always.

Your Father
