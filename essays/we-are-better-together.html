WE ARE BETTER TOGETHER

By George Gondron

WE ARE BETTER TOGETHER Life changes, transforms, evolves But it does so, not through exclusion Not as a result of omitting what presently exists But always including and incorporating what already is Making it better, making it stronger, making it more complete There is no 'I' in togetherness, there is 'we' There is no 'me' in wholeness, there is 'us' The only way to overcome the less than Is to become more than, together What divides us is our own inability to feel secure Insecurity, whether physical, emotional, or spiritual Is grounded in the emotion of fear, not knowing Closing the door before the unfamiliar can become Familiar Wrapping ourselves in just the known, the imagined 'safe' The 'things' that you and I gather, the people we know The same context, the same interactions, nothing changes And, as a result, what I do is right How I think, what I pursue, and what I believe All else, all others, may not be all 'wrong' But not as 'right' as myself, they don't see as clearly Don't comprehend and understand as well as I And again, There is no 'I' in togetherness, there is 'we' The kernel of love hidden within each of us Must be nurtured in order to blossom In order to experience our relational nature We learn it from our role models, our culture, our society We either learn that what's important are people Connections, interdependence, and inclusion or We learn that things, success, doing, and constant gratification Are the important pursuits of life, spoken or unspoken Genuine love begins with self first, the acceptance of self The good and the bad, the beauty and the beast It is the path, the journey of being better together Loving my total self, loving others, loving creation All as a result of grace given freely from Love Itself

George G
