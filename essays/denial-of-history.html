DENIAL OF HISTORY

By George Gondron

DENIAL OF HISTORY There has been, is now, and will be in the future A movement against historical accuracy Historical truth Let me repeat that, historical truth The current battle falls under the acronym of CRT Critical Race Theory I’d be willing to bet no one can define CRT In conjunction with CRT we are also banning books That touch on another acronym, LGBTQ+ History is important for three important reasons Understanding, understanding, understanding The most significant and accurate book Written about the history of this nation Is ‘A people’s History of The United States’ By Howard Zinn It should be required reading in every history class One would think that it’s more important To know the truth, than deny the truth But then again, we live in a nation of deniers Controlled primarily by white males, old white males That control is pushing us backward Writing new laws that are restricting hard-won rights Fought for by many minority groups in this country Every anti-movement, whether it’s banning books Or restricting voting rights is fear-based Fear-based without exception Most history books are written with a bias A white male bias, including the Bible I’m sure that last sentence will piss off a lot of people A lot of white male people Not until we are able to look at our history Without blinders, without sticking our heads in the sand Without prejudice, bigotry, and denial Will we ever be redeemed, ever become whole Examine yourself critically, become a part of the solution Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, With Presence – empty your mind, listen to your heart

George G
