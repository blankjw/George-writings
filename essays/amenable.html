AMENABLE

By George Gondron

AMENABLE The mind that is closed and unpersuadable Is a phenomenon that is more and more prevalent today It is a mind that listens only to itself Or to those of like-mind, ‘birds of a feather’ mentality The exception today is the person who is amenable Open to suggestions, open to criticism Open to discussion without bias or prejudice Likely to listen with a receptive mind Their thinking is more nondual than dualistic Life is not black and white, but situational Their understanding is not limited to only their beliefs But their understanding does necessitate valid data And not just hearsay, rumor, or conjecture They believe in the saying, ‘trust but verify’ In a nation divided, such as ours today It is much easier to sell the lie than the truth There are many sources ‘selling the lie’ If you look behind the curtain Behind the ‘smoke and mirrors’ of those that Are attempting to convince us of something Using ‘fear’ tactics There is always a payoff, a benefit for their efforts Some reward awaits if they succeed with their Conspiracy theory/theories And we are not the recipients of those rewards Of course, behind it all are three things Power, control, and wealth for the few, not the many So, no matter the story, the tale, the fantasy, the illusion Always ask yourself, what is it they are really ‘selling’ What is it they really want, and what is their ultimate goal? They are easy to recognize, they are dead from the neck down It shows in their behavior, their words are not to trusted Stay connected to your own heart, soul, and spirit They are the needed balance for the mind, the ego Without them, we become negative and fear-based as well Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Find your inner truth and healing, It is there, waiting

George G
