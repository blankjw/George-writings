A HOLE IN THE SOUL

By George Gondron

A HOLE IN THE SOUL When a belief system, by any name, fails to promote Virtues of kindness, empathy, inclusion, compassion And most of all love, what fills that inner yearning? Either those belief systems fall into the trap of motivating Through orthodoxy as opposed to orthopraxy Ot they are abandoned altogether, replaced with, For lack of a better explanation, what never satisfies The human species, ever since the emergence of consciousness Has always felt a connection, unspoken or otherwise, to a Mystery beyond explanation, an originating source Of this vast entity we call the universe That originating source has many names, some personalized Others explained as energy or universal consciousness That yearning to know beyond mere faith, an existential Experience, even for proclaimed atheists, whispers from the Deep recesses of our consciousness, patiently waiting In our Western culture we replace that yearning with the Next shiny object, the nearest distraction, the material and tangible We define ourselves through what we do, what we think With no credence at all given to the fact that we are unique As individuals and at the same time connected in our humanity We are gifts to one another, filled with creativity and potential But we are few who march to the beat of our own drummer We are few who seek to experience Mystery existentially We are few who attempt to be present to each moment of Everyday, our minds living in yesterday or worrying about Tomorrow and we miss the only reality which is now The normal of a fully human life must include Mystery Not just an occasional thought but a lived participation It has nothing to do with a formal religious organization Who’s rules, rites, rituals, doctrines, and dogmas I follow It has to do with relationship, experienced relationship A meditative/contemplative practice grants that experience A few minutes each day of silence, stillness, and Presence A time to empty our ‘monkey minds’ and focus To listen to the whispers of our hearts, souls, and spirits They hold our true self, have our answers, and patiently wait
