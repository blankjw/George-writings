DAILY CHALLENGE

By George Gondron

DAILY CHALLENGE Maybe we need to add to our normal daily routines A time for personal challenge, in some form Challenges that ‘pull us forward’ Away from the familiars that only keep us stuck Clinging to thoughts, feelings, and actions That really offers no challenge whatsoever A little doubt, a little anxiety, some unanswered questions Are a challenge to an ego that has ceased to learn Real learning Not ‘follow the leader’ learning Not learning that requires little or no thought Simply swallow whole what’s being offered Follow the one that stirs our emotions The one that makes us the ‘good guys’ And all others the ‘bad guys’ The venue is not important For they can be found everywhere And have an opinion, have an answer, on every subject And when closely examined Their opinions and answers have no factual basis at all Empty and hollow words that support their inflated egos The ‘carrot on the stick’ routine Take time to occasionally challenge your beliefs Are those beliefs a result of your own personal experience Or perhaps from proven facts Unfortunately, most people form their beliefs Not on experience or facts But rather ‘he said’, ‘she said’, ‘they said’ For those who understand the importance of learning Continued learning, not just formal education Their beliefs will change as their awareness And consciousness expands and grows The enemy to personal growth is resistance An ego that believes in its own internal dialogue Of always being right, and never wrong Take time to challenge yourself, you will be rewarded You will become more fully human

George G
