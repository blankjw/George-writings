LIFE IS RELATIONSHIP

By George Gondron

LIFE IS RELATIONSHIP Why is there life, why a universe at all? If it is intentional, what is the intent? If we suppose it was created from love And not just some experiment by a curious God Then, there can be only one intent And that intent must be relationship The Christian idea of God itself is relational Father, Son, and Spirit, outpouring circular love That which creates must have a relational nature A relational nature with that which is created How that relationship is expressed on a human level Is up to the human, through free will How it is expressed exclusive of the human nature Seems to be spontaneous and instinctive All of nature seems to know how to be itself It is only the human that can go against its nature Go against its own divine essence Seek its own godliness, its own right to power For that which is created from Love Is not a loving creation without free choice Without its right to accept or reject That which creates That is the risk of human creation That is the risk of all relationships Human and divine But at some juncture, through many human failures Breakthrough, breakout, or breakdown will occur The string will run out, the struggle will weaken Relationship will prevail It cannot do otherwise Love always wins Always

George G
