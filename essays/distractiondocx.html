DISTRACTION

By George Gondron

DISTRACTION Distraction, diversion, interruption, confusion, interference, Amusement, beguilement, entertainment, all of these Used to deflect, to take attention away from something or someone And place your attention elsewhere Don’t look here, look there, don’t watch that, watch this Don’t listen to he, she, or them, listen to me It’s called manipulation, propaganda, the spreading of misinformation And the only purpose for all the aforementioned is, control Plain and simple, for no other reason, it’s certainly not for truth For truth doesn’t need all the above Truth stands on it own, and ironically on some rudimentary level Inside we know the difference between fact and fiction Between the truth and the lie And also, ironically we don’t have the courage to challenge the lie Much easier to go with the ‘flow’, follow the piped piper Dance to the beat of someone else’s drum55 We can learn to live with ourselves and justify our own Unwillingness to seek the truth and challenge the lies After all, everyone does it, right? No one is perfect But somewhere, in the recesses of our minds there are whispers Are we willing to compromise our own integrity, our morals? Are we willing to choose ignorance over provable facts? Just watch a little social media and you’ll soon find the answer It is beyond reasonable to grasp what people will believe With no evidence whatsoever, just third-party information If you stir the emotions of the masses, they are more likely to Believe fiction over fact, the lie over the truth For the ego does not like to do its own critical thinking That’s way too much trouble, just become a sponge, much easier Drink the ‘Kool-Aid’, the flavor of the day Fear is always a better motivator than love, love demands sacrifice Fear only demands fealty, allegiance to the propagandist Within each of us is a ‘truth seeker’, a spirit of discernment A few minutes each day be silent, be still, embrace Isness Empty your mind, listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, And spirit – they hold your true self, they have your answers The truth lies within you, discover it, embrace it, live it

George G
