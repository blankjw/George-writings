OUR CULTURAL CIRCUS

By George Gondron

OUR CULTURAL CIRCUS Has our culture, our society, become a circus And we its monkeys Living under the 'big top' of distraction and illusion When the world of reality has been turned upside down And the lie is now the truth False narratives, alternative facts Are they now the normal Do we even notice, do we even care In the main ring of this three ring circus We have political dysfunction, partisanship extreme In the other two rings we have Corporate greed and individual selfishness Individualism, what matters is me first All others second Compete, don't cooperate Wall street over main street The main ring of our circus will probably remain Continue to be our main attraction for awhile Our politicians love the limelight, regardless the party Maybe our outer attractions, the smaller rings Will become complacency and blame Pretending that all is well with the world At least our little piece of it No need to be concerned, secure in our own bubble Loving our distractions, clinging to our attachments Anyway, all that's wrong and upside down Is someone or something else's fault, not ours How could we possibly be responsible We simply mind our own business And all others mind their's, suits us perfectly Don't rock the boat, right? However, what if the boat is sinking And worse, what if we can't swim Or the circus tent is burning down and there's no escape I do know this, if we don't become concerned If we don't care about this circus, if we remain monkeys We will suffer, future generations will suffer, all will suffer

George G
