DEFENDED

By George Gondron

DEFENDED Who needs defended behavior? No one What does defended behavior accomplish? Nothing Where does defended behavior lead? Nowhere When does defended behavior work? Never How does defended behavior succeed? It doesn't Why does defended behavior exist? Egoism Each question begs personal reflection Each question points to a position of doubt Each question is necessary for positive change My defended behavior erupts from suspicion My defended behavior bursts forth from disbelief My defended behavior springs from want of faith My defended behavior is driven by ego My need to be right, never wrong My need for security, protected by 'things' My lies to myself If we've lost our essence, what is there to defend? A persona that's not real, a fabrication of falseness An empty container, a hollow vessel Our reason to be lies hidden within Our purpose in life resides in connection The Voice we shutout will answer our doubts There is no basis for defended behavior

George G
