UNSPEAKABLE

By George Gondron

UNSPEAKABLE Watching the innocent wonder of a child in a new discovery Gazing deeply into another's eyes as they softly gaze back Glimpses of nature that take the breath away The recognition of the heart's unspoken yearning Sharing the pain of loss with another Softly sobbing for the hurts of a lifetime Experiences of sensed profound meaning and value unable to be expressed in words All connections to the Unutterable, connections to Existence, cherished connections to Love Ineffable, indescribable, unspeakable

George G
