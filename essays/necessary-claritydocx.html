NECESSARY CLARITY

By George Gondron

NECESSARY CLARITY Hopefully at some point in your life you will attempt To seek clarity around some experience, some interaction, Some thought, feeling, or action that perhaps is still unclear For if you do, with objectivity, without bias or prejudice I promise you will arrive at a level of understanding, of awareness And consciousness that will change you forever, for the better But unfortunately, most do not want clarity, it’s too revealing It has the power to open us up to our own weaknesses, our Own shortcomings which we seek to keep hidden, even from Ourselves, our own shadow, our own self-righteous ego Clarity, for most of us, is the unspoken enemy For on some level we know our own faults, our judgmental Thoughts, our opinions without substance, the ease with which We accept what’s offered without a second thought It fits our agenda, our character flaws, our refusal to learn Life, thankfully is filled with twists and turns They are there to teach us, to strengthen us, to make us better It’s not an easy path, nor is it meant to be, but it is necessary There’s no ‘pass go and collect $200’ or ‘get out of jail free’ card But there is the responsibility of becoming our best self Even if that means standing alone and risking rejection The path of individuation, of self-actualization leads not only To clarity but to freedom, especially freedom from fear The person who can acknowledge their weaknesses with clarity Is a person of both courage and humility Change is a constant in life, it is the only way forward To recognize the necessity of personal change is a gift But most are too concerned about changing others that they Rarely see with clarity their own need to change Our journey is singular, our path is singular, make it important To aid in your ability to experience necessary clarity Add a meditative/contemplative practice to your life Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Isness Empty your mind, focus on your breath or a short mantra Breathe in, breathe out, the mind will wander, rinse and repeat Listen to the whispers of your inner guides, heat, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, they wait

George G
