THE LANGUAGE OF THE EYES

By George Gondron

THE LANGUAGE OF THE EYES The unbelievable power of the eyes Is it their ability to so easily communicate That prevents the prolonged gaze? We so seldom look into the eyes of another What is our hesitation, our unwillingness The eyes can reveal more about us than our voice They can tell a story that can't be hidden Most struggle just gazing in the mirror At their eyes, the windows of their soul Perhaps it's the fear of seeing a stranger Someone I really don't know, but pretend to be Or maybe it makes clear what I pretend to hide There is no emotion the eyes cannot express They are the lenses of our immediacy, our now A veracity of the present moment, are our eyes Pleasure, pain, joy, and fear They smile and laugh, cry and weep, our eyes They cannot disguise the feelings within Whether excitement or distress they speak true They reflect past memories, reveal future hopes Maybe our eyes can be our redemption To rediscover what's been lost, who we are By gazing deeply, longingly, into our eyes As the reflection in the mirror gazes back at us Hold that gaze, don't turn away The feelings will rise, let them linger Breath in the experience, get out of the mind Let the Healer within be your guide Take time each day to look into your eyes Take time each day to seek the Healer within Take time to be with yourself

George G
