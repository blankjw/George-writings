FALSE EUPHORIA

By George Gondron

FALSE EUPHORIA Our ego, our sense who we and what we are Can easily become inflated when disconnected From our true self held within our heart, soul, and spirit We can assume a false euphoria Moments when we pat ourselves on the back Because we’re not like other people, we’re better than Moments when our minds are closed, we have the answers Moments when we are unwilling to confront our own shadow False euphoria lives in the realm of ‘yes but’
