THE DISINTEGRATION OF REASON

By George Gondron

THE DISINTEGRATION OF REASON In the current culture and society of the Western world Reason is dying, a slow seemingly intentional death Brought about as a result of values focused on anything And on everything except the survival of humanity Except the value of human life, in fact all of life Including the very planet on which we live The current narrative is not only designed to ignore reality But to silence those who sound the alarm of the dangers That threaten our very existence At a very early age our children learn what’s really important It can be described in many ways But in the end, it amounts to control, power, and wealth By the few over the many In the current environment, we don’t even stop to question To doubt, to wonder, to reflect, or critically ponder The true nature of what is right before our eyes We simply listen to the false narrative that appeals to our Emotional well-being which is not well at all We are guided by knee-jerk reactions devoid of reason altogether We no longer critically think, we react Soothed by the sound of the ‘piped piper’ of our choice We are led around by our emotions at the risk of ignoring Our intellect, our intelligence, our free will Unable to really define with factual information what it is We truly believe ‘I don’t know, I just don’t like him/her, or it’ Or we follow the ‘alternative facts’ and call it the truth Rational arguments, civil discussion, cooperation, Bipartisanship and conciliation have disappeared Replaced with insult, denigration, and accusation Reverting to the childish narrative of ‘he said/she said’ Behavior designed to not only belittle one another But more importantly, to silence one another Please, take time to be, to reflect, to think critically Take a few minutes each day for silence, stillness, and Presence Empty the mind and listen to the whispers of your own Heart, soul, and spirit – they patiently wait

George G
