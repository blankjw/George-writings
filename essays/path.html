By George Gondron

THE PATH

IT’S ALWAYS JUST AHEAD –THE PATH IT ONLY REQUIRES A SMALL STEP FORWARD – A LETTING GO THE LETTING GO IS THE DIFFICULT PART WE BECOME TRAPPED IN OUR ILLUSIONS OF FAMILIARITY ILLUSIONS THAT STILL LEAVE US WITH ANXIETY AND DISAPPOINTMENT, UNHAPPINESS, EMPTINESS THERE IS A DIVINE SPARK WITHIN ALL OF US THE SOURCE OF OUR BEING – CALLED BY MANY NAMES IN THE QUIET STILLNES IT’S ALWAYS AVAILABLE AVAILABLE TO HELP US BEGIN THE JOURNEY A JOURNEY DOWN THE PATH OF SPITITUAL AWAKENING FOLLOW THE PATH

GEORGE G
