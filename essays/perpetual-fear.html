PERPETUAL FEAR

By George Gondron

PERPETUAL FEAR All negative emotion has its origin in fear Just as all positive emotion is grounded in love There are no better means of controlling another Of controlling a group, no matter how large Than through fear Discrimination, prejudice, bias, segregation, exclusion All are rooted in fear In our digital age, an age of instant information How easy it is to spread that fear It's almost as if the mind thrives on being against No longer can we leave our doors unlocked, night or day No longer can the children dare be outside after dark Suspicious of the stranger Wary of the passerby that seems different from myself Fear destroys trust Turns faith into an impossible conviction It is a malaise that hangs like a cloud Not quiet out of consciousness Lingering in the dim corners of the mind We dare not venture into unfamiliar surroundings, alone The negativity of fear is like an unseen energy Trapping our spontaneity and binding our flexibility Fear, the unseen invisible enemy Through the constant refrain of 'what if' Fear can't be driven away or out by force Even though there are those who 'shout' that it can Force creates only more fear What does remove fear, doubt, angst, and aversion Only love, kindness, compassion, and empathy Love changes everything It is the most powerful force in the world Where all else fails, love succeeds To know love is to know the One who is Love That knowing we carry in our innermost being Found in the silence and stillness In each breath we take, each beat of our heart It is there, it is Life, it is Love, It overcomes fear

George G
