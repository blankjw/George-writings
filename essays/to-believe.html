TO BELIEVE

By George Gondron

TO BELIEVE ‘Believe nothing you hear and only one half that you see’ A famous quote from Edgar Allen Poe in 1845 Which today has little validity, especially with the explosion Of social media in all its many forms, we are now more likely to Believe what we see and add to what we hear because of our Emotional tendency to be motivated by fear as opposed to love What has happened? Do we no longer use critical thinking, Personal reflection, question, doubt, and just merely accept? Believing and knowing are vastly different and relying on belief Alone can lead us to very dangerous end To know is to experience, personally Again, what has happened to us that we can be so easily manipulated And fooled, believing ‘alternative facts’, hyperbole, half-truths, And obvious lies that even a child would question Can we no longer read and research reliable resources of information They’re not that difficult to find, or are we that lazy? Are we that susceptible to the propaganda and ‘gaslighting’ That cares only about manipulating and controlling us Using us as a means to an end, their end? Do we prefer ignorance over truth, over learning, and education? Do we lack understanding, awareness, consciousness, and conscience? To simply believe without experiential knowing is to be the fool Is to be part of the problem and not the solution Do morals, ethics, integrity, empathy, and compassion no longer Matter, does truth no longer matter, does love? We are leaving our children and grandchildren, through our Own dysfunctional behavior and choices, poor examples of what we Human beings are called to be The irresponsible way, the easy way, the uninformed way Is the way whose journey will end in failure If you believe, believe what you know through personal experience Adopt a meditative/contemplative practice, awaken your inner core Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Focus on your breath or a short mantra, breathe in, breathe out The mind will wander, the ego will resist, rinse and repeat Awaken your power to discern, listen to your inner guides Your heart, soul, and spirit, they have your answers, they wait

George G
