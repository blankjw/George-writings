ESSAY

POWER

By George Gondron

POWER Power cedes nothing without a struggle, without a fight The purpose of power is to control Nothing more, nothing less Control exists for one reason, fear A fear that manifests in many forms Number one being the loss of power Power and authority are not the same Power is taken, authority is given Power intoxicates, authority delegates The identifying characteristic of the powerful is selfishness History is filled with self-serving leaders Leaders interested only in their own power Control through power always leads to suffering Suffering in one form or another Pertaining to one group or another The tentacles of power reach into every aspect of life From governmental to religious And with no exception, it's always and ever about control Eventually an attempt to control every aspect of life What we do, and how, where, and when we do it Freedom is not free, it comes at a price That price is to stand against the powerful To stand against the manipulation and conditioning Against the propaganda and programming Of our every thought and action Stand against what divides and separates us as people The powerful know that the most efficient way to control Is through division, through pitting one against the other And because they point to difference rather than sameness It's easy to do Our common connection is relationship Our sameness before our differentness Love, compassion, kindness, and altruism are the enemy The enemy to the powerful If you seek to know what is not power Gaze at the naked body hanging from a wooden cross Gaze at the image of Love, find it within yourself

George G
