MASTERPIECES

By George Gondron

MASTERPIECES Works of art, awe-inspiring and extraordinary Living sculptures of flesh and bone Unique in conception and appearance Designed as living treasures of value and worth Each, a gift to the others Each a manifested form of image and likeness Each imbued with possibilities yet to be discovered A wonder unequaled in all of creation Evidence of a desire for relationship, for involvement How to respond to such a gift? How to demonstrate gratitude? How to relate to the Giver? How to be present to the ineffable? In quiet moments In the silence I am overwhelmed with the fragility of existence Protect me from the snares of harming another In words In thoughts In deeds I seek guidance to value all of life as I am valued To love as I am loved To be and let others be You, I, us, the greatest creations of the Creator Let us be worthy of our conception, of our origin

George G
