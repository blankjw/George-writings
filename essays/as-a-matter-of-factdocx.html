AS A MATTER OF FACT

By George Gondron

AS A MATTER OF FACT Not any longer Now, it’s a matter of alternative facts Since when did facts become ‘alternative’? Wait, I know – when the lie became the truth After all, if you keep repeating the lie, doesn’t it become the truth We currently live in a political environment where the lie Has become more important than facts, than reality Smoke and mirrors is now the preferred method of communication Don’t look here, look over there Please, don’t listen to he, she, or them, listen to me Don’t examine the voting record of senators and congressmen Which is readily available by the way Rather drink the Kool Aid flavor of the day being offered For your enjoyment, after all our elected officials don’t lie Right? Surely they don’t take money from corporate lobbyists Attempting to manipulate and control how they vote Ah yes, don’t do as I do, do as I say do And this my dear friends, is how our current government works We the people don’t’ matter in the least If you really do care about who benefits in the nation Check out under what administrations the following were passed Women’s right to vote, Social Security, Medicare, Medicaid, Civil Rights Act, Voting Rights Act, The GI Bill of Rights, The New Deal, The Labor Dept, The NLRB, NATO, The Peace Corp, FEMA, etc., etc. Then check out under what administration taxes for the wealthy And for Corporations were cut – the answers are easy to find If you really do care about the truth, the future, the children If you care about progress for all our citizens About equal justice under the law, which no longer seems to matter If it has not become obvious that the loudest voice The voice of fear and retribution is real the enemy, we are lost Please, adopt a meditative/contemplative practice Listen to your inner guides, they have your answers They hold your true self, not the egoic false self Each day be silent, be still, embrace Presence for a few minutes Listen, discern, understand, you matter, our future matters

George G
