WHERE LOVE ABIDES - Copy

By George Gondron

WHERE LOVE ABIDES The bonds of genuine love can never be broken Even through death, they remain We too often use the word love in describing that Which is not love at all It’s really gratification, emotional satisfaction, or Pleasure in the immediate moment As a result, we dimmish the true nature of love The power that abides in love which is not conditional Not only for others but also for ourselves For if I can’t love myself How can I possibly love others without conditions I can’t For there will be judgments, criticisms, doubts, and blame Love is not about feeling at all It’s about action, sacrifice, and willingness Willingness to put others first Which often is the exception and not the rule The ‘me’ mentality rules supreme The ‘us’ is a distant second, if second at all Our current culture is deeply divided For one underlying reason, fear What has happened to love that acts without reward That embraces the pain, anxiety, and distress of others? There are a few examples of sacrificial love There are many of desire, attachment, and selfish behavior Our lives have become all about the externals More is better, bigger is better, faster is better There is a loss of an internal reference, an inner core There is no balance between inner and outer Make the choice to spend a few minutes each day in Silence, with stillness, with Presence, your image of God A committed meditative/contemplative practice Awakens the truth we hold inside The truth and healing held within the whispers of Our hearts, souls, and spirits Gifts given to us with our birth, lost in the ego’s desires Take time to listen to those whispers, they patiently wait

George G
