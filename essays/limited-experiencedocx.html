LIMITED EXPERIENCE

By George Gondron

LIMITED EXPERIENCE There is a direct correlation between the attempt to know and the refusal to learn One leads to experience, greater understanding and awareness The other is a path of chosen ignorance and a closed mind One challenges what they take in through further education, critical thinking, openness, research, questions and doubt The other accepts without question that which stirs their emotions That which aligns with what they already believe, not what they know For their actual knowledge is very limited and narrow Only a fool would deny that our potential for learning, for experiencing more of our human potential is far greater today than one hundred years ago, yet fools we are as we absorb the content of social media as it were true and factual And the results of such behavior are far too obvious to deny The results are polarizing and divide us more than ever The two ‘camps’ are easily distinguished One wishes to not only drag us back to earlier times But wants to do so through ‘fear mongering’ and threats Their narrative is more against than for More discriminatory than inclusive, more divisive than unifying The other ‘camp’ is open to beneficial progress for all, not the few They expose the belief that life is relationship, we are all more alike than unalike, they believe in kindness, compassion and love They believe that open minds can solve any problem in conciliation There is no such thing as ‘one way’, there are many ways If you have the willingness, all information can be verified The ‘alternative facts’, the ‘gaslighting’, the false propaganda have only one purpose, to benefit the protagonist, not you Their only desire is to manipulate and control We are nothing more than a means to an end, their end I encourage you, seek your own experience, discover the truth At birth you were given the gift of consciousness, free will, and choice Please use them, they are needed now more than ever Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Mystery Focus on your breath or a brief mantra, breathe in, breathe out Listen to the whispers of your inner guides, heart, soul, spirit They hold your true self, have your answers, they wait

George G
