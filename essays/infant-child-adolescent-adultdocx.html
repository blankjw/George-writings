INFANT, CHILD, ADOLESCENT, ADULT

By George Gondron

INFANT, CHILD, ADOLESENT, ADULT The evolving of the human, the physical maturing The body's natural wisdom of survival How does the intangible evolve? How do we teach a young mind and spirit? How does one evolve emotionally, spiritually? Is it necessary for survival? Words are not teachers, words are educators Education is focused on intellectual survival In years gone by, it was show me, don't just tell me How to show the young, healthy emotional behavior How can I show them, if I'm emotionally broken? Religion has fallen into the same trap, just words Membership and words, words and membership Healthy spiritual development is demonstrative Show them what compassion, mercy and justice look like It is not about a building with walls, it is not about worship Worship can be the distracter of the real message Worshiping the messenger, not hearing the message It is about love, inclusion, sharing, and caring In the silence and stillness, my emotional healing begins In the silence and stillness there is a spiritual awakening, that goes beyond worship In the silence and stillness there is a Relationship There is new Awareness There Is

George G
