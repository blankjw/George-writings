DIFFERENCE OF OPINION

By George Gondron | Source: DIFFERENCE OF OPINION.docx

DIFFERENCE OF OPINION An increase in consciousness A new level awareness Manifests in new opinions, new ideas, new beliefs Different opinions from the norm Great statesmen, great teachers, great leaders Results of different opinions The ones who think and perceive differently The ones who arrive at their opinions through experiential knowing Not through mental gymnastics The ones that grasp reality beyond the intellect An intuition gained from observation and reflection Grounded in experiences so deep they are willing to be a Joan of the Cross Willing to be a Mother Teresa, caring for the poor Able to become a Francis of Assisi Able to see and hear what most of us miss in our ever so frantic and busy lives They listen to the small whispers as our minds are overwhelmed by incessant noise They, the few, the ones with different opinions help maintain the sanity of an insane world It is a gift to ourselves to listen and observe those with different opinions There is always, albeit small, a truth in another's belief There is always validity, in some form, of another's opinion Take time to listen, observe, discern your own inner whispers You may discover a different opinion

George G
