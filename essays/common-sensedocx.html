COMMON SENSE

By George Gondron

COMMON SENSE Never before in recent memory has common sense Been so uncommon Has the human psyche been so fractured Choosing to believe the illusions and the lies Rather than the truth and the facts Clinging to their negative emotions While their intellect bemoans their choices Common sense now dictates we live in fear rather than love We choose conspiracy over reality The deep state over evidence to the contrary What seems to motivate us are all things negative The positive nature of humanity seems to have vanished Our ability for compassion, kindness, and love Has been replaced with doubt, fear, and ‘what-about-ism’ It has become more and more difficult to have An open-minded discussion We hear and see only what we believe But not what we have experienced Our lives are motivated by fear as opposed to hope Never suspecting we might just be wrong ‘They’re coming to get our guns’ Tell me, when has that ever happened I do know this A life that does not question, doubt, reflect, learn, And critically think, is not life, it is living death Here’s a fear you can reflect on Is your right to vote being threatened? Are your earned benefits of Social Security, Medicare, Medicaid Being considered for reduction or even elimination? Pay attention, they are being suggested Kind, good, well-meaning people are not the problem Corporate interests, power, and wealth are the problem I urge you to spend a few minutes each day In silence, with stillness, in the presence of Presence Empty your mind that you might hear the whispers Of your heart, soul, and spirit -your inner truth Awaken and discover your ‘true self’, it has your answers

George G
