THE JOURNEY OF AUTHENTICITY

By George Gondron | Source: THE JOURNEY OF AUTHENTICITY.docx

THE JOURNEY OF AUTHENTICITY The human struggle to be authentic is one free of deception Focused on the integration of unconscious content into consciousness Content that often deals with our own Individual ‘shadow’ And that shadow material can be both negative and positive Incorporating not only our shortcomings but our gifts as well The coming to consciousness is one of unconditional acceptance Acceptance of the good and bad, the positive and negative It is the realization that authenticity is always a matter of both/and Not simply either/or, it will include the full spectrum of Human emotions, the deepest love, and unbearable suffering Both lead us to become fully human and authentic, believable, Credible, genuine, our words and thoughts match our behavior Inauthentic people are easy to spot What they say and what they do don’t match They are selfish versus selfless people Or as we say in Texas, ‘all hat and no cattle’ They are the ‘don’t do as I do’, but ‘do as I say do’ people They have an answer to every problem And of course, the answer always benefits themselves Another clue in recognizing inauthentic people They lack humility, they have inflated egos They are never wrong, he, she, it, or they are to blame Their words of kindness and compassion are false Hiding behind a make-believe smile or sarcastic laughter They are only satisfied if they are in control Maintaining a position of authority and power The perfect example of a ‘wolf in sheep’s clothing’ The puppeteer pulling the strings, the Wizard of Oz behind the curtain And when things go awry, it’s never their fault Pointing a blaming finger elsewhere, never at themselves The best we can do as individuals in the human family Is to become authentic ourselves Improve on our kindness, compassion, sharing, and caring Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, and Presence Empty your ‘monkey mind’ and learn to focus Breathe in, breathe out a short mantra, rinse and repeat Listen to the whispers of your inner angels, they patiently wait

George G
