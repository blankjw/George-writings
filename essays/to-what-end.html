TO WHAT END

By George Gondron

TO WHAT END To what end do we close our ears to obvious lies To what end do we close our eyes to humiliation To what end do we accept without question That which demands to be examined and verified To what end will we continue to follow like sheep The shepherd dressed in wolves clothing It baffles the human mind what we are willing to tolerate And accept that is so destructive and polarizing We have become fools being duped by a con artist Who could care less about us as individuals, as persons We are nothing more than a means to an end An end where only a few will win and most will lose And in truth, those few are also dispensable To what end trust a failed business person To what end support a political party that benefits the wealthy That supports tax cuts for profitable corporations There is a saying If it quacks like a duck, waddles like a duck, and fly’s like a duck It’s a duck If you don’t know the definition of a sociopath, look it up And if you do know, and you still support the ‘duck’ You are not part of the solution, you are part of the problem How can anyone believe life would be better under the Auspices and control of the ‘duck’ and his MAGA group? It defies all intelligent reason, all logic, all common sense As the saying goes, ‘it’s cutting your nose off to spite your face’ There is no logical reason to follow a ‘pied piper’ Who’s only interest is his personal agenda, to feed his ego To what end follow what will assuredly lead to the downfall Of our democracy, throw away our Constitution To take a cancerous path that eventually kills its host Stop and think, educate yourself, don’t drink the Kool Aid Spend a few minutes each day in silence, be still, be present Connect to your inner core, your internal resources Trust your intuition, your own discernment process Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, have your answers, and they wait

George G
