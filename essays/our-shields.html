OUR SHIELDS

By George Gondron

OUR SHIELDS We shield ourselves from what we don't want to know From what could make us better as a person We build our protective walls To keep out and push away That which may challenge us That which may move us from our familiar Some of us have stronger shields than others Some even able to resist until death It's not a mystery, it's a very solvable problem But you see, it is our nature, our ego's nature To fight against rather than fight for Our own wellbeing Our egos have us convinced to remain within our prison Our self-constructed barriers in order to be safe But the lie is this Clinging to the familiar, unwilling to let go Refusing to risk or be vulnerable Is a self chosen path oppositional to our true nature It is not safe at all, it's very dangerous Always against and seldom for Against change, against inclusion, against equality And God forbid, please don't make us think That's the last thing the ego wants us to do, think Don't think, don't question, don't doubt, don't ask Just follow, be part of your chosen crowd Maintain your shield, protect yourself at all times You see our shields work two ways Not only do they prevent something from coming in They also keep anything from within escaping The innate abilities of our heart, souls, and spirits That remain unheard at the ego's insistence You want to let go of your shield? Learn to be silent, be still, experience Presence Discover as a result of new awareness and consciousness The wonder that you truly are, a part of the Whole Your capacity for kindness, compassion, and for love Know your relational connection to everything, to everyone

George G
