UNDERSTANDING

By George Gondron

UNDERSTANDING Understanding is a gift, a promise, or a genuine search Maybe all the above and more But for sure, understanding does matter Where there is no understanding, no clarity, assumption creeps in Opinions arise with no valid or logical basis And of course, you remember the ole saying about opinions, right? Yep, we all have them, along with something else Almost always what remains not understood becomes misunderstood For our imaginations tend to reject what we don’t understand And that rejection will usually lead to fear, to something negative For after all, a lack of understanding is not our fault But in the end, it really is If we’re open minded and willing to learn, understanding is possible And once we have some amount of understanding we can reject fear We may not like the understanding, but that’s not the issue The issue is to be better informed, better able to decide Better able to reach some modicum of clarity pro or con The only other option is to remain ignorant and assume How we learn, how we obtain knowledge matters greatly To use social media as our only source for learning is dangerous For social medias has no filters and requires no effort There is a saying, ‘everything worth having takes effort’ It’s a four-letter word spelled ‘work’, not ‘luck’ So those who really want to know are critical thinkers They question, doubt, search, discern, and research They’re not just sponges, being spoon fed their emotional desires They are seekers, risk-takers, believers of truth and integrity Unwilling to accept out-of-hand what’s offered without question Unwilling to chase the ‘carrot on a stick’, the ‘pied piper’ The one with the biggest megaphone or bully pulpit We must remember opinion is the lowest form of knowledge Start a meditative/contemplative practice, balance to your life Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Isness Empty your mind, focus on your breath or a short mantra Breathe in, breathe out, your mind will wander, rinse and repeat Listen for the whispers of your inner guides, they have your answers Your heart, soul, and spirit hold your true self, they wait

George G
