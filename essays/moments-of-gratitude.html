MOMENTS OF GRATITUDE

By George Gondron

MOMENTS OF GRATITUDE I reflect more now than then I'm more present now than I was Every failure Every success Every experience, now a moment of thanks I look back, not with regret I look back to remember To remember lessons missed as well as learned Memories, moments that teach Teach what not to repeat Memories, moments that are cherished Cherish what's important, what's meaningful People are the difference, make the difference All paths cross for a reason Perhaps unknown in the moment An experience of small significance With undeniable results One soul touches another Both changed forever The thread of life continues through encounter Encounter with other lives, other places, other times Creating moments of gratitude Grateful for life Grateful for being Grateful for Love Grateful

George G
