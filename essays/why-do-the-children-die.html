WHY DO THE CHILDREN DIE

By George Gondron

WHY DO THE CHILDREN DIE? Why does a child die every three seconds from hunger and malnutrition? From disease that could easily be cured by inoculation. Is it because the CEO's insists that they receive their bonuses? The religious leaders must live in opulence and luxury? The dictators must have their power? The politicians must satisfy the lobbyist? The culture demands an endless cycle of production and consumption? Or does life have so little value if it's out of sight and out of mind? Perhaps the question is not worth asking. Then again, perhaps it is.

George G
