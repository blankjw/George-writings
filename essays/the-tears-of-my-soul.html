THE TEARS OF MY SOUL

By George Gondron | Source: THE TEARS OF MY SOUL.docx

THE TEARS OF MY SOUL The pain I can't acknowledge The tears I can't shed They are held in my soul, with tenderness, in silence And it is true for each one of us From the strongest to the weakest What you and I dare not show or reveal Of the hidden hurts, the invisible scars Are always held in our souls, with great care With deep compassion They never go unnoticed or forgotten by my soul, your soul
