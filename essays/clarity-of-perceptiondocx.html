CLARITY OF PERCEPTION

By George Gondron

CLARITY OF PERCEPTION Accuracy of response is directly correlated to Clarity of perception So how do we perceive clearly? If we want to respond accurately? It’s probably easier to say what clarity of perception Is not, rather than what it is Just as it’s easier to say what love is not Than what love is Clarity happens when bias, prejudice, and judgment Are absent, when minds are empty of preconceived ideas Of prior programming, conditioning, and manipulation When they can be placed aside and put on hold And honestly, there are few that can do the aforementioned Our minds are too full, our hearts are too empty This is true for sure It is impossible for the dualistic mind to see clearly Nondualism is the only way Either/or thinking doesn’t lead to clarity Only the awareness that reality is a both/and phenomenon Will one be able to discern with clarity Take this premise one step further When negativity is present, there is never clarity, never But there is judgment, selfishness, and egocentricity There is a story of a committee of men given the task of Designing a horse After hours, weeks, and months they finally arrived At a design – a camel – (they left out the women) It is not possible to arrive at clarity through ego alone When morality and spirituality are absent in the nation Clarity will never prevail, it can’t, it’s impossible The energy and knowing of the heart, soul, and spirit Are absent, are missing, are forgotten Correlated with that is also missing kindness, compassion, Peace, mercy, equality, and justice Things are more important than people – prove me wrong For your benefit spend a few minutes each day in silence, In stillness, with Presence – learn to see with clarity

George G
