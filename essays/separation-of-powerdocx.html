SEPARATION OF POWER

By George Gondron

SEPARATION OF POWER It is an astonishing fact that the founding fathers were able to draft the documents which represent our Constitution and democracy Especially the idea of separation of powers between the executive, legislative, and judicial branches of our government An idea that has currently been usurped by the weaponization of all three branches under the current administration Our freedoms, our very democracy, our way of life is slowly being eroded to a form barely recognizable from its original state And sadly, there is little evidence that it will not become autocratic Separation of power, as it was originally intended, no longer exists It has become a fantasy versus a reality, an after thought versus a truth And it seems the vast majority of the citizenry hardly notice They wear ‘blinders’, bury their heads in the sand, and refuse to see what’s behind the curtain, a pathological ‘Wizard of Oz’ A figure head controlled by more sinister characters that remain hidden in the shadows reaping the rewards of their ill gotten efforts And as each day passes we loose a little more of our humanity By ignoring what is so evident we slowly build our self-made prisons We slowly become immune to the obvious, becoming pawns on their chessboards, nothing but fodder for their selfish desires Our progeny will suffer the ‘slings and arrows’ of our ignorance One must wonder, is it too late to correct course, too avoid this slippery slope? Only if we’re willing to ‘wake up’, use critical thinking, research, question, doubt, and use the power of our right to vote will we be able to stop the ‘take over’ of our democracy and the dismantling of our Constitution If your only source of information is social media, you limit your possibilities of discovering the truth, especially in politics Add a new context to your life, adopt a meditative/contemplative daily practice A few minutes for silence, stillness, and Presence Focus on your breath or a short mantra, breathe in, breathe out The ego will resist the mind will wander, simply rinse and repeat Listen to the whispers of your inner guides, heart, soul, and 0spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, they wait They are a part of the Whole, we are loved

George G
