AWARENESS

By George Gondron

AWARENESS New awareness leads to a greater understanding Without which we take the easier path The path of judgment It’s almost as if we have a predisposition to judge Without understanding, just a ‘knee jerk’ reaction Rather than an experiential educated response We seem to have lots of opinions without supported evidence After all, if it’s on social media it must be right New awareness is foundational to becoming fully human To maintain an objective both/and approach to reality To be able to see the forest and the trees To seek awareness is the catalyst for new possibility For it is not that we can see and hear But how we see and hear that matters It is only through clarity of perception That we can respond with accuracy, with unbiased objectivity It’s easy to judge, to express prejudiced opinions Easier than to have critical thinking, to reflect and research We live in a culture where distraction rather than being present Seems to be our ‘go to’ behavior Especially in how we value all human connections To point fingers, to blame, to deny To express in our thoughts, feelings, and actions That the problem always lies outside of ourselves For we are faultless as we thrive on self-deception Here’s a new suggestion for greater awareness Spend a few minutes each day in a committed practice Of silence, with stillness, in the presence of Presence A meditative/contemplative practice that awakens Our innate nature that was given us from birth Our internal references, our internal core The truth and understanding held within our hearts, Our souls, and our spirits The wisdom we knew as children is now buried through ego needs Buried through external influences at the cost of internal neglect Listen to the yearnings, the whispers of those internal resources They hold all the answers, they are your true self, listen

George G
