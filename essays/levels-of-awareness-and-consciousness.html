LEVELS OF AWARENESS AND CONSCIOUSNESS

By George Gondron

LEVELS OF AWARENESS AND CONSCIOUSNESS Awareness and consciousness grant us the abilities To comprehend, to understand, and to know But without the conscious decision to learn they are useless Then of course how do we define learning Many believe that simply attending to our favorite Social media source is all that’s required Just take in what’s offered with no filter, no discernment process And suddenly we ‘know’ the unvarnished truth No critical thinking, no fact checking, no questions, no doubts The perfect scenario for the manipulation and control of Our thoughts, feelings, and actions It has been theorized for decades that we use approximately Ten percent of our brains, allowing for ninety percent growth Just as the universe continues to expand and evolve So too, are we invited to become more fully human To expand our limited horizons, our brain power And become more than just sponges with no filters To accept the significance of the gift of being human Is also to accept the responsibility, to become more To continue to expand, to grow, to become more aware That responsibility asks of us to be inclusive First, of all our gifts, not just our cognitive abilities To connect our brains with our heart, soul, and spirit To become aware of our innate inner core given at birth The first internal organ to form in the fetus is the heart The same cells found in the brain are also found in the heart One would suspect that the heart is more important than Just being a blood pump Since recorded history authors have written about ‘heart knowing’ In our Western culture we lack balance between inner and outer We are all about the externals I urge you to embrace a meditative/contemplative practice For just a few minutes each day empty your ‘monkey mind’ Be silent, be still, be in the presence of Isness, of I Am Learn to listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit Learn what it means to be a human being, not just a human doing Discover your true self, discover your truth, they wait

George G
