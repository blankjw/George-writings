IN THESE TIMES

By George Gondron

IN THESE TIMES The loss of personal freedom begins very slowly It can be something as mundane as historical teaching Who decides what needs to be taught to the young? When certain historical facts are eliminated from The history books, a little freedom has been lost Governments. religions, and educational institutions In the past and still today decide what is worthy of Publication and what is not, and freedom is lost When truth is compromised, a little freedom is lost When facts become alternative facts When science no longer matters When values are more about me than you When ethics are more about us than them When we no longer listen to our inner truth A truth that never lies Truth is compromised, freedom is lost And who is responsible for this loss of freedom We are, we all are Allowing others to speak for us Allowing others to determine what is worthy to be taught Personal freedom is always given away to the powerful To the deceitful, the manipulative, the selfish, always For the leader who is not selfish Insists that each of us maintain our freedom Love is about freedom Fear is about loss of freedom Those who motivate with fear do so for one reason Control and power Whether that power resides in one individual or a group Secular or sacred, matters not, it's about control It's easy to recognize those who insist on their freedom But not on yours, simply listen to their words The responses of 'yes but', 'you should', 'I don't care' Words that indicate a closed mind, not an open mind We lose our freedom listening to the 'naysayers' Fight for your freedom, everyone's freedom, fight for truth Chose love, not fear

George G
