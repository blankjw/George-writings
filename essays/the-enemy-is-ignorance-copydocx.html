THE ENEMY IS IGNORANCE - Copy

By George Gondron

THE ENEMY IS IGNORANCE In a nation filled with information overload ignorance reigns Social media provides us with an unlimited array of choices Covering every imaginable topic the human mind can conceive And almost one hundred percent of the time Most will choose what stirs their emotions, not their intellect As a result, we cease to reflect, question, doubt, or critically think We merely absorb what is offered willingly Having an uninformed mind that is easily manipulated Simply taking in information does not make one smart, intelligent It conversely leads one to be fooled, closing their minds To anything and everything that is counter to their emotional Attachments, enamored by conspiracy theories, alternative facts To ignore the obvious is to suffer the consequences There is no positive outcome from remaining ignorant Ignorance is easily remedied if we are sincere about learning If we care about our own personal growth and individuation There is no ‘off button’ to learning, learning is forever But living in a culture whose mantra is ‘produce and consume’ There’s little time left for honest critical learning It’s so much easier to just be spoon-fed by you know, Those that insist we stay on the ‘treadmill’ of consumption Most people identify through their thoughts, their ego desires Which is not the same as their intellect at all Not to mention their emotional intelligence Being ‘knee-jerk reactors’ as opposed to ‘reflective responders’ There is nothing more important than one’s own journey Of self-discovery, and few they are that pursue that task We each are a gift to humanity, but few unwrap the package To reach the fullness of our human nature we must connect To our own inner core, our internal reference Please, adopt a meditative/contemplative practice Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Presence being your image of Mystery, Isness, God Focus on your breath or a short mantra, breathe in, breathe out The mind will wander, simply rinse and repeat Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They are your true self, have your answers, and they wait

George G
