By George Gondron | Source: MOMENT BY MOMENT.docx

S/HE MOMENT BY MOMENT

S/He is the thinker, we are the thought – moment by moment S/He is the dancer, we are the dance – moment by moment S/He is the singer, we are the song – moment by moment S/He is the narrator, we are the story – moment by moment Without God, there is no thinker, dancer, singer, or narrator Without God, there is no I, you, we, or us Without S/He, there is no moment by moment

George G
