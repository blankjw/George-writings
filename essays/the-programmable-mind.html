THE PROGRAMMABLE MIND

By George Gondron

THE PROGRAMMABLE MIND The overwhelming availability of social media In its many forms, is a blessing and a curse For those who understand how the mind operates Especially in our Western culture Our minds gravitate primarily toward Fear on the one hand, or love on the other Through a negative outlook toward life The conspiratorial mind Or a positive attitude, the possibility mind One of the great weaknesses in our culture Is our refusal to learn beyond our formal education It is so much easier to be ‘fed’ Through all forms of social media Than it is to think, to doubt, to question, to research To be critical in our analysis of any and every subject We follow the narrative, the person, the group That stimulates and stirs our emotions For example, look at politics Liberals are more concerned about giving Conservatives are more concerned about maintaining One more concerned about progressing The other more concerned about the status quo It’s easy to control the individual, the group, the crowd When you understand what stirs their emotions What attracts them and holds their attention Fear is opposed to compromise, conciliation, Cooperation, conceding, and concession In other words, fear operates from a closed mind Love welcomes all the above with an open mind Fear is exclusive, love is inclusive Religion is no exception to the above narrative For a religion that espouses love on its terms Is nothing more than disguised fear To insist that you must think, feel, and act In a particular manner, is fear-based Empty of love, regardless of the arena Be a critical thinker, use love, reject fear

George G
