SAFE ZONE

By George Gondron

SAFE ZONE Life has only one safe zone It is where love abides Love that is not conditional Found within the human heart and soul Outside of love there is no real safety In fact, outside of love, there is only opportunistic action Regardless of its origin or purpose or good intent Actions formed outside of love have winners and losers Anything done in the name of love that harms another Is never love Whatever is perpetrated in the name of love That excludes another, is not love Safe zones of love Do not demean, segregate, divide, or belittle others They do not allow for callous judgment or discrimination For genuine love doesn't know how to do that It is too unfortunate that many religious systems, beliefs Do not demonstrate genuine love, but only profess the same It is in their words, absent in their actions If we could not speak, utter a sound We would soon discover those that love And, those that do not So, in a certain sense, love is always tangible It is a 'hands on' experience A demonstration through action A gentle smile, a knowing wink A squeeze of the hand, an arm around the shoulder Giving more than receiving, a helping out-stretched arm A labor, called love, lifts up and unites Safe zones contain peace, mercy, justice, equality and freedom For that's what love is We are born with an internal 'safe zone' Awakened and found through silence, stillness, and Presence And it never fails us, for Love cannot fail Love is life, life is relationship, relationships of 'safe zones' Find your 'safe zone', find your relationship It is there, waiting, patiently waiting

George G
