WHEN OPPORTUNITY KNOCKS

By George Gondron

WHEN OPPORTUNITY KNOCKS Much more often than not We participate only in what we selectively understand And we miss so much Every thought, every feeling, every sensual experience All have meaning, all our potential teachers All, too often rejected out of hand Lack of understanding is always a choice It is never a given Understanding brings clarity, not necessarily agreement The opportunity to understand another Brings new awareness and consciousness Expands the possibility of conciliation and compromise Disagreement doesn't automatically mean division It doesn't mean the only solution is separation But the refusal to understand only breeds hostility Creating a barrier that focuses on difference And not a bridge that sees our sameness The process of all learning, of all understanding Has no limits or boundaries of time or distance No demarcation that states, so sorry You've reached your capacity to experience anything more There is a terminus for your continued understanding Refuse to listen to that 'voice' It's only the 'voice' that must be right, never wrong There is never a time to cease our learning Never a moment to not understand It's always, always a matter of choice The choice to be safe and always secure Is a cowards choice that disguises a pain A pain only soothed by taking a chance When next there is a knock on the door The door of your senses, your thoughts, and your feelings There is a possibility to discover something new Don't miss the chance, don't let it pass by Don't let it be a missed opportunity One that may never return

George G
