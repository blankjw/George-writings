OUR INNER BEING - Copy

By George Gondron

OUR INNER BEING It is within, hidden in the recesses of our being Universal truth dwells What we call the truth of the mind, the ego Is subjective truth only The truth of limited consciousness and awareness Limited through our experiences Limited through our resistance to learn And become more fully human, more alive We settle far too easily in this journey called life For the mundane, the so-called normal Our ability to learn, to grow, to evolve Is all dependent upon our choices There are no limits, no barriers, no boundaries To restrict our potential Only ourselves, we alone are responsible The door is there to be opened, it’s not locked But only we can turn the handle Only we can make the decision, the choice To settle for what is as it is, are move beyond Thinking won’t make it so Filling our minds with third-party information With social media, won’t make it so It is the integration and unification of our deeper self That makes it so That yearning that we all have to know To know what connects to that same yearning A yearning that can only be experienced Can only be known in our quieter moments When our ‘monkey minds’ aren’t doing their usual ‘thing’ Which is mostly repetitive in nature day after day There’s a reason most people avoid silence and stillness In a deliberately chosen manner, let’s stay busy I promise you this, you could not benefit yourself more Than to develop a consistent daily meditative/contemplative practice – a few minutes each day of silence, stillness, with Presence, a time to empty the mind To be present – hear the whispers of the heart, soul, and spirit

George G
