THE GIFT OF STORY

By George Gondron

THE GIFT OF STORY We have only one, one story, our life It is not in the telling of my story that is my gift It is in the listening to your story, that's my gift It is in the telling of one's story, something unknown seems to arise Something we have not felt before A new awareness, a new consciousness, a new connection Told in a sensed atmosphere of safety, in trust Like a piece of a puzzle, once lost but now found It is risky to tell one's story, in honesty, no pretenses No hidden agenda, no expectation Revealing the wounds that have never healed The dreams never fulfilled, the love never received A history of conditional existence, yearning for unconditional acceptance I listen attentively with focus and concentration Listen to the meaning behind the sometimes painful, sometimes joyful words Our commonalities are crystal clear Your story, my story, so similar, just different players We are all connected in this journey of life How often we forget our commonalities Each a piece of the universal puzzle, a piece of the whole One life One story One journey One dream No end, for eternity Hidden in the unconditional love of the Lover

George G
