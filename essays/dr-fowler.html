ESSAY

DR FOWLER

By George Gondron

May 20, 2023 Ms Alexandra Please forgive me for addressing you as Alex. When I first called I was told I needed to talk to Alex. My sincere apologies. I’ve enclosed two separate mailings from Humana to which I have not responded. I did have two separate phone conversations regarding the cost of Jardiance early on and was told by two separate representatives that if they determined I was eligible, the cost for a 90-day supply of Jardiance would be approximately $95. My current drug regimen is Metformin, Atorvastatin, Hydrochlorothiazide, and Losartan. All four are less than $20/month. My A1C was 8.2 when I first met with Dr. Fowler approximately 18 months ago. Over that time period, it dropped to 7.9 but is now 8.1. The Dr encouraged me to exercise which I have attempted to do as an eighty-three-year-old, but because of the cold sweats I experience every night I’m only getting about four to five hours of sleep each night. Interrupted sleep. Dr Fowler has prescribed Trulicity, Farxiga, and Zanubia in the past but they all exceeded $500 per month with my insurance. There will not be a generic form of Jardiance available until 2025. I guess I’ll have to stick with Metformin until then. I do appreciate all the time and effort you’ve extended to help me. If there’s anything I do further, please let me know. Respectfully, George Gondron
