SUCCEED

By George Gondron

SUCCEED I’ll share a little secret with you To succeed in the world today All you need to do is blame and complain, that’s it And as a result, you don’t need to change at all We can all remain stuck in our familiar ways Stuck in our thoughts, feelings, and actions Stuck in our dualistic thinking, we are right, they are wrong It’s said that a ‘rut’ is nothing more than a grave With both ends open The resistance to change is a ‘rut’ The resistance to change is to cling to blame and denial Fault always lies somewhere else, not in us A success that is progressive experienced failure first Progressive in the sense that it benefits all humanity Not just the few, the privileged, the elite What is long-lasting in positive progress Is not just the result of an intellectual effort But the result of the emotional energy needed From the heart, soul, and spirit The energy that connects us all, one to the other The energy that holds kindness, compassion, and love As necessary ingredients for any promise forward There is but one path that is necessary for success The path that leads us to becoming fully human Embracing not just our minds, our intellect But our capacity to be caring human beings Our intuition that dwells in our hearts, souls, and spirits To hold fast to the fact that life is defined as relationship With all of creation, not just with our small group A meditative/contemplative path is necessary for change A time each day for a few minutes of silence, stillness, Presence A time that leads to balance, inner and outer A time to escape our incessant and repetitive thoughts That go nowhere and do nothing, ego satisfaction only In those silent still moments, we learn to listen Listen to the whispers of our ‘true self’ held within our Hearts, souls, and spirits – they patiently await

George G
