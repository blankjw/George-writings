IT IS IMPOSSIBLE

By George Gondron

IT IS IMPOSSIBLE The definition of insanity, repeating something over and over again and expecting different results Most people live with the fantasy that what needs to change is everyone else, but not themselves And as a result, they spend much of their time and energy attempting to change others – insanity If you don’t already know, I have an amazing fact for you The only person you have the power to change is yourself Yet 70% of people who seek counseling do so to fix others Not themselves, he, she, or they are the problem You know the story, ‘if they would change everything would be great’ You see, few of us have the courage to look in the mirror and say, ‘I am the one who needs to change’ Is it because of some flaw we possess that resists change? Or perhaps the ego disconnected from our heart that is always right An ego in fact, that almost always when presented with a new option will insist on clinging to what’s familiar, even when painful Remember, choosing not to do anything is also a choice Increased awareness, deeper understanding, reflective self-criticism All necessary components for changing ourselves, not others As the great Carl Jung said, ‘thinking is difficult, that’s why most people judge’ – so much easier to blame others If we refuse to learn, refuse to educate ourselves, nothing changes And real education, real learning is experiential It’s not staring at a screen, attracted to our favorite media source The word for that is ‘addiction’, monkey see, monkey do With all the available distraction in our culture it really does take a dedicated individual to embrace personal change Because honestly, the culture would prefer you simply follow Whatever the agency, secular or sacred, just follow Here’s a suggestion, choose a meditative/contemplative practice Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Connect head and heart, awaken your inner guides Focus on your breath or a short mantra, breathe in, breathe out The ego will resist, I promise, rinse and repeat, practice Listen for the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, have your answers, they wait

George G
