MALIGNANCY

By George Gondron

MALIGNANCY There is a virulency that extends beyond the body It arises from a cancerous culture A malignant society, whose treatment Whose scheduled chemo and radiation Is an occasional dose of religious obligation To hold the disease in restraint Or perhaps it's the next gratification, the next fix A malignancy that looks through and not at Placing 'things' before people, before essence A deadliness with a blind eye and a deaf ear Ah, don't blame me, I'm not the problem I have my own agenda, neither am I the solution Therein lies the origin, the beginning The first cell of selfishness Self-indulgence that's leading to a vanishing era What was once a strong middle-class, disappearing More and deeper and wider polarization Politically, economically, religiously, relationally A culture on a slippery slope A malignancy of greed and power Destroying the fiber of democracy Is there an answer, a possible solution? To avoid this catastrophe? There is It lies in the heart and spirit of you and me In each individual, not in the collective Not in the group, not in polarization and dualism The sum of the parts that change, each of us Can change the whole, it can happen It must happen

George G
