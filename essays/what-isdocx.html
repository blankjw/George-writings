WHAT IS

By George Gondron

WHAT IS

I forgot an appointment yesterday Now I'm concerned about a project for tomorrow In the process I miss what is That's actually all that exists, what is How to be present to the moment, each moment How to see what we miss so easily How to hear what's really important The cohesive mind, stuck in the past, lost in the future Cruel words can't be unsaid An unkind act can't be repealed Past moments can't be relived The future is not now Now is, what is Did you see the children playing? Did you hear the old man weeping? Did you notice the embarrassment of the woman digging in the dumpster? Did you glimpse the fear in the teenager's eyes How very much we look past and see beyond How very much goes unnoticed It's not my concern Lost in yesterday and hidden in the future If we only understood, Isness is now Isness resides in the present moment In Isness there is no yesterday or tomorrow There is only now

George G
