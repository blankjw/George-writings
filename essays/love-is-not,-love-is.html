LOVE IS NOT, lOVE IS

By George Gondron | Source: LOVE IS NOT, lOVE IS.docx

LOVE IS NOT / LOVE IS It is much simpler to say what love is not, than what love is. There many things we can easily find that love is not Love is not exclusion, discrimination, unfairness, or bigotry Love is not selfish or fearful Love does not celebrate the failure of others, nor denigrate their opinions Love does not push others down or criticize their differences Love finds no joy in negative speculation and condemnation of others Love does not control in any fashion Love takes no advantage of the weak, the poor, the disenfranchised Love lifts up, increases hope, strengthens faith Love cares about the forgotten, the lost, the helpless Love listens, listens to all Love is always present, as near as our breath Love is the force that pulls each of us to become all that we can be There is no why to Love There is only It's existence There is no when to Love There is only It's immediacy Love, a changeless, perpetual, immutable Being No beginning, no end, always now Love Is

George G
