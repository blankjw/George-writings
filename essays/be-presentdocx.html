BE PRESENT

By George Gondron

BE PRESENT We, all of us waste untold hours living in the past And worrying about the future And in so doing, we miss the now And it is only the now moment that has meaning The now moment where life abides And the possible becomes probable And if we never change that pattern At the end of our life, we will be left with the thoughts I wish I would have And most of those ‘would haves’ are about relationships The relationship we have with ourselves as well as others When it comes to our level of awareness and consciousness There is always potential for further understanding It is a simple matter of choice That further understanding brings us toward Our potential for becoming more fully human Choosing to live in the ‘now moment Able to maneuver around the many distractions Especially the distractions so prevalent on social media When we’re present to our now moments we can act On our own behalf and that of others as well Rather than ‘I’ll do that later’ Make that call, send that text, write that letter, etc. When we’re not present to ourselves and to others What follows is a misunderstanding, connected to assumptions And almost always those assumptions are negative Unfortunately, the ego is driven mostly by negativity The heart on the other hand abides in love and seeks truth The secret is to connect mind, heart, soul, and spirit The path for that undertaking is a meditative/contemplative Daily practice of a few minutes of silence, stillness, and Presence Presence being your image of a creative Source, your God It is also the path of learning to be present to now A few minutes to empty the mind of its incessant And often repetitive thoughts, that go nowhere and do nothing A time to listen to the whispers of the heart, soul, and spirit Where truth and your true self abide, they patiently await

George G
