FREEDOM

By George Gondron

FREEDOM Freedom of spirit, spirit of freedom Both the same The spirit alone grants freedom Freedom at any price, is never freedom It's an illusion, a fantasy of the ego Ah, but the prison of the mind Appearance and comparison Denial and blame Power and control These are the prisoners of the mind Anger, envy, hatred, and greed The jailors of my cell Only the spirit can unlock the bars Only the spirit holds the key Compassion, peace, mercy and justice These alone arise from the spirit Kindness, gentleness, sharing and caring All emerge from an eternal spirit The spirit unites and never separates The spirit includes and never rejects The spirit embraces and never expels Whether prisoner or free It is all held by Love But the choice is mine alone, no one else's To live in the mind Or live in the spirit Experience both, and there is no choice There is only freedom

George G
