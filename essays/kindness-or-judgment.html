KINDNESS OR JUDGMENT

By George Gondron

KINDNESS OR JUDGMENT Sadly, our go-to posture when encountering the unfamiliar Whether that’s a person or a situation Is to evaluate or judge before being kind I often wonder how being kind first would matter How being open rather than closed would be better
