WHAT IF

By George Gondron | Source: WHAT IF.docx

WHAT IF I have, you have, we all have At one time or another Out loud, in a whisper, or just in our thoughts Asked the question, what if And then, after the question Our minds begin to wander and wonder Imagining where that what if might have led Which is always about an unanswered mystery Mystery is always an invitation to be in relationship And that in essence is the meaning of life, relationship Our 'being-ness' is integral to relationship We don't exist in a vacuum What the mind is capable of asking The 'what ifs' of our lives Has already within the question, within the asking The possible and potential answer Just as what was once only imagined 100 years ago Has now been accomplished, no longer just a 'what if' Our 'what ifs', our potentials are unlimited It is only the limits we put on ourselves We put on others, that stifles the 'what ifs' The voice that we listen to, internally Determines our possibilities more than anything else Consciousness, the gift of knowing Doesn't have an 'on/off' switch The 'on/off' switch is controlled by our own egos It is our ego that controls the willingness or unwillingness To pursue 'what if' The excuse that if it weren't for he, she, them or it I could have followed my 'what ifs' Is nothing more than the voice of a controlling ego The voice that encourages us to resist, resist change It's never the message of the heart, the soul, or spirit The heart is the dreamer of dreams, of 'what ifs' And the mind is the means of carrying those dreams to fruition But, it also can stand in the way of their fulfillment Trust your heart, follow your 'what ifs', don't suffer the loss

George G
