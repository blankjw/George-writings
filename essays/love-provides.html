LOVE PROVIDES

By George Gondron | Source: LOVE PROVIDES.docx

LOVE PROVIDES It's all here, nothing is lacking, all is within reach The problem lies not in the lack of; of anything All that's necessary for health For happiness For well-being For all of life, is readily available Has been provided since the beginning From the moment life began Your life, my life, all life Love has no other choice, but to provide For Love is always about the other So, what has happened, when did it happen How is it there are those who have not Who are not provided for when there's plenty for all Who suffer at the hands of others It really is not a mystery, not an unanswerable question It's actually very simple, the real reason When 'I' became more important than 'you' When 'we' became more valued than 'them' That's when it began, and begins, and continues That's the real reason And since it began, it has never ended And continues to this very day, this very moment And all the greatest arguments in the world Can be used to justify the intentions, the cause, the results All reduced to one and one reason only Selfishness Whether for the single individual or for the group Translated in today's vernacular as greed and power For in truth it's the lack of love, the lack of kindness The attachment to everything except love Therein lies the problem For Love is never selfish, cannot be selfish, will not be selfish Love is and will always be, selfless, giving, to excess Love knows no other way Always and forever Love provides

George G
