BELIEF WITHOUT EVIDENCE

By George Gondron

BELIEF WITHOUT EVIDENCE The most dangerous form of belief is belief without evidence For it has literally killed millions of people Innocent people whose only crime was believing differently Than those who were their killers, their murderers This form of belief is alive and well And unfortunately, it’s religious belief At different periods throughout history One religion or the other was at war Killing in the name of their professed God Death is the extreme result of those beliefs without evidence But there are numerous other actions short of death They separate and divide because of religious beliefs Discrimination, prejudice, bigotry, bias, to name a few And let us not forget the rights of women Even today they struggle to have an equal footing with men When these beliefs are held in place when they survive Who suffers, all humanity suffers How many will be shocked to discover that God may be Not masculine, but feminine How much better would humanity be if it acknowledged The power of the feminine spirit The nurturing that is natural for the feminine So desperately needed in today’s world I urge you to reflect What evidence do you have to support your own beliefs? And further, are your beliefs only a matter of your mind Only a matter of what you may have read or heard Or is your belief based on personal experience I hope it’s the latter History reveals that belief without evidence is deadly Is manipulative, controlling, and destructive for all To know is not only a matter of the mind but the heart also Pursue a meditative/contemplative path Each day spend a few minutes in silence, be still Be in the presence of Presence, your image of Isness, of God Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, and they wait

George G
