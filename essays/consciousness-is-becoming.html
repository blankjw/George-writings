CONSCIOUSNESS IS BECOMING

By George Gondron | Source: CONSCIOUSNESS IS BECOMING.docx

CONSCIOUSNESS IS BECOMING To follow the natural path of evolving consciousness for the Individual, is the journey of individuation or self-actualization To integrate conscious and unconscious content, to become whole At this juncture in our society, an increase in consciousness and awareness is individual, a choice made by each to become more As of yet we haven’t experienced a paradigm shift in awareness and consciousness for humanity as a whole, we can only hope But the vast majority don’t choose that path, preferring to cling to the familiar, the habits and routines that don’t challenge the mind An increase in awareness and consciousness is like any other pursuit we choose, whether it’s career or job related, relationship related, virtually anything that piques our curiosity or interests But here’s the dilemma, in the current environment of social media we tend to take the path of least resistance We may sincerely seek change, seek to know more, but inevitably we absorb what’s offered requiring no effort on our part We don’t use critical thinking, research, question, doubt, we gravitate to what in essence is already a built-in bias or prejudice, unknowingly In other words, we don’t measure up to the challenge of seeking what is true, it’s too easy to just take in what’s offered without any mental exertion, no risk, no reward, and no change To further complicate the issue, the first ‘link’ we click on in our social media exploration ties us to an algorithm followed next by a ‘cascade’ of new input to support our already identified preferences, and amazingly we gain support for our bias And lastly, and probably most importantly, we end up with a lot of beliefs and with no personal experience, only he said, she said Trust is a matter of experience, personal experience, not belief I wish you a curious mind, a critical mind, a mind interested in the learning that demands hard work, research, and effort I recommend a meditative/contemplative practice to bring balance in your life, awaken your innate internal resources Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Isness Focus on your breath or a short mantra, breath in, breathe out Ehen the ego resists, the mind wanders, rinse and repeat Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, have your answers, they wait

George G
