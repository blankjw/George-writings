THE DARK SIDE OF RELIGION

By George Gondron

THE DARK SIDE OF RELIGION All organized religion has its dark side The more obvious of course are the millions that have died Because their God is the ‘right’ God And if their God is a caring, loving, compassionate God Then their God weeps at what is done in its name The Christians have their Crusades and Inquisitions The Muslims have their Jihad The Jews have their God of wrath Then there’s the more subtle side of the darkness of religion The bigotry, discrimination, racism, and literalism The dark side of religion isn’t interested in the ‘good news’ It rather prefers the ‘bad news’ the ‘judgmental news’ The rules and regulations meant to shackle people Rather than liberating them and setting them free The ‘fear-mongering’ of a God that will only love you ‘If’ Rather than a God that loves unconditionally A God that cannot not love A religion, or rather a teacher, preacher, or priest Of that religion, who teaches with fear is part of that Dark side of organized religion Love, mercy, forgiveness, and compassion Cannot flourish, cannot survive the dark side of religion In 1976 Christianity represented 91% of the population In 2022 that number has dropped to 64%, a major decline Most mainstream religions morph into forms of worship They miss the whole purpose of relationship and practice Relationship with the one they profess they believe And practice of what they taught We each carry the DNA of a creative Source of our choice Whatever we give the name of the God in which we believe We have an inner core, an internal reference That holds our ‘true self’, that holds our truth, our answers Choose a daily meditative/contemplative practice For just a few minutes each day empty your ‘monkey mind’ Be present to Presence, be silent, be still Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They patiently await, they are your guides

George G
