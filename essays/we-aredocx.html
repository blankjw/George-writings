WE ARE

By George Gondron

WE ARE Can you guess? Do you even have a suspicion? We are, for the most part the creators of our own reality Especially our feelings and emotions, our psychology We abandoned our ability to think and be spiritual long ago We have become nothing more than ‘sponges’ Soaking in our favorite narrative of the day, which is almost entirely negative or accusatory, the old ‘blame game’ of course And spirituality, our connection to Mystery, the greatest gift of all Lies in the dust supplanted by organized religion crying out loudly you must believe in us, belong to us, support us, pay us Every major and minor philosophical belief system has a major focus on love, love for all, not just the few, the selected, but selfless love But as Gandhi once said, ‘I love your Christ but not your Christians, they’re nothing like your Christ’, an obvious observation The average college graduate reads one book a year, and that’s almost entirely fiction, again an obvious reality as we listen to conversations As we witness the hyperbole, ‘gaslighting’, the alternative facts, the BS Most are so indoctrinated to ‘follow’, to be ‘sheep’, they would not know where to begin to seek the truth or critically think And we wonder, how did we get to this divide in our culture How do we abandon decency, kindness, compassion, inclusion, sharing, caring, and love of all humanity? I know how, and I think you do as well Our integrity, our values, our ethics have suffered a lapse in memory The love spoken about within all the belief systems has vanished Oh, I know, there are exception, some that still listen to their hearts But they seem to be fewer and fewer as the years slip by And the rest never run out of excuses for their lack their lack of love The atrocities committed by our nation are unlimited, and we to this day, deny them, and those that don’t or ostracized January 20, 1977 Jimmy Carter took office January 20, 2025 Donlad J Trump will take office, God help us Two human beings as different as day is to night, as good is to bad Please adopt a meditative/contemplative practice Connect inner and outer, spend a few minutes in silence, stillness, with Mystery each day, listen to your inner guides They hold your true self, have your answers, the patiently wait

George G
