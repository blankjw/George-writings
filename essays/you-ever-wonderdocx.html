YOU EVER WONDER

By George Gondron

YOU EVER WONDER? You ever wonder why we seem to follow opinion over fact? Could it be opinions are more about ‘gaslighting’ Facts are more about truth, who wants the truth, right? Ever wonder why we follow conspiracy theories? Could it be we it’s easier to blame than to know Easier to find a ‘scapegoat’ and point fingers, right Ever wonder for change to happen it must be someone else And never ourselves? You know, like the couple that goes to therapy for counseling It’s always their mate’s fault and never their own Ever wonder why we ignore obvious facts and embrace lies? Could it be we may have to change our minds? Oh, and here’s a good one If we profess a formal belief system Jew, Christian, etc., etc. We treat it like a menu of choices, what to follow, what to ignore I believe that approach is called being hypocritical Isn’t it easier to have a closed mind rather than an open mind? After all a closed mind doesn’t need to critically think Reflect, question, doubt, discern, research, it’s so much easier I think we still have freedom of choice, at least some of us Of all the social media available that we can choose What to watch, what to listen to, but here’s what’s scary It’s too obvious that most of us choose what’s negative Not what’s positive, what stirs our emotions, not our intellect What elicits our fear and not our love What separates us rather than unites, ignorance is the enemy As human beings we have the gift of choice, of free will We can choose to become aware or remain ignorant Awareness is expansive, inclusive, ignorance is restrictive For lack of reliable information, we truly do become Our own worst enemy, blind leading the blind Choose growth, adopt a meditative/contemplative practice Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Isness Empty your ‘monkey mind’ of incessant and repetitive thoughts Focus on your breath or a short mantra, breathe in, breathe out Listen for the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, they wait

George G
