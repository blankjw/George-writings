MATTER AND SPIRIT

By George Gondron

MATTER AND SPIRIT In the Western mind the idea that matter and spirit Or the material and spiritual are inseparable Is not even a question to be entertained For they are considered two different realities If considered at all The ego, the mind, and the intellect perceive the material Easily, with no problem It’s what we experience through the five senses Taste, touch, smell, hear, and see The spiritual on the other hand suffers the belief That region and spirituality are the same But they’re not, religion can be taught Spirituality can only be experienced Experienced through the material Consciously through the individual human being And probably through everything that exists Maybe that’s the true meaning of incarnation Matter and spirit united, the essence of all life A good analogy or comparison would be If we stopped breathing the heart would eventually Stop beating In like manner, if our heart stopped beating We’d eventually stop breathing Heart being the material, breath the spiritual Both are required for human life Our ability to understand or comprehend Is always linked to a context We don’t exist in a vacuum, every emotion is contextual A spiritual experience happens in the context of Being without thoughts and distractions Moments of being unguarded, undefended, in the present A meditative/contemplative practice is a path to Spiritual experience – silence, stillness, with Presence An emptying of the mind of its incessant repetitive thoughts Dwelling on the past or the future, missing the now Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit Discover your Truth, It patiently waits

George G
