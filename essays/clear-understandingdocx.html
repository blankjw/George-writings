CLEAR UNDERSTANDING

By George Gondron

CLEAR UNDERSTANDING The understanding we receive through our five senses Taste, touch, smell, hear, and see we take for granted Unless we experience a sensation of discomfort Then we pay attention, too hot, too cold, too loud, etc. Our next level of understanding connects to our intellect The thoughts of our mind, our consciousness And simultaneously we can include our emotions, our psychology Since our thinking and feeling can be distorted or incomplete The clarity of our understanding becomes ‘cloudy’ or not so clear In honesty we are limited to our personal experiences And yet, we become adamant in our beliefs without Clear understanding at all, only hear-say Our ‘false self’, our small ego, the persona and masks we wear Are never wrong, and as a result we live very polarized lives Very dualistic in nature, right/wrong, good/bad, no compromise And finally, our most important level of understanding Our own spirituality never even enters the realm of possibility Superseded by ‘organized religion’ resulting in further division Further exclusion, discrimination, judgment, and hypocrisy There’s nothing like a fool, especially an old fool Rather than pursuing paths that can lead to clear understanding We have become proficient in creating walls, barriers, obstacles, And never-ending obstructions and complications to thwart clarity One could surmise, we are ‘hell bent’ on destroying ourselves Because ‘we’ are right and ‘he, she, and they’ are wrong Pogo’s words ring true, ‘we have met the enemy, and it is us’ For clear understanding all our potential must be included Our senses, thoughts, emotions, and spirituality Nothing can be eliminated or excluded, all must be experienced To be fully human and fully alive is not easy, its’ work But the effort is worth the reward, no one is left behind I encourage you, discover your own spirituality Adopt a meditative/contemplative practice, it matters Spend a few minutes each day in silence, be still, embrace Presence Empty your mind of its incessant and repetitive thoughts Focus on your breath or a short mantra, breathe in, breathe out Listen to your inner guides, they hold your answers, they wait

George G
