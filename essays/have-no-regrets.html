HAVE NO REGRETS

By George Gondron | Source: HAVE NO REGRETS.docx

HAVE NO REGRETS Gratefulness, appreciation, recognition, admiration Too often, unspoken thoughts Conflict, strife, coping, survival Too often unacknowledged struggles Too often the obvious remains unexpressed The father who can't find the words The mother who pretends not to see The child whose value is how 'good' they are The words of lovingness stick in our throat Aching arms remain at our sides We've become what we saw We've learned what we heard Conditioned by others Most often well meaning But, how can there be an excuse An excuse for what's possible, now I didn't say enough, the words in my heart I am thankful for your being, for your presence Not for what you do or have done But, simply for who you are I'm not afraid to ask for a hug To say 'forgive me' if I have been absent Absent of what's important in life Too concerned with what in the end has no value Gratitude is never too late Acknowledgment is never too soon If these words have meaning for you If they stir your heart and your soul Act now, don't wait Have no regrets

George G
