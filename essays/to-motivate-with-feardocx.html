TO MOTIVATE WITH FEAR

By George Gondron

TO MOTIVATE WITH FEAR Unfortunately fear is a greater motivator than love History supplies us with unlimited examples of fear motivation Even today in our modern world, fear works really well Just look at what’s happening in our own nation today And be sure you notice those that perpetuate the fear And for what purpose, fear is always self-serving You can be assured that what fear attempts to sell Is not worth buying Ignorance and lack of ‘common sense’ are both fertile Environments to plant and instigate fear Our refusal to educate ourselves and be critical thinkers Also leaves us open to embracing unrealized fear For example, ‘They’re coming to get our guns!’ – really? Fear narrative has one main focus – to divide, not unite To exclude, not include, to control and deny freedom Those who use fear as a means of influence Are of low intelligence but high in self-righteousness They have no clue about the power of love And show little or no understanding about empathy, kindness, Compassion or genuine human relationships If we do not recognize these personality types within our own Society, then we have buried our heads in the sand We cannot see the forest for the trees, we are the fools And we will suffer the consequences for our lack of awareness Simply following social media is not education It’s more a form of manipulation and propaganda Selling us alternative facts, half-truths, and lies True education is for the mind what nourishment is for the body Please, educate yourselves, question, doubt, research Don’t just drink the ‘Kool Aid’ or follow the pied piper To be fully human we need balance in our lives, inner and outer Add a daily meditative/contemplative practice for your benefit Spend a few minutes in silence, be still, be present to Presence Empty your ever busy mind from incessant and repetitive thoughts Focus on a short mantra, or just the breath Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, they wait

George G
