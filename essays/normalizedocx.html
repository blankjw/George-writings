NORMALIZE

By George Gondron

NORMALIZE To make normal or cause to conform to a standard What we think, feel, do or do not do, can be normalized Without exception To complain about what is without a viable solution Normal To accept ‘alternative facts’ as real Normal To accept the murder of children through mass shootings Normal To accept illegal activity and defend the same Normal To marginalize people of color Normal To persecute the less privileged without cause Normal To blame, accuse, and ostracize without evidence Normal To turn a deaf ear and a blind eye, to voters' demands Normal For the rich to become richer, and the poor to have less Normal For corporations to pay no tax but make billions in profits Normal For education and health care to be limited for those with means Normal When organized religion is a weapon and not a blessing Normal When fear and dread replace love and understanding Normal Here’s what’s not normal Kindness, compassion, equality, and justice for all Voter equality, gender equality, representative equality Affordable care for the elderly, the handicapped, the orphan A livable wage for all people A society in which people are more important than ‘things’ Take time for reflection, examine your normals Listen more to your heart, soul, and spirit – change your normal

George G
