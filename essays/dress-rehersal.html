DRESS REHERSAL

By George Gondron

DRESS REHEARSAL One fact life is not, life is not a dress rehearsal It is one and out, moment by moment Time does not repeat itself There are no do-overs You don't practice life, you do life Most reach the end and have missed the journey You don't practice love, love is Most wait to be loved than being love What we speak, we cannot take back What we miss saying is gone eternally Every act, small or large, is writ forever Missed moments of a tiny kindness Moments that could have made a difference Assumed meanings without confirmation Meanings that divide and not unite Caring, seen by some as interfering Thoughtfulness perceived as imposing When abuse is present what action is necessary? It lies within our hearts to know The spirit discerns exploitation Regardless, the opportunities afford the time The time to love, to care, to intervene, to speak up Don't miss it, the time, the moment Don't let it pass you by It may make the difference between love and apathy Between someone's self-worth and apparent failure The opportunities surround us It's not pretend, not a dress rehearsal It is real, we are on stage, for the full three acts It is now It is
