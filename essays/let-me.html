LET ME

By George Gondron

LET ME Would you help me, please? I'm caught in the confusion, in the meaningless drama The senseless absurdity of contemporary life The unceasing struggle for, I'm not quite sure what I have forgotten the safety of another's arms The soft beating of a mother's heart Lost from my cultural programming of need and success I can no longer see I can no longer hear Don't tell me what to believe Teach me how to know How to connect with the yearning I feel inside If you can Do we all follow this empty and hollow path? Do we convince ourselves of false happiness? There is no life where life is not felt Only temporary comfort, temporary solace to ease the ache within Let me see again, let me hear again, let me feel again Let me
