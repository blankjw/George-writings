THE ENEMY HAS A NAME

By George Gondron

THE ENEMY HAS A NAME In a nation filled with opportunities to discover the truth The name of the enemy is ignorance The refusal to use our own brain, our own intellect Our own curious mind to separate fact from fiction To desire to know the truth as opposed to the alternative facts To be able to filter through the misinformation on social media That focuses on the manipulation of our choices to their benefit Of course, the captain of this ship of ignorance is our ego An ego that expects the path to the truth to be a superhighway Paved with no detours and no rough patches in the road An easy journey straight to reality If the discovery of the truth were that simple There would be no need for marketing, advertising, conditioning, Programming, manipulation, and propaganda There would be no need for expanded learning at all And ironically, that’s what the uninformed mind prefers Let’s take the highway and avoid the less traveled roads Let’s drink the Kool-Aid and listen to the snake oil salesman It’s so much easier and more convenient than exerted effort Social media has a way of attracting our ‘laziness’ Why go through the struggle of learning when we Already have all the answers, right at our fingertips Just ‘click’ on your favorite channel, problem solved Answer provided, simple and easy, no muss no fuss It is shocking how many people live through the narrative of ‘So and so’ said, and ‘so and so’ loves it It’s called ‘you gonna believe me or those lyin eyes and ears’? There is a saying, ‘wake up and smell the roses’ The same is true about expanded awareness and consciousness Put forth the effort to learn, reject the paved highway The benefit of anxiety and tension is that it gives us the Opportunity to grow, to become more than we are The purpose of a meditative/contemplative practice is to Awaken the gifts we have internally, already present Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Listen to the whispers of your own heart, soul, and spirit They hold your truth, they patiently wait

George G
