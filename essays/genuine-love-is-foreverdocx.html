GENUINE LOVE IS FOREVER

By George Gondron

GENUINE LOVE IS FOREVER Too often we confuse love with warm fuzzy feelings Those moments of temporary insanity When we are literally 'out of our minds' Making decisions with everything except our intellect Decisions based primarily on desire On attachment, on need, on longing, and craving The subject of our desire if we're honest, in the beginning Is not a subject at all, but more of an object An objectified human, there to fulfill our needs With disguised behavior to meet their every wish Convincing ourselves they are first, we're second They remain first as long as the feelings are positive But, when there's a 'bump in the relationship' When the 'road gets a little rough' The 'seas get a little stormy' And suddenly, the warm fuzziness is gone We confuse love again, as a feeling Genuine love has never been a feeling Genuine love is an action, a relational action It is wanting the 'other' to become all that they can be Present or absent, near or far, today and tomorrow Relationships based on expectations are lacking in love And most relationships that end in disappointment End in heartbreak, in resentment, and anger Were never based on genuine love Love is learned, learned over time, and never forgotten It sees beyond the obvious, beneath the surface Accepting the little flaws and imperfections that we all possess Love that is genuine cannot be withheld It is never conditional But when abused, it can say no It can say, I will go this far and no farther And still be genuine, even in separation Love is what gives us life, creative Love It is the Love that cannot, not love It is a genuine Love It lasts forever

George G
