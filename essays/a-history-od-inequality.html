A HISTORY OD INEQUALITY

By George Gondron

A HISTORY OF INEQUALITY A vast majority of of the world’s male population has never treated the female gender as coequals, except the indigenous cultures A truth that prevails even today And is ever present in our nation, our history A history that stands on the false premise that all people are created equal, an obvious lie as evidenced by our behavior The earliest known figures of worship that have been discovered in our ancient past are feminine not masculine I suspect that changed when men began to fear women For after all women carried life in their bodies, the new infants In those early beginnings they didn’t know it took a sperm and an egg to activate a new life, when that realization slipped into awareness nothing changed for now women were chattel and to a great extent remain so today, they needed to be controlled They have had to fight for equality throughout the centuries and as scary as it may sound, if the White Evangelical Christian movement succeeds their plight will become much worse, and God weeps The son of Mary and Joseph, Jesus, did not come to form a new religion, he also was a good Jew His life was all about relationships and love and called The Way He freely associated with women, and some were his disciples Mary Magdalene, Susanna, Joanna, to mention a few And yet in 591 AD Pope Gregory I insinuated in a homily that Mary Magdalene was a prostitute which has since been refuted Women to this day have been relegated to lesser status is education, in the workplace, in financial rewards, and religious positions The world, especially this nation, suffers from the lack of feminine intuition, compassion, knowledge, and intellect And we continue to pay the price from that lack, and Jesus weeps I am convinced that if Jesus returned today, it would be Christians that would crucify Him again, especially white evangelical males I urge you to adopt a meditative/contemplative practice A few minutes each day be silent, be still, be in the presence of God God doesn’t want our worship, only our relationship, our love Focus on your breath or a brief mantra, breathe in, breath out The mind will wander, the ego will resist, rinse and repeat Listen for the whispers of your inner guides, heart, soul, spirit

George G
