RESISTANCE AND CHANGE

By George Gondron

RESISTANCE AND CHANGE Two inescapable corollaries, resistance and change Life is about change, change is inevitable We see change all about us, while fighting change within ourselves Death is about resistance, remaining the same One can be physically alive and at the same time spiritually dead The ego prefers resistance, accepting change on its own terms Preferring sameness gives the ego a sense of control, a sense of power Resistance, a form of stability, an inability to risk Change, a stance of welcoming, excitement of the new A yearning within that assures us of something more That knowing of something not yet found, but yet present An urge to stretch for what's just out of reach An understanding of one's own evolution, one's own mystery Seek change, your change, your life Avoid resistance, avoid spiritual death To know Life is to know change To know change is to know Life No change, no Life

George G
