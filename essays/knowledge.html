KNOWLEDGE

By George Gondron

KNOWLEDGE The first state of knowing is through our senses Taste, touch, smell, hear, and see Then thinking and feeling In conjunction with our doing, our action All these forms of knowing are experiential Knowing through experience is the foundation of our truth Our personal truth Any truth outside of personal experience Is second hand truth We rely on the words of another Spoken or read The images seen through social media And when our learning, our education, our seeking ceases Almost all input that is not the result of hands on experience Has become second and third party information We then remain vulnerable to the ego's desires Declaring that what is true, is what meets The ego's agenda, for better or for worse It is at that point that personal learning ceases Resulting in a false fabricated life, without truth Built on the narrative of whatever holds sway at the moment Whether we agree or disagree Whatever the 'ism's' declare Nationalism, patriotism, consumerism, militarism, Protestantism, Catholicism, atheism, any 'ism' And yet 'ism's' don't have consciousness, only individuals There is a source of knowledge that is much greater Than all the above It resides within in each of us, and is seldom experienced It is the innate knowing of our heart, soul, and spirit The knowing that does not shout, demand, or even request That we listen, reflect, question, or doubt It only gently whispers in the recesses of our souls It is heard in moments of silence and stillness When we slow down enough to empty our minds To be present to Presence, to be in the Now Be true to your essence, learn to listen, learn to be

George G
