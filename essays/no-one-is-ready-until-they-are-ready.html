NO ONE IS READY UNTIL THEY ARE READY

By George Gondron

NO ONE IS READY UNTIL THEY ARE READY In this journey, we call our life Each one of us will have struggles Struggles known by others; struggles known only to ourselves Some are willing to face and deal with those struggles Others remain hidden for perhaps a lifetime The latter become our personal prisons Effecting our lives in negative and damaging ways Those are our personal demons in thought, feeling, and deed In one way or another, they have a measured negative impact On all our relationships, directly or indirectly For unawares, they are restricting our wholeness Our ability to be fully human and free in our interactions We live a guarded life To express it bluntly, we live a lie A lie known only to ourselves The underlying emotion that shackles us from being honest Honest mostly with ourselves, is fear, always Fear restricts, fear weakens, fear controls, fear deceives Fear dwells behind a pretense of wellbeing Fear says you’re never ready to ‘let go’ The right moment never seems to present itself Tomorrow I’ll be ready to change, to risk But not today There’s no requirement for becoming ready for personal change There’s only the choice The false self, the egoic self is never ready The decision to act on one’s own behalf, to become more To be free of our self-made prison, for some can seem impossible Especially in a world filled with distractions A mind that never turns off, full of repetitive thoughts Characteristic of a life without balance between inner and outer To tune out and tune in, choose a meditative/contemplative practice For a few minutes each day choose silence, choose stillness, Choose to be in the presence of your image of a creative Source No words, just presence,a silent relationship Listen to the innate knowing of your own heart, soul, and spirit They patiently await, they whisper gently, they are ready

George G
