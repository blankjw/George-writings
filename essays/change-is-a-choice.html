CHANGE IS A CHOICE

By George Gondron

CHANGE IS A CHOICE If we’re willing to be honest We live in a society where change is for the other person Not for me, I’m just fine There are three paths that can lead us to change Or at least present us with that option Deep emotional pain, the experience of unconditional love And a meditative/contemplative practice The first is easily avoided through medication and distraction Legal or otherwise The second really is not possible for most, even for religious For it requires some form of spiritual relationship It’s experiential, a felt reality Not an intellectual concept based on knowledge or belief When you ask this person if they believe in a Creator The person who has experienced unconditional love They will tell you, ‘No, I know’ The third path can only happen when we’re willing to let go Let go of what’s familiar and choose to experience The benefits of silence, stillness, and Presence Few there are that choose the third path The third path is a ‘letting go’, embracing one’s shadow It is to experience ‘the dark night of the soul’ To go through the pain of greater awareness and consciousness To arrive at a new place, free from the unnecessaries of life To fully participate in their own personal growth Not as a burden but as an adventure To understand that life is relationship and connection There is a reason people resist silence and stillness Or better stated, the ego resists, fear of loss of control Control of what?, control of who? Choose a few minutes of silence, stillness, and Presence Each and every day, a meditative/contemplative practice Empty your mind of its incessant and repetitive thoughts That honestly go nowhere and do nothing Experience the innate knowing of your own heart, soul, spirit Listen to their whispers that hold your inner truth They have the answer to every problem, they patiently await

George G
