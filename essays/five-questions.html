FIVE QUESTIONS

By George Gondron

FIVE QUESTIONS Who, what, where, when, and how? Who are the splinter groups that have become more extreme Since January 6th, 2021 in our nation? The Proud Boys, Oath Keepers, 3 Percenters, Groypers, KKK What are the demographics of these groups? Far-right militants, white nationalists, and very conspiratorial In their outlook, a gateway to the alt-right, supporting fundamental Evangelical Christianity, centered on violent revolution What are their goals?, they are many and varied The greatest being the preservation of white supremacy And far-right fundamental conservative Christianity Supporters of nationalism, isolationism, militarism, Conservatism, authoritarianism, dogmatism, and fascism Where can they be found? Literally everywhere, but especially in small communities In Red states, the dark web, any far-right social media outlet When are they active? - always Wherever a progressive assembly is present they will Be there to protest, the ground of their belief is anti Their definition of liberty and justice for all Is exclusive, not inclusive – their liberty, their justice How do they operate, and how do they survive? They prey on the emotions, they motivate by fear They are extremists with extremist ideas Strong on gun rights, against common sense gun rights Flag wavers, camo wearers, open gun carriers The good ole boys Anti-science, anti LGBTQ+, anti-immigrant In fact, anti just about anything that’s not like them They don’t hide, they’re easy to recognize, they are dangerous They believe in winning by force, but only in numbers The thrust of their attack is hate, not love, fear not compassion They are dangerous because they are willing to follow The lies and half-truths, the alternative facts, without question If you’re not one of them, you are against them And if they become stronger, democracy as we know it will fail The rule of law will vanish, and autocracy will reign

George G
