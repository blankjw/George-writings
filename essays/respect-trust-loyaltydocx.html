RESPECT, TRUST, LOYALTY

By George Gondron

RESPECT, TRUST, LOYALTY Three peas in a pod The reliable ingredients for healthy relationships When present, fear is dissipated and nonexistent If these qualities were demonstrably present In every human being, there would be no conflict, no war The three are so united in purpose that when one is broken When one has been violated, fear immediately steps in The three together promote compassion, cooperation, charity Separated, pronounced differences begin to arise Between and among individuals and groups The struggle for power, control, and manipulation creeps in In religion in particular no one wins and God loses Love turns to apathy, kindness turns to greed Now exclusion, separation, segregation, and differences rule All becomes me and mine versus you and yours When the three are not present love disappears, and fear rules For the underlying negative thoughts and feelings Are always fear-based, absent of love Thinking is very polarized, right/wrong, good/bad Balance is nonexistent, no inner, all outer A genuine path of return to equanimity Of bringing into harmony respect, trust, and loyalty Is a meditative/contemplative daily practice Just a few minutes each day of downtime A time to empty our ‘monkey minds’ of their incessant And mostly repetitive thoughts that go nowhere And truly solve nothing of substance A time for committed silence, with stillness Spent in the presence of one’s image of God, a creative Source The name has no significance, the relationship is what matters We have within our inner core an innate knowing Held within our hearts, souls, and spirits Where dwell all the answers to whatever the questions It is where we discover our true self As opposed to the ego’s fabricated false self In those dedicated moments, we will hear the whispers From the place of yearnings, of our truth, they patiently await

George G
