OBEDIENT TO DYSFUNCTION

By George Gondron

OBEDIENCE TO DYSFUNCTION

THERE IS A TERRIBLE PRICE TO PAY FOR BEING OBEDIENT TO ANOTHER’S DYSFUNCTION. THE PRICE IS EVENTUALLY LOSS OF THE TRUE SELF. THE “ANOTHER’S “ ARE MANY. FAMILY CHURCH POLITICAL PARTY NATION CORPORATION ETC., ETC., ETC. WE BECOME SO HYPNOTIZED BY THE DYSFUNCTION THAT WE SOON BECOME COMFORTABLE WITH ITS EFFECTS. SOMEWHERE INSIDE A SMALL WHISPER FROM THE DIVINE SPARK OF OUR CREATION GNAWS AT OUR PSYCHE – SAYING THIS CAN’T BE LIFE. TRUST THAT WHISPER STOP THE MERRY-GO-ROUND FIND A QUIET PLACE REFLECT AND BREATHE YOU WILL BEGIN TO UNEARTH THAT WHICH BINDS YOU THE MIGHTY OAK IS IN THE TINY ACORN YOUR TRUE SELF IS WITHIN THAT DIVINE SPARK

GEORGE G
