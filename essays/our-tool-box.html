OUR TOOL BOX

By George Gondron | Source: OUR TOOL BOX.docx

OUR TOOLBOX Except for the very, very few who are handicapped We all are born with the potential to become our unique self Having all the necessary tools with which to accomplish Our eventual hopes, wishes, and dreams, our creativity Unfortunately, most of us become sidetracked in our journey As well for most, those detours, roadblocks, or dead ends Are a matter of choice, conscious or otherwise It’s the ‘otherwise’ that becomes our main obstacle One of the weaknesses of an unbalanced life is dependency On the ego alone with no connection to our inner core No connection to an innate internal reference, only the mind The ‘go to’ position of encountering resistance, resistance we Ourselves create is to defend, blame, point fingers, and deny At some point in our lives, we will use all the aforementioned As well as several others, they become our chosen roadblocks Consequently, many of the tools available to us for our Potential growth and success, lie dormant never to be used There is a saying, ‘if the only tool you have is a hammer, you tend To treat everything as a nail’ One could use the analogy of the wheels on a car to represent Our mind, heart, soul, and spirit – four wheels But if we’re only using one, our mind, it’s not a promising journey Healthy choices are inclusive of all four wheels, not just the mind The phrase ‘peace of mind’ is an oxymoron, for in truth when You’re in your mind alone, you’re seldom at peace, but when You’re ‘out of your mind’, empty of thoughts, peace slips in The task becomes how do we begin to use all our tools How do we find balance, have an open mind We can do what the ego will always resist, begin self-discovery Continue to educate ourselves beyond social media Adopt a meditative/contemplative practice Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Isness Be committed be consistent Empty your mind of incessant and repetitive thoughts Focus on your breath or a short mantra Use all the tools in your toolbox, heart, soul, spirit, and mind ThEy have your answers, they hold your true self, they wait

George G
