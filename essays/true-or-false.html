TRUE OR FALSE

By George Gondron

TRUE OR FALSE Two words that can inflame any discussion Usually by those with no evidence of either For what's true today may not be tomorrow What's false today may be true tomorrow What's true when we're young changes with age What's false when we're young may also change If neither never change, neither do we Our level of awareness remains naive Our consciousness unable to risk To know what is true and what is false Can only be found through experience If all spoke the truth, and knew the truth There would be no need for propaganda No need for market gratification For false advertising and only profit motive Failure is a blessing, for it leads us to truth Risk is a gift, for it increases our awareness Vulnerability is a path for knowing both A meaningful life is not for the timid Not for the fearful, nor the insecure Not for the one who has all the answers But for the one that embraces the mystery The one who knows they don't know There is only one changeless, immutable truth Love Is There is only one permanent falsity Love is not Trust the Truth Trust Love

George G
