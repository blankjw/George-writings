THE VOICELESS

By George Gondron

THE VOICELESS Who speaks for the voiceless, those unheard The millions who have no platform No way to plead their cause, their plight What major media mogul presents their case Or maybe it's not worthy of 'prime time' The ratings just don't justify the 'air time' It makes no money, too risky The Nielsen ratings would plummet How about a network devoted to the homeless The wounded warriors with PTSD and worse The single moms living in poverty The abused children wandering the streets The handicapped and disenfranchised The mentally ill, the hungry, the unemployed Those with no chance for education Where is their 24/7 media coverage It would seem, if it's out of sight it's out of mind And then there's the case for the unborn A sensitive subject, some pro, some con But they, certainly are voiceless Regardless of your position, they are speechless Mute, unable to state their case Guilty before proven innocent, in the case of abortion They have no rights, according to some Somehow, it all makes no rational sense Somehow, the arguments are empty rhetoric When there's enough for all, more than plenty Whatever the need, the resources are there But yet there remains what cannot be explained The voiceless continue their struggle They need a voice, my voice, your voice, all voices

George G
