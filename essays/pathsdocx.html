PATHS

By George Gondron

PATHS Each path we take, at the time of its choosing Seemed to be the proper path, the 'right direction' In looking back of course, many were not And now, after thoughtful consideration we discover We needed the many that were not The many that were not the 'right direction' Have become the ones from which we received The greatest lessons, the most important learning's It truly is a reality that only through failure Do we become successful If you've never known the feelings of failure You'll never appreciate those of success Success that doesn't arise from false pride Success that stems from a knowledge of inner strength The strength to not give up on a meaningful dream In spite of the 'naysayers' and failed attempts The strength to believe, 'this time it will work' The accomplishments of one's life That carry the most meaning Are those of a selfless origin Grounded in the benefits for others The efficacy of life is a giving away Not a taking from It's always the path of 'I - thou' Not a path of 'me - mine' The discovery of that path is only found through balance Balance of our whole human nature It cannot be found through intellect alone Through just the thinking mind It's inclusive of heart, soul, and spirit as well It's always relational, not about just me But also the other We are born from Love, a love that's relational It cannot be otherwise For Love doesn't exist only for Itself Love exists for what Love has created Love will never live alone, It lives for others

George G
