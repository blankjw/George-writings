BE STILL

By George Gondron | Source: BE STILL.docx

BE STILL Be still, be silent, be present to Mystery, be All of the above are supposedly impossible for the Western mind It seems, here in the West, we struggle to maintain balance in our daily lives because of external stimuli, thoughts, and feelings alone Very few of us have an interior life and without one we are fertile ground for polarized, dualistic, either/or thinking In that scenario situational ethics doesn’t exist for everything is right/wrong, good/bad, black/white, a lie/the truth As well words such as conciliation, cooperation, understanding, compromise, concession, mutuality, and bipartisanship have little or no meaning for a person with a closed mind We have become a nation not guided by critical thought and self-reflection but one guided by negative emotions, power, and greed, reactors versus responders Noise, whether it is our incessant repetitive thinking or external distractions is our constant companion We are influenced by our emotions much more than our ability to think, it’s easier and takes less effort Sadly, what the ego resists is exactly what we need to embrace For what we deny or push away is often our greatest teacher Without some ‘down time’ daily we have no connection to our Inner core, our internal resources We lack the ability to maintain balance, inner and outer We remain dualistic and polarized in our thinking and feeling We can’t grasp the reality of situational ethics of both/and thinking, stuck in right/wrong, good/bad, black/white thinking In addition our minds are closed, we listen to defend not to learn Our learning is a result of what we feel and not our intellect to remain ignorant, stuck in the past or future and miss the now Christianity in particular does not teach the importance of a meditative/contemplative practice, except for religious orders For your health and well-being spend a few minutes each day in silence, be still, be in the presence of Mystery Focus on your breath or a short mantra, breathe in, breathe out The ego will resist, the mind will wander, simply rinse and repeat Listen for the whispers of your inner guides heart, soul, spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, they wait

George G
