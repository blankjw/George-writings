LOSS OF FREEDOMS

By George Gondron

LOSS OF FREEDOMS It seems since January 20th here in these United States Someone’s freedom has been restricted or lost entirely And we’re only six days in We are being dragged back decades to appease the emperor As his cronies do not have the backbone to resist his hunger for unlimited power and control Muttering under their breath, it’s the mandate of the people Ignorance has no mandate when it violates justice and equality Justice and equality legislated previously by rule of law But currently our infamous leader is above the law Immune from prosecution, hiding behind the robes of SCOTUS The latest political arm of our government Lady Justice is no longer blind, and she weeps Nothing this new oligarch, pretending to be our president, intents to do will in any way benefit those who are not white, not wealthy, not highly educated, not already in positions of power For after all, he loves the poorly educated and they have rewarded him, he now has the keys to the kingdom And if he survives his current term, I doubt he will let go His ego is so inflated he truly believes our enemies are his friends As they laugh behind his back knowing how easily manipulated he is, can you show me one example of his behavior that is honorable, kind, compassionate, unifying, and loving? His fealty test is undying loyalty, regardless of the circumstances The qualifications of his cabinet picks are those of ‘yes men/women’ Nothing else matters, just jump when he shouts ‘frog’ Kiss his ring, agree with everything he says and wishes or suffer the consequences, after all he is a stable genius I am not a fearful person, but I fear the future for the disenfranchised, for minorities, for women, for LGBTQ+, for immigrants, etc. If you fear also adopt a meditative/contemplative practice Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with your God Awaken your internal resources, connect inner and outer Focus on your breath or a short mantra, breathe in, breathe out Your mind will wander, the ego will resist, simply rinse and repeat Listen for the whispers of your inner guides, heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, they wait

George G
