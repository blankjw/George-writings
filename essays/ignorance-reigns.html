IGNORANCE REIGNS

By George Gondron

IGNORANCE REIGNS When truth and facts no longer matter, ignorance reigns When important decisions are made without intelligence Without reflection and intentional clarity Ignorance reigns Today we are in a period immersed in ignorance Where conspiracy, complacency, and smugness exist Ignorance reigns When emotions replace obvious facts and the past Is a bastion of defense, ignorance reigns When the only argument is ‘Well, what about’ And the ‘what about’ is used only as a defense Ignorance reigns When after three years ‘election deniers’ beat their drums In the face of factual evidence to the contrary Ignorance reigns What is it in the human psyche that prefers the lie Rather than the truth, that prefers ignorance over intelligence Maybe we’d rather be fed a dose of BS Than expand our awareness, our consciousness, our minds To match the current reality as opposed to the illusions It seems we prefer the social media hype of our choice Don’t ask me to question, to doubt, to reflect or Critically think, just feed me my choice of ignorance Where egos are inflated, and compassion, kindness, and love Are nonexistent, ignorance reigns Where there is a disconnect between head and heart No internal reference, no inner core, ignorance reigns Where our thoughts are about the past or fearing the future Ignorance reigns, the now is not important Please, add a meditative/contemplative practice to your life Spend a few minutes each day in silence, be still, empty the mind Be in the presence of your image of a Creative Source Of God, if you prefer, be in relationship with Love Listen to the whispers of your heart, soil, and spirit They have your answers, they hold your true self Awaken your inner core, your innate truth Become aware, be here now, don’t wait

George G
