THOUGHTS AND FEELINGS

By George Gondron

THOUGHTS AND FEELINGS Our feelings arise from input Input from our thinking and our five senses Taste, touch, smell, hear, and see We alone create what we feel and what we think Created from what we take in No thing or no one is responsible for our choices As the saying goes ‘garbage in, garbage out’ To further complicate the matter We use the word feel to describe a thought And those thoughts are almost always about other or others ‘I feel like you/they should’ - fill in the blank Not a feeling at all – definitely a thought And of course the flip side of that coin When you ask someone how they feel They almost always respond with a thought, not a feeling Sadly, most cannot give an honest answer to what they feel And even if they can They are hard-pressed to acknowledge they created the feeling Our thoughts and our feelings are choices, always No one is holding a gun to our heads Those of us who react rather than respond to situations Who don’t take the time to reflect on what has occurred Are very hard-pressed to take ownership of their own Thoughts and feelings, it’s much easier to blame Or use any of the many psychological defense mechanisms For our inability to own our choices and reactions Most people are never able to acknowledge That whatever is happening in their lives is by choice Even to the point of whether to stay or whether to go Whether to cling to the painful familiar or make a change Regardless of the situation or circumstance Choose a different fork in the road Spend a few minutes each day in silence, with stillness In the presence of your image of a creative Source, of Isness Learn to listen to the whispers of your own heart, soul, and spirit We are born with an innate truth We have within us all the answers, the patiently await

George G
