GESTAPO STATE

By George Gondron

GESTAPO STATE Literally, overnight we have become a Gestapo State with Elon Musk as our Heinrich Himmler Ironically, the Republican party, the party that has railed against government invasion of privacy, through the auspices of our new monarch has given a non-governmental employee who has not been vetted, with no background check, the ‘keys to the kingdom’ Everything about each of us is now exposed for public observation Name, social security number, address, gender, race, tax returns, level of education, profession, job status, etc., etc., etc. Our privacy has literally been taken away from us by the richest man in the world, what could possibly go wrong? Elon Musk, the ‘hatchet man’ has been given the power to destroy millions of lives, and the spineless Republicans stand idly by with their ‘heads up their asses’ doing nothing, a sad day indeed And now with a Supreme Court giving Trump total immunity from anything he does as our current President, we hold our breath We literally are nothing more than ignorant sheep being led by a wolf, our future is very dark indeed, we have chosen chaos Everything is vanishing before our very eyes, our Constitution, the separation of powers, the rule of law, our freedoms, our very democracy, and our valued privacy Nothing it seems, is beyond the reach of this current administration And it is we, the citizens, who will suffer Not the millionaires, the billionaires, the ‘white elites’, only us We, the backbone of this nation, the hope of this nation, the future of this nation are being ‘hoodwinked’ and we have only ourselves to blame, our lack of critical thinking and education combined with a willingness to live through our emotions has brought us to this point, can we reverse this course, can we recover, it is doubtful Unfortunately, there’s not an antidote for lack of understanding, awareness, lack of consciousness, and chosen ignorance There is only a committed individual choice to become Please, adopt a meditative/contemplative practice, become more Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Empty the mind, focus on your breath or a short mantra The ego will resist, simply rinse and repeat Listen for the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit, they wait

George G
