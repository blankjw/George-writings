NOT ENOUGH TIME

By George Gondron

NOT ENOUGH TIME Too many realize, too often, too late Time has been wasted, squandered, abused There's not enough time Who suffers most from our constant busyness From our perpetual doing Relentlessly grabbing for the 'brass ring' Chasing the elusive 'rainbow' Looking for what's 'around the next bend' We suffer the most Our relationships suffer the most When 'doing' replaces 'being' If we can stop the doing long enough We are faced with, who is this person This person I call 'me' This person who has become what they 'do' This ego, void of spirit, empty of heart With our limited, routine, habitual, relationships Whose beliefs are all intellectual, all in the head With no experiential basis, no hands on knowledge Feelings, no more than just random thoughts Not feelings at all Who can say, 'I don't just believe, I know' In the realm of humanity, we know only one way Only in relationship, not in doing Only in sharing, in connection, in embracing Silence, stillness, and Presence are our teachers Fullness emerges from 'nothingness' Union rises from 'emptiness' Don't let there be, 'not enough time' It is yours to decide, it is yours to choose It is

George G
