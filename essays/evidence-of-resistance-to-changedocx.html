EVIDENCE OF RESISTANCE TO CHANGE

By George Gondron

EVIDENCE OF RESISTANCE TO CHANGE Change is inevitable, it can’t be stopped And the evidence of resistance to change is easily recognizable Nature doesn’t resist change, only humans resist Listening is a skill, most people don’t really possess They’re busy formulating a response to what they think they hear An open mind has no ‘axe to grind’, a closed mind does Most discussions with closeminded people are never about The real problems, but imagined ones They only want to focus on what they are against Rather than the underlying causes and possible solutions When confronted with actual facts they usually respond With ‘What about ism’s’ as if comparing is the issue Also notice that their narrative is defensive, another indication They’re not listening all, only clinging to their familiar What they believe is the truth is almost always a source From social media, not their concerted efforts about learning Not their critical thinking or reflection , their questions or doubts To believe that learning is only about acceptance of What is seen and heard, is to remain ignorant, part of the problem Pay attention, almost all topics discussed with closeminded Or selfish people have an underlying response based in fear Not in love, empathy, compassion, inclusion, or unity Our government in all branches, our educational institutions, Our churches, our extreme capitalism, all are failing the needs Of our citizens, our democracy, our Constitution, our Republic Pay attention to the narrative and character of those who wish To be and are leaders in our nation Does their narrative attempt to unite or divide? To lift us up or tear us down, to go forward or pull us back? To benefit the wealthy and corporations or all people? Be a change maker, not a resistor, resist fear embrace love Bring balance to your life through a meditative/contemplative path Just a few minutes a day be silent, be still, be present Present to your image of a creative Source, of Isness, of God In the silence focus on your breath, in and out Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They have your answers, they hold your true self, they wait

George G
