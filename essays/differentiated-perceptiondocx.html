DIFFERENTIATED PERCEPTION

By George Gondron

DIFFERENTIATED PERCEPTION Unfortunately the vast majority of people do not use differentiated perception – the varying ways we interpret sensory information There is a saying, ‘clarity of perception leads to accuracy of response’ Especially if what we see and hear is accepted without question, without doubt, then we can be easily manipulated and controlled Even if what we see and hear leaves us with an uneasy feeling, most will cling to a familiar habit, belief, or practice in their interpretations In other words, we take the easy way out A practice that only leads to ignorance and acceptance of misinformation – i.e. alternative facts, gaslighting, half truths Those who deal in propaganda are very adept at preying on a person’s emotions versus their intellect Stir up their emotions and the battle is more than half won Fear and blame work much easier than love and understanding The individual who uses differentiated perception is rare They are the exception to the rule in our culture For it seems the lie is much easier to sell than the truth And by the time the lie is revealed the damage has been done Or as the saying goes, ‘the toothpaste is out of the tube’ It’s bad enough how we’re currently confronted with social media and its many faces, but now we also must deal with AI Artificial intelligence may become our ultimate downfall making the lies, alternative facts, and half truths so much easier to hide and to disguise The reasons we’ve reached this point in our culture are many and varied, but one remains prominent It is our refusal to learn, to research, to doubt, to question, to use critical thinking We truly are, as Pogo once said, ‘our own worst enemy’ We are never at the fault, the fault always lies elsewhere We would much prefer pointing fingers than acknowledge our own ignorance, and the ‘gas lighters’ know it Please, adopt some sort of meditative/contemplative practice Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Connect with your own innate spirituality, your inner guides Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit, they wait They hold your true self and have your answers

George G
