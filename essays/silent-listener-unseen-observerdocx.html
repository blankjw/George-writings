SILENT LISTENER - UNSEEN OBSERVER

By George Gondron

SILENT LISTENER - UNSEEN OBSERVER An inner witness, an ever present sentinel We all have one Overlooked, tuned out, even denied Out of sight, out of mind She is our truth sayer He is our ever vigilant bringer of light Constant companions Struggling to increase our awareness Patiently waiting for an increase in consciousness Hearing every word we speak Seeing every act we commit Knowing every thought we have Never judging, never accusing, never condemning Waiting When will we tire of our games? When will we let go of our attachments? When will we love ourselves? Moving through life asleep As if in a dream, or perhaps a nightmare When will we wake up? When will we seek our inner advocate? When will we trust? When?

George G
