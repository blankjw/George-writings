HOW FAR

By George Gondron

HOW FAR How far from here to there? How far from mere existence to being alive? I know a few who struggle to survive But are more alive than some that are successful And strangely the successful don't even know it So trapped they are in their illusions Are maybe it's their definition of being alive Rubbing elbows with the 'right' people Traveling in the best 'circles' Belonging to the appropriate group Safe, secure, and protected But if they had to share their story It would not be of their struggles to survive Of the moments of living from hand to mouth It would be of their successes, and accomplishments Not just what they have, but how much of it they have If you ask them 'who are you?' They will tell you what they do or have done For they really don't know who they are The lights are on, but there's no one home They don't dare say 'I don't really know who I am' 'I push away this emptiness inside' 'This hole in my soul' 'I've been afraid to ask the question' 'Who am I?' It's not far from here to there Ask the question, you have the answer And if you don't like the response You can change that as well You can, only you, no one else

George G
