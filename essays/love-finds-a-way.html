LOVE FINDS A WAY

By George Gondron

LOVE FINDS A WAY When all else fails When the darkness seems unbearable When the burden is too heavy Love waits, resting within our soul In the depth of our pain, gently whispering I am here, I have always been here I have never gone, never left you But in the business of our lives We forget Losing track of what brought us into this existence We are not an accident, we are intended to be here An extension, a part, a connection of and to Love We are all seeds Germinating in the soil of Love We sprout, we grow, we mature Only through Love, aware or unaware, we are And through this journey of life We are pulled, we are tugged, we are pushed To and fro, up and down, around and around A few by choice, most by a thousand influences Until maybe we learn to sail our own ship Wake up to what's real and not an illusion Take ownership of the gift that we are Discover our uniqueness, in our sameness And realize our obvious connections to all that is In Love, for Love, for each other Within, among, and between, a part of the Whole No matter our situation, no matter our dilemma No matter how hopeless life may seem at times Love never gives up, never abandons, never releases Once loved by Love We cannot not be loved, that's a guarantee It's not a matter of degree, of being worthy or not Once created we are forever, forever in Love So, when it seems life can't be any darker Be still, be silent, listen everyday for a few moments And you will hear the small whisper that is Love, it's there

George G
