GRATITUDE

By George Gondron

GRATITUDE I have a suggestion, well actually it’s more of a request Every day, be grateful for at least one thing No matter how small or large it may be, find something And while you’re at it, appreciate someone in your life In our relationships, no matter how tense they may be There’s something we can appreciate about the other person Yes, gratitude and appreciation ought to be a daily task Not just for others, but for ourselves as well
