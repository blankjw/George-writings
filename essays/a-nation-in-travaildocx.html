A NATION IN TRAVAIL

By George Gondron

A NATION IN TRAVAIL We have a saying in the south, ‘everything’s going to hell in a handbasket’ and with very little effort it’s obvious to see why Even though we insist on the continued use of the psychological defense mechanisms to deny the obvious, it begins with the lack of education, the disturbing levels of illiteracy, and the rampant ignorance of millions using social media as their source for truth Words are nothing more than labels, they are not experience I know fire burns because of experience I know that a person has integrity, is decent because of their behavior, not their words But for reasons that can only be attributed to ego, the mind without any reference to the heart, soul, and spirit we fall into the trap of following the hyperbole, gaslighting, and propaganda of those who are self serving We live in a culture that by all measure is morally and spiritually bankrupt – prove me wrong, I welcome it For sure mainstream organized religion has not been the answer and it can be said that in many ways it is the problem Unwilling to ‘speak truth to power’ for fear of retribution I’m talking about the truth of kindness, inclusion, nondiscrimination, equality, justice, and most importantly love for one another If you’re one that believes this infinite universe is an accident That there is no such thing as Mystery, God, Isness, Creator then you need read no further for you have been brainwashed to be a follower a protagonist of production and consumption, a means to an end If you’re fortunate, as you grow older you also grow more aware, more conscious, more understanding If not, you cling to the familiar even though it’s painful We have a choice, either we become part of the solution or we remain a part of the problem, there is no neutral ground No choice is a choice, always Choose a meditative/contemplative practice for balance Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Learn to discern, connect inner and outer, focus, reflect, think Listen to the whispers of your inner guides, heart, soul, spirit They hold your true self, have your answers, they wait You contain a spark of the Divine DNA

George G
