THE CANCER OF DESPOTISM

By George Gondron

THE CANCER OF DESPOTISM The cancer which the despot spreads is fear Fear is their motivational tool Supported by their perpetual lies It is a deadly cancer with no medical cure Able to spread misunderstanding ignites the fire For what we don't understand, we learn to fear Step two, fear then leads to hatred Step three, where there's hatred there is no love Step four, with no love, no compassion Step five, no God The despot thrives on division Division be any means, no matter who gets hurt Their dysfunction feeds their small ego Living in a world of their own making Where they imagine they have all the answers Answers to questions that make no sense Where people, all people are only means to an end Their end They seek counsel from those who are 'yes people' Love the crowds that bow down to them Denigrate the ones that don't Fear, fabrication, and lies are their daily diet Their only enemy, truth They trust only other despots, praising their regimes Every deal is a business deal Devoid of any value of equality, justice, or human promise In the forefront of their mind is the constant thought What's in it for me Their high, is inflating their ego, tooting their own horn Demanding loyalty of others, but not of themselves Left unchecked they will like a cancer Destroy their host And it will be a slow miserable, painful, death Where many innocent people will suffer They will never willingly give up their false power Nor their inflated ego It has to be taken from them, or all will perish

George G
