TRANSPARENCY, COMPREHENSION, UNDERSTANDING

By George Gondron | Source: TRANSPARENCY, COMPREHENSION, UNDERSTANDING.docx

TRANSPARENCY, UNDERSTANDING, COMPREHENSION One of the unfortunate results of the explosion of social media Transparency is not so transparent What we see and hear is more about manipulation It is more about propaganda and half-truths than clarity Our ability to understand, and comprehend the facts Has become more and more difficult Especially as we become more and more influenced Through our emotions than the use of our intellect The lines of what’s moral have become very blurred indeed Rather than becoming more tolerant and understanding We have become more judgmental and condemning Social media, to some extent, has rendered us more Closed-minded and less open-minded Considering all that’s negative is primarily a result of fear Or lack of transparency, understanding, and comprehension And all that’s positive is a result of clear perception and love We obviously have become more fear-based and less love-based The process is as follows What we don’t understand, comprehend, or is not transparent We learn to fear, fear leads to hate, and where there is hate There is no love Most, if not all, that’s on social media is not there To expand our awareness and consciousness in a positive manner But only to manipulate, control, and convince us Of something negative, not positive Social media has become an art form in the use of the Psychological defense mechanisms, blame, denial, etc. Social media never takes ownership of its own shortcomings The fault always lies elsewhere; he she, it, or they are to blame It is not a matter of what we see and hear, but how we see and hear With an open understanding mind or a closed resistant mind Without a paradigm shift in human consciousness We only change individually, one at a time, embrace change Choose a meditative/contemplative practice, daily Spend a few minutes in silence, be still, sit in the presence of Isness Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit Discover your true self, your inner truth, they wait

Georg G
