THE RESULTS

By George Gondron

THE RESULTS Where, what, is the evidence of love Of friendships, of meaningful relationships Is most human interaction merely acquaintance? Superficial only, out of sight, out of mind Are they intentional or habit, our relationships Even with those we called 'real' The ones known as 'BFF' Is it what we get or what we give to the other Is it, 'I wonder why I haven't heard from them' 'I must not mean as much to them' 'As much as they do to me' Self-talk, but, I don't initiate contact The only solution to self-talk about relationship Is contact, reaching out, making an effort If you're afraid to ask for what you want You're in the wrong relationship If the worth of any relationship is valuable It is manifested in behavior, not thoughts A phone call. a note, a card, an invitation There are many ways to make contact And if you're handy with the gadgets There's email, text, facebook, and twitter Love, care, concern, and friendship All, are always shown through behavior Not thoughts So, when that special person crosses your mind Be resourceful, make contact Care, concern, and interest are not just thoughts They are actions Thoughts are fantasy, action is reality Just as love is not love, until it's given away

George G
