EVOLUTION, AN ETERNAL PROCESS

By George Gondron

EVOLUTION, AN ETERNAL PROCESS Everything evolves, all that is, changes Nothing exists which is not subject to change or alteration Including our ideas of a creative Source, subjective or objective Even the Bible itself is a historical narrative of a dynamic God One that not only acts upon creation But One that can also be influenced by His/Her creation Maybe it’s the evolution of human consciousness Of human awareness that grows in comprehension and perception That can comprehend the obvious nature of evolution Small minds tend to be caught in endless feedback loops Unable to see beyond what has become their familiar Unable to wonder, to risk, to be vulnerable Able to live with sameness, with similarity, with monotony Scientific evidence can more than support evolutionary facts It is not the science that is the problem, but the human mind Especially in this country, we seem to stagnate Once formal education has been completed Significant learning seems to cease We change into people who want to be spoon-fed We gravitate toward the negative side of social media We seek those ‘soothsayers’ of a similar level of awareness A like-mannered consciousness Which, if they were proponents of our continued Positive growth would be wonderful But alas, they seem to be more interested in Tooting their own horn, asking us to march to the beat Of their drum, which in the end benefits only them The real evolution of the human psyche is a matter of choice Not following the ‘pied piper’, drinking their ‘Kool-Aid’ It is a journey within, awakening our own internal resources Strengthening our own inner core Discovering our ‘true self’ through personal individuation Spend a few minutes each day with a meditative/contemplative Practice – in silence, with stillness, and Presence Listen to the whispers of your own heart, soul, and spirit Embrace a spiritual relationship with the One that waits

George G
