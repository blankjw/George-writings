ESSAY

DANCER

By George Gondron | Source: DANCER.docx

DANCER All of life, each individual being Has a creative ability, a gift unique to that person Most lie dormant, and are never awakened But for those who do awaken, we have a deep respect To witness the creative artistry of an incredible dancer Is indeed a profoundly moving spiritual experience A gift, any gift must be nurtured Nurtured with incessant and continuous hard work For the dancer it is a combination of body, soul, and spirit The daily repetition of movement and balance Pushing themselves beyond the limits of physical endurance Aching muscles, bloody and blistered feet All greatness embodies a personal sacrifice A sacrifice known only to the dancer, to the artist And as we watch in awe, a moving performance We seldom reflect on the effort and preparation That allows them to move and display such incredible grace We catch our breath and wonder how is that possible And their offering of symmetry and balance Is from their creative nature, their yearning to dance The true artist has no other choice If there were no audience at all, they still would dance It's not done for reward or recognition or praise Granted, appreciation is deeply acknowledged Graciously and humbly received But it is not the driving force behind their desire to dance It is the feeling of becoming the dance, becoming the movement And not just the dancer, not just the performer It must be how God feels dancing creation We are the dance and God is the Dancer I have many special memories of a dancer that I know Of times and places throughout the years Images that remain as if they were yesterday And I'm sure one of the last memories that I'll have As I pass from this life Will be of that dancer Becoming the dance

George G
