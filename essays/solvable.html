SOLVABLE

By George Gondron

SOLVABLE Almost every human tragedy that exists in the world Hunger, poverty, health care, housing, education, Safety, security, and religious division are solvable But for one reason Man’s inhumanity to man The dynamic of the human ego that insists on its ‘rightness’ It’s insatiable desire and need for power and control For riches and unlimited wealth, enough is never enough It is the weakness and frailty of the human condition Absent the ability to be self-critical or self-reflective As the saying goes, ‘power corrupts and absolute power Corrupts absolutely’ The few live off the labor, the backs of the many Christianity and Islam comprise more than 50% of the World’s religious followers Their history, when examined closely, is one of blood And destruction, power and control Extremely dualistic in their practice Poor representatives of a loving Creator Their focus is on worship as opposed to practice When there is no connection between the head and the heart When we as human beings are more focused on doing Than being, the results are plain to see How else can life be defined except as relationship With one another, with all of creation Yet since the beginning of time, we’ve been unable to Honor that definition with any sense of commitment Free will is a great gift from Love But without an acknowledgment of an inner core An internal reference, then all becomes ego driven Which never ends in a compassionate and loving place A daily few minutes of silence, with stillness and Presence Awakens the innate knowing held within each of us The gentle whispers of our heart, soul, and spirit The experiential connection of our own spirituality With the Isness of the universe, a relational reality Embrace a daily contemplative practice, it matters

George G
