ESSAY

LISTEN

By George Gondron

LISTEN How difficult to find a good listener The one who offers no advice Who doesn't say, 'I know how you feel' Who looks into your eyes with acceptance No pretense, just presence Few they are, many are the others The ones who pretend they understand I'm not seeking understanding The ones who give their advice I'm not wanting advice The ones with the blank 'don't bother me’ look Who really could care less 'I'm very busy now, but I'll stop', with frustration The many who talk at one another The few who talk with one another Do you really want to hear me? Do you really want to listen? Most listen to reply, not to learn We are very alike, you know How could we not be? We all have a story, it is our history We all have value, it is our life We all are connected, we are not separate Each a part of the Whole Are you a good listener? Can you be present? You can if you will It is a matter of choice A mind that is open, listens with purpose A mind that is closed Really doesn’t listen at all

George G
