APPREECIATION

By George Gondron

APPRECIATION Here’s a novel idea How about giving yourself a message of appreciation Every morning before you even get out of bed It’s more important than you can imagine I’d be willing to guess That seldom, if ever do you appreciate yourself Just a simple appreciation without ego involvement For example Appreciate your ability to think, to feel, to question Those simple appreciations bring awareness Of how very much we take for granted And we all know about assumptions We assume far, far too much The more we learn and express Appreciation for ourselves The more we begin to recognize and appreciate others For, after all, we’re more alike than unalike We are drawn together in our sameness And we grow together in our difference As you begin to express appreciation for others Family, friends, co-workers You will soon discover a new dynamic In your interactions with others Power struggles disappear Competition becomes cooperation Discrimination and separation become Unity, harmony, and collaboration When someone feels appreciated Topics that need to be discussed Conflicts that need to be addressed And worked through, become so much easier Tension and negative energy dissipate The ability to be open versus closed increases So please, learn to appreciate yourself Listen to your heart You will begin to express appreciation for others Remember, nothing ventured, nothing gained

George G
