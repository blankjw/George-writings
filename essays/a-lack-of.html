A LACK OF

By George Gondron

A LACK OF The negative issues that abound in this culture Are certainly a major part of its dysfunction But as well, it’s what we’re lacking that is as important If not more important We swim in an illusion of not being able to see The ‘forest for the trees’ We seem to be unable to step back and take a broader view Of a much greater problem Our focus is all on the externals, the material, The financial, the political, and now the religious There is a prevailing threat lingering on the horizon A threat from those religious far-right fanatics Who wish to see no separation between church and state And the threat is very, very real It’s really an oxymoron when one realizes that the greater Threat is the obvious observation That we are a culture that is spiritually and morally Bankrupt The highest form of morality is a matter of conscience We know innately the difference between right and wrong It has nothing to do with religion Also, in the words of Teilhard de Chardin ‘We are not human beings having a spiritual experience. We are spiritual beings having a human experience’ Again, this is not a religious issue, it’s a relationship issue If life has a definition, it is relationship, with everything To entertain only the externals of life is to be a part of The problem, not the solution To be embroiled in the negativity, not in the possibility Balance in life asks of us this one dynamic Engage the inner as well as the outer Have an internal reference as well as an ego Nurture your inner core as well as your mind Both are necessary for a full and fulfilling life Each day for a few minutes engage your heart, soul, and spirit In a meditative/contemplative practice of silence, stillness, With Presence -discover your inner Truth, It waits, patiently

George G
