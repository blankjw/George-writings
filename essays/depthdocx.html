DEPTH

By George Gondron

DEPTH The depth of our emotions, our intellect, our relationships All depend on the degree to which we are able and willing To be vulnerable, to risk To go beyond our familiar To resist clinging only to the illusions we call real To resist believing the obvious lie, the fabricated truth And by all evidence, few are willing The real truth; it is much more painful To be attached to the perceived false known Than to risk the greater unknown Freedom only happens in the letting go The path of human potential and growth Has no end What's important is the journey, not the arrival Of the many rest-stops along the way Most remain stuck, few more on As a result of our conditioning, our programming, Our habitual repeated patterns, day in and day out We settle We call the actions in our daily lives progress But if they do not deepen our awareness and consciousness Do not deepen and expand our relationships They are not progress, only the repeated matter-of-facts They are simply gathering more of what we desire They are merely repeated thoughts, feelings, and actions We simply reinforce the walls of our self made prison Resisting the inner urge to pull us forward Forward to greater depths of our human potential The inspiration for possible change may come from the outside But the work itself is an inside job We have within us all the tools we need for change We already hold the greater truth of who we are Begin with silence, experience stillness, witness Presence Empty your mind, listen to the whispers The innate knowing of your own heart, soul, and spirit That of the default divinity which is your true self There is a greatness within us waiting to be experienced

George G
