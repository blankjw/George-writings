THROUGH TH E EYES OF FEAR

By George Gondron

THROUGH THE EYES OF FEAR It is impossible to have clarity of perception when our Emotional energy is motivated by fear and not by love Rarely are we totally one or the other, motivated by fear or love But commonly we are usually more likely to be motivated By fear than love in our culture, our society So, what are the ‘tell tell’ signs we can recognize within Ourselves that indicate how we normally deal with Day-to-day reality from a fear base, here are just a few Bias, prejudice, closemindedness, unfounded judgements, Discrimination, defensiveness, following media sources that Do all the above, our unwillingness to ‘carry our own water’ In other words, do our own research, use critical thinking, Question, doubt, discern, reflect, trust science, etc., etc., etc. In a very real way, ignorance is the enemy, our willingness to Accept without knowing, to be controlled by our emotions As opposed to learning from our emotions Fear makes cowards of us all and keeps us divided So, what are he signs that we are more motivated by love A curious open mind, we can agree to disagree and remain cordial We are inclusive, welcoming, connected, understanding we are All more alike than unalike, we are gifts to one another even in our Differences, we’re good listeners, we seek to live in the present Moment, not in the past or the future, but the now We are kind, compassionate, nonjudgmental, believing that true Equality and justice are for all without exception, we are forgiving and Can admit when we are wrong, our way is not the only way We believe every human being has value, is creative and gifted We believe in inclusion, cooperation, conciliation, and community We believe our differences add to our human potential We believe no one is lesser or better than anyone else We know that in the realm of human possibility whatever the Question, love is always the answer To be loving and fearless, adopt a meditative/contemplative practice Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Focus on your breath or a brief mantra, breathe in, breathe out Listen for the whispers of your inner guides, heart, soul, spirit They hold your true self, have your answers, they patiently wait

George G
