IMAGINATION - A GIFT

By George Gondron

IMAGINATION – A GIFT Our imagination is the perfect example Of our ability to have a nondual mind It is both a blessing and a curse It is not either/or, it is both/and It is proof of how we can live in the past And we can live in the future All the while missing the now The arbiter of all our worries and fears – imagination And it can be our guide for kindness and compassion In truth, most of our feelings Are the result of our imagination, nothing more Our imaginations are proof of the saying ‘I have met the enemy, and it is me’ We construct our own self-made prison Through our imaginations And in most instances, it’s an emotional prison One in which we can blame others for our feelings Here’s a surprise As long as we have free will Blame doesn’t work It never has and never will Here’s another surprise We all hold the key to the prisons we create Only we can free ourselves from that prison No one else When there is a significant disconnect Between the head and the heart Between fear and love The results are almost always negative We are born with nondual minds But we morph into dualism very rapidly Through conditioning, programming, and patterning Rediscover the wisdom of your heart Spend a few minutes each day in silence In stillness with Presence Choose love over fear and worry Choose to live in the now, choose to love yourself

George G
