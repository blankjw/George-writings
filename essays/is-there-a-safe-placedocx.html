IS THERE A SAFE PLACE

By George Gondron

IS THERE A SAFE PLACE Elementary, middle school, high school, college Church, Temple, Mosque, Synagogue News rooms, shopping malls, theaters, clubs All have been venues of mass shootings Children, young people, adults, and the elderly Victims of white male 'gun totin' want-a -be's We are no closer to a solution now Than we were 10, 20, 30 years ago In fact as time moves on the problem has become worse The vast majority of American citizens Are in favor of sensible gun legislation Including law abiding gun owners Beginning at least with the discussion, a conversation So where does the problem lie? The tentacles of resistance are many But the underlying reason regardless of the argument Is simple It's always and ever about money and power Hiding behind the Second Amendment Gun manufacturers and the NRA pouring millions of dollars Into political coffers, owning the politicians It's always about money folks, always The government is not trying to take away gun ownership What is so difficult about passing legislation for Universal background checks Banning assault military type weapons Eliminating large capacity magazines How many shots from a semi-automatic weapon Does it take to kill a deer? It's interesting to note that the requirements for Driving a vehicle, for adopting a child, having a bank account For building a house, for holding a professional license For voting, for filling a prescription, for getting married Are more restrictive than buying a deadly weapon And then there's the fear motivating hyperbole If all else fails, use fear, fear works, right? Yes, is there a safe place, it appears not

George G
