WHEN WILL WE DECIDE

By George Gondron

WHEN WILL WE DECIDE? What right do we have to complain If we do not think? If we simply swallow the lies as though they were truth If we do not question or doubt Demand the proof, the facts, the evidence, the confirmation For if we do not pursue the veracity of the wordiness Of the propaganda and dualistic verbage Assuming that Facebook, Youtube, and word of mouth are truth Then the day will come when no matter the lies It will be too late All the 'I told you so's' will be hollow and empty It will no longer make sense, who was right, who was wrong Our kneejerk reactions will not defend our mistakes All our excuses will fall on deaf ears Yes, when will we decide to reason and reflect To educate ourselves, expand our minds, learn from experience Instead of listening to the 'hucksters' and 'charlatans' Who play the game to simply gain control Or even worse, to remain in control Who, in the end care about only themselves And convincingly claim it's for the good of us all Yes, it is true, power corrupts and corrupts absolutely The rich get richer, the poor get poorer And those in between disappear No matter how promising and glorious the words may sound The selling of a lie never has a good end Promises made from the ego alone Without the influence of the heart, soul, and spirit Or no more than empty words, with no real substance Holding out false hope for those who want to believe That surely this time the lies will be true Surely this time the promises made will be fulfilled Surely, this time We must Listen to our hearts, let our spirits be still Hear the whispers of our soul and learn the truth We will know from the Essence given with our life Then, perhaps then, we will decide, to reject the lies

George G
