EXPERIENCE DENIED

By George Gondron | Source: EXPERIENCE DENIED.docx

Generally, we human beings don’t embrace change New ideas can easily be denied if they are too far outside our familiar However inner experiences, experiences of a numinous nature can’t be denied, they have a tendency to ‘hang around’ Like a small pebble in our shoe, an irritation that lingers until dealt with in a conscious intellectual manner, sadly infrequently Experiences gained as a result of our five senses, taste, touch, see, hear, and smell are precursors to our thoughts and feelings And everything we take in since birth as a result of our senses becomes our personal data base that impacts our present day thoughts, feelings, and actions How we know what we say we know matters Do we know as a result of our own personal experiences, or because of another’s input To objectively examine one’s own personal beliefs is a daunting task Daunting for the dualistic mind that is very polarizing However, the more we pursue a both/and approach to this journey we call life rather than an either/or approach, the more we will trust our own experiences, inner and outer, to aid us in making our best choices each moment of the day We are creatures of habit and our behavior can be very predictable with little effort, especially if we cling to our emotions as opposed to our intellect when making decisions Carl Jung, the famous Swiss psychotherapist and founder of analytical psychology, was instrumental in the writing of the Big Book by Bill W, the twelve step program for addicts Jung hypothesized that addictions are an attempt to experience one’s own personal spirituality, misguided by what we ingest and impulse disorders The addict lacks balance, inner and outer, focused on doing as opposed to being and consequently denies inner experience A catalyst for inner awakening is a meditative/contemplative practice, a few minutes of silence, stillness, and Presence daily A time to just be, to listen to the whispers of our inner guides It is our heart, soul, and spirit that bring balance and awaken our spirituality, our connection to the Divine, Universal consciousness They hold our true self, they have our answers, they wait

George G
