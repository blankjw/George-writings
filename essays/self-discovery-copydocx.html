SELF-DISCOVERY ! - Copy

By George Gondron

SELF-DISCOVERY The greatest gift we each have to offer humanity Is the gift of self-discovery As Shakespeare wrote ‘This above all - to thine own self be true, and it must follow, as night follows day, thou canst not then be false to, any man.’ But as well, from the author M. Scott Peck Few there are that choose ‘The Road Less Traveled’ Self-discovery is not the path chosen by many, only a few Few they are that are even aware of an internal yearning Held within the psyche of each of us that whispers ‘There is more to you than you realize’ Our limitations are self-imposed when there is no connection Between our heads and our hearts When the ego rules, the mind controls, and the intellect is supreme Without the heart’s knowing there is no balance Just as night balances day, darkness balances light We also are creatures yearning for equanimity, for balance In a world that focuses on the material, the external We exist only as consumers, perpetrators of our own emptiness Harbingers of our own fear and uncertainty Lacking the awareness that there is an internal source An inner core in each of us that is a well of compassion Silently waiting to soothe our frenzied lives Focused on more is better, bigger is better, faster is better Without that needed balance in our lives We look in the mirror and ask ‘With whom am I having the pleasure?’ There is so much more to us than just our thoughts and feelings More than our day-to-day incessant repetitive ramblings To discover our ‘true self’ requires a change Not a physical change or a mental change But a spiritual change, a different path of self-discovery A time-out each day for a few minutes of silence, stillness, With Presence – Presence being your image of the creative Energy of the universe, the Isness and Source of all In the quiet listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit Listen to the Truth, embrace your healing, come home

George G
