SUBJECT AND OBJECT

By George Gondron

SUBJECT AND OBJECT Subject and object can apply to an infinite number of things But in regard to humanity, human beings are always subjects At least that’s what we’re supposed to be But more and more it seems we are nothing more than objects Unawares, we each are guilty of treating others as objects When we have silent expectations of others, when they are Supposed to fulfill our unspoken wants, needs, and desires as Adults, we become objects, not subjects Maybe it’s a carryover from infancy when of course our needs Where assumed by the adults who took care of us We are no longer infants, but some would beg to differ There are two primary reasons we don’t express our expectations We know on some level they are our own responsibility, or we might Hear that awful word ‘no’, I can’t do that, and of course we Immediately hear that word ‘no’ as meaning there’s something wrong With us personally – that’s a problem unto itself If our relationships are healthy, we can discuss our expectations Rather than ‘dancing’ around the issue, making assumptions As far as our culture, our society, the larger problem is the fact That we all are being treated as objects by the extreme ideals of Capitalism run rampant; we are nothing more than a means to An end and of course that end is always about money, power In fact, all ‘isms’ have a mission statement that speaks to their Membership and if the ‘dos’ and ‘don’ts’ of that statement Can be translated into control and demands of its members Those members are being objectified, no longer subjects As long as the ideals of freedom, equality, and justice for all Is a lived reality, we are subjects, if not, we are objects If we are not all free, if we are not all equal, if we all do not Receive justice, none of us do In our Western culture we lack balance, inner and outer Adopt a meditative/contemplative practice for connection Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Awaken your discernment capability and critical thinking Focus on your breath or a short mantra, breathe in, breathe out The mind will wander, the ego will resist, rinse and repeat Listen to the whispers of your inner guides, heart, soul, spirit

George G
