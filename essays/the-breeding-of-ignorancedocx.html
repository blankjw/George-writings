THE BREEDING OF IGNORANCE

By George Gondron

THE BREEDING OF IGNORANCE Living in ignorance has only one way to survive Has one way to spread, to breed, to disseminate Through fear Fear, unresolved, leads only to more ignorance The lack of critical thinking, a biased media Unable to question, to doubt, stuck with the familiar All of these fuel the flames of fear Ignorance depends on our unwillingness to be intelligent To focus only on the negative, only on difference not sameness To allow our reality to be formed by outside influence To refuse to 'check it out' for ourselves That is ignorance, not intelligence Most of what we claim we know, we don't It is merely 'third party' information Sadly, some of the greatest perpetrators of fear are religions If you don't believe what I believe, worship the way I worship You are different, and difference is fearful The dualistic mind cannot but live in fear For it perceives all areas of life as either/or situations False perceptions that leave no room for compromise Either you're for me, or against me Ignorance, in order to survive, has to have 'rules' There is no flexibility, inclusiveness, or respect for all life Only my life, or our life, not theirs Ignorance follows a path disguised as life, but is death Death in many forms Ask those who have suffered through persecution Persecution of their beliefs, their gender, their race Only because of fear, the breeder ignorance The passage out of the realm of fear and ignorance Is through the heart, soul, and spirit Through equanimity and balance, not through dualism If spirituality is not an intentional and consistent part of life The result is always ignorance formed from fear Love of your God, love of your neighbor are not religious concepts But the way of relational wholeness, not sectarian separateness They are the evidence of a transformed, fearless person

George G
