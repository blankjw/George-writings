THE HYPOCRISY OF MORAL AUTHORITY

By George Gondron

THE HYPOCRISY OF MORAL AUTHORITY Where do we find the Department of Moral Authority? It's certainly not a governmental office, never has been Nor can it be found in our capitalistic economic system As far as I know, no corporate entity Has a Moral Authority Department, perhaps they should Then maybe their real goals would be service to others Rather than self service, bottom line service, profits first Sadly, even in the helping professions, what stands between Those that are served and those that provide the service Is money, controlled mostly by managed health care By insurance companies, drug companies, medical entities Only so much 'pro bono' service is available So when the self righteous use words such as 'moral authority' They really are being judgmental, 'tooting their own horns' Using others as scapegoats to carry their own shortcomings You know the type Having a beam in their own eye Attempting to remove the splinter in someone else's eye Or casting stones as they live in glass houses It's easy to find those who use the language of Moral Authority Just listen carefully, they are the ones who speak for God The ones with all the answers Who can tell you exactly what's wrong with your life As they cling to the form of their religion But have no spiritual connection to the One they call God They don't enter into the substance of their beliefs But dwell on the edges, being faithful members And paying their 'fire insurance' Moral authority is found in only one place It is found within the human heart, soul, and spirit The whispers of which are drowned out by our culture Through meaningless distractions, incessant noise Through the ego's desires and attachments Truth is a matter of the heart, compassion a matter of soul And spirit a matter of relationship Moral authority lives within each of us, waiting to be awakened

George G
