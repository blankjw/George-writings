ESSAY

DIFFERENCES

By George Gondron

DIFFERENCES There are those who see only difference Only that the other, is not like them But a few, see uniqueness within sameness In truth, we are all more alike than unalike We all in essence seek the same secure life All human, all relational, all with beating hearts It is those who see perceived differences Rather than sameness, that create division That cannot see unity within diversity Seeing only the obvious and not the hidden Judging by externals, deciding by skin color Inferring through belief, appraising by gender Value neutral is an unknown concept to those Unknown to the inflated ego, the arrogant mind To the person who thrives on intellect alone Empty of difference between knowledge and knowing The world is full of those who see only difference The 'rumor mongers', the 'third party informers' The 'ladder climbers', the 'I told you so' The 'chasers' after what does not last Those who thirst for power and control What helps us see the sameness? The search to know ourselves, our own individuation Our acceptance by others we perceive as different Our willingness to be vulnerable, to risk To explore our own nature; heart and soul To let go of that which is not factual, only fantasy And most important, discover our own spirit Discover the essence that is not singular That is universal and given to all Discover that Essence, that Life, that Love; discover That

George G
