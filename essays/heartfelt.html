HEARTFELT

By George Gondron

HEARTFELT Heartfelt, indicative of profound unfathomable feelings Feelings of extreme joy, of happiness, of ecstasy Of unimaginable sorrow, pain, and agony as well That without notice spontaneously brings tears to the eyes Only possible when our thoughts cease When the incessant chatter in the mind fades away When we're caught in the moment The moment of what's happening now, being present All conditions of having heartfelt moments The heart never leads astray, never deceives, never betrays It is the mind that is the great deceiver The ego willing to accept the lie An uninformed mind easily influenced by distraction And unwilling to search for the truth More than capable of being conditioned, programmed, And manipulated More than open to being sensationalized Through tempting propaganda and fancy words But the heart can discern fact from fiction Yet we never listen to our hearts To the truth that is carried within the human soul We much prefer the externals We avoid critical thinking and self-reflection It’s so much easier and convenient to deny and blame As long as we can point our fingers elsewhere We never have to point them at ourselves Where the real problem lies We have no one to blame for our own Thoughts, feelings, and actions They are always a matter of choice, always But to take ownership of that reality requires Self-acceptance and having an open mind An increase in awareness and consciousness Is an intentional process discovered through A meditative/contemplative practice A few minutes each day of silence, stillness, with Presence We already possess the truth, it dwells inside

George G
