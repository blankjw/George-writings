HOW DOES IT HAPPEN

By George Gondron

HOW DOES IT HAPPEN? What awakens that which seems asleep A new awareness, a deeper consciousness Could it be unimaginable emotional pain Perhaps unconditional forgiving love Or, a deep absolving silence Possibly an unparalleled tranquil stillness Maybe, maybe not For some, one or all will suffice For others, none can penetrate the will The will to resist change To cling to what the mind can control At least to what it thinks it controls The resistance translates to a loneliness I may not confess the fact, but it's there In quiet moments I feel the emptiness A faint but present yearning But only briefly, and then it's gone I'm able to distract once again Able to avoid that which will awaken me Is it spirit, soul, or heart that seeks me Is it an essence within from Love itself The Hound of heaven that never gives up Never abandons the chase for my kinship So, maybe the question is not how does it happen But, perhaps, when will I stop running? Stop resisting and embrace the yearning Welcome the Pursuer Discover Life Encounter Love When will I?

George G
