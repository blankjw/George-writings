WHAT THE HELL

By George Gondron

WHAT THE HELL? When the vast majority of our citizens believe in common sense gun legislation why is nothing being done. It’s doesn’t take an Einstein to give us the correct answer. It’s plain – it’s called money It’s called caring more about being reelected than protecting innocent lives. How do these ‘asshole’ legislators sleep at night? Thoughts and prayers – BS!!!! Thoughts and prayers will never solve the problem. If you vote for anyone who takes money from the NRA you are at fault just as well as the elected official. Over 200 mass shootings in this country already and we’re only in May. I believe in the Second Amendment. I do not believe we need assault weapons for hunting. I do not believe we need 30 round clips for hunting, I do believe every gun should be registered, period. In no other country are there assholes entering schools, churches, shopping centers, grocery stores, nightclubs, and public facilities killing innocent people – none. The lack of meaningful response is unconscionable and beginning with offering thoughts and prayers is beyond hypocrisy. When did thoughts and prayers stop a bullet from an AR15 killing a nine-year-old child? Maybe we need a new law. If you take money from the NRA then you must recuse yourself from voting on any law regarding common sense gun legislation. I am sicken to my core when I know that those who are strong advocates of ‘pro-life’ are the same people who only care about offering sincere condolences for these innocents who have died because we have failed to do anything. This cannot continue. It must stop. If not, it confirms that what’s important in this nation is not people. It is everything else.

George G
