THE HEART'S PATH

By George Gondron

THE HEART'S PATH The heart's capacity to love far exceeds the confines of the human body Its path exceeds mere sight It extends beyond the universe Beyond gender, beyond all human emotion Encompassing the vastness of creation and much more Its love knows no limits, has no boundaries Suffers no rejection and endures all Accepts all It carries the potential and possibilities of all goodness Made in the image and likeness of its Creator How could it be anything less? Left to its own nature, the heart cannot but love Just as its Source Ah, the nemesis of the heart, the mind The stumbling block of love, the ego But we have a defender, free will We chose to love or the heart withers We chose compassion or the heart weakens We chose life or we cling to external gratification Follow the path of the heart Follow Love Follow

George G
