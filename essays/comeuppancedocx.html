COMEUPPANCE

By George Gondron

COMEUPPANCE A deserving fate as a result of our human frailty, comeuppance Somehow, we have forgotten how to be thankful for our frailty The secret to success always lies in failure first And yes, if you never risk, never become vulnerable You will never fail, right? Wrong The refusal to become, by its very nature, is a failure Within each of us is a gift, the ability to create The path to becoming fully human includes failure Includes our comeuppance lessons, I hope you’ve had many But more than that, I hope you learned from those lessons Addictions of all sorts, feelings, thoughts, actions Are maintained and supported through our refusal to learn To accept the fate of our choices, blame is so much easier Most of our suffering has only one source, ourselves Our choices, our decisions, our chosen ignorance, a refusal to learn All of these have an impact on who we now are in this moment And the effects of all the aforementioned are our comeuppance As they saying goes, ‘We will reap what we sow’ Most of what we do is not consciously ill-conceived or malicious But rather is the result of living in a Western culture That is focused on consumption and has little or no connection To an internal reference or inner core, only thoughts matter But what changes you, me, everyone, is experience not thoughts If I may, let me suggest a new experience Throughout history spiritual teachers and mystics Have written about the importance of some form of inward journey As John of the Cross wrote, a ‘dark night of the soul’ from his Book by the same title Adopt a mediative/contemplative practice, the ego will resist Take a few minutes each day for silence, stillness, and Presence Presence being your image or idea of the Mystery of the universe Isness, Universal Consciousness, or God – the name matters not What matters is creating the context for an experience Empty your mind, focus on your breath or a short mantra Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, they wait

Gorge G
