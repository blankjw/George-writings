REALITY

By George Gondron

REALITY Words, we fall in love with words Especially our own Words can never express the reality Whatever that may be We don't think in words We think in images But images simply arise out of memory They are not real in the present Our too many words become distractions Distractions from the real Our too many memories clutter our minds Pushing away the now Reality exists in moment to moment awareness Present moment freshness Allowing clarity of perception And accuracy of response Response to what is, not what was or will be What's real arises from the silence From the spaces between the too many words From the emptiness of the mind Surprised by the mystery, not by the already Experience the silence Take time to be still Listen to the quiet Allow the whisper Discover the Real You may find your self

George G
