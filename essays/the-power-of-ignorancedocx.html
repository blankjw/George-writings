THE POWER OF IGNORANCE

By George Gondron

THE POWER OF IGNORANCE Or its’ opposite, the importance of continuing education And sorry, expecting to be educated through social media is like expecting to win the lottery, good luck In our current environment with access to the necessary tools for continued learning, to remain ignorant is a choice A choice for two reasons, we think we already know the answers or we fear knowing the answers Ignorance under any circumstances is never the answer It is better to know than to not know, always And reliable knowing is not, I repeat, is not following the internet Last year our country ranked 16th in science, 9th in reading, and 34th in math, compared to other nations In leading educational systems we rank 31st, the previous year we ranked 1st Last year 21% of adults were illiterate, could not read or write As well, 54% of adults have a literacy level below 6th grade World wide we rank 36th in literacy Also, there is a direct correlation between poverty and literacy The poverty level per person is $15,060 and we currently have approximately 40 million people living in poverty, in the richest country of the world and the only way to honestly justify that reality is because of greed: power, control and excessive wealth Our current President and his billionaire buddies love the poorly educated, a phrase he repeated regularly at his rallies A willingness to accept without knowing is called ignorance And to say you believe without knowing, is also ignorant To be governed by those who see voters as a means to an end, their end, is to be the fool, we seem to have an abundance of fools We each have within us a ‘truth teller’, a discerning ability rejected by our ego, innately given with this gift we call our life Adopt a meditative/contemplative practice to awaken your inner Resources, each day choose some silence, stillness, with Presence For a few minutes focus on your breath or a short mantra Breathe in, breathe out, the ego will resist, simply rinse and repeat Listen for the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They are your inner guides, they hold your true self They have your answers, and they patiently await

George G
