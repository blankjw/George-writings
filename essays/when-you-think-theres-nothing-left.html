WHEN YOU THINK THERE'S NOTHING LEFT

By George Gondron

WHEN YOU THINK THERE’S NOTHING LEFT There is, and here’s the secret You won’t find it outside of yourself, it’s onside of you, waiting We live in a culture that seldom talks about our internal resources Even organized religion is mostly about the externals About a relationship with ‘Other’, not with ourselves To know ourselves is a daunting task, it takes great courage For most of us identify with a role, our thoughts, our beliefs, Our gender, even our feelings, but we are so much more
