CONSCIOUSNESS

By George Gondron

CONSCIOUSNESS In some circles there is the belief all of creation has consciousness From the micro to the macro, the small to the large But it is only the human that has the capability to direct Its consciousness through choice, or free will And as well, humans have an unconscious Mislabeled by many as the subconscious and sub means inferior But the unconscious is anything but inferior And in truth the unconscious plays a significant role in Many of our choices, our so called free will, unawares In fact the famous Swiss analytical psychologist, Carl Jung Compared the conscious mind to the unconscious mind As a cork floating on the ocean Obviously the ocean has much more power than the cork The process of individuation or coming to wholeness Is in general terms bringing unconscious material Into consciousness or bringing our ‘shadow’ into light Our unwillingness to individuate to engage our unconscious Results in all the negative and destructive behavior That is so apparent throughout the world If also results in denying our own creativity and uniqueness The gift we each are to all humanity We suffer a ‘double whammy’ cut off from our unconsciousness And cut off from the knowing of our heart, soul, and spirit Living through our ego, our mind alone Like a ship adrift on the ocean without a rudder We fall into the trap of believing we are only our thoughts And worse, following our emotions, living through our defenses Human ‘doings’ grasping and consuming, we forgot how to be Change happens in many ways, context and interaction are two Science and medicine are revealing more and more the benefits Of a meditative/contemplative practice, which the ego resists For after all doesn’t the ego know what’s best for each of us? Spend a few minutes each day in silence, be still, embrace Presence Focus on your breath or a short mantra, breathing, breath out Pay attention to your dreams, they are your messengers Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, they wait

George G
