CONNECTIONS

By George Gondron

CONNECTIONS The fullness of life The wholeness of being each individual innately possesses Is about connections Connections within, between, and among It begins with self We live in a culture, a society whose primary focus Resolves around the physical and intellectual All evidence to the contrary reveals that in our behavior We seldom show any indication of a connection To heart, soul, and spirit We are pro-fear and anti-love in our thoughts, feelings, and actions Our level of awareness and consciousness is about others Not about ourselves, especially our shadow self When we, as individuals, do not have a healthy connection With all our resources, inner and outer, positive and negative How can we ever have healthy connections with others? How can we begin with sameness rather than difference Look for possibility rather than peril Without some paradigm shift in human consciousness All positive change begins with us, you and I That change can only happen when we are connected To the totality of our humanness Body, mind, heart, soul, and spirit Our ego can be translated as our false self, our perceived image Our true self can only be known experientially Through the experienced connection of heart, soul, and spirit Connected to our thoughts, feelings, and actions To live and operate from wholeness, not incompleteness A guaranteed method of connecting and living each day With wholeness is a meditative/contemplative daily practice A few minutes of silence, stillness, in the presence of Presence A time to empty our ever-busy ‘monkey minds’ Of their incessant and often repetitive thoughts To seek a genuine connection to our heart, soul, and spirit A connection to your image of a creative Source A Source that is relational, personal, that inner yearning Become a human being, not just a human doing

George G
