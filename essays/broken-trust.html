BROKEN TRUST

By George Gondron

BROKEN TRUST One of the most painful experiences Between individuals, primary and secondary Is broken trust The kind of trust that is not repairable A violation so deeply felt there is no recovery It has nothing to do with love or forgiveness One can still love, one can forgive But through the experience Something has been so deeply infringed upon So disrespected and offended, so broken That there is no desire for the relationship To continue Is there a sense of loss and unbearable sadness? Of course there is, but time heals all Slowly but surely, we regain our balance We focus on the better, let go of the loss Move beyond the pain It is a process, as is life in general, a process Our assumptions, our ‘taking-for-granted’ attitudes Our belief that our relationship is secure Is an unspoken denial that life does change And suddenly we experience the opposite It is like being ‘blindsided’ Like our heart has been ripped from our chest Difficult to comprehend Impossible to understand We have all experienced broken trust And when there is no clear indication Of one’s part in that reality It makes the experience even more difficult to grasp We run a feedback loop in our minds of ‘What did I do wrong?’ And after an honest self-examination You find nothing, don’t beat yourself up – let go A daily practice of a few minutes of silence, stillness, With Presence is a healing process – give it a shot Empty the mind, listen to the whispers of your heart

George G
