GULLIBILITY

By George Gondron | Source: GULLIBILITY.docx

GULLIBILITY When you have an adult citizenry, 54% of which have a literacy rate of 6th grade or lower, gullibility is more determinative of their beliefs than factual information Alternative facts, hyperbole, gas-lighting, and propaganda are now the rule oF the day In addition, the more social media input is directed toward ones emotions, the easier it is to manipulate the outcome All success is preceded by failure first, nothing ventured nothing gained, no risk no reward, no pain no gain When we don’t apply critical thinking, question, doubt, or do our own research we should not expect a positive outcome To be gullible is to be easily deceived or cheated The choices we make as a result of our critical thinking versus our emotional attachments is vastly different Consequently, those who attempt to influence us do so through our emotions rather than an appeal to our intellect In addition, they almost always use fear based language as opposed love based language to reinforce their message Their words paint a negative versus a positive picture When we process their message we do so through images not through the words themselves No matter how exaggerated the rhetoric the more our emotions take control rather than our critical thinking When others are labeled as rapists, drug dealers, murderers, looters, thieves, and scum our minds create images of the same To be gullible is to be willing to accept the unacceptable without any direct personal evidence whatsoever To embrace the lie or the alternative facts is to be the fool As we grow older we become more set in our ways And as a result change becomes more difficult The ego doesn’t like change, preferring the familiar Even if the familiar is painful Incorporate a meditative/contemplative practice into your life A few minutes each day of silence, stillness, and Presence Presence being your image of the Creator of the universe Focus on your breath, breathe in breathe out, rinse and repeat Listen to the whispers of your inner guides, heart, soul, spirit

George G
