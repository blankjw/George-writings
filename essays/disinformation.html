DISINFORMATION

By George Gondron

DISINFORMATION What is it about the human mind that is more attracted To disinformation as opposed to misinformation or the truth? Disinformation is the spreading of false information deliberately To deceive people or to manipulate them in order to Accomplish some ill-defined goal dressed in fancy rhetoric It preys on their emotions versus their intellect And it is primarily directed toward the least or poorly educated Those with a low level of literacy and strangely enough The literacy level in our culture is 6th grade or lower for 54% Of all adults, and one in five adults cannot read or write Those are frightening statistics, a substantial basis for a fascination With social media in all its many forms All the protagonist need do is feed the population Its propaganda that lights up the screens or fills the airwaves Of all the available digital products which are easy to acquire Of course, there is more than one reason for low literacy levels Our educational goals and the means to attain them have failed It’s not the teacher’s fault, their hands are tied They are forced to teach a student how to pass a test first And how to learn is secondary, just pass them to next grade Because of the human need to control, because of money, Because of power, because of politics, we fail our children The mind’s natural inclination is curiosity, to learn, to wonder But when the mind is overwhelmed with information It will follow the path of least resistance, the emotional path Much easier to be controlled by our emotions than critically think Than do our own research, question and doubt, and seek the truth It seems we much prefer to listen to the ‘wizard behind the curtain’ Follow the ‘pied piper’ or the ‘carrot on the stick’, the ‘beat of Someone else’s drum’ than practice our own due diligence Our lives ask that we be responsible and not just follow blindly For balance, adopt a meditative/contemplative practice Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Isness Empty your mind, focus on your breath or a short mantra The ego will resist, the mind will wander, rinse and repeat Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They reveal your true self, they have your answers, they wait

George G
