SMOKE AND MIRRORS

By George Gondron

SMOKE AND MIRRORS We live in a time of hypocrisy and sensationalism Driven by personalities needy of recognition Social media provides them with a platform Which satisfies and inflates their egos Hiding behind smoke and mirrors Like the character in the Wizard of Oz Their underlying objectives are threefold They are seeking power, control, and wealth They are the modern-day pied pipers And we, unawares, are their followers Sheep following our chosen shepherd Sadly, we are convinced that what we are doing Who we are following is perfectly warranted They have discovered which emotional buttons To push that keep us in line, don’t question or doubt We cling to our decisions, our choices And they are choices, the right choice of course The stage upon which they play is unimportant Political, religious, conspiratorial, nationalism, Isolationism, socialism, capitalism, racism, In fact, all the ism’s Whatever is required to keep us separated and divided They abhor unity and congeniality And avoid wholeness, integrity, and harmony If they can keep us fighting amongst ourselves They win, we lose As a result, those at the top make the rules Those at the bottom must follow those rules And those in the middle disappear As long as they can keep us from being Self-reflective, self-critical, and introspective Nothing gets better, all remains the same If anything, they get stronger, we only get weaker The process of individuation is a path to wholeness To wellness, connection, inclusion, and kindness Choose that path, look behind the smoke and mirrors Before it’s too late, find your true self

George G
