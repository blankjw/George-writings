LIAR IN CHIEF

By George Gondron

LIAR IN CHIEF The real issue is not that we have a President that cannot tell the truth The real issue is that people believe his obvious lies Maybe Hitler’s Joseph Goebbels, Minister of Propaganda was correct, you tell a lie enough times and it becomes the truth Trump’s lies have become so outrageous, fact checking is a waste of time, it’s nothing more than the expenditure of wasted energy when even an adolescent can easily recognize the meaningless BS and therein lies the conundrum, adolescents didn’t vote for him adults did, who are supposed to know better Has our level of ignorance become so severe that we willingly vote against our own best interest? The ‘proof is in the pudding’, yes, it has So now we have a Commander in Chief who is clearly ignoring the constructs of a democratic system of government and the Constitution, which is its basis, including the 27 Amendments Hitler was able to topple the democratic government of Germany in 53 days, Trump is clearly following his play book He has managed, as a result of his Republican loyalists in the Senate, to put people in positions of power that will do his ‘bidding’ Whatever that ‘bidding’ may be, and we are witnessing the immediate results which will have a negative impact on us all His chosen ‘lackey’ Elon Mush is not about eliminating fraud and waste, those are only secondary goals, the real purpose is the destruction of government institutions without regard for who is affected and to what degree, people don’t matter That is the real issue, we don’t matter, and if you can’t answer this question, who does matter? You need to do some serious ‘soul searching’, the answer is abundantly clear, the wealthy, the powerful, the oligarchs We are nothing more than ‘pawns’ in their chess game Nothing more than a means to an end, their end To fulfill their insatiable need for control, power, and ego inflation Small minded men who care nothing about humanity in general Please, awaken your ability to discern, to critically think Don’t be a ‘sheep’, spend a few minutes in silence, stillness, with Isness every day, connect inner and outer Listen to your inner guides, heart, soul, and spirit, they wait

George G
