INTELLIGENCE VS INTELLECT

By George Gondron

INTELLIGENCE VS INTELLECT All humans have an intellect, a brain And we attribute to the intellect the power to reason But the use of reason only through the mind Intelligence is a very different matter indeed For intelligence embraces all the human faculties Thoughts, feelings, our five senses, and intuition As well as our heart, soul, and spirit Not just the mind With no exception, we are all and each addicted Addicted to our way of perceiving, thinking, and understanding Those who comprehend with the mind alone Are vastly different from those that use all their resources The mind alone reacts to most input in a narcissistic fashion With dualistic opinions and narrow constraints Awash in their own subjectivity, unable to be objective Those who live only in their ego, have no other choice No other choice but to be smug, complacent, controlling They have all the answers and suffer from few doubts To do otherwise would be to lose a sense of self

Albeit, a false-self Those who live through their intelligence Receiving input from all their resources They know one thing for sure That they don't know, but are willing to search, to question To live not only with humility, but importantly With compassion, inclusion, and connection They see the possible as opposed to the impossible Recognize sameness before difference They are more concerned about the present moment Than the event of yesterday or tomorrow They refuse to cling to the status quo Reliving life's 101 courses, but are ready to move on They understand the importance of a spiritual life Of a connection to the One they call God And not just the same religious confines They are; people of the truth and not the lie

George G
