NONSENSICAL

By George Gondron

NONSENSICAL We have reached a point in our political discord Where the narrative lacks any intelligible meaning And if you’re really listening, paying attention And attempting to ‘shift’ through the senseless and ridiculous Statements made by one of the major political nominees For the highest office in our nation, your will easily discern Fact from fiction, and the lie from the truth You will also understand that what is being appealed to Is not your intelligence but your emotions The irrational absurdity of the ramblings of a grown Man, are too obvious to ignore or deny The phrase, ‘the emperor has no clothes’ is a description of A phenomenon when people are unwilling to acknowledge Or admit obvious issues, flaws, or shortcomings in the narrative Of a powerful person Exaggeration is the lynchpin of the ‘bully’, the ignorant To be manipulated or swayed by a fool is to become a fool If you believe in truth, search for the facts Don’t fall for the hyperbole meant to separate and divide us Don’t be ‘gas lighted’ by emotional discord that fuels conspiracy Theories and deep state fallacies that are preposterous and insane All of which, once again, are meant to divide not unite The words pro and con have Latin origins, ‘pros and contras’ Meaning for and against, it is obvious one nominee is a ‘con’ He has demonstrated clearly that he is above the law Clearly, that he is the law, all that matters is what he decides It takes little effort to fact check his background, his history His business failures, bankruptcies, lies, and lawsuits In no way is he qualified to run our nation, our democracy He is a dictator in waiting, a king seeking a crown, not a leader Of the people, for the people, concerned about equality for all Please, use your intellect, not your emotions, to vote in November Add a contemplative/meditative practice to your life for balance Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Isness Listen to your inner guides, your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, have your answers, they wait Be a discerning person, use due diligence, it matters

George G
