AND THE CHILDREN SUFFER

By George Gondron

AND THE CHILDREN SUFFER I am pro-life And as well, I am pro-choice I do believe all life is sacred but unlike many pro-lifers Who are really more anti-abortion, than pro-life I believe the life of the child, once it takes its first breath Is as important as its life inside the mother’s womb It is ironic that once the child is born somehow Too many pro-lifers no longer have an interest in the child’s life Out of sight, out of mind, someone else’s responsibility Yet we witness on social media each minute of the day The millions of children suffering worldwide, including here Have you ever seen a child dying of dehydration and malnutrition? Perhaps you should Have you ever seen a child’s body torn apart by bullets From an AR-15, perhaps you should Do you know the fear that a parent experiences When faced with the possibility that their child or children May be forced into terrorist gangs or worse Perhaps you should feel that experience Thirty thousand five hundred children die each day Of hunger and curable diseases, ten million a year One percent of the world’s population owns 48% of the world’s Wealth, and the children suffer Children suffer for one reason only, adults Adult power, greed, ego, and ignorance The most sacred gift in life is that of a child, yet the most abused In the Christian scriptures, the human Jesus advocated For violence In only one instance, when a child is abused As adults seldom do we realize the influence and power we have Over the life of a child; physically, intellectually, emotionally, and Spiritually – and we suffer the consequences as they become adults The abused become abusers Spend a few minutes each day in silence, be still, connect to The creative Source of the universe In your mind embrace the image of a suffering child, hold on to it Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit, learn from them They hold your truth, they have your answers, they wait

George G
