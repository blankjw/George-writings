WORDS AR E ONLY LABELS

By George Gondron

WORDS ARE ONLY LABELS Words can be deceptive, but behavior can be trusted The mind, consciousness, awareness, understanding, and perception Take what is heard or read, regardless of the source, and forms images in the mind, not images of the words themselves, but what they represent based on our curet level of knowledge and experience So, if we have limited knowledge and experience about a particular topic, we can be easily influenced by what we hear and read Those images, filtered by our own mind, can lead to bias and prejudice, especially if our thinking is already polarized A socio/psychopath can then, through their words, influence an unsuspecting mind, especially if they hold a position of power The importance of factual historical data, of a reliable education cannot be overstated, yet today those in power deny history and reject the importance of valid education They are anti CRT (critical race theory), anti DEI (diversity, equity, and inclusion), they are above the law, they are the law They motivate through fear, calling others rapists, scum, vermin, crooked, poorly educated, deranged, weird, dumb, crazy, dopey, fake, radicals, goofy, etc., etc., etc. In psychology we have a word for all the above, they are called projections, the inability to see in ourselves what we imagine in others, the perfect example of a ‘bully’ Yes, words are just labels, labels to which we assign meaning Unfortunately, our current leadership fails to use words of inclusion, unity, kindness, compassion, equality, freedom, and love The importance of critical thinking, research, self-reflection, and continued education cannot be overstressed To simply take in what’s offered without critique is beyond foolish History is full of the consequences of doing so, let’s not repeat history Continue to pursue your own education with valid information Social media is not the answer, it is the problem Adopt a meditative/contemplative practice, balance inner and outer Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Presence being your image of God, Mystery, Isness, Creator Focus on your breath or a short mantra, breathe in, breathe out When the ego resists, the mind wanders, just rinse and repeat Listen to your inner guides, they have your answers, they wait

George G
