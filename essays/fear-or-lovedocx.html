FEAR OR LOVE

By George Gondron

FEAR OR LOVE One of our most common emotions, fear Maybe not called by that name Maybe it's anxiety, dread, or emptiness But it all originates from fear All fear has as its basis, the fear of loss Always about the unknown, the insecure The reluctance to risk The hesitancy to be vulnerable The unwillingness to venture without a guarantee Or, maybe it's the suspicion What if I do step outside the familiar I may have to change And what will that change bring? Will it change my thoughts, beliefs, my feelings? Will it be worth the uncertainty? I do know this If the motive behind the risk is selfish It is not a risk that brings an honest reward It is not a risk that brings change Fear thrives in a dualistic mind Fear survives with polarized thinking Where there has to be right vs. wrong, there is fear To welcome the unknown is not to fear To embrace the mystery is to live life To say no to fear is to accept the possible To accept the possible is to welcome change To welcome change is to become aware To become aware is to love To love is to be Love, real Love, turns fear into Itself

George G
