UNDERSTANDING IS POSSIBLE

By George Gondron | Source: UNDERSTANDING IS POSSIBLE.docx

UNDERSTANDING The ability to comprehend with clarity, without bias This is accomplished through the use of many faculties We have seven resources to aid us in understanding Our five senses, taste, touch, smell, hear, and see Our feelings, which include intuition And seventh, our intellect, our ability to think critically The more objective we are in utilizing those seven resources The closer we are to the truth of the matter Eliminating the objective use of any of our resources Leads to a lack of clarity and transparency If we are in the habit of listening to respond As opposed to listening to learn, we are not objective The words, none so blind as those who will not see And none so deaf as those who will not hear, adequately apply The less we use reflection, critical thinking The less we question and doubt The more we’re attracted to rhetoric that stirs our emotions And the more we use our emotions to guide our choices The less we understand, we see through a ‘glass darkly’ The responsibility of being fully human is inclusive It’s not just being emotional or intellectual It’s not just relying on our five senses It is the necessary balance of all our resources together Think, feel, and act – all a matter of choice We tend to supplant our intellect with our emotions Even in our conversations, ‘I feel you should (fill in the blank) Which is not a feeling at all, it’s a thought Another way of saying don’t think at all, just listen to me There is a truth sayer within each of us, mostly ignored Lying dormant, yearning for our attention, an innate gift Add this to your daily life, a few minutes of silence, stillness, In the presence of Presence, your image of a creative Source Your image of God, of Isness, of Universal Consciousness Empty your mind of all distractions, all thought Listen to the whispers of your inner resources Your heart, soul, and spirit, your ‘true self’ They patiently await, yearning for your relationship

George G
