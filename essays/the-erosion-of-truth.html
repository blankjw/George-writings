THE EROSION OF TRUTH

By George Gondron | Source: THE EROSION OF TRUTH.docx

THE EROSION OF TRUTH We are witnessing, moment by moment, the erosion of truth Truth, the ability to separate fact from fiction, is dying a slow death It is not by accident, it is intentional, purposeful, and dictatorial The power of ignorance becomes obvious when we can be led like sheep to believe, what even at first glance, is an obvious lie Along with this breakdown of truth is the disablement and fragmentation of the law It has become very clear; we are all not equal under the law Not even close, which translates we are not equal on any level Over the last few decades, we have slowly but surely compromised our values, our integrity, our decency, our ethics, our compassion We choose a supposed ‘safe’ group to which we belong and become disconnected from the rest of humanity, islands in an ocean We are, as some of the great teachers of history have foretold unaware, unconscious, we are asleep, awake We take the gift of consciousness and treat it with a level of disregard that is shameful Living through our emotions, ignoring our ability to engage critical thinking, to question, to doubt, and to discern The lack of reasonableness and intelligence now governing us as a nation is beyond comprehension and understanding We truly, cannot ‘see the forest for the trees’ We have become accepting of our own demise through chosen ignorance, but pointing our fingers elsewhere, it’s their fault The gift of life carries with it a great responsibility, blame doesn’t work, excuses don’t matter, but our choices matter greatly We live in a culture that ignores the fact that as human beings we have internal resources, we’re not just egos We lack balance, we need to connect inner and outer Add a new context, adopt a meditative/contemplative practice Spend a little time each day in silence, stillness, with Mystery Empty your mind of its incessant and repetitive thoughts That go nowhere and do nothing, focus on your breath The ego will resist, the mind will wander, rinse and repeat Listen for the whispers of your inner guides, heart, soul, spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, they wait Make this a consistent practice, it is so very necessary

George G
