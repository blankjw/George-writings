MY PATH IS A CHOICE

By George Gondron

MY PATH IS A CHOICE At times in my life it would be much more palatable to say, 'I did not chose this' But I did Paths that lead to failure, pain, and disappointment My choices Paths that resulted in hurt of others My choices There may not be a visible warning like the street sign But it's there, in the recesses of my mind, the warning So faint, it's easy to ignore and push away Never suspecting the truth always lies in the whisper How many of my choices have led to peace of mind? Not many Gratification is not peace To change my path I must accept my prior choices I must acknowledge they were mine and no others My ego's demand to blame will no longer work My deeper self will not abandon the truth The yearnings sound from the depths of my soul I will listen to the whispers I will change my path I will nurture the Connection within me I will not abandon the familiar, but I will follow a new Path

George G
