WE NEVER KNOW

By George Gondron

WE NEVER KNOW Thar which is negative arises from fear That which is positive is the result of love Fear brings death, love brings life Unless we are reflective people, self-aware people Seldom do we consider whether our actions, Our thoughts, and our feelings Are the result of fear or the companion of love We never know Because emotions ignore the facts A mind grounded in fear is a closed mind To live in the present moment is to become aware Aware of how our actions impact others, near and far Of how our thoughts turn into those same actions Of how the feelings we cling to create our reality Whether we understand or not We are ever-choice makers, choosing moment by moment Our created emotions speak louder to us than logic And in the process, intuition, intellect, and truth Are lost, our emotions create our reality Ignoring proven facts and obvious truths Our truth then rests in what we feel, not what we know Fear can easily overpower our personal experiences Fear does not perceive clearly, love always does Without clear perception There can be no accuracy of response, only reaction Self-reflective critical thinking cannot exist In a psyche filled with emotional turmoil We can teach ourselves to become more aware To question, doubt, seek out the truth Rather than being sponges with no discernment at all Choose a new path – a meditative/contemplative path Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, in Presence Presence being your image of a creative Source, of God Empty the mind of its incessant and repetitive thoughts That you might hear the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit Discover your ‘true self’ given at birth, find your truth Your answers abide within, they patiently await

George G
