WITHOUT A DOUBT

By George Gondron

WITHOUT A DOUBT Maybe, sometimes we need to ask What do we really believe without a doubt I know one Without a doubt we all will die, eventually It is obvious that many of us believe a great deal Most of which falls under the heading of Third party information, believed without a doubt Even in the face of facts that are contrary We have this unerring ability to believe otherwise Another interesting observation If the third party information is negative or 'anti' We have this knack to jump on the 'bandwagon' Much quicker than if it's positive or unifying What is it in the human psyche that wants to go against That wants to compete rather than cooperate That wants to exclude rather than include Especially among those of us who have enough There must be some underlying fear To cling to what we don't need Especially if it would benefit another Yes, without a doubt most of our behavior When questioned, is followed with a 'yes but' Without a doubt we know much less than we think we do We have all the answers, but without evidence And certainly, if asked 'what do you think I should do?' Without a doubt we will tell you I do know one other thing, without a doubt Love is the most powerful energy in the universe It is connected to life, to relationship, free of charge It is what breathes us, makes our hearts beat And never fails, but always wins Because of the many things we believe without a doubt We miss the obvious, we look beyond the apparent Wrapped in our thoughts, in our anxiety Deaf to the truths of our hearts, we miss the only truth Without sacrifice there is no love And without love, there is nothing

George G
