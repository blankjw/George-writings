CONDITIONING

By George Gondron

CONDITIONING We all, without exception have been conditioned Other descriptors are programmed, propagandized, Manipulated, brain-washed, and indoctrinated Some of that conditioning was positive Especially if we were fortunate enough To grow up in an atmosphere of nondualism And encouraged to think for ourselves If so, we are the exception The vast majority of us grew up in an environment Of dualism, right/wrong, black and white thinking An atmosphere of don’t do as I do Do as I tell you to do That conditioning began in our family And spread from there to every aspect of our lives Religious, educational, political, and social It’s unavoidable for we don’t live in a vacuum The more conditioned we are The easier we can be controlled And that control is mostly accomplished Through our emotions, not our intellect For those who wish to propagandize us Don’t want us to think, or question, or doubt They only want us to follow and believe their narrative That reality only ends in division and separation It cannot end in harmony or cooperation For it is by its very nature dualistic It does not speak to a both/and reality It only proclaims an either/or reality One of its primary tools is fear and negativity Break the bonds of conditioning Spend a part of each day questioning, doubting Use critical thinking, don’t drink the Kool Aid Use your gift of intellect for cooperation For collaboration and compromise Not for division, separation, and closemindedness Kindness, compassion, and love Are always the better choice

George G
