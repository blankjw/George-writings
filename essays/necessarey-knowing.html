NECESSAREY KNOWING

By George Gondron

NECESSARY KNOWING To be able to fully empathize with another’s experience Means I also must have a necessary knowing Of that same or similar experience Every human being at some point in their lives Will suffer a loss, it cannot be avoided In fact, it’s part of what makes us human To the degree that you know the power of love To that same degree you will know the pain of suffering Whether it’s a personal suffering or to witness The suffering of others Anyone in a position of power, or control, who has not experienced Necessary knowing, both positive and negative of the Human emotions of gain and loss, of acceptance and rejection He or she is a very dangerous person For their ability to empathize is limited or nonexistent Their love is limited to self-love with an unduly high opinion Of their abilities and capabilities, very egotistical They exaggerate their own power, importance, and reputation They are always right, never wrong, and demand loyalty People are nothing more than a means to an end, their end And in every culture, including ours, they are alive and well Wolves in sheep’s clothing in all arenas, including religious, Political, educational, economic, and social media The current anxiety and fear that exists in our nation Is not accidental, we suffer from a lack of necessary knowing When separation of powers no longer works When our judicial system is compromised When minds are closed, and we suffer emotional turmoil Violence will erupt and the results will be catastrophic History repeats itself and ignorance reigns Let us trust that hope does spring eternal That our children and grandchildren will have a free future We are teetering on an abyss of self-destruction.; With eyes wide-closed, shackled by our closed minds Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Let us come to our senses, realizing what we are risking Listen to your heart, soul, and spirit, they have your answers

George G
