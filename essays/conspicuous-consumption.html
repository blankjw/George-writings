CONSPICUOUS CONSUMPTION

By George Gondron | Source: CONSPICUOUS CONSUMPTION.docx

CONSPICUOUS CONSUMPTION If you haven’t noticed or even considered What drives our economy, our country, our very lives Is consumption The nature of our humanity Its guiding force and purpose is to consume Not to relate An economic culture that demands more and more Of what does not work Because all of its citizens are not served equally Equality has been nothing more than a false platitude Not a factual reality Those who have the wealth, the power, and the control Could care less about equality in any form Including equal justice, it is a false premise Justice has never been equal and it seems The highest court in the land has become a legislative body An interpreter of the Constitution that wears blinders Ah, but let me regress, back to consumption The focus of almost all social media is to sell us something Which in most cases has nothing to do with our wellbeing Not in the sense of our relationships, our connections, Our sense of a healthy ‘self’, our growth in awareness Our further education, our peace of mind It’s about spending more and more of what we do not have Money is what greases the wheels of extreme capitalism Not healthy, happy, human relationships, especially with ourselves They convince us that happiness is connected with consumption But it isn’t, and it never will be There is an emptiness in the Western human psyche We lack an internal reference, an inner core A loss of connection between mind, heart, soul, and spirit Choose a daily practice of a few minutes of silence, stillness, Presence Empty your mind of its incessant and often repetitive thoughts Dwelling in the past or the future and missing the now Listen to the whispers of your own heart, soul, and spirit They hold your ‘true self’ given at birth They hold your truth and healing, they patiently wait

George G
