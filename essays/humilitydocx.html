HUMILITY

By George Gondron

HUMILITY? How did it disappear, where did it go? Is it the fear of being wrong? Appearing insufficient, unknowledgeable, not having the right answer It's not in the haughtiness of the politician Not in the liberal, conservative, or independent with their presumptive overconfidence and arrogance Certainly not within anyone with all the answers and all the power, all the control The always correct answer whether it's Religious Economic Educational Environmental Relational The always correct is void of humility, full of assurance Often boastful, arrogant, self-centered, and vain Lacking a gentle, quiet, unassuming, unpretentious, and peaceable quality Three short phrases those without humility struggle to say - I am sorry I was wrong I don't know Perhaps humility is dead - I hope not Perhaps the humble only exist among deeply spiritual people Those with a non-dual mind, a both/and mind Those that sense the power of the Divine within Those that realize the amazing gift of what is called life Those that experience all is grace, incarnation, blessing Those that have few answers but many questions George G
