NOISE

By George Gondron

NOISE The great distracter, noise Surrounding us, within us, between us Even in the quiet there is the noise of the mind The endless thoughts, most of which are useless Most of which are repeated, incessantly With the same unfulfilled results With the same hollow messages With the same unanswered questions The TV is on, no one is watching The music is playing, no one is listening My eyes scan the pages with little comprehension All mere distractions, to avoid the loneliness To avoid the emptiness To avoid awareness How can I stop the noise? Even more, do I want to stop the noise? Am I afraid to hear the silence, to listen to the quiet? To listen to the whispers from the depths of my soul To dare hope To dare being vulnerable To dare risk an increase in consciousness An expansion of awareness An awakening, an enlightenment, a new understanding I can make the choice I can seek the possible I have that freedom Failure is not an option I commit to change

George G
