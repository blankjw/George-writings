WILLFUL IGNORANCE

By George Gondron

WILLFUL IGNORANCE The refusal to learn is willful and the result is ignorance Most, if not all, that’s available on mainstream social media is not there to educate you, it’s there to condition you to manipulate and control you To sell you something you don’t need and convince you of something that is unbelievable under normal circumstances Gaslighting, hyperbole, alternative facts, and propaganda are just a few of the mainstays and algorithms used to keep the masses ignorant, after all the now leader of our nation loves the poorly educated, the sheep follow their shepherd There is a word that defines those who blindly follow that which over and over again will only lead to their own pain and suffering They are called masochist, who suddenly awaken one day and ask ‘How did this happen?’, and the answer willful ignorance The path that leads to reliable education is not social media It begins with critical thinking, research, doubt, questions You know, don’t believe what you hear and half of what you see But it seems, we don’t like hard work, we much prefer being fed We would rather have our emotions stimulated than our intellect challenged forgetting the axiom, ‘nothing ventured, nothing gained’ And now our newly elected leader wants to eliminate the Dept of Education, do away with DEI, deport all immigrants, and pardon adjudicated criminals, I wonder why? It is no wonder that 54% of adults have a literacy level of 6th grade or less, that our nation has fallen behind in every measurable way related to the important educational topics needed for progress Stop and ask yourself this question, ‘who benefits from this debacle?’ If you are not deaf, dumb, and blind or willfully ignorant there is only own answer, those with wealth, power, and control The rest of us are merely a means to an end, their end On the conveyor belt of production and consumption Adopt a meditative/contemplative practice, be discerning Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Focus on your breath or a short mantra, breathe in, breathe out The ego will resist, the mind will wander, rinse and repeat Listen for the whispers of your inner guides, heart, soul, spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, they wait

George G
