THE GIFT OF A CHILD

By George Gondron

THE GIFT OF A CHILD There is no greater gift than that of a life The gift of a child As you read this, realize, you are a gift You once were that child Whether planned or accidental, doesn't matter The best gifts are those unexpected The moments after birth begin the journey Begins the responsibility of cherishing the gift For which there is no handbook, no directions The rearing of a child has to do with many things Mother's instinct, cultural norms, time and place Financial well being, safety and security The list is endless, as well as daunting The new born of all species have one thing in common They need touch, closeness, protection The human child is dependent for the longest Fortunate if raised in a loving family Where love was not conditional But, if born to parents who don't know how to love Or, use love as a reward, withhold it as punishment They are more likely to experience deprivation Emotional deprivation, abusive touch, emptiness We become what we have learned, or have not learned From one generation to the next Until the cycle is broken We are all gifts, we are all special Gifts from the Giver of life Which is known as Love, unconditional love A Love that cannot, not love Discover the gift that you are, it dwells within Discover the Lover

George G
