PERHAPS TOMORROW

By George Gondron

PERHAPS TOMORROW What was that? Hmmm, maybe tomorrow. There's always so much to do and so little time. Surely you can't be serious, just sit in silence. Now that's an example of a waste of time. How in the world can one accomplish anything? You're not being very productive doing nothing. My idea of sitting still is watching T.V. or texting. Or perhaps reading a juicy novel, or doing a crossword. I can relax by blogging on facebook or twitter. Lots of good info on you tube. Silence and stillness? Perhaps tomorrow.

George G
