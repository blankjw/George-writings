GOD, YAHWEH, I AM

By George Gondron

GOD, YAHWEH, I AM For most, a convenient afterthought For many, an object to be worshiped For few, a subject of relationship An indefinable interpersonal connection What is the possible reason? The logic of Omniscience, to create a universe To conceive of forming in the image and likeness Of itself, of its essence, of its divinity We, us, those called human beings What could an 'all-knowing' God Possibly gain from this vast creation? It has to be an 'I, Thou' answer Life, existence only flows from Love Love, real love, can only create It cannot destroy or separate Yet, so many worship, so few relate Relationships are partnerships They are not ownership Worshippers relate to owners, to the disconnected To that which is beyond, distant, the Omnipotent Worshippers seek the favor of, favor with In Love there is no favor, no separation No difference Love seeks only love, only itself We flee the 'hound of heaven' Holding fast to 'worship', avoiding connection We will lose the race, we cannot escape Love will win It always does Always

George G
