MOMENTS

By George Gondron | Source: MOMENTS.docx

MOMENTS Life, created in moments One after the other, all at the same speed Really, none more important than the now moment The present moment, and we have a choice Either the moment creates us or we create the moment We simply become bystanders or initiators of our moments Awake yet asleep, aware or unaware Life moves on, a constant process of change In which we consciously or unconsciously participate And for most, at some point in time we say Where did my life go? The life lived in the now, never asks the question The life lived in the now doesn't cling, is not attached It is a life that looks forward to change Immersed in every now moment
