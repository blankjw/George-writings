THANKSGIVING

By George Gondron

THANKSGIVING

In addition to our normal thanks on this day Let's be thankful for what we often overlook Past connections that touched our lives, though now forgotten Our failures that seemed unbearable at the time But in looking back, taught us meaningful lessons We often forget what brings us to this present moment To every present moment Our senses; the ability to taste, touch, smell, hear and see To think and feel, yes we take those for granted as well For some, days gone by hold broken relationships There is always something to be thankful for in those relationships Whatever the experiences of the past Some painful, some joyful, there was something to be thankful for The way our hearts continue to beat The breath fills our lungs, all unbidden, let us be thankful Every significant event in our history, our story Has brought us to where we are today Be thankful for having the ability to choose Choose to stay in your current status Or choose to change, if even a small amount And of course it goes without saying We are thankful for those presently in our lives Parent, spouse, child, or sibling In-laws and outlaws, relatives all Lovers, partners, friends, and acquaintances And last, but not least The Spirit that dwells within That which gives us our life That makes it all possible The Divine part of our DNA that will continue through eternity Connecting us to ourselves, to others, to the universe Connecting us to Love, to the Ground of All Being Let us give thanks This Thanksgiving Day

George G
