ALGORITHYMS

By George Gondron

?ALGORITHMS The predictors of choice; algorithms United with AI, artificial intelligence, become powerful tools They are able to elicit human emotions by pressing the buttons In the mind that represent hate, prejudice, greed, and racism Every link we ‘click on’, every social media platform we follow That promotes division and separation That blames and points fingers, that denies and offers Alternative facts and half-truths are promoting hate And reducing trust and disregarding the law And when the foundations of a governmental system Are no longer trusted, when the separation of powers No longer works and opposing factions become polarized The next step is dictatorship, the echo of Benjamin Franklin’s Comment, ‘we have formed a Republic, if we can keep it’ Begins to raise its head and we must ask, is it possible? If we have eyes to see, ears to hear, we must know It is very possible When minds are closed and ignorance reigns, truth dies And who is the enemy, we all are Who is complicit and responsible, we all are By refusing to know, to learn, to critically think To question, doubt, research and examine, we rather, Follow our protagonist of choice, primarily the one Who pushes the buttons in our minds that generate negativity As the buttons of kindness, compassion, empathy, inclusion, Unity, bipartisanship, and love itself, lie dormant, untouched Being an innocent bystander is no excuse There are no innocent bystanders We each are either part of the problem or the solution It is only when we have clarity that we can respond accurately The road to chaos is through the emotions, not the intellect Be a reflective person, bring balance into your life Embrace a meditative/contemplative practice Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Empty your mind, focus on your breath, breathe in, breathe out Listen for the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, they wait

George G
