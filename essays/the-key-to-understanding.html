THE KEY TO UNDERSTANDING

By George Gondron

THE KEY TO UNDERSTANDING The most fundamental goal of understanding Is to know the truth We live in a period when knowing the truth Is not worth the personal effort needed to understand It is so much easier to follow what engages our emotions Than our ability to discriminate fact from fiction The mind is easily manipulated and distracted When we lead with our emotions or an inflated ego Doubt is much more open to learning than certitude I’d much rather trust an ‘I don’t know’ response Than a self-righteous ‘I know’ egocentricity They’re great talkers but poor listeners They lead with a closed versus an open mind Self-assured in their position primarily because So-and-so said Never doubting or questioning the ‘why’ of what So-and-so said Anytime someone is attempting to convince you of something As opposed to encouraging you to grow personally They’re not interested in you discovering for yourself They’re only interested in you believing them ‘You going to believe me, or those old lying eyes?’ Honestly, personal growth and individuation are not an easy path But it is the path that leads to your ‘true self’ A self that knows there is no one right answer But there is a right truth discovered through personal experience We live in a time of unlimited conspiracy theories All fueled by fear, insecurity, and negativity Fueled by those who only want recognition, the naysayers Only desirous of having a band of followers Willing to swallow what they are selling Do yourself a favor, choose a meditative/contemplative practice Learn to listen to your own heart, soul, and spirit A necessary balance for the mind, the ego Spend a few minutes each day in silence and stillness With Presence, listen to the inner whispers Whispers that hold your personal truth and healing

George G
