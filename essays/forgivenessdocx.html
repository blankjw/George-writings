FORGIVENESS

By George Gondron

FORGIVENESS TO MY HUMAN FAMILY

FOR THE TIMES I’VE SPOKEN TO YOU IN ANGER – FORGIVE ME FOR THE TIMES I’VE NOT LISTENED – FORGIVE ME FOR THE TIMES I’VE GIVEN ADVICE WITHOUT IT BEING ASKED FOR – FORGIVE ME FOR THE TIMES I’VE INTERFERRED – FORGIVE ME FOR THE TIMES I HAVEN’T BEEN PRESENT – FORGIVE ME FOR THE TIMES I WASN’T AVAILABLE – FORGIVE ME FOR THE TIMES I MAY HAVE BEEN DISRESPECTFUL – FORGIVE ME FOR THE TIMES I’VE IGNORED YOU – FORGIVE ME FOR THE TIMES I HAVEN’T CELEBRATED YOUR JOY – FORGIVE ME FOR THE TIMES I HAVEN’T CELEBRATED YOUR SUCCESS – FORGIVE ME FOR THE TIMES I’VE JUDGED YOU – FORGIVE ME AND MOST IMPORTANTLY, FOR THE TIMES I HAVEN’T LOVED YOU – FORGIVE ME

GEORGE G
