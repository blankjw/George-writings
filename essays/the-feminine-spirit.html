THE FEMININE SPIRIT

By George Gondron

THE FEMININE SPIRIT Even today the much-needed feminine spirit struggles to survive In a world dominated by male ego, in every culture Yet without the feminine humanity would not exist Imagine what would happen if women refused to become pregnant Refused to carry within their own bodies the future of humanity? Of course, we all know what would happen They would be forced to bring life into the world Forced to endure another man-made control over their own bodies Something is wrong, terribly wrong in a world dominated by men Which excludes equal protection under the law for all women Not to mention equal pay, equal education, equal health care The oldest known images of worship were feminine The feminine represented the manifestation of continued life Not just in human form, but in all forms Women became subjugated in early history for one reason only They had the power to produce life, to give birth It was not known in those early years it took a sperm and an egg So women became chattel, objects to be owned and controlled No better than second-class citizens, used and abused That pattern throughout the world continues even today There is virtually no area of life in which women are not Considered inferior, secular or sacred At best, given only lip service for their important contributions In all the major metaphysical beliefs male prophets have risen Proclaiming the importance of the feminine spirit To little or no avail I for one honor, encourage, support, and lift up the feminine I encourage all men, discover the feminine spirit within yourself It is there and it is sorely needed in our current time Creativity in every form has a touch of feminine energy In so many arenas, women have succeeded where men have failed But they receive little or no accolades for their achievements Add a meditative/contemplative practice to your daily life Spend a few minutes each day in silence, be still, embrace Presence Presence being your image of a creative Source, or God if you prefer Empty your mind, breathe in, breathe out a short mantra Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit, they wait

George G
