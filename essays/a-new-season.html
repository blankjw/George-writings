A NEW SEASON

By George Gondron

A NEW SEASON Spring, summer, fall, and winter have a new partner But unlike the four seasons, this seems to be perpetual Some have longer winters, others have longer summers But this new season thrives everywhere all the time Stronger in some parts of the country, weaker in other parts But it’s always present, the season of conspiracy These conspiracy theories began to gain momentum After the election of our first black President They’ve always been here, since the founding of our nation But now they are gaining power and strength Witness January 6th, 2021 Witness the rise of all the ‘anti-movements’ Witness the complete transformation of the Republican party Witness the rise of fundamental evangelical Christianity One would think that at least one of these conspiracies Would have a positive, promising, encouraging bent But from all indications, they are all negative in nature Fueled by half-truths, lies, and the new phenomenon Alternative facts It’s truly mind-blowing to know some people actually believed That a Pizza parlor was running a pedophile ring And that ring was being run by a major political personality One would think with the dozens of Federal Agencies Investigating crime in this nation someone would have known But alas, it turned out to be a total fabrication The human psyche is easily hooked by intrigue and fantasy And there are those more than willing to fan those flames And they make millions of dollars in the process All they need do is stir our emotions and we grab the bait Hook, line, and sinker The path to chaos is paved with negativity and fear Not with kindness, compassion, and love Beware conspiracy theories, question, doubt, Think critically, do your own research Connect with your heart, soul, and spirit Spend a few minutes in silence, stillness, with Presence Remember love is stronger than hate

George G
