NEVER CEASE TO LOVE

By George Gondron

NEVER CEASE TO LOVE Never is there a time to not love Whether it's a spouse, a child, a partner, a friend Always love, even the stranger For love is the energy of the universe It is what creates It is what heals It is what unites Love has no option, no choice, but to love It is the nexus of all existence The origin of kindness and compassion The foundation of all relationships Love heals our pain, pushes away our doubt Allows us to find our way, for love is the way There is no prerequisite for being loved Being sincere it always gives, holding nothing back Love is unconditional acceptance And has no power to reject Love is the carrier of truth The revealer of lies Love brings clarity of perception And abides in the luminous darkness of our doubts Since time began there is no moment That has been void of love, it's not possible It is only fear that pushes love away It is only fear that clings to self pity It is only fear that tells us we don't matter Push away negative thoughts, don't listen What drags us down is never love, it's only fear Even fear can't reject love, but it tries The first step in one's life is acceptance of what is Before what can be will emerge Accept this, we are never not loved, never What brings forth all of creation, the entire universe Is nothing less than Love The Consciousness that continues to pull us forward Forward into the wholeness of ourselves Gifts to one another, filled with creative energy, with love

George G
