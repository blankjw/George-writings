By George Gondron

December 25th - A Moment In Time

For the finite mind an incarnational moment, seen as a singular event For the Infinite an always now, no beginning, no end, no time, no space, eternal event The Word that was, is now, and will always be, the Alpha and the Omega Filling the universe with its incarnational blessing This one day, an experience in the finite human mind, a day to 'waste a little time' with family, friends, or alone with ourselves To 'waste a little time' with; not a negative event at all but an action that reveals nothing is more important than wasting time with the ones we care for I wonder how it must feel and what it must mean to the Ground of All Being for us to 'waste a little time' with the One that gives us our incarnational experience freely with no strings attached; no expectations With no requirement that we even acknowledge the gift, ever The result of and definition of unconditional love How meaningful it is for us and for those we love to 'waste a little time' together A waste redefined as a blessing Wasting a little time each day with our image of creation, our image of the Divine, becomes a blessing for us both There is no greater gift than to acknowledge the Giver for that which I have received December 25th, a moment in time, an eternal now event, every day

George G
