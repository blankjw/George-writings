COMPRHENSION

By George Gondron

COMPREHENSION Comprehension is always subject to the single individual’s Level of awareness, to the extent of their personal knowledge The challenge for continued growth, on all levels Is to engage another, or others, in a manner that Creates just enough curiosity to whet their appetite To want to know more, to engage their imagination Curious for further understanding For you see, the potential for growth, intellectually, psychologically, And spirituality is unlimited, there are no barriers or, bounds There is only the choice, to engage or to resist Without the mind, heart, soul, and spirits engagement On any chosen path forward With only a willingness to trust but not verify How do we know that the input we are receiving is Beneficial, has valid meaning, and can be trusted? Every potential source, whether a single individual or a group Has its limitations, its weaknesses, and its agenda ‘All that glitters is not gold’, without exception The value of choice is in the act of choosing For every choice brings new experience And it is only through new experiences that we change To cling to the status quo will never bring change It will only sustain a narrow point of view with a closed mind Repetitive thinking, repetitive behavior, has no future It only remains stuck in a dysfunctional pattern of illusion A caring, sustainable, happy, responsible, and healthy life Must have balance to become fully human A balance between inner and outer Most of our time is spent ‘doing’, absent the importance of being There are more paths to being than one But there is one that never fails – a meditative/contemplative path A few minutes each day to shut out the noise To quieten the mind of its incessant and repetitive thoughts To embrace the silence, with stillness, and with Presence No words, just listening Listening to the whispers of our own heart, soul, and spirit Discovering our ‘true self’, our inner Truth, our purpose

George G
