WORDS VWESUS ACTIONS

By George Gondron

WORDS VERSUS ACTIONS The phrase, ‘Believe nothing you hear, and only half of what you see’ by Edgar Allen Poe is more significant today than it was in 1845 when it was first spoken But unfortunately, today it seems the opposite is more applicable especially with the explosion of social media in all its forms When those in position of power adamantly propose something one day and the opposite the next and we believe it, who is the fool? The ready acceptance of the truth today and the lie tomorrow is the current political situation, it even has a name, alternative facts Or the basis for the famous saying, ‘You gonna believe me or those old lying eyes?’ If you want to know who someone is, watch what they do, don’t listen to what they say But then again some of what they say is so preposterous, they actually do sometimes act it out, it becomes the norm The words we hear or read are not seen in the mind as words but as created images For example, ‘all these immigrants are killers, rapists, drug dealers, thieves, freed from prisons and insane asylums’ An individual who hears or reads that type of rhetoric has an option to question what they take in, an option to examine the images created in their own minds and decide the level of truth contained therein, generally all-encompassing statements are seldom true As a rule, the lower the level of literacy, awareness, intelligence, comprehension, and understanding, the more distortion and exaggeration is believed, ergo ‘I love the poorly educated’ Remember, we don’t see in words, we see in images and if those Images stir our emotions and not our intellect then in all probability we are being manipulated and propagandized The message becomes, don’t think for yourselves, I’ll think for you Add that to your life which enhances your awareness, understanding, comprehension, and interpretation Choose a daily meditative/contemplative practice for balance Focus on your breath or a brief mantra, breathe in, breathe out When the ego resists or the mind wanders, rinse and repeat Listen to the whispers of your inner guides, heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, they wait

Gorge G
