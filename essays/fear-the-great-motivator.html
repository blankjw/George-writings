FEAR^J THE GREAT MOTIVATOR

By George Gondron | Source: FEAR^J THE GREAT MOTIVATOR.docx

FEAR, THE GREAT MOTIVATOR Throughout history the despots of the world have used fear more than anything to accomplish their goals History has also proven that fear more than love seems to be more effective as a change agent Those figures that have attempted to motivate through love are like islands in a sea of fear, clinging to hope for a better more inclusive world Even today, Bishop Mariann Budde asking our current despot to’ have mercy’ on the marginalized and the refugee during her pastoral message at Washington National Cathedral was greeted with a sneer and a scoff from the now President as he sat on the front row with his family and Vice President surrounding him And the world watches, and the world lives in fear His political rallies were laced with fear, almost entirely And a narrative of fear, that has no substantial basis, unsupported by reliable facts and data, becomes larger than life, exaggerated beyond any common sense, but it works Fear has been, is now, and will always be the enemy, not love The list of those individuals that throughout history have and do promote love are to be held in the deepest regard for theirs has been a journey of suffering, pain, and even rejection It’s easy to see that the atmosphere in our nation is one of fear with islands of love here and there, seek those islands To follow the fearmonger never ends well, history has proven that History’s greatest teachers expound the virtues of kindness, compassion, empathy, decency, truth, integrity, and love And to this day we have not learned the message Unfortunately, the change that is required resides within the individual human psyche, the human soul We can still hope for a paradigm shift in human consciousness Add a meditative/contemplative practice to your daily life Spend a few minutes in silence, stillness, with Mystery, Presence Focus on your breath or a short mantra, breathe in, breathe out I promise the ego will resist, simply rinse and repeat Listen for the whispers of your inner guides, heart, soul, spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, they wait Connect to your innate inner core, learn to discern

George G
