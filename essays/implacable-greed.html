IMPLACABLE GREED

By George Gondron

IMPLACABLE GREED Greed, which is relentless, which can’t be satisfied, is everlasting There is no place, no point for or to the cessation of implacable greed And it does not matter the consequences, does not matter who suffers as the result of this manifestation of greed There is always a guaranteed outcome, the rich get richer, the poor become poorer, and those in the middle disappear Witness the malignant cancer slowly devouring our nation It does not matter the consequences visited upon our citizens as a result of greed, and it never will, we are a means to an end An end to those who never have enough There is a direct correlation between increased wealth and increased poverty, and honestly the wealthy don’t want to solve poverty, it’s much easier to assign blame and take the focus off themselves than be a part of the solution There is only one reason for the wealthy to seek favor from governmental officials, the potential for more wealth, i.e. governmental contracts or the avoidance of regulations Witness the number of millionaires and billionaires ‘sucking up’ to our current president Ironically those same millionaires and billionaires could easily solve the poverty, homelessness, and health care problems that currently exist in this nation, and still remain wealthy, sad It’s interesting to note that indigenous cultures have no problems with greed, as well as those cultures that are family oriented What they seem to have in common is a connection to their internal core, not just their minds, people matter In our culture that connection is sorely lacking, it is obvious that ‘things’ are more important than people As well when that connection is missing, fear and manipulation are stronger than love and inclusion, implacable greed is an enemy Become a person of balance, connect inner and outer Adopt a meditative/contemplative practice Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Empty your mind of its incessant and repetitive thoughts Focus on your breath, breathe in, breathe out, rinse and repeat Listen for the whispers of your inner guides, heart, soul, spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, they wait

George G
