MEANINGLESS WORDS

By George Gondron

MEANINGLESS WORDS Words are meaningless, they are not reality We give them credence, call them truth But, without the experience, they have no meaning Words, words, words, too many words More often than not, simply meant to convince us Of this, or that, of something else
