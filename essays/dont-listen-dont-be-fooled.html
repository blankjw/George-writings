DON'T LISTEN DON'T BE FOOLED

By George Gondron

DON’T LISTEN, DON’T BE FOOLED We are amazing beings We have no clue how truly amazing we are Capable of every emotion from A to Z A creative potential beyond the imagination We even have the power to heal ourselves Heal each other, healthy mind, healthy body We deny our feelings, we squash our human potential We focus on lack rather than plenty, on negative versus positive We really have no clue about how connected we are How important are all human relationships How selfless our true nature, how kind and compassionate Unfortunately, our enemies are many but they are asleep Unaware of their own human potential The users and abusers, the selfish and manipulators The con artists and silver-tongued devils Swimming in their own false narrative Their own ‘pipe dreams’ using others as a means to an end An end where there are no real winners, all eventually lose You, I, all, are already enough We carry within us all the answers, all truth, given at birth We are not accidents Every life before us, those now, and those yet to come Have purpose, have meaning, have a gift to offer humanity Without exception physically fit or with an assumed handicap All belong, you and I, us – how do we not know these things? We get lured by the naysayers, trapped by negative voices We tune out our own intuition, our inner voices We have become easily controlled by our emotions We accept without question, we refuse to pull back the curtain We no longer think, educate ourselves, reflect, or doubt We trust others too much and ourselves too little Change, do this, embrace a meditative/contemplative practice Spend a few minutes each day in silence, be still, embrace Isness Choose a short mantra, i.e .breathe in wellness to yourself Breathe out wellness to the universe, be gentle Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, they wait

George G
