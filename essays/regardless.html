REGARDLESS

By George Gondron | Source: REGARDLESS.docx

REGARDLESS Regardless, a seemingly insignificant word Used with determination to oppose Used with fervor to support Therefore, a non-dual word, supporting either/or However in most cases it's a refusal A refusal to see the other side, the other point of view How can I comprehend the other, understand our differences, seek our common ground I must take my 'regardless' and turn it into openness I must put aside my 'regardless' to nurture possibility We cling to our 'regardless' for safety, for security We hold fast our 'regardless' fearing the unknown We can't risk there being a rightness beyond our limited viewpoint We can't gamble being transformed, less we lose Regardless of our reasons Regardless of our dis-ease The risk of openness, of being receptive Of engaging the different, the unfamiliar Is worth the reward Is worth the endeavor Regardless

George G
