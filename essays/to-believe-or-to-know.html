TO BELIEVE OR TO KNOW

By George Gondron

TO BELIEVE OR TO KNOW Only if we have no internal reference, no connection to our innate inner core, do we merely believe When our reality is grounded only in cognition, only in thinking When there is no balance in our life When our existence is about polarization and dualism When our perception is limited to good/bad, right/wrong, up/down, positive or negative do we limit our full potential to know and consequently mostly just believe And yet we srill cannot perceive the origin of consciousness or the mystery of how life begins We don’t instruct our hearts to beat or our lungs to breathe, but they do And our greatest struggle is the dynamic and importance of human relationships with ourselves and with others Individuation and self-actualization are ‘inside’ jobs, matters of the heart, soul, and spirit Not matters of our ‘monkey minds’ and incessant and repetitive thoughts that go nowhere and do nothing To be fully human, life demands balance, inner and outer It is that balance that is the catalyst which brings knowing and not just believing For knowing is the result of personal experience And personal experience happens through using our senses, taste, touch, smell, hear, and see What we think and feel about any event, personal or otherwise does not necessarily make it true without research, critical thinking, doubt, and a questioning mind In our nation today, people trust their emotions and seldom use critical thinking or any research on important topics, and it shows Please, adopt a meditative/contemplative practice for balance Become a critical thinker, embrace silence, stillness, and Presesnce for a few minutes each day Focus on your breath or a brief mantra, breathe in, breathe out Your ego will resist and will wander, simply rinse and repeat Listen for the whispers of your inner guides, heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers They connect you with universal Consciousness, they patiently wait

George G
