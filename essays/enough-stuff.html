ENOUGH STUFF

By George Gondron

ENOUGH STUFF What is enough stuff? How is stuff defined? Not enough comes in many forms In what I can gather, material goods In what I hold on to, thoughts and feelings In my identification with others, comparison In my ego needs, power and prestige All of it in the end is just stuff Strange, how my stuff is probably different from your stuff Insignificant to some, extremely important to others We foolishly identify with our stuff, how sad I become what controls me You become what controls you We both loose who we really are, in our stuff No longer relating in our common humanity Relating as creatures controlled by our stuff I think our stuff was meant to serve us Somehow, we become imprisoned by our stuff Letting go is exceedingly difficult for most Not letting go is being controlled by our stuff What I'm attached to has power over me, over us Regardless of its nature, regardless of its character Let go Let go of the stuff Become detached It long ago became enough

George G
