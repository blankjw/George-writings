LISTEN TO LEARN OR LISTEN TO DEFEND IS A MATTER OF CHOICE

By George Gondron

LISTEN TO LEARN OR LISTEN TO DEFEND IS A MATTER OF CHOICE Moat of our formal education is dualistic in nature There is a right way and a wrong way to get the right answer By its very nature, this leads to dualistic polarized thinking Either/or versus both/and, pro or con Consequently, education becomes a matter of getting The right answers on a test, rather than actual learning Taken one step further, after our formal education Our character, our personality will lean more one way or the other We will either be for or against in our basic nature Seldom will we be both, situational ethics is nonexistent Now we’ll probably marry, have a family, earn a living And for most, our learning stops, at least formally Now what we take in is not from our efforts Our critical thinking, our research, our desire to learn But what attracts our particular character Fed to us by characters of like-mindedness Especially those that hook our emotions, not our intellect And who better to fill that bill than politicians and religious Why go through the process of doubting, questioning, wondering If someone else will do it for us, go to our favorite media source For the answer, left or right, never down the middle All the aforementioned only makes for a society That is exclusive, not inclusive, segregated not integrated Here’s the real danger, once we’ve taken our stance For or against, no amount of verifiable data to the contrary Will change our minds, we will defend, we will not listen We will use ‘what-about-isms’, and speak about years past Follow the ‘pied-piper’ of our choice regardless of the results No one wins, all lose, closed minds are very dangerous minds And electing leaders with closed minds will destroy this nation Challenge yourself to learn, expand your awareness and consciousness Not with boisterous rhetoric but with facts Add a meditative/contemplative practice to your daily life Spend a few minutes in silence, be still, be present to Presence Discover your internal resources, your true self Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit, they wait

George G
