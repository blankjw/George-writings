INTENTION

By George Gondron

INTENTION Nothing in life is accomplished without intention Not in your life, not in my life, nothing at all Our thoughts, feelings, and actions, our behavior Depends on our intentions, our purpose, our goals And the reality is, they are chosen They are not accidental, they are the result of ego decisions And if we’re healthy, happy, whole human beings They are also the result of heart, soul, and spirit choices Ego decisions alone are disconnected from selflessness There is no corporate meeting between The mind, heart, soul, and spirit There’s only the mind and the mind’s intentions No one engages life day in and day out without intentions All those in leadership positions also have intentions When examined carefully we ask, what is their purpose Is it for self-aggrandizement, for their ego needs For power and control, loyalty beyond reason And the venue doesn’t matter It’s especially prevalent in politics as well as religion A ‘my way or the highway’, mentality If you really want to know who a person is In relation to other people, regardless of their status Ethnicity, gender, rich or poor, color, handicapped, etc. Observe their behavior that’s where you’ll find their intentions It’s not their too many words, words are empty Our actions reveal who we are, our intentional choices Conscious or unconscious there’s always intent behind Our choices, our actions, our behavior, always To know the intent behind every thought, feeling, and action Is vastly more important than merely reacting A suggestion; spend a few minutes each day committed to An intentional meditative/contemplative practice Experience silence, and stillness in the presence of Isness Empty the mind of its incessant and repetitive thoughts That you might hear the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit Discover your innate ‘true self’, your inner truth and strength They hold your truth, they patiently wait

George G
