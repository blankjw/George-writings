SOUL MATES ARE FOREVER

By George Gondron

SOUL MATES ARE FOREVER There are those connections one makes in a lifetime That are known at first encounter to be something more than Beyond and more than the usual relationship They are connections that are deeper than normal friendships Those people with which we feel a sense of ease A recognition of like mindedness on first meeting It more often than not comes about as a 'surprise' An unintentional happenstance A pleasant and unplanned accident Whether from a heightened awareness of being present to Or merely the analogy of 'like attracts like' They become our soul mates People with and for whom we have special feelings Who are both strangers in one moment And trusted confidants the next Unlike most of our relationships They seem to 'blossom and mature' all at once Not gradually over time as the others And if we are lucky, truly favored We may find one in a lifetime that is formed from a deep love A love that far exceeds anything experienced before A love that is heart to heart Soul to soul Spirit to spirit Like no other relationship, union, or connection One for which there is no restriction, no boundary No concept of time or distance A feeling that it has always been The only description that come close to this reality Is that love recognizes Love I sense the gift of the other The compassion, kindness, yearning, and generosity of the other And even those words are barely enough I see what they don't even know of their self I wish for you that experience I wish for you that kind of connection It is beyond measure, beyond telling, but not beyond knowing

George G
