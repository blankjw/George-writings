MAY I TEACH YOU ABOUT LOVE

By George Gondron

MAY I TEACH YOU ABOUT LOVE Be still, empty your mind, relax, and listen Listen to the words that paint images in your imagination First, love is a gift, freely given with nothing asked in return No demands, no expectations, you can even reject the gift And reject the giver, there are no strings attached For that’s what real love is about, it’s unconditional It cannot, not love Second genuine love doesn’t stop to ask Is this person deserving of love, it’s an irrelevant question For love makes no distinction between deserving or undeserving All are loved in equal measure Female, male, young, and old, all receive this gift Everything that exists in this universe is the result of love It's sad, that we have reduced the word love to an emotion For if love is based on how we feel, it’s not love at all For love is an action, it’s something we give away It is what sustains our very life now and hereafter Third, it's a necessary sacrifice, that gives favor to another The experience of this love is not intellectual It happens in only one way, in relationship Not in words, only in action, only in being present to What we do for one another out of selfless action Is love, without if, why, when, where, or how There is no time to express this love, it is always now When we express and act of love through our actions We also expend our time and energy as evidence We are telling the other or others I value you this much And I expect nothing in return, my love is my gift I hope you have given love in this measure, as gift I hope as well it has been reciprocal and received as such My deeper hope is that you have experienced this love From the Giver of all that is, the Universal Giver I recommend a path of meditative/contemplative practice A time each day for a few minutes of being present to To be silent, to be still, to be in the presence of the Giver With an open heart, a yearning soul, a welcoming spirit The Love of the universe eagerly awaits your relationship

George G
