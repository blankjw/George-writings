MORE AND MORE

By George Gondron

MORE AND MORE The mindset of needing more of what ultimately does not work Is not a social construct at all If anything, it is the focus of extreme capitalism The concept of needing more is nothing less than A form of addiction disguised as a ‘pot of gold’ At the end of the rainbow, it’s never attainable The whole premise that happiness can be purchased Is a false premise for true happiness is uncaused It is spontaneous If happiness has a cause then it also can have an effect When the cause doesn’t exist what then takes its place Unhappiness? The whole premise that people have the power to make us happy Carries an unspoken message They also have the power to make us sad Fear is the primary motivator for all that is negative Love is the primary motivator for all that is positive If we are to believe the evidence so obvious in this culture The result is we are much more fear-based than love based
