MOTIVATION THROUGH FEAR

By George Gondron

MOTIVATION THROUGH FEAR All potential despots, autocrats, dictators, tyrants, authoritarians Have one thing in common, they motivate through fear Separate and divide, us versus them mentality In addition, they have inflated egos, puffing themselves up To be the ‘savior’ of all, the answer to all your problems But upon closer examination, their history is filled with failure Failure in business, in relationships, in feigned friendships They have no loyalty, to anyone, and if you cross them You will pay a price in one way or another Upon close examination what they proclaim to be true Is nothing more than fabricated lies, alternative facts Stating they love the poorly educated, that’s their target Their words are ‘laced’ with bias, prejudice, bigotry, intolerance, And blame – they are never at fault, lacking any humility They love to victimize others while proclaiming they are the victim The quintessential con, holding themselves out to be perfect They don’t want you to use critical thinking, to question or doubt Don’t look behind the curtain, you may discover the truth They would rather isolate and exclude than integrate and include They engage in conspiracy and ‘deep state’ fabrications Meant to create even more fear and separation Like muddy water they endeavor to keep the emotions of those Who follow them ‘stirred up’, unable to see the forest for the trees If their followers could ‘back away’ just long enough For clarity to develop, they would soon lose their attraction But like all despots they know the power of fanning the flames If we are unwilling to see behind the ‘smoke and mirrors’ And continue to swallow the unending stream of lies There will be only one casualty, us, not the despot We each have within us a ‘truth teller’, an inner guide Take a few minutes each day for silence, stillness, and Presence Empty your mind, focus on your breath or a short mantra Your ego will resist but be committed, stay with it A consistent dedicated meditative/contemplative practice Reaps many rewards and answers many questions Listen for the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, and they patiently wait

George G
