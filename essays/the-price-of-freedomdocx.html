THE PRICE OF FREEDOM

By George Gondron

THE PRICE OF FREEDOM The price of freedom is constant vigilance Not of what stirs our emotions But of what challenges our beliefs A mind that cannot change is a dangerous mind Plato once said, ‘If you do not take an interest In the affairs Of your government, then you are doomed to live under The rule of fools’ Unfortunately, it seems most people choose to remain Informed by that which satisfies their comfort zone The price of freedom requires that we remain objective That we conscientiously seek the truth, the facts That we remain open to what may challenge our beliefs And not reject out-of-hand what creates discomfort Far too often we choose the social media platform That satisfies our personal agenda The one to which we can proclaim, ‘right on brother!’ In truth, an increase in awareness and consciousness Happens in an atmosphere of tension, a little anxiety The person with a closed mind, a fixed point of view Cannot grow, evolve, or become a better person By definition, they are usually defensive, not informative They dwell in the past, fear the future, and miss the now They lack balance and have no connection to an inner core No internal reference, just their repetitive negative thoughts Freedom and equality for inalienable rights for all people Not just the powerful, the wealthy, the satisfied We are never free if we are not all free We are never equal if we are not all equal Compassion, kindness, and love never judge But blame, denial, and self-righteousness always judge A meditative/contemplative path promotes balance Brings clarity to what freedom and equality for all means Frees us from our defenses and judgments Brings awareness of compassion, kindness, and love So desperately needed in today’s world Spend a few minutes each day in silence, be still, embrace Presence Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit, they await

George G
