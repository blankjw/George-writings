ESPLOITATION THROUGH FEAR

By George Gondron | Source: ESPLOITATION THROUGH FEAR.docx

EXPLOITATION THROUGH FEAR To exploit through fear is the hallmark of a bully The proponents of rhetoric driven by creating anxiety Are also the ones who ban books and deny history History written through the eyes of the conqueror, The oppressor, the powerful and wealthy Is always distorted and seldom ever factual Three primary things fuel all historical atrocities Fear, lack of understanding Greed, you have what I want And difference, you ae not like me And in the history of our nation, it all springs from White is right, especially white male, and it still exists Their power is exploitation through fear, not through reason Not through facts, or true history, or reliable education In fact, even today there are attempts to eliminate This nation’s historical truth from the classroom We must honor, respect and support those who risk all To fight against this terrible attack on our freedom For if we cannot have truth taught in our classrooms We will never be free; we will live in the dark When ignorance reigns it’s not the fault of an honest teacher A teacher who cares about history as it actually happened A teacher who cares about the formation of the human intellect It is the result of minds that only want to be stimulated by ‘Spin’ and being ‘gaslighted’, using negativity for political gain It’s interesting to note that Christianity is supposedly a religion Of inclusion, compassion, empathy, and love Today, there is little evidence of the above, right or left If we live through our emotions we are easily manipulated, Exploited, and controlled – please use critical thinking To do otherwise is to be controlled by a selfish ego For balance add a meditative/contemplative practice to life Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Focus on your breath or a short mantra, breathe in, breathe out The ego will resist, the mind will wander, simply rinse and repeat Listen for the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, they wait

George G
