UNINFORMED

By George Gondron

UNINFORMED Social media, in all its forms, has accomplished a phenomenon Never before seen in our society Millions of our citizens honestly believe they know the truth About politics, about religion, about climate control, about All welfare programs, about foreign aid, about immigrants, About anything and everything that is controversial Anything that is polarizing and thrives on dualistic thinking To be truly informed, one must understand both sides of An argument or a discussion, not just one side One must be openminded, not closeminded One must engage both their intellect and their emotions One must be able to comprehend the difference between An either/or approach to life as opposed to a both/and approach With just a little discernment one hopefully can grasp the fact That seldom is something always right or always wrong It’s mostly situational, the circumstances always matter Life is not black or white as some ultra conservative Christians would have you believe, or for that matter Ultra conservative politicians Almost all progressive movements are the result of a Need to improve a human condition that was lacking Social security, Medicare, Medicaid, civil rights legislation, Women’s right to vote, Universal Health Care, Voter’s Rights Act, Child labor laws, Minimum Wage Laws, Clean Water and Clean Air Legislation, worker’s rights to unionize, all began as progressive Movements voted against by the wealthy and one of the major Political parties, which shall remain unnamed Be informed about what you believe, seek more than one source Continue to educate yourself, it’s important, very important Either we believe the phrase ‘I am my brother’s keeper’ Or it’s only ‘lip service’s at best Life is not black and white, it is gray and demands balance For balance add a meditative/contemplative practice For a few minutes each day be silent, be still, embrace Presence Your image of God, of a creative Source Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, they wait

George G
