WHO IS LISTENING

By George Gondron

WHO IS LISTENING? Who is really listening to the narrative that now exists? And even further, listening with an open mind, few For most, their minds have been made up They have chosen a side With little or no factual information And the most frightening reality When they see or hear data Counter to their belief, they remain committed To what is now an obvious falsehood Clinging to the phrase ‘I may not be right, but I’m never wrong’ It is truly a dilemma that appears to be unsolvable When you ask a person How do you know you’re right? And their response is tied to a social media Commentator who attempts to gain a larger audience Through a different conspiracy theory Or even better when the reply to How do you know you are right? The response is, ‘I just know’ Give up, you will never get through You only end up frustrating yourself – let go As a health professional, I can attest to the fact That few people know how to listen At least listen actively – most are passive listeners Missing 95% of what is being communicated When the mind is made up It’s impossible to be a good active listener As well as a good learner The same problem exists in most organized religion We seem to be good at worship Poorly lacking in active listening In being in an experiential relationship With one’s belief in a Creator God, in Isness Take time each day for silence, stillness, Presence Empty the mind, listen to the whispers of the heart They wait, patiently

George G
