MUDDY WATER

By George Gondron

MUDDY WATER Most of us don't drink muddy water Clear water being readily available A pond that is muddy becomes clear left undisturbed Boiling dirty water renders it safe to drink Water, so necessary for survival of the body How does the mind survive? Without the experience of muddy water settling? Without the distilling in the silence of the mind's contaminates? Such a simple analogy, muddy water Such an uncomplicated image Muddy water, becoming clear through stillness Becoming clear through silence Our minds are no different Contaminated with noise, static, old programs Contaminated with propaganda, advertising, marketing Contaminated with other's thoughts, beliefs, ideas Perhaps we, like the muddy water, need a little stillness and silence Perhaps as all the great mystics tell us, it's the way to de-contaminate the mind The way to touch the Spirit The way to be touched by the All The way to know unconditional Love Perhaps
