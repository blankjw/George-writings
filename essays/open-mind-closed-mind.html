OPEN MIND, CLOSED MIND

By George Gondron

OPEN MIND, CLOSED MIND Take any topic prevalent in modern society And open a discussion, begin a dialogue Within a few moments, you will recognize Whether the discussants are open or closed Suffice it to say, you will discover most are not open The preferred status, unfortunately, is a closed mind A mind that already has the correct answers The right beliefs, an accurate analysis, which can’t be challenged Obvious facts, scientifically tested hypotheses, do not matter All of which goes to prove the fact that education does not matter There are those that still believe the Earth is flat That sexual orientation is a choice That the Holocaust never happened And we never traveled to the moon Not to mention the many unprovable conspiracy theories That are now prominent, and growing exponentially The underlying emotion of this closemindedness is fear The unwillingness to expand their consciousness Beyond that which they currently possess And yet from their current state of awareness They are hard-pressed to be accountable and responsible For their own dysfunctional behavior, thoughts, and feelings These same closed minds live with angst, dread, and yearnings Which they are unable to address, unable to risk, be vulnerable All because of their unwillingness to let go of their surety Their overwhelming need to be right When closely examined it is an empty life Filled only by their egos, their false self, unable to be self-critical To be rescued from a closed mind choose a new easy path Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence A meditative/contemplative practice, a guarantee to the true self A process to connect inner and outer To awaken your inner core, your internal reference Found in the innate knowledge of the heart, soul, and spirit For every question, there is an internal answer A committed daily practice is transformative Your inner resources are patient, they gently await

George G
