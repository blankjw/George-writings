OPEN MIND - CLOSED MIND

By George Gondron

OPEN MIND – CLOSED MIND There is a significant difference between the two minds One is willing to explore, question, doubt, research The other already knows the answers, has no doubts One knows life is about change, progress in a positive direction The other is willing to either maintain the status quo Or revert back to earlier times, ‘the good old days’ One mind is more interested in truth, facts, personal experience The other is easily manipulated through their emotions Both have one thing in common, their ability to choose And all our choices result in consequences Just as the universal law of cause and effect If the choices we make are the result of emotional experiences With no reliable basis for what we like or dislike It’s safe to say, the consequences from those choices Will more likely than not, fail to meet our expectations As the saying goes, ‘all that glitters is not gold’ If the opinions you have are the result of information from One social media source, you are driven by your emotions Not your intellect When a society’s primary focus is not on its citizens Maintained by those in power, elected or otherwise The society will eventually fail What becomes more important is power, control, and wealth All fueled by production and consumption, endlessly By all indications we are fast approaching that status Things are more important than people We are motivated more by fear than by love We seem to have lost the necessary human characteristics For kindness, compassion, empathy, and care for all Open minds are not either/or minds, they are both/and minds A meditative/contemplative practice produces and open mind It brings balance to life, awakening our inner resources A reliance on our own intuition and discernment, innate within Spend a few minutes each day in silence, be still, embrace Presence Empty your mind, be in relationship with your image of God Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They reveal your true self, have your answers, and they wait

George G
