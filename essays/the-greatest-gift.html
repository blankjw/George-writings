THE GREATEST GIFT

By George Gondron

THE GREATEST GIFT We all know of gifts given and received In our personal lives, and in the lives of others Some more significant, some less so Some earned, others not deserved, obligations only The gifts that flow more from excess And those that are given out of sacrifice The reciprocal gifts, they gave me one, I give them one Tit for tat Gifts of all genres and shapes, both large and small The gift with deliberate thought behind the giving And the one that is symbolic only, with no thought at all Birthdays, holidays, baby showers, weddings Graduations, anniversaries, promotions, and retirements Gifts for every occasion, every event, every celebration And as the years flow by, as time marches on Is it more of an obligation, than a sincere recollection? More of a requirement, than an honored opportunity? We each have our answers, hidden in our thoughts At the appropriate time we'll decide what they are And after deciding we'll choose once again To give our gifts for whatever reason, real or imagined But there is a gift much more important these Greater than annual remembrances, whatever they may be The gift that arises from compassion and kindness A situation that pulls at the heart and stirs the soul That brings tears to the eyes as a result of some suffering The suffering of another through no fault of their own A gift that can bring wellness and safety to the helpless That provides hope and promise where it once was lost It is only through love that this greater gift can be known Love that cares more for the other than for itself Rare indeed is this special gift, this kind of love Rare indeed is the one who knows Understands, we all are connected body and soul Sisters and brothers from one common Source Through the One that creates and gives life to all Rare indeed

George G
