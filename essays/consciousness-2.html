CONSCIOUSNESS 2

By George Gondron

CONSCIOUSNESS There are approximately ten hypotheses about the Origins of consciousness The most recent is from quantum physics Which postulates that the universe itself is conscious Meaning consciousness has always been As a result of a creative source, which some call God What we humans call consciousness, excluding the original Source Is theorized to be an evolutionary process Assuming one can correlate awareness with consciousness In other words, everything is conscious But not everything or everyone has the same level of awareness We need not look very far back to understand How our awareness as a human species has evolved One hundred years ago, for instance, the internet was unimaginable As well, three hundred years ago, planes, trains, and automobiles Evolution, change, progress, innovation, and consciousness Are all pulling us forward, despite our resistance to change It seems we live more and more in a state of clinging To the status quo, or let’s return to the ‘good old days’ To become more conscious, more aware Is just too difficult, too demanding, it’s a distraction It requires that we question, doubt, and use critical thinking After all, it’s much easier to choose our preferred Social media outlets, they will inform us of all we need to know And if we happen to profess a particular religious belief It also will tell us what to believe, think, do, and worship Everything is provided for us; all we need to do is choose Our particular poison, it’s readily available Here’s some news if you’re not already aware We each have internal resources that we never use Only because our existence is lived in our heads In our egos, our minds, our intellect, our incessant repetitive thoughts While our hearts, souls, and spirits patiently wait Wait until we decide to spend a few minutes each day In silence, with stillness, and with Presence, to listen Listen to the whispers of our inner truth, our true self It is there we find our healing, our balance, our Source

George G
