HOW FAR AWAY IS NOW

By George Gondron

HOW FAR AWAY IS NOW? Seems at first to be a ridiculous question Now is now, right here But for the most part we live as if it's not Either locked in the past or fearing the future We somehow miss the now altogether And is there really anything more important than now? The present moment
