HOW DOES A CHILD LEARN TO HATE

By George Gondron

HOW DOES A CHILD LEARN TO HATE The answer is simple They are taught, either directly or indirectly Monkey see, monkey do They learn to hate those who are not like themselves In every measure possible, outer and inner They hear the discussions of their parents They notice how their parents respond to different people Different situations, not just verbally, but their facial expressions Their eyes, their voice tone, they pick up every cue What their parents watch on TV, on social media Who their friends are and their relatives The type of literature their parents may subscribe to or read Who they are allowed to associate with And who they are not allowed to associate with The immediate bastion of influence is the family, the home If their parents follow a religious belief, a political party These are the next two powerful influencers for the child And if you do not comprehend and understand the hate That is so obvious in today’s politics and religion Then you are by default teaching the child that same hate Where fear and ignorance reign, hate flourishes And where hate flourishes love has no foothold I am in my eighth decade Never have I witnessed such division and hatred We are surely reaping what has been sewn, on all sides And those who will pay the price are the young And if certain factions take more and more power And exercise more and more control, political and religious It will happen sooner than later This Republic, our democracy will be consumed In the flames, they will ignite We need to wake up, cease being led by our emotions Be critical thinkers. reflect, question, and doubt Connect our minds with our hearts, souls, and spirits For too long we’ve ignored our inner core, our internal resources Be silent, be still, and listen to those inner resources, they wait Embrace Isness, discover your true self, not the egoic self

George G
