MORE THAN EVER

By George Gondron

MORE THAN EVER We now live in a period of our history Where more than ever we are more divided Than we are united, more apart than together Those divisions reveal themselves for two primary reasons Money and power, or if you prefer money and control These ruptures occur for only one reason When ‘things’ become more important than people Those ‘things’ can be measured in a variety of ways Money and control assuredly, but also appearance, status, Influence, position, materiality, religiosity, gullibility, And one of the most insidious, just plain ignorance When a society ignores the importance of education When the level of consciousness is simply a matter of Taking for granted what we hear and see to be true With no confirmation whatsoever, based only On our emotional attractions First, following the social media source that attracts Our level of awareness and consciousness, the easy way But more importantly, refusing to do our own research Our own critical thinking, our own reflection Drawn to the source which pacifies our ego, Our attachments, our desires, and our addictions And ironically we are able to defend our beliefs and behavior With little factual evidence whatsoever, just a feeling This applies to the individual as well as the group Political, religious, social, ethnic, educational, etc., etc., etc. We all it seems are capable of wearing ‘blinders’ What’s lacking, what’s our weakness, our ‘Achilles heel’? It’s a simple concept, it’s called balance The recognition and care of our own internal core, inner reference A connection to the innate gifts we each have, consciously Make a choice to spend a few minutes each day in meditation A contemplative practice of silence, with stillness, and Presence It’s a way to be in relationship with universal Consciousness One’s image of a creator God, the name doesn’t matter Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, your truth, your healing, they wait

George G
