MORAL

By George Gondron

MORAL The difference between the moral and amoral person is simple The amoral person has no constraints Their choices are unlimited whereas the moral person limits their choices to what is true, what is good, and what is humane So, the observation that we live in a morally bankrupt culture is easily proven when we ask of our leaders, are their decisions true, good, and humane, and if we’re honest, they are not Their decisions for the most part are the result of what benefits them, not all of the citizens of this nation And even more hypocritical are those who profess a Christiaan belief and deny their own immorality, they have no constraints The underpinnings of immorality are three, fear, greed, and hate They are the pillars of support that motivate their choices To discriminate against any person when making a decision that touches other people’s lives, is by definition, immoral Under the current administration and the establishment of DOGE we witness daily, the discrimination of certain citizens that don’t meet their paradigm of who has value and who doesn’t And in so doing, we distort and lie about our own history And as a result, our children grow up under an umbrella of lies We are failing to teach our history, world history, and civics in the classroom, and consequently we are selecting our morality For in reality, when we pick and choose what is or is not moral we compromise our on values, our own ‘so called’ morality To deny aid to those in need, the disenfranchised, the handicapped, through gender preferences, through racism and misogyny is nothing more than immorality in a nation that could easily solve poverty, homelessness, lack of health care and hunger All because of fear, greed, and hate, there is no other reason Maybe we all need to look in the mirror occasionally and share with our self-image what we perceive our own morals to be, I hope so Often, what we’re unwilling to examine, we take for granted As a suggestion adopt a meditative/contemplative practice Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Focus on your breath or a short mantra, breathe in, breathe out Listen to the whispers of your inner guides, heart, soul, spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, they wait

Geore G
