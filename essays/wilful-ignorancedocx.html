WILFUL IGNORANCE

By George Gondron

WILLFUL IGNORANCE The word discernment is a very important mental ability It is the capacity to understand and discriminate The polar opposite of willful ignorance Which doesn’t attempt to perceive clearly But rather ‘soaks up’ what is offered without question Especially if what is offered stirs the emotions, not the intellect Those who act irresponsibly do so through a knee-jerk reaction Which is almost always tied to a fear-based emotion The inclination of the willfully ignorant is a path of Accusing, Judging, blaming, and condemning To have an open discussion with such a person/persons Is virtually impossible, their minds are closed There are those who wish to know And those who already know The willfully ignorant refuse to evaluate their own behavior
