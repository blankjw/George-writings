THOUGHTS

By George Gondron

THOUGHTS If useless thoughts were changed into gold We'd all be wealthy, beyond our wildest dreams But they can't, and they won't, and we continue What is the purpose, the goal, the results? To distract, to avoid, to waste energy Our thoughts distract us just as our activities We avoid reality and a life of substance And we waste energy, energy, and more energy Becoming exhausted with no positive results And most of these thoughts are negative, repetitive Wanting someone or something to change And they never do, or it never does Yet we cling to useless thoughts Seldom are our thoughts analytic Reflective, pensive, or contemplative Mostly accusatory, complaining, or critical Never about myself, always about the other Thought; 'if only they' - Reality; 'if only I' So, how do we stop our 'flypaper' minds? These 'monkey' minds that never cease Increase the neural groves beyond three or four There are ways, it's a matter of choice That's what life is , you know, choosing But, most only blame for what they have chosen We could see more each day instead of just looking And listen more each moment instead of just hearing And then there's the big risk, silence and stillness A few minutes each day to empty the mind Let the thoughts go, shut out the noise The rewards will surprise you, they won't disappoint It is your choice, it is your life, it is a way, it is

George G
