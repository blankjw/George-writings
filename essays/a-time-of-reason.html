A TIME OF REASON

By George Gondron

A TIME OF REASON There will be moments in this journey we call our life When there will be ‘breakthroughs’ As a result of unbearable pain, overwhelming love Or simply as a matter of grace For some, it will be immediate, for others a gradual process And for many never at all – the ego’s tendencies of Defensiveness and resistance have tremendous power There will be milestones interspersed along our journey That will incorporate words and phrases such as Awareness, understanding, discernment, self-reflection, Ownership, critical self-analysis, clarity of perception, Increased learning, adaptation, open-mindedness, and compromise At each milestone, each signpost, there is a fork in the road One path leads to and embraces the new possible The other path clings to and defends the old familiar One promotes the reality that life is not about us Rather we are about life, lived to its fullest Who we are to the world, to each other, is what’s important Our value lies in our relationships, our connections Our objective selfless reasons have more merit than Our subjective selfish desires and attachments Our inability to change, to let go, is not self-control It’s no control, ‘wearing blinders, not seeing the forest for the trees’ Life’s journey is not a straight road It’s filled with twists and turns, each with a choice point As the saying goes, ‘Take the road less traveled’ Challenge yourself, the familiar offers nothing new Mystery lies in embracing the new, the unfamiliar, the possible We are creatures of habit, in our culture it’s about the externals About production and consumption We are human ‘doings’ who have forgotten how to be human beings Incorporate balance, your inner core, your internal references Adopt a meditative/contemplative daily practice For a few minutes each day choose silence, stillness, and Presence Empty your mind, use a short mantra Listen for the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, they wait

George G
