MISINFORMATION AND INTENTIONAL LIES

By George Gondron

MISINFORMATION AND INTENTIONAL LIES Misinformation and intentional lies lead to only one thing, possible Violence, and in most cases, it is violence to innocent people Through our Constitution and our First Amendment we are Guaranteed free speech, thankfully Question, when that free speech is uttered by an influential person And is so false, is an obvious lie, declared so by reliable persons Persons in authority, and leads to fear and even violence to the Innocent persons, who is at fault, who is punished? Recently there have been two court cases involving a mass Shooting where for the first time the parents of the shooter Have been convicted and held to be guilty by a jury of their peers As well as the shooter, all three going to prison Obviously, a case of guilt through direct knowing of the potential Extreme danger of the perpetrator of the crime Here’s a question for you, when people in positions of power foment Undesirable or violent behavior that leads to injury or death Even though they did not themselves commit the acts Do you think they should also be accountable? According to the Supreme Court the First Amendment Does not protect speech that creates a clear and present danger Screaming ‘fire’ in a crowded theater when there is no fire It doesn’t take an ‘Einstein’ to recognize that the information Available on social media is very polarizing and almost all That information is meant to stir your emotions, not your intellect It is more about opinions than facts, more about exaggerations, Misinformation, and actual lies than truth Here’s a truth, we’d rather be fed hyperbole and ‘gaslighted’ than Discover the truth through our own efforts, the ego is lazy Through nonparticipation, we become responsible for the final Results of any and everything that effects our lives, our freedoms The ‘finger’ points back at us, blame will not work Allow your ego to have a partnership with your heart, soul, And spirit – listen to your conscience, learn to discern Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Seek clarity that you might respond accurately Listen to your heart, soul, and spirit they hold your true self They have your answers and they patiently await

George G
