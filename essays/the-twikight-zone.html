THE TWIKIGHT ZONE

By George Gondron

THE TWILIGHT ZONE Back in the 50’s and 60’s Rod Serling hosted a weekly TV show, The Twilight Zone Where the unimaginable, unbelievable, unthinkable, and the impossible was portrayed on our TV screens It has now been brought back to life in ‘living color’ by our new TV personality, the Orange Trumpster with his cohort Elon ‘Hitler’ Musk, but now it has become very believable Every moment of every day brings news that is almost impossible to comprehend and difficult to accept News which violates the Constitution and ignores the rule of law All perpetrated by the person who is supposed to defend our Constitution and democracy and uphold the law, but is nothing more than a habitual liar, fraudster, and con artist The more shocking reality is how many millions of people deny the obvious which is exactly what the Germans did in the 1930’s And history is very clear about how that ended Even more disturbing is the fact that those who can and should oppose him, do not have the courage nor the spine to do so Fear is a great motivator, but the results of giving in to fear are never beneficial and those who suffer the most are the marginalized, the disenfranchised, the poor, the disabled, the minorities, those living below the poverty level, the voiceless The enemy is not undocumented immigrants, those on welfare, the single moms with children working several jobs to get by The enemy is extreme wealth, power, control, male ‘whiteness’, the far right ‘evangelical Christians’ who speak for God Yes, the ‘Twilight Zone’ is alive and well and one must wonder when will enough be enough? We are losing our freedoms moment by moment, there is no equal justice under the now ‘Trump Law’, no equality We are a nation divided by a narcissistic, egotistical, pathological, liar Psychologically a sociopath/psychopath, without a conscience Please adopt a meditative/contemplative practice, be a critical thinker – sit in silence, stillness, with Presence each day Awaken your true self, the answers are inside you Focus on your breath or a brief mantra, breathe in, breathe out Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit, they wait

George G
