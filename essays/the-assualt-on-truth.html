THE ASSUALT ON TRUTH

By George Gondron

THE ASSUALT ON TRUTH An underlying premise in a democratic system Is truth Truth inherent in the principles and beliefs of Freedom, equality, and justice for all its participants When truth no longer matters, democracy fails And ours is failing, awash in alternative facts We are closer to an autocratic system of governance More now, than we have ever been And there are strong indicators that we are in fact An oligarchy, a government run by money Controlled by money and directed by corporate lobbyists As more and more of the wealth finds it way Into the hands of fewer and fewer It then becomes just one small step From becoming a dictatorial system Our basic form of governance and constitutional values Are being eroded by a total disregard for the belief in What the 'founding fathers' called a separation of powers If we are not alarmed by what's happening in Washington Especially from the top down We should be We're 'losing the farm' one piece at a time A path forward which has been forged in the crucible Of concern for all its citizens is being dismantled Piece by piece in almost every cabinet position available It would seem to an outsider that ignorance now rules A bully in the truest sense grasping the 'bully pulpit' All who disagree in the slightest degree Discover they are soon the outsider who was once in Those who remain have a 'CYA' attitude Always looking over their shoulders Wondering if they are next to suffer the infantile Antics of one who is supposed to be 'Commander in Chief' There will be a point of no return When concern that should have been, no longer matters Be concerned now, think objectively now, be critical now Don't wait until it's too late or too risky to speak out

George G
