ADVOCATE

By George Gondron

ADVOCATE Do you have an advocate? I hope so The person who defends you, stands up for you Has your side when others walk away But as well, challenges you, sees more in yourself Than you see in yourself Unfortunately, we do not live in a culture That encourages us to evolve, to individuate To become more aware and conscious If anything, the focus is about ‘follow me’ ‘Me’ being those in power Those who have more control than we do When we take time to reflect We discover these ‘me’s’ are everywhere At all stages of life, in every venue Prodding, encouraging, urging us To follow their narrative Never to discover our own truth Only rely on and believe their truth or their lies I defy you to pronounce one area of life Witnessed through our interactions and contexts That in some way is not manipulated To a greater or lesser degree for us to do something And that something is almost always not of our choosing Propaganda, marketing, advertising, conditioning Promotions, packaging, the list is endless in order To convince us to spend, buy, believe, and follow And sadly, we do it A reflective person easily recognizes what’s important We are nothing but a means to an end We are consumers, purchasers, do’ers Learn to advocate for yourself, have an internal life Become a human being, not just a human puppet If the primary importance in your life is not relationships Then ask yourself this question – what is it? Spend a part of each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Let the incessant repetitive thoughts fall silent Listen to the whispers of your heart, find your truth

George G
