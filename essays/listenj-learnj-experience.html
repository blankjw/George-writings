LISTEN^J LEARN^J EXPERIENCE

By George Gondron

LISTEN, LEARN, EXPERIENCE At some point in our personal journey, listening is no longer A matter of continued learning but simply a means to either defend, React, or respond to what we already believe Notice, I didn’t say what we already know A curious mind is a listening mind, open to new information Little children for example love to ask questions, they are a joy Their minds are like sponges seeking meaning about everything Their senses, thoughts, and feelings bring to them As adults we become so accustomed to the way we perceive life That we become trapped in our ‘familiar’ and suffer the loss of Being good listeners, we construct self-made prisons that don’t Allow for further education and learning, for greater understanding We follow only what we already believe, most of which has little Experiential basis, rather than relying on personal truth Belief alone allows us to distort reality as we construct scenarios in Our minds that support those same beliefs A way of having an answer before the question is even asked A mind that sincerely wants to learn, to listen, is an open mind In our culture, those with open minds are becoming more and more Difficult to find, they are few and far between A curious mind is a great gift, a closed mind is an immediate danger One is teachable and listens to learn, the other already knows All belief, without personal knowing, is simply following the ‘party Line’, regardless the party, we need more knowers and fewer believers We have reached a state in our society where most information is Disinformation and with the advent of AI the abyss is closer What the mind calls progress, disconnected from the heart, is Never equal, fair, and just for all, ever, history proves otherwise If we’re unwilling to learn, listen, educate ourselves and only accept Third party information as truth, we are blind and deaf to reality We are saying to ‘life’ we don’t want to know Seek balance, connect inner and outer, awaken your conscience Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Empty your mind, focus on your breath or a short mantra Breathe in, breathe out, the mind will wander, rinse and repeat Listen for the whispers of your inner guides, heart, soul, spirit They hold your true self, have your answers, they patiently await

George G
