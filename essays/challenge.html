CHALLENGE

By George Gondron

CHALLENGE A challenge is good for the soul For most of us what we do each day Is only a repetition of yesterday And will be repeated tomorrow We are creatures of habit Seldom changing anything in our daily lives The same routines day after day Not just in our actions But in our thoughts and feelings as well It seems our unfounded beliefs And most of our beliefs are unfounded They are not the result of personal experience But they are the carrier of many of our so-called Problems A closed mind is a dangerous thing It limits our awareness and consciousness And in fact, it is the reason for most of our fears And negative emotions As the saying goes ‘Small minds create the biggest problems’ And they are the most defensive For you see, it’s not what we think That is the true problem It’s our reluctance to explore To explore that which is beyond what we think For some reason, at some point in our lives We cease to learn, we simply accept Dare to learn something new Dare to risk and be vulnerable There’s nothing to lose and much to gain Without use, all things stagnate and atrophy Our minds, our brains are no different There is no endpoint to continued learning In fact, we have barely scratched the surface Of our full human potential Choose to grow, intellectually, emotionally, spiritually It’s beneficial for us all

George G
