INFORMATION IS NOT TRANSFORMATION

By George Gondron

INFORMATION IS NOT TRANSFORMATION Information is not transformation Just as education is not experience In fact, it's almost impossible for change to happen Individually in our current heady, intellectual, intoxicating society An openness of the heart is what's necessary A quieting of the mind, an escape from our too many thoughts We cannot think our way into a new way of living The ego cannot solve what the ego has created It requires much more than just knowledge for change to happen And we convince ourselves, through our distractions That all is well Our setbacks are small and few, unimportant We truly cannot see 'the forest for the trees' We are unwilling to recognize how we are destroying Piece by piece, inch by inch, our planet, ourselves, our lives We are like the monkey who is able to reach into the hole The hole in the coconut to extract the 'sweet meat' But is unwilling to relax its grasp in order to remove its paw We are unwilling to see how our lack of concern For all of humanity is bringing only more destruction In the face of what we call 'progress' and 'development' We must live our way into a new way of thinking Through the use of our hearts, our souls, and our spirits A change of mind happens only as a result of experience Through pain, through great love, through a contemplative mind It will happen no other way You can have all the intellectual knowledge in the world And it will not change you one iota, the source is irrelevant But surely, if everyone were more religious life would be better There has been more destruction, death, and war as a result Of religion, than any other reason, and God weeps For religion is of the head, spirit is of the heart An open heart transforms, connects, and relates An open heart grasps sameness, not difference An open heart understands peace, mercy, compassion, and justice An open heart sees itself in the other, brother and sister Ask for an open heart, seek the One that gives us life, seek Love

George G
