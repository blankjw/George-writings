TAKE ANOTHER PATH

By George Gondron | Source: TAKE ANOTHER PATH.docx

TAKE ANOTHER PATH Women, men, girls, boys, young and hold Please stop comparing yourself to anyone Whether it's color, ethnicity, religion, sexual orientation Profession, education, political affiliation, rich or poor Please cease, please halt Comparing and contrasting is only a dead-end street A never ending maze with no solution Please take another path, choose another road It is only fear that focuses on difference Only ego seeks to discriminate, never assimilate The ego can only win through dissociation and separation Through division and alienation The ego approaches life as a problem to be solved Rather than a mystery to be lived Putting itself in 'first place', and others second Never understanding that we are stronger together Than we are apart, each life is a unique gift Like fingerprints or snowflakes, there are no duplicates We are alike in our humanity We connect through our sameness We grow through our diversity Every human story has value, it is important If we cannot connect in our humanity We will never hear each other's stories Our story is who we are, it is our truth We have a choice, we can choose our direction Or we can allow life to just be a happening We can be proactive and involved in our own journey We can choose to not just settle and cling to the familiar Take the path of fullness A fullness that includes mind, heart, body, and soul Take the path of love, abandon the path of fear Stop and smell the roses, listen to the breeze Discover the inner whisperings of your heart, soul, and spirit That's where you'll discover your truth, your true self Not the false self of compare and contrast Choose wisely, choose fullness, choose love

George G
