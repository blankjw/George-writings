ROME IS BURNING

By George Gondron

ROME IS BURNING The Republic that is now burning Unlike the Roman Republic of 64 AD Is the Republic called the United States of America Unlike the Emperor Nero and his cohorts of that time The fiddlers are currently Emperor Trump and his lackeys And we are too blind to see, too deaf to hear Nero blamed the Christians of his day for the burning Trump blames his distorted definition of 'liberals' Blames hard fought regulations that are now being dismantled And who suffers? We all do, even those who support the dysfunction The politicians don't, the president doesn't They, in the dead of night Slip off to their Mar a Logos and their villas Wrapped in the security of the 'perks' they gave themselves The full retirement and medical care, to mention a few Lining their back pockets with corporate dirty money Assuaging the masses with their 'double-speak' Unable to tell the truth if their very lives depended upon it Our Republic is burning Our Constitution is being torn to shreds The fear-mongering, the finger pointing, the blame Nothing more than gasoline thrown on an open flame The man who vowed he was going to 'drain the swamp' Has only filled it with more greedy and selfish people The lie is now the truth, the truth is now a lie Facts matter not at all, but the illusions do Life is upside down Those in power, those in control Smiling behind the masks of their sinister and selfish motives Know exactly how to take advantage of our chosen ignorance And it is a choice, we refuse to educate ourselves Social media, third party information, are not education It's easy to find out how politicians vote It's easy to find who contributes to their coffers We desperately need to educate ourselves, follow the money Only we can put out the fire, they never will

George G
