LOVE HAS NO PREJUDICE

By George Gondron

LOVE HAS NO PREJUDICE Where there is prejudice, bias, discrimination, or injustice There is no love Yet it is only the power of love that can alter A judgmental mind Can defeat and overcome fear The underlying emotion of all that is negative The antidote to fear can only be unconditional love It is what the spiritual masters and teachers throughout history Have taught, have written about, have demonstrated The mind alone takes a path of power and control For the success of its ultimate goals, not a path of love In a nation ruled, by extreme capitalism Love languishes, and the ego rules supreme The existing mantra is more is better, bigger is better. And faster is better, the result of things being more important Than people, more important than meaningful relationships More important than peace within, peace between, peace among We have become emotionally bankrupt And intellectually ignorant There is a vast difference between intellect and intelligence We are now ranked twenty-fifth in IQ quotient Among all modern nations The advent of the social media explosion Has not brought us closer together It has driven us further apart Our level of individual comprehension and understanding Has diminished as we open our minds to what attracts What stimulates our emotions and bolsters our egos Bright and shiny on the outside, dead on the inside The negative aspects of the mind are strengthened When there is no internal reference or core When the connection to heart, soul, and spirit is lost Spend a few minutes each day in quiet reflection Silence, stillness, with Presence awakens our inner core Empty the mind that you might hear the whispers of Your own heart, soul, and spirit, be connected Love has no prejudice, only Love has the power to change

George G
