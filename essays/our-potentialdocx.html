OUR POTENTIAL

By George Gondron

OUR POTENTIAL One of the greatest benefits of learning is the fulfillment of Our capacity and potential for expanding our awareness and Consciousness, becoming better human beings Every choice we make has an impact not just on our lives But also, on the lives of others, for we are all connected The responsible person takes ownership for their choices They don’t blame or use one of the many psychological defense Mechanisms, but they live with or correct their choices They are rare indeed, they are the exception They don’t use excuses for their choices, they don’t ‘yes but’ Or ‘what about’, or ‘remember when he, she, or they’ They focus on what is important, the present moment We don’t have the power to change the past nor predict The future, we only have the now, the only time when We can fulfill our potential, become more understanding The past is our history, the future our hope, the now our teacher Maybe that’s why most adults cease to learn Too busy living in the past as a result of resent and anger Or living in the future as a result of fear and insecurity There is little doubt that the ego resists change Once our familiars have become habitual Seldom do they change, even those that are painful Thoughts, feelings, and actions do become patterns And in our current culture they unfortunately form from Negativity and defensiveness as opposed to positivity And openness and connectedness, and it’s always the fault of Others, not ourselves, that need to change, always And leaders, real and potential, that spread hate and division That seek to divide, control, and create fear, are the enemy The enemy of unity, of peace, of true understanding, of justice Low for them is nothing but a four letter word, not an action To enhance your own potential, choose a meditative/contemplative Practice – bring balance intro your life, inner and outer Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Isness Focus on your breath or a short mantra, breathe in, breathe out Listen for the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, have your answers, they patiently wait

George G
