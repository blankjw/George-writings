THE DIVIDED STATES OF AMERICA

By George Gondron

THE DIVIDED STATES OF AMERICA Through the explosion of social media in recent years We are witness to the increasing divide between and among us Racism, misogyny, xenophobia, homophobia, sexism, Religious fundamentalism, separation, and segregation Conspiracy theories flourish along with their namesakes The Proud Boys, Oath Keepers, KKK, QAnon, Righteous Army There are 1,225 hate and antigovernment groups in the USA Gun ownership along with incarceration percentages Far exceed any other nation in the world The economic divide between the ‘haves’ and the ‘have nots’ Grows ever greater and greater The truth of our history is being ‘white washed’ and denied Becoming more of a fantasy than a reality The rule of law is hidden behind wealth and power The ‘Golden Rule’, those who have the gold make the rules Our Democracy, our Constitution approaches the brink of failure As political power rests in the hands of Corporate corruption Our elected officials are bought and paid for by lobbyists When the people’s will is second to the Board Room We risk chaos between and among us Not to mention the loss of personal integrity, ethics, and truth Where are we headed? What are we missing? What happened to freedom and equality for all people? Have kindness, compassion, altruism, and well-being for all Disappeared? Do our relationships suffer as our focus is on consumption? Is there a more beneficial way forward? There is – critical thinking, self-reflection, personal growth Making a concerted effort to connect mind, heart, soul, spirit To awaken our inner core and internal reference given at birth To embrace our ‘true self’ and relinquish the egoic ‘false self’ To choose a meditative/contemplative practice A few minutes each day of silence, stillness, and Presence To be in relationship with our belief of a creative Source To listen to the whispers and yearnings of our hearts Of our souls, and of our spirits, they patiently await

George G
