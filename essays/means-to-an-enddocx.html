MEANS TO AN END

By George Gondron

MEANS TO AN END As individuals in our current society, we are important for one Primary reason – as consumers Production and consumption are the main functions of this culture And as a result, there is only one winner, the producer The more monopolized the product, the greater the benefit In reality, the producer controls the final price to the consumer And as well, when retail prices rise, and wages don’t Guess who suffers, not the producer and eventually Even the small retailer is squeezed out by large co-ops Such as agricultural and beef, poultry, and pork feed lots The winners in this production/consumption merry-go-round Are those at the top, the CEO, CFO, COO, etc., etc. etc. But the actual hourly worker, the laborer His/her pay changes very little, so actually as prices rise You know, what we call inflation, those at the top never suffer The ‘fat cats’ could care less, the wage earner suffers Because his/her wages don’t increase correspondingly with Price increases Sadly, the ones that ‘grease the wheels’ of our economy Are the ones that benefit the less When capitalism becomes extreme and its primary function Is a focus on excess profits for those at the top Or it’s for the ‘shareholders’ The wealthiest 10% of Americans own 89% of all corporate stock Eventually there is a price to be paid for selfish greed It is like a cancer and will sooner or later devour its host Greed is like a drug, enough is never enough It’s built on a foundation of fear, the opposite of love There was a time when corporate interests where about Improving human lives, and providing opportunities for all Diligent work, hard labor, imagination and innovation built This country, now those at the top take home millions in bonuses As they, along with the corporations they run, receive tax breaks Please, give serious consideration to the character of those you may Choose to vote for – it’s extremely important Use critical thinking, question, doubt, reflect, listen to your Inner guides, your heart, soul, and spirit

George G
