WE THE PEOPLE

By George Gondron

WE THE PEOPLE I’m curious, what does term ‘we the people’ really mean? I know it’s in the preamble of our Constitution, the Constitution of these United States Does it include the LGBTQ+ community, the orphan, the widow, the widower, the handicapped, the disabled, the PTSD veteran, the homeless standing on the street corner with their cardboard sign, the single mom with children holding down two jobs. And of course, we know it can’t include the millions of undocumented immigrants who contribute more than 80 billion in tax dollars, state and federal from which they receive no benefit, annually Those who pick our fruits and vegetables and labor in our meat packing plants, beef, poultry, and swine How about the underpaid teachers paying for their own school supplies, oh, and let’s not forget the indigenous people, you know the real native Americans from whom we stole this country Oh, speaking of stealing, almost half of our nation was stolen from Mexico, we seem to have forgotten that as well And then there’s people of ‘color’, black, browns, Asian, are they ‘we the people’ They built our nation’s capital, picked its cotton, built its railroads And during these times, past, present, and hopefully not the future ‘we the people’ who are primarily represented are clearly the wealthy white males Take a picture of the current administration, what do you see? White male millionaires and billionaires whose leader has only one requirement, complete fealty, remain loyal and bend the knee Yes, ‘we the people’ has become a very small group And the rest of us are just ‘stepping stones’ utilized for their benefit Don’t take our democracy for granted, be involved Be a critical thinker, do your research, question and doubt To aid in this process adopt a meditative/contemplative practice Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Isness Focus on your breath of a brief mantra, breathe in, breathe out The mind will wander, the ego will resist, rinse and repeat Listen to the whispers of your inner guides, heart, soul, spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, they wait

George G
