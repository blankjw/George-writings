TO BELIEVE THE LIE

By George Gondron

TO BELIEVE THE LIE To consciously and intentionally believe the lie Is to accept the consequences of such a belief The consequences of the lie are always negative Never positive, the truth never needs to lie Alternative facts are nothing but disguised lies Just a manipulation to convince us of something false Something to benefit the narrator and hide the truth The truth never needs to be defended, the lie does If we simply accept what is said, without question or doubt Even if it is fabricated and false If repeated enough, it becomes the truth Without critical thinking, discernment, and research With the vast amounts of data available through social media The truth gets buried beneath too many words and labels Intentionally meant to capture our emotions Not our intellect, not out capacity to think The lie doesn’t want you to think, only to believe without question Within the human psyche is held a truth sayer It is called our conscience, The liar ignores their conscience, denies their conscience For only their ego holds the truth, their truth To believe the obvious lie is nothing short of embracing The liar within ourselves As the saying goes, ‘There but for the grace of God, go I’ Or as Pogo says, ‘I have met the enemy, and it is me’ The most damaging and dangerous lies are the ones We tell ourselves, for they are intentional Closed minds, narrow minds, dualistic thinking Are all prey to the liar, they live through their emotions They are easily manipulated, led, and controlled And the politician says vote for me, I’ll solve your problems The TV evangelist says send me your money and you will prosper Make a change, add a new path Spend a few minutes each day in silence, be still, be present To your image of a creative Source, to Isness Awaken your innate inner truth Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit, they wait

George G
