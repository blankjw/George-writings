LIFE

By George Gondron

LIFE Our most sacred gift, our very lives Yet, to this day science does not have a complete Explanation of life’s origins One would think that the mystery of life is so significant That we would have a greater appreciation for all life But clear evidence to the contrary indicates we do not The supposed caretakers of all life are humans But again, clear evidence to the contrary indicates Humans are the greatest enemy of life itself As the saying goes, we are willing to cut off our noses. In spite of our faces If ever we bring our beautiful planet to a point of Utter desolation, it will be because of humans' actions Nothing else Man’s inhumanity to man only leads to destruction Collateral damage doesn’t matter Whether to innocent people, to the planet, to all species There is a disconnect between the ego and the soul The virtues of kindness, compassion, empathy, and love Are matters of the heart, soul, and spirit Matters of inclusion and unity versus separation and chaos The ego is more interested in self rather than other More interested in what’s better, bigger, and faster Concerned about getting versus giving The sacredness of life doesn’t apply to a few It applies to all, universally Not until we awaken our inner core given at birth Not until there is a meaningful connection between Head and heart, will there be a significant change A change that is beneficial for all life For a few minutes each day choose a meditative/contemplative path Experience silence, and stillness, with Presence Awaken your inner core Discover your true self, not the egoic false self Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your healing, they have your answers They patiently await

George G
