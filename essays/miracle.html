MIRACLE

By George Gondron | Source: MIRACLE.docx

MIRACLE How do you describe a miracle when you are the miracle? I betcha never thought of that question Everyone ever born, past, present, and to be born Is a miracle, like our fingerprints, brains, and tears No two being alike, a pretty amazing fact There is no one else like you and never will be, you are unique Unfortunately, we grow up comparing, contrasting, competing As if those were valid human measures, which consequently Only lead to shame, self-doubt, or negative thoughts Because we don’t ‘measure up’, we don’t fit the ‘standard’ Excuse me, but that, to put it bluntly, is total ‘bull-shit’ Few, if any of us, grew up in an atmosphere free from criticism Verbal or nonverbal, at home, school, church, or among peers Oh, and let’s not forget that human condition called marriage No criticism there, right? How is it we learn to put people down rather than lifting them up? Well, this is not a treatise on psychology but suffice it to say Too many of us feel better about ourselves when we put others down And if you have the least bit of consciousness, you will notice Almost all social media is a ‘put down’ a weapon to be used To promote and maintain ignorance and support ‘closed minds’ If you don’t have one now, seek a context that supports you That supports humanity in general, you are a miracle, we all are Your human potential and creativity are beyond your imagination Your ability to be kind, compassionate, inclusive, and loving is Also, beyond your imagination The world becomes a better place not because of our thoughts But because of what we do or do not do We are relational beings connected to one another To become whole, fully human, we need balance in our lives We are not just our thoughts we are so much more Add a meditative/contemplative practice to your life Spend a few minutes each day in silence, be still, embrace Presence Empty your mind, focus on your breath or a short mantra Breathe in, breathe out, rinse and repeat Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, they wait

George G
