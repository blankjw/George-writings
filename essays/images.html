IMAGES

By George Gondron

IMAGES Images of the mind The words we hear Words transformed into pictures Power lies in the image, in the picture Not in the words All remain, either present or hidden Some in our awareness Some blotted from memory When words are forgotten The image remains In recalling a memory It arrives in an image, not in a word The energy is carried in the form The consequence is disclosed in the scene An indelible etching on the psyche, the soul Past images tell our story Stories of pain or promise Tales of hope and failure Moments of love and passion All are our teachers, these images Through ignoring the lessons The images repeat themselves The characters may change, the process remains Let us learn from our images Let us see beyond the experience Let us continue our journey Let us be present to life Let us love Let us

George G
