HOW MANY GODS

By George Gondron

HOW MANY GODS If the universe was created Does it really matter by whom? Isn't what matters the reason Was it just an idea in the mind of God? For God's pleasure and distraction The answer can only be found in experience It can't be intellectualized My experience tells me God is relational Not an academic deliberation Desiring, longing, yearning for relationship The name does not matter, the name of God Names are only for worship, not for relating Yahweh is not a name It is the sound of the breath, inhaling, exhaling Yah-weh, un-nameable Do I believe the man Jesus died for my sins? No Do I believe he was an example of what God wishes? Yes An example of how to live, to relate, to love I think we've missed the message I think if He returned, the result would be the same Feed the poor, clothe the naked, heal the lame? Visit the prisoner, give water to the thirsty? Turn the other cheek, love my enemies? Do good to those that harm me? Love God, love my neighbor as myself? Yes, the results would be the same The only way to grasp these concepts Through relationship, through the heart Not with the mind God's name does not matter Mine, yours, theirs - it does not matter The matter lies within The matter is relational The matter is heart felt It is

George G
