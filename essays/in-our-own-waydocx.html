IN OUR OWN WAY

By George Gondron

IN OUR OWN WAY In our own way, we seek meaning in our lives We bend toward the gifts of our own creativity And as well we, in our own way, embrace the distractions That enables us to avoid our deeper self, our true self Satisfied living with the ego’s false self The self that has all the answers Chooses all the right paths, and yet Only sees through a glass darkly With no clarity of perception Therefore, no accuracy of response Merely defensive reactions at best And little self-awareness, absent the examination of our Own conscience, avoiding self criticism at all costs Our frame of reference is very limited But our judgments of others is very broad Never really grasping the understanding that The finger we point toward all else Points back to ourselves as well Genuine self-love takes the focus off all others And makes an objective examination of itself Very difficult to do in our Western culture It’s not easy to see in the clear light of day one’s own Bias, prejudice, narrow-mindedness, and partiality It’s hard to recognize one’s own dualistic thinking It’s humiliating to discover we have an enemy And it is ourselves A path of awakening includes the realization that The thoughts, feelings, and actions we have, are a choice No one is holding a gun to our heads Take a risk, be vulnerable, choose a meditative/contemplative path Spend a few minutes each day in silence, with stillness and Presence Become aware that a belief in a Creator is about a relationship Not about rites, rituals, dogma, and doctrine Not about worship but about accepting unconditional love From Love, from Isness, from universal Consciousness Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit The innate knowing you hold within, they patiently await

George G
