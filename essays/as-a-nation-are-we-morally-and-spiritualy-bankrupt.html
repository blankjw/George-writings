AS A NATION ARE WE MORALLY AND SPIRITUALY BANKRUPT

By George Gondron

AS A NATION ARE WE MORALLY AND SPIRITUALY BANJKRUPT? Please, tell me where I can find a mainstream media source That talks about either issue, morality or spirituality They don’t exist and why? They don’t sell, they don’t bring in dollars You can certainly find TV evangelists, and isn’t it interesting that the Most successful are very wealthy, tell me does that equate to morality? Morality correlates to individual human character Having the qualities of decency, integrity, honor, virtue, fair play, Ethics, goodness, honesty, right conduct, principles, and equality And then there’s spirituality as opposed to religion The former is about an existential relationship with one’s belief and Knowing of a benevolent Source, which some call God but has many Names beyond itself, always and ever about an intimate relationship Like morality, it is particular to each individual, it’s not communal Religion on the other hand, is more intellectual and dualistic in nature More about rites. rituals, dogma, and doctrine with a human hierarchy Always in control dictating the rules about right and wrong The ‘business’ of organized religion is a detriment to personal knowing Organized religion requires that we become dependent upon its Dictates, its rules and regulations, which has yet to be the path that Results in world peace, inclusion, equality, and harmony And now, through our electoral process, we elect ‘foxes’ To guard the ‘hen house’, those of good moral character Who are willing, through governmental laws, rules, and regulations, To join ‘church and state’ and further reduce the freedoms of its Citizens, further decide what can and cannot be taught in our schools We are ‘awake asleep’ as our rights, freedoms, and privileges are Slowly being eroded and replaced by dictatorial mandates Promulgated by both secular and sacred authorities We are no longer our ‘brother/sister’s’ keeper, if we ever were Who we each are in relation to our personal freedoms, our belief In a creative Source of this universe, our own morality Is singular in nature, it’s not communal Yes, as a nation, we are morally and spiritually bankrupt Please adopt a meditative/contemplative practice For just a few minutes each day choose silence, stillness, with Presence Awaken your own conscience, your own discernment and critical thinking Focus on your breath or a short mantra, the mind will wander Come back to the breath again and again Listen for the whispers of your inner guides, heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, have your answers, they wait

George G
