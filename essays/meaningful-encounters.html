MEANINGFUL ENCOUNTERS

By George Gondron

MEANINGFUL ENCOUNTERS It begins with self-understanding A process that's never ending Understanding that we are more alike than unalike Then with more clarity, deeper perception We are able to see beyond the obvious Listen for what's not being said in the conversation Be present to the other, not self-absorbed The chance for a meaningful encounter is possible A difficult task for the mind alone More easily attained with the heart, soul and spirit as well
