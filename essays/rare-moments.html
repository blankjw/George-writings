RARE MOMENTS

By George Gondron | Source: RARE MOMENTS.docx

RARE MOMENTS Some never see them Never experience them Are never overcome by them They are still there, those rare moments We are engulfed by them, invisible to most What makes the ordinary so uncommon? What makes this moment impossible to experience? Impossible to see, unattainable, this moment I am awake, yet sound asleep Conscious, yet totally unaware What wonders I miss Magical moments, mystical participation Soul encounters, encircling us unseen My heart knows My mind refuses to know My heart trusts the numinous My mind avoids the ethereal My heart seeks the Relationship My mind worships the image My heart is a participant My mind is a member Like the 'Little Prince' said "One sees clearly only with the heart Anything essential is invisible to the eyes." Experience your heart knowledge Encounter the possible Engage the Mystery The rare moments

George G
