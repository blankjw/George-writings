RECITAL INFORMATION 2016

By George Gondron

RECITAL INFORMATION 2016 Balances due by May 10th to participate in recital BACKSTAGE: During recital...only backstage moms, dancers & faculty allowed Only office personnel may save seats, all other seating is open....theatre doors open 1 hour prior to the performances Do not use the side doors in the theatre during the performances...only the doors at the back of the theatre and only between dances NO FOOD OR DRINKS ALLOWED IN THE SEATING AREA INSIDE THE THEATRE Drink only water while in costume...do not eat in costume unless you are covered DRESS REH. #1 FRIDAY JUNE 3: These levels arrive @ theatre 3:30, All Kinder. classes, Creat. Move., Pre K & Kinder. Hip Hop /Hip Hop I, find dressing rooms, put on first costume & sit in audience with your class. Apply stage makeup if you have time. You can apply between dances if necessary. We will run these classes in this order: #1 Kinder. Ballet, #2 Creat. Move., #3 Kinder. Tap, #4 Pre K, #5 Kinder. Hip Hop/Hip Hop I

*ALL OTHER DANCERS IN LEVELS I, IIA & IIB ARRIVE 4:00 P.M. *Mini's and Junior Co. dancers will reh. next. I then hope to run the rest of the reh. in the performance order...please be patient

DRESS REH. #2 SAT. JUNE 4: 9:00 am sharp!! Pointe, Ballet III w/II B, all Level III classes, all co. pieces and faculty pieces. Warm-up @9:20

ALL OTHER DANCERS ARRIVE AT THEATRE 1:00 PM, **FINALE RUN THROUGH 1:30** Run through show in order, no stops 2:00-4:30ish No costumes for run through, class attire with appropriate shoes for each dance. Bring food, water,pillow & blanket to rest...touch up hair & makeup....rest until warm-up. Warm-up on stage 5:45-6:30.

NOTE: Creat. Move., Pre K & Kinder. levels may leave the theatre after run through, but must return by 5:30. LEVELS I & UP REMAIN AT THE THEATRE PERFORMANCE #1 BEGINS @ 7:00 pm SHARP

Come to the theatre w/hair and makeup done, back stage moms are not responsible for dressing your child or doing their hair & make-up Do not wear costumes from home or out in the audience without a cover-up, unless it's dress rehearsal Bring extra bobby pins, hairnets ( all ballet buns need to be covered with a hairnet), ponytail holders, makeup etc;..... ALL HATS, HAIR RIBBONS OR FLOWERS MUST BE SECURELY PINNED ON If you take your child out into the audience during recital, TELL THEIR BACKSTAGE MOM AND THEY MUST BE IN THEIR DRESSING ROOMS AT LEAST 8 DANCES BEFORE THEY PERFORM. If a dancer is missing when it's time to go on stage, I will not stop the recital to find them. DO NOT STAND UP while a dance is in progress....please wait until it has finished to leave ABSOLUTLEY NO FLASH PHOTOGRAPHY ALLOWED IN DRESS REH. OR PERFORMANCES. This if very dangerous for the dancers!! Taking personal video is only allowed at Dress Reh., we will have a professional videographer for Sunday's Perf. No fingernail or toenail polish, no underwear under costumes, that is what tights are for...NO JEWELRY!!! If you recently got your ears pierced, cover it with a piece of flesh colored band aid. Dance Solutions has the shoes & tights you will need for recital All Ballet classes: Bloch Pink seamed full foot tights, Creat. Move.,& Pre K do not have to have seamed tights, however please purchase the Bloch Pink color, pink leather ballet shoes with elastic sewn on (Ballet III w/IIB wear Capezio Light Suntan tights) Jazz: Capezio Light Suntan full foot tights and whatever color and style jazz shoe specified by the teacher Lyrical: Capezio Light Suntan full foot tights and tan slip on jazz shoes unless otherwise specified by the teacher Hip Hop: Capezio Light Suntan full foot tights and black converse sneakers (Hip Hop III check w/Miss Jessica about shoes) Tap: Capezio Light Suntan full foot tights, Kinder. dancers wear black Mary Jane w/buckle, all others black leather lace up shoes unless otherwise specified by the teacher Leap & Turns: Capezio Light Suntan full foot tights with the shoe style specified by the teacher *REMEMBER TO WRITE YOUR CHILD'S NAME OR INITIALS IN EVERYTHING, FOR SHOES ON THE INSIDE NOT THE SOLE

SUNDAY JUNE 5: Dancers arrive to theatre 2:00 pm.. Come with hair & makeup done, go to dressing rooms and get ready for warm up Warm up on stage for levels I & up 2:45-3:30

PERFORMANCE #2 BEGINS 4:00 PM SHARP

Have fun dancers, this is what you've worked so hard for all year. You've done a fantastic job!!!
