SIMPLICITY

By George Gondron

SIMPLICITY I sometimes wonder at the complicated nature of living The artificiality, the complexity, the entanglement Too many choices, too many options, too many Choice is good you say, I agree But I wonder, when is enough, enough How many brands of cereal do we need? How many pairs of sneakers can one wear? How many channels can one watch? How many shades of lipstick are sufficient? Choices become distractions Distractions become allurements Allurements become habits Habits become insatiable Insatiability cannot be appeased The complicated leads to more The complicated becomes confusion The simple leads to less It is within simplicity that happiness lies It is within simplicity that peace is found It is within simplicity that love abounds Avoid the complicated Opt for need Accept less Find Love

George G
