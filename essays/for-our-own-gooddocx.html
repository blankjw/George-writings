FOR OUR OWN GOOD

By George Gondron

FOR OUR OWN GOOD I have a challenge for you, for us, for all We seem to do very well wrapped in our own misfortunes Which are insignificant when compared to most of humanity And honestly, most of which we create through our own choices Choices, I might add, influenced by a dysfunctional system The focus of which is based on continuous consumption Grounded in all the psychological defense mechanisms Which are available for and to us simply to defend our own Behavior, for no other reason For you see, it’s much easier to defend our thoughts, feelings, And actions than to admit with clarity they are our choices Not mandates, no one is holding a gun to our heads Sometimes the best way to have a clear picture of the Mountains we make out of molehills Is to know intimately The life of someone much less fortunate than ourselves Rather than being too quick to judge and point a finger elsewhere We instead seek to know and understand who and what we Don’t like – a single person or a group of people Reflect on how easily we may be influenced by negative Versus positive information, by fear versus love By exclusion versus inclusion and by skepticism, doubt, and Uncertainty versus empathy, kindness, and compassion Clarity of perception, fact versus fiction, understanding and Unvarnished truth leads to accuracy of response The clearest path to truth is the telling of a story Listen to the stories of others, especially the unfortunate The ostracized and outcast, the disenfranchised and downtrodden For within each of us resides all the above It is only a fool who listens only to him/herself If you don’t have a daily practice of a meditative/contemplative Nature – find one – spend time in silence, and stillness Be present to Universal Consciousness, to Isness, to God We need to educate ourselves to escape the swamp of ignorance That is so prevalent in our society today To be for one another and not against one another In the silence listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, have your answers, know the truth

George G
