LOVE

By George Gondron | Source: LOVE.docx

LOVE Love, more than a feeling, not just an act Easier to say what love is not, than what love is A power that sustains the universe A power that supports each heartbeat, every breath It has no cost, demands no return, freely given I love you if, is not love I love you only, is not love I love you when, is not love Any restriction, stipulation, reservation, is not love Love accepts rejection, indifference, dismissal, and continues to love Love has no beginning, no ending, love is eternal If love were not, what would there be? No you, no me, no us, no they, no life Seek that which seeks us Seek that which carries us Seek that which breathes, sings, and dances us Seek Love

George G
