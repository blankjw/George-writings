CHANGE THE CHANNEL

By George Gondron | Source: CHANGE THE CHANNEL.docx

CHANGE THE CHANNEL It goes without saying that we are much more aware as adults Than we were when we were infants Like our physical bodies, our intellect, our minds mature as well But how do we continue to increase our knowledge, our awareness? It seems our main source of information currently is social media Libraries, newspapers, magazines have almost become antiquated Our senses are stimulated through ‘screens’ in our hands, our Homes, our vehicles, and our workplaces And unfortunately, we seem to settle on the sources that stir Our emotions as opposed to our intelligence And in most cases those emotions are of a negative nature We ‘pitch our tent’ at the ‘campsite’ of one or two channels And they become our basis for all truth, for all facts, for all reason In a way we revert to infant behavior when we didn’t want To eat our ‘veggies’ and experience other foods We cling to what satisfies even when it’s unhealthy One of the signs of intellectual and psychological maturity Is the ability to be open to that which is not familiar To be able to hold that tension long enough to discover its validity To test its hypothesis, to verify its facts, to question and doubt If not, we simply remain ‘addicted’ to ‘my way or the highway’ Thinking, you know, ‘I may not be right, but I’m never wrong’ Rigidity, die-hardism, incompliance, and implacability or counter To flexibility, openness, curiosity, wonder, and objectivity And honestly, counter to positive progress to the benefit of humanity All humanity, not just a select few, not just the wealthy and elite I offer this suggestion, we each have an internal ‘truth teller’ Some call it conscience, some soul, some spirit, some all three Adopt a meditative/contemplative practice Learn to change the channel, often Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Presence being your belief or image of the creative Source of the Universe, the name does not matter, the experience does Belief without experience is not knowing, it remains only belief Focus on your breath or a short mantra, breathe in, breathe out The ego will resist, the mind will wander, simply rinse and repeat Listen to the whispers of your internal guides, they have your answers

George G
