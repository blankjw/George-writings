WHAT DWELLS WITHIN

By George Gondron | Source: WHAT DWELLS WITHIN.docx

WHAT DWELLS WITHIN Within every soul; past, present, and future There lies a tiny indestructible seed Within every spirit a divine spark, a light in the darkness Within every heart the immutable truth of the Ineffable And within every mind, the right to choose, our free will Our ability to think, to reason, to reflect It is our behavior that reveals our choices How we live our lives, a testament to our deliberations The seed in our soul is nurtured through our giving Watered with our benevolence and generosity The divine spark in our spirit grows brighter through our mercy Our ability to forgive and to pardon, as has been given to us The stable truth of our heart, strength through compassion The small kindnesses we hold out to the less fortunate Our lives flow like a gentle river toward wholeness One that is inclusive and embracing Or one that is turbulent and filled with rapids and waterfalls Crashing against life, resisting its natural relational purpose How we approach our existence is a result of many influences Our cultural conditioning and programming The values of the families in which we are reared And most importantly how we deal with the pain in our lives Through avoidance or acceptance, denial or ownership We can survive through our constant distractions In the form of work, climbing the perpetual ladder of success Through feigned happiness known as gratification Which is always short-lived, until the next brass ring But the fully human life, the authentic life Is molded in the crucible of all our innate gifts Not just our mind, our intellect, our ego But in the gift of heart, soul, and spirit as well In our relational connection with unconditional Love Which is the ever evolving Energy of this vast universe Pulsating with the eternal heartbeat of the Creator Expanding with each breath of the Ground of All Being Ever present in the now of each moment More present to us than we are to ourselves, look within

George G
