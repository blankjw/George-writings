TO BE OF SIGNIFICANCE

By George Gondron

TO BE OF SIGNIFICANCE Spoken or unspoken we all want to matter Matter beyond what we do Regardless of our accomplishments What we have or do not have It doesn't matter, our beliefs, our creeds Our persona, status, gender, or race None of these matter The ones who give us a sense we matter They are the ones who know themselves Accept themselves, their entire essence They don't deny their history, their story, their journey Beyond what's seen only, not just the apparent They know themselves to the depth of their being Their shortcomings, their flaws, their weaknesses They know their shadow Their dark and their light, made friends with both They know it's a passage, self discovery It's not a goal to be reached, only a process The only reward, a certain peace of mind The only prize, a small understanding The only gratuity, continuous transformation The ultimate gift, the experience of love So, when we know we are significant to another Significant for just who we are, for no other reason We will be in the presence of one who seeks Who seeks their own self-understanding They know our sameness, respect our difference They honor our presence, care who we are When we meet those people We will know we're significant

George G
