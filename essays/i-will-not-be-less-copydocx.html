I WILL NOT BE LESS - Copy

By George Gondron

I WILL NOT BE LESS Of all our human emotions, fear makes us less And that fear comes in many forms Fear of loss, fear of abandonment, fear of not being accepted, Fear that we don’t measure up, and even if we’re brave enough To risk, to be vulnerable, fear that we may fail We will even say yes just to fit in, but inside we’re saying no We remain in abusive relationships, both physical and emotional Only because of fear If we truly loved ourselves, selfless love, we would reject fear If we don’t value ourselves, fear lurks just around the corner If we’re always comparing ourselves to others Our relationships, our jobs, our education, our credentials We open ourselves to fear that we might be less than Our thoughts are more illusory than they are factual Thoughts that keep us stuck, whatever the circumstance Only because of underlying fear, fear that makes us less than There are moments when fear is appropriate When our safety is at risk, physically But most fear is not of that nature When there is unfounded doubt, insecurity, uncertainty, Suspicion, distrust, or probability, there is underlying fear It is usually the result of an overactive imagination To accept abuse physically and emotionally is to be less than To tolerate behavior that is demeaning at any time, in any form Is to be less than To say no to fear and doubt requires courage Especially when it is connected to relationships To say, ‘I have gone this far, but I will go no further’ Is a scary awakening, but it is a blessing as well When we give ourselves away with no recompense or appreciation We do not value ourselves and we are in truth, less than Love does not mean we are meant to become ‘doormats’ Adopt a meditative/contemplative practice, seek balance Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Isness Empty your mind, focus on your breath or a short mantra Listen for the whispers of your true self, your inner guide The whisper that says you are more than, it patiently waits

George G
