FAILURE BY DEFAULT

By George Gondron

FAILURE BY DEFAULT The ego eventually fails at life, while succeeding at being right The ego's interest is gratification, rather than happiness We all have within, unopened gifts, unwrapped desires, unmet potential The ego's voice whispers, 'you're all you can be', there is nothing more A voice that tears down, does not build up The tiny whisper of the child's voice remains within The child who at one time ran and said, 'look mommy, what I did for you' Holding a crayon picture of their creativity Placed on the refrigerator with love There is no perfection, only expression Hidden within each of us is: the unwritten narrative the unsung melody the unplayed tune the unpainted canvas the unsculpted statue the undanced movement the unuttered prayer Find yours, it is never too late The heart knows what the mind refuses to understand The heart knows there is no failure by default

George G
