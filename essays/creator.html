ESSAY

CREATOR

By George Gondron

CREATOR I feel You in the warmth of a noon day sun Your whisper in a soft summer breeze Your vastness encompasses the star-filled night The fragrance of Your love lingers in a field of wild flowers Your power abides in the rolling thunder of a heavy rain You nourish our bodies, souls, and spirits in Your givenness You smile from the innocent face of a child And weep through the broken hearts of the many What You are not a part of, cannot be named For Your essence fills the universe and all that it contains You grant us autonomy and choice, the depth of Your love And for most of us, that's too much freedom We do not yet comprehend the graciousness of Your love We do not understand that all is gift So we make up stories to distance ourselves And in so doing we miss the obvious Unaware of Your closeness, Your grace, Your love The early beings called You by different names With a simple understanding of Your infinite nature Present in all places, at all times, with no exception But we have lost our connection, our inner yearning And we cling to rules that leave us empty and cold The inner fire of Your love has become a mere ember Replaced with what does not give life, melting slowly away Replaced with the next distraction And the next, and the next, with endless desire Perhaps if we become tired of that which only gratifies Until the newness wears away, just a temporary joy Then we may learn that You are our joy, our purpose You have given us all that we need to be happy You have given us each other, You have given Yourself We are slow to learn that in giving we receive We are slow to learn that all life is relationship We are slow to learn that what's important is now You are as close as our breath, the beating of our hearts And in Your unconditional love, You patiently wait For in the end Your love will win, eternally given, forever new

George G
