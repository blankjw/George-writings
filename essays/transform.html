TRANSFORM

By George Gondron

TRANSFORM What we don’t transform emotionally in our lives We end up transmitting The important step we eliminate in our search To relive our emotional suffering is the search for meaning Put another way, we’d rather defend than own the possibility That we are instrumental in the continuance of our own suffering Part of that defending process is a refusal to own our choices Regardless of what you and I may believe We do choose what we think, feel, and do or do not do No one is holding a gun to our heads Or as Pogo said, “We have met the enemy and he is us” A major part of our own transformation, our individuation Is owning our choices Blame is simply the barrier, the obstruction, to ownership Part of our human weakness is to believe without knowing To be mesmerized by the propaganda of the protagonists Of ‘pipe dreams’ and conspiracy theories All meant to control. making us each a means to an end Their end When we listen to the voice that is telling us what to think, Feel, and do, rather than encouraging us to seek, search, and Critically think and to learn, we fall prey to the lie In essence choosing ignorance over education or knowledge We have the necessary tools to discover the truth To think critically, to educate ourselves, to learn But for most, they lie dormant and unused We have become sponges, Kool-Aid drinkers Like the donkey following the ‘carrot on a stick’ Or believing the Wizard behind the curtain, the lie is now truth We are all more alike than unalike, and at the same time, unique We are born with the power of creativity, we have a gift Seek to learn, transform your pain, offer your gift Add a few minutes of silence, stillness, and Presence to your Daily routine – empty your mind, focus on your breath Or choose a short mantra, breathe in, breathe out, repeat Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, they wait

George G
