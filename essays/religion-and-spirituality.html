RELIGION AND SPIRITUALITY

By George Gondron

RELIGION AND SPIRITUALITY Religion feeds the mind, spirituality feeds the soul The Bible is a history of the Jewish people and their Relationship with their belief in Yahweh, their God Spirituality is about a personal relationship beyond belief That has nothing to do with cognition and everything to do With an existential experience that can’t be explained But can only be known, and once known, never forgotten The right-wing Christian Nationalist movement has taken it Upon itself to speak for God, interpret scripture, and mostly To promote fear with a self-righteous indignation that Has nothing to do with the human Jesus whom they say They follow, and especially nothing to do with love I’m sorry, I’ve read the four Gospels in the New Testament And obviously I missed the message I can’t find a MAGA Jesus or a God who loves this nation and insists We become a Christian country, especially in our politics It’s also interesting these MAGA Jesus believers have nothing To say about the Beatitudes, or feeding the poor, giving drink To the thirsty, clothing the naked, visiting the prisoner, And oh, let us not forget, if I read correctly, a few verses in Matthew, Mark, and Luke, to love the Lord your God with all Your heart, soul, mind, and strength and your neighbor as yourself Honestly, there is nothing about the Christian Evangelical Nationalist movement that is attractive and Jesus probably weeps Mystery, Isness, Universal Consciousness, all concepts for God Cannot be known intellectually If you have a belief in a Creator, he/she/it can only be experienced The human mind insists, especially the male mind, on creating Rites, rituals, dogma, and doctrine to maintain and promote their Particular beliefs – the Bible has been interpreted hundreds of times It is the finger pointing beyond itself to an existential Mystery Include a meditative/contemplative practice in your life Just a few minutes each day be silent, be still, empty your mind Be present to the Mystery, your image of God Focus on your breath or choose a short mantra Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, have your answers, they patiently wait

George G
