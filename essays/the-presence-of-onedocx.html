THE PRESENCE OF ONE

By George Gondron

THE PRESENCE OF THE ONE

IN THE IN FANTS COOING – THERE IS THE ONE IN THE CHILDS LAUGHTER – THERE IS THE ONE IN THE LOSS OF OUR FIRST LOVE – THERE IS THE ONE IN THE FEAR OF PERECEIVED FAILURE – THERE IS THE ONE IN THE TEARS OF PAIN AND DESPAIR – THERE IS THE ONE IN THE FRAILTY OF OLD AGE – THERE IS THE ONE IN DEATH – THERE IS THE ONE. WE DO NOT HAVE TO WAIT FOR A STRUGGLE TO SEEK THE ONE WE ONLY NEED TO BE AWAKE, PRESENT, AWARE THE ONE IS ALWAYS THERE

GEORGE G
