RACISM AND XENOPHOBIA ALIVE AND WELL

By George Gondron

RACISM AND XENOPHOBIA ALIVE AND WELL Dare to read what follows, take in the words Or are you a part of the continuing problem? Are you blind, color blind, blind to reality? To pretend nothing is wrong, and ignore the obvious Is, to a great degree, a very great degree The problem itself A turned back, a bowed head, is participation by default The racial prejudice is not just against blacks, against color It is systemic about what is not like 'me' Whether in color or in belief It's obvious in 'skin-heads', in 'purist', in 'white elitist' It lies hidden in conservative Christians, in politicians In the rich, in the fundamentalist, in the powerful In the so-called educated and degreed Education has a way of making bigots of us all Wealth has a way of turning us into fanatics and zealots And lastly, it's found in us all We let color and belief stand in our way In the way of connection, of sameness, of humanness How does it change, how can it change? It does not change through written laws You cannot legislate the mind It does not change through teaching and preaching It changes one person at a time, separately It changes through individuation Through seeing within myself that which I judge outside myself It changes through communion with Through dialogue, communication, association, acceptance Through seeing the other as person first Hearing their story, understanding their history All change begins with self Self understanding, on its deepest level Erases prejudice, eliminates racism, negates discrimination It is not until I understand myself, in all its colors That I can hope to know the other Begin now, don't stop End racism, end discrimination, end ignorance

George G
