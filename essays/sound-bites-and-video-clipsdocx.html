SOUND BITES AND VIDEO CLIPS

By George Gondron

SOUND BITES AND VIDEO CLIPS Most of us rely on sound bites and video clips To form what we believe is the truth When in fact what we believe are opinions, not truth As a general public, we have more or less Ceased to read, investigate, or research Critical thought has disappeared We simply take the opinions of others and call them factual And to further complicate or distort the issue We are primarily attracted to the sources that best Stir up our emotions, not our intellect And those that wish to influence and control us Know how to do that quite well History is replete with ‘snake oil salesmen’ They seem to thrive very well in two environments Political and religious Not to mention marketing and advertising Forever proclaiming, ‘buy this, it will make you happy’ Truth doesn’t need to be defended, opinions do White lies do, half-truths do, and factual misstatements do When enamored by opinions, consider the source Consider what you’re being ‘sold’ and who benefits Accurate journalism is and has been disappearing Giving way to money and alternative facts Be prudent, be wise, do your research, use critical thinking Most importantly, choose a path of meditation/contemplation Spend a few minutes each day in silence, with stillness And in the presence of Presence, your image of God Empty your ever busy ‘monkey mind’, your repetitive thoughts The answer to life’s most important questions are found Within – there lies your truth A full life insists on balance between inner and outer Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They await patiently, they are your guides

George G
