NORMAL DOES NOT MEAN HEALTHY

By George Gondron

NORMAL DOES NOT MEAN HEALTHY A great deal of what one would consider normal Does not come close to being healthy The society in which we currently exist Is one of discrimination, prejudice, bias, injustice, and inequality It is the norm, but it is not healthy, not even close The nation is very polarized, divided, anti versus pro It is clearly demonstrated in every arena Political, religious, gender, ethnic, and race There is a palpable angst just beneath the surface A tinderbox waiting to be ignited as we hold our breath No, the current normal is not healthy at all Conscience is an individual matter Corporate entities and most large groups have no conscience Their goals, mission, and purposes are rarely For the benefit of the single individual and their wellbeing There always seems to be a price to pay for gratuity Always a string attached, a quid pro quo When what is dysfunctional becomes normal The end result is never healthy Is it normal to turn a blind eye, bury our heads in the sand In an atmosphere that is clearly dysfunctional and destructive? Is it normal to ‘buy into’ the politician's rhetoric Who’s only purpose is to be re-elected? How can these ‘normals’ be healthy? Maybe the better question is to ask ourselves What are our normals as individual human beings Do we listen to our conscience or only our egos? Do we reflect on our own integrity and our own values? Do we question, doubt, reflect, or simply accept Or do we listen to the one with the biggest megaphone? The one or ones that stir our emotions For normal to be healthy there must be balance We must have a connection to our own internal resources Our innate nature given at birth which holds our truth Spend a few minutes each day in silence, in stillness In the presence of your image of a Creator Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit, they wait

George G
