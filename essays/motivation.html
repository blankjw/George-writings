MOTIVATION

By George Gondron

MOTIVATION Unfortunately, there are two primary paths of motivation In our current society, our culture One is through fear, lies, half-truths, and alternative facts The other is through love, kindness, compassion and empathy An outside observer would have no problem identifying Which path is the one most traveled? It is the former, clearly Which begs the question, how does this happen? If we even, consider asking the question We don’t have to search very long to find the answer Two realities emerge, lack of education and lack of common sense The uninformed mind will always take the path of least resistance Just feed me what you want me to know Don’t ask me to think, reflect, question, doubt, or research Propaganda experts have known for years how to motivate And it is always through the emotions, not the intellect Always through fear, not love Always through lack, not abundance Always through discrimination, not inclusion The importance of continuing education throughout life Cannot be stressed enough, comprehensive education Historical and all-inclusive truth, nothing left out It seems deception has become more believable than truth As the saying goes, ‘you gonna believe me or those lying eyes?’ We’re taught very little about the gifts of intuition and discernment They fall into the realm of being too ‘airy-fairy’ But they are as important, if not more so than cognition and intellect Akin to that nagging gut feeling that won’t go away Something doesn’t pass the ‘smell test’, to good to be true Ask yourself this question – is what motivates you reliable, Truthful, factual, and easily researched, or is it hear-say Third-party, finger-pointing emotional rhetoric Awaken your own internal and inner innate resources Spend a few minutes each day in silence, be still, embrace Your belief in a creative Source, the Isness of the universe In the quiet, listen to the whispers of heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, your truth, your answers, they wait

George G
