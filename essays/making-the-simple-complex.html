MAKING THE SIMPLE COMPLEX

By George Gondron

MAKING THE SIMPLE COMPLEX These few reflections are for those who say they believe in God Unless of course your God is so small as to be limited Or perhaps I should say small minds that limit God to exclude Exclude science and evolution, if so, read no further Approximately 13 to 14 billion years ago this universe began Evolving over all those years, evolving now, and in the future Most believe it is purposeful, benevolent, and directed Created by a loving, humble, compassionate God However, it would seem that God's experiment of the human being Was failing, unlike the success of the rest of nature, and then Unlike no other belief system God choose to become human Human in the form of Jesus to help this failing experiment It is recorded that He was mostly about human relationships Not so much about religious law Often being quoted as saying, 'it is said, however I say' Always deferring to the human relationship, and we all know It got Him crucified, but He won anyway, He resurrected And it would seem, that for the most part His teaching Has had a tremendous impact on the world But not what one would expect from His teachings His teachings of compassion, kindness, peace, mercy, and justice He summed it up with what He called the two great commandments Love God with all your heart, soul, mind, and strength Love your neighbor as yourself, ah but here's the rub Love by its very nature is relational, it is relationship It seems, that still today, religion is more concerned About how to be religious, how to follow the rites, rituals, Dogma and doctrines, than it is about relationship, about love How to buy into the fallacy that God sent His only son to die To die for our 'sins', really? is that the image of a loving God? I think not, love only creates, it does not destroy But if Jesus returned today, history would probably repeat itself The mystery really is, why does the Christian religion not teach Teach about relationship, about spirituality, about love That's the $64,000 question, and I think somehow it's about control In the silence and stillness you will find that relationship You will discover It within yourself, in the presence of Presence It is that simple, it is not complex, it is a choice George G
