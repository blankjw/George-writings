HOW THE LIE SURVIVES

By George Gondron

HOW THE LIE SURVIVES Distortion, exaggeration, manipulation, hyperbole, deceit, Deception, embellishment, overstatement, the list is endless But the real reason is the willingness to accept without question What we see and hear No questions, no doubts, no research, no critical thinking Being hooked through our emotions and neglecting our intellect The mind it seems, much prefers ‘entertainment’ over reality Being attracted to the ‘snake oil’ salesman, ‘foghorn leghorn’, The repeated lies that eventually are assumed to be true While ignoring the voices of reason Please don’t confuse us with the facts Like the Roman games in the Colosseum, we prefer distraction Never before in our history have we had as many resources Available to us to seek what is true with accuracy and clarity But it seems we much prefer to blame, point fingers, deny, and Use all the defense mechanisms to support our own dysfunctions When we fail to educate ourselves, what results is ignorance And the path of ignorance, of uninformed minds, Never has a good end, ask those who live under the auspices of Dictators, autocrats, tyrants, despots, and bullies Those personality types use whatever is available to them To distort, deny, and destroy the truth, truth is the enemy Their lives are grounded in fear, and the greater the fear The more outrageous their behavior and their rhetoric They are a God unto themselves, the savior of humanity No need to ask them, they tell you in hundreds of ways How perfect they are, how smart they are, but not how dangerous Here is a fact, we choose what we think, feel, and do Choose wisely, use your own due diligence to find truth Remember, the greater the exaggerations the bigger the lie To add to your potential, adopt a meditative/contemplative practice Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Balance inner and outer, connect mind and heart, learn discernment Focus on your breath, your life source, your unique essence Listen for the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit Trust your inner guides, they hold your true self, they have Your answers, they patiently wait for your attention

George G
