THERE ARE NO SHORTCUTS

By George Gondron

THERE ARE NO SHORTCUTS In this journey we call our life We all begin at point A, our birth And we all end at point Z, our death It is the in-between experiences that create our story Every story matters, each story has value and merit Some stories are longer than others, some very brief But regardless the duration, there are no shortcuts If we had the power, with our current level of awareness To examine our entire journey We would discover two very important facts We either embraced our lives fully Or we resisted what life had to offer In either case, there are no shortcuts Our lives are not predetermined, our choices do matter Free will is one of our greatest gifts But along with free will is the small matter of acceptance To accept the results of our choices, to be responsible A full life doesn’t take shortcuts It will experience all the human emotions From great love to unimaginable fear From debilitating pain to unbounded joy We all, without exception, are influenced and formed By people, places, and things beginning with family Our list of influences is endless In this culture, we have available to us a number of Choices that attempt to shortcut us from experiencing a full life They fall under three main categories: addictions, distractions, pills None of which in the end work, there’s still that inner yearning That enters our awareness when we’re unguarded A choice to engage in a meditative/contemplative practice To experience a few minutes of silence, stillness, and Presence Each and every day will put us in touch with those yearning To empty our minds of their incessant and repetitive thoughts That we might discover through the quiet whispers of our own Heart, soul, and spirit our true selves The self that refuses to take shortcuts Make that choice, experience That which waits patiently

George G
