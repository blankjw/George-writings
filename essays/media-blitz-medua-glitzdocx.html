MEDIA BLITZ - MEDUA GLITZ

By George Gondron

MEDIA BLITZ – MEDIA GLITZ The thoughts running through my mind at this moment Will place me skating on thin ice for perhaps many But hopefully not for all As I watch the national news and hear the experiences Of the hostages being released by Hamas They are heartbreaking at the least, dehumanizing at most Having only rice and bread to eat, waiting hours Before being allowed to use a toilet, etc., etc., etc. Here’s an interesting fact that doesn’t make the news There are 400 trillion dollars of wealth on this planet Nine million people die of hunger every year At the height of COVID billionaires’ net worth Increased by 5.2 billion per day while at the same time 24,000 people per day died of hunger Quiet a paradox wouldn’t you say? Ten percent of the world’s population lives in hunger Approximately 828 million, 46 million more than The previous year, in this nation alone forty-four million Why doesn’t the media shine a light on these facts? This information should be startling and embarrassing I ask the question when is enough, enough And I hear the answer in the recesses of my mind – never Has the greatest nation on earth become the most selfish Are we not fed up electing politicians with no moral ethics Fed up with TV evangelists fleecing widows out of their pennies So they can live in mansions and fly jets? I won't apologize if I’ve ruffled some feathers, I hope I have It has become blatantly obvious what’s important in our society And it’s not people, not human beings, it’s money, things, power They matter, you and I don’t, especially if you’re not rich Now, allow me to take a deep breath and suggest this Begin a meditative/contemplative practice, each day Spend a few minutes in silence, stillness, with Presence Presence being your image of a creative Source, God if you prefer The name doesn’t matter, the relationship does Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your truth, they have your answers, they wait

George G
