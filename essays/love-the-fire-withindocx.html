LOVE, THE FIRE WITHIN

By George Gondron

LOVE, THE FIRE WITHIN There is a purifying fire within love Love prevents nothing and creates everything Love does not control Love does not compare Love does not discriminate Love accepts and embraces all Love cannot do otherwise There is no substance to fire It cannot be held It cannot be grasped Just as love Love is not tangible, love is being One does not grasp love One becomes love The substance of love is life itself I know love only through the experience Only through being loved The experience arises in the silence In the stillness With the Presence Embrace the Fire A fire that does not burn A fire that takes us out of mind, into our hearts An undying flame Known by many names But known best as Love

George G
