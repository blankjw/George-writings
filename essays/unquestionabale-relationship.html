UNQUESTIONABALE RELATIONSHIP

By George Gondron

UNQUESTIONABLE RELATIONSHIP We are always in relationship with all that is, forever connected It is only the mind, the ego that separates and divides It is the nature of science to examine ‘parts’ of the whole But as well, there is the saying the whole is greater than the sum Of it parts There is evidence that suggests, not only do our actions have a Direct effect on all that is, but our thoughts as well Lao Tzu: ‘To the mind that is still, the whole universe surrenders.’ Clarity of perception arises from a still mind Something we in the Western world avoid, a quiet mind Those who seek power and control know quite well Their road to success is ‘divide and conquer’ Pit people against each other through lies and half-truths A selfish mind doesn’t have the capability to grasp We are all a part of the greater whole, we are not separate We are all more alike than unalike; or as the founder of Family therapy, Virginia Satir said, ‘We come together in our sameness And we grow together in our differentness.’ Each one is a gift to the other, the same but unique As the saying goes, ‘Beware the wolf in sheep’s clothing’ They are alive and well sowing their seeds of misinformation It is obvious we are a divided nation, thinkers and feelers The rational and irrational, the fact checkers and the Kool-Aid drinkers, the open-minded and the close-minded, The absolvers and the blamers, the learners and the ignorant, The forgiver and the finger pointer, etc., etc., etc. I hope you are one of the former and not the latter I hope you have the courage to question, doubt, reflect, and Critically think, rather than just being a sponge, being spoon-fed Conspiracy theories and deep-state rhetoric are false idols Meant to create division and separation – we are one Our wholeness includes body, mind, heart, soul, and spirit Together, not separate, choose a meditative/contemplative practice Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Empty your ‘monkey mind’, focus on your breath Listen for the whispers of your inner resources, your true self They have your answers, they hold your truth, they wait

George G
