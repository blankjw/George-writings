HOW HAVE YOU CHANGED

By George Gondron

HOW HAVE YOU CHANGED? What is different in your awareness, your consciousness Today, than it was last year or ten years ago Or for that matter even yesterday? With the unlimited resources available to us With the unrestricted possibilities With the immeasurable opportunities For personal growth and conscious change Can you reflect on your life and clearly state I am more today than I was I am more fully human, more whole, more connected I am less biased, prejudiced, and judgmental Or do I still harbor the same thoughts and feelings That constantly repeat themselves in my life Or perhaps are even more constricting today Than they were before Do I seem to be attracted to the negative? Blame and point fingers Always willing to criticize other people’s behavior But never my own Am I always the victim, poor pitiful me? Am I closed or open-minded? Is the glass half empty or half full? And lastly and most importantly Do I have an internal reference, an inner core Or is my life lived from the neck up? All in my mind, my ego, never in my heart All the above really do matter For our thinking, absent any reflective awareness Absent that internal reference, governs our lives Impacts every waking moment I can assure you of this truth If you choose to begin a meditative/contemplative practice Consistently committed to, daily Just a few minutes of silence, stillness, with Presence A time to empty our ever-busy ‘monkey minds’ You will grow in awareness and consciousness You will heal and discover your truth, you will

George G
