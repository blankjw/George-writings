SEVEN DAYS

By George Gondron

WHAT IF? WHAT IF YOU ONLY HAD SEVEN MORE DAYS TO LIVE? WHO WOULD YOU FORGIVE? WHAT HURTS AND DISAPPOINTMENTS WOULD YOU LET GO OF? WHAT MEANINGLESS THOUGHTS WOULD DISAPPEAR? WHAT MOUNTAINS WOULD BECOME MOLE HILLS? WHAT ANXIETIES WOULD BECOME INSIGNIFICANT? WHAT WOULD YOU DO, OR RATHER WHAT WOULD YOU BE? A HUMAN BEING OR A HUMAN DOING? WHAT WOULD REALLY BECOME IMPORTANT? WHAT WOULD REALLY BECOME LOVING AND COMPASSIONATE? FOR YOURSELF AND FOR OTHERS. TAKE THE TIME TO REFLECT ON YOUR NEXT SEVEN DAYS. IF IT WOULD BE DIFFERENT THAN NOW; DO IT. NO MORE – “YES BUT” BE STILL BE

GEORGE G
