THE COWARDICE OD RTHE SUPREME COURT

By George Gondron

THE COWARDICE OF THE SUPREME COURT Three Supreme Cout cases have weakened our Democracy since 2010, Citizens United, Reversal of Roe V. Wade, and Presidential Immunity – all under the control of a Conservative Court Citizens United opened the flood gate of unlimited political contributions resulting in the formation of political PACS In 2010 when the Court rendered their opinion there were 403 billionaires, we now have 801, an increase of 398 in 14 years Guess which political party reduces taxes for the wealthy and large corporations? and guess who gives that party money? The Reversal of Roe V. Wade now takes away the freedom of choice I am not proabortion, but I am prochoice, a woman has a right to choose – there are currently over 400 laws governing a woman’s body There are zero governing a man’s body – could it be men are afraid of women – again a result of a Conservative Court, three of the judges appointed by Donald Trump, all swore to prior precedent under oath And then there’s the case of Presidential Immunity Whatever the President does or does not do in his performance as President (therein lies the fatal error) grants him immunity Thus ego, status, self-image, doth make cowards of us all And the Justices (not all) become untouchable, influenced by money and favors, given and accepted without guilt or shame Since the Reagan Administration the Republican focus in every subsequent victory is cut taxes for the wealthy, individual or corporate, and like magic we now have more millionaires and billionaires and more profitable corporations And the result, the middle class is disappearing and the poor become poorer, while our deficits and debt grow There are none so blind as those who will not see and none so deaf as those who will not hear, there’s a price for being a ‘sheep’ There’s also a price to pay for unconsciously eliminating Government services without the slightest idea of the detrimental results Become consciously involved, don’t be a ‘sheep’, wake up Adopt a meditative/contemplative practice, daily Spend a few minutes in silence, stillness, with Isness Focus on your breath or a brief mantra, breathe in, breathe out When the ego resists, the mind wanders, simply rinse and repeat Listen to the whispers of your inner guides, heart, soul, and spirit

George G
