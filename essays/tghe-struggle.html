TGHE STRUGGLE

By George Gondron

THE STRUGGLE It’s not our successes that bring us to become more fully human That increase our awareness and consciousness That hopefully awaken our own connection to Mystery It is rather our individual daily struggles Our honest attempts to know ourselves on a deeper level Beyond our superficial daily activities that for the most part Are nothing more than repetitive thoughts, feelings, and actions Patterns of behavior that have become our familiars Familiars that when viewed objectively, reveal we are The perpetrators of our own mostly imagined dilemmas Our personal discomfort, except for physical pain, Is mostly self-imposed as a result of our desires and attachments Our thoughts and feelings all of which are personal choices That last statement is the ‘biggy’ The ego refuses to acknowledge that ‘it’ is its own worst enemy So much easier to blame and defend than accept and change Yes, our struggles can be the deliverance from our ‘false self’ To our ‘true self’, our higher self, our more fully human self But it seems the ego would rather struggle than transform For after all if we transform, we must then take responsibility For our own choices, our own thoughts, feelings, and actions It’s no longer the fault of he, she, or them, it’s me, it’s you The task of understanding, of clear perception, is not an easy one It is part of the struggle But if we have eyes to see, ears to hear, it will happen To abandon the struggles is give in to weakness, to ego To strive for success is to gain strength and connect to our Inner core, our internal resources, our true self Don’t avoid the struggle the reward is too great Don’t be like most, take a pill, find the next shiny object, distract You are special, you are unique, you are a gift to humanity Take a little time each day for silence, stillness, and Isness Focus on your breath or a short mantra, breathe in, breathe out Rinse and repeat, you and I are stardust, the DNA of the universe Listen to your inner guides, the whispers of heart, soul, and spirit They have your answers, they hold your truth, they patiently wait Embrace the struggle, cherish the journey, you will find success

George G
