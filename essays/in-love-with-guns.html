IN LOVE WITH GUNS

By George Gondron | Source: IN LOVE WITH GUNS.docx

IN LOVE WITH GUNS There's no other way to say what's obvious And the vast majority of these gun lovers are male There are as many reasons given by these possessors Of all these guns as to why they own them As there are possessors The majority of which make no sense whatsoever When you attempt to have a rational discussion About their 'gun addiction' it soon goes 'south' That's a colloquialism for you might as well be talking to a wall Even when you dissect their rationale with objective facts, it doesn't get beyond the kid in the sandbox They'll just take their ball (gun) and go home Of all the major industrialized nations on this planet Ours far, far exceeds all forms of gun violence elsewhere Far, far exceeds There is a term for being unwilling to even discuss any measure of realistic gun legislation Yours may be a little kinder than mine But for me, it's nothing short of selfishness, male ego Addiction, fear, machoism, 'my way or the highway' If a person uses a gun to hunt and feed their family Well and good, but with an AR-15 and a 30 round clip? Really? How about a fishing pole to catch food I don't recall the last time someone entered an elementary school and killed children with a pole How many are gonna die needlessly before change will happen, how many more Maybe one day we won't have to ask that question Maybe

George G
