THE SECRET

By George Gondron

THE SECRET We all want to matter, we all want to belong To think or say otherwise is nothing more than A lie we tell ourselves That's the secret And we fill our lives with distractions Positive and negative True or false In order to live with our secret In order to survive Those who take their own lives do so for one reason They are unable to reveal their secret Their need to belong, to matter, to be accepted Here's the dilemma, the mystery, the tragedy We learn we're not acceptable, we don't belong From others just like us Who have the same hopes, same desires, same wishes So, who sets the standards for who's in and who's out We do, all of us That is the danger of a 'right/wrong' mentality Of a dualistic or fundamentalist mind A mind that cannot hold an either/or position A mind who believes the narrative of any 'ism' Be it patriotism, nationalism, tribalism Communism, Nazism, socialism, or the 'ism' we call Religiosity All of these don't have consciousness Only the individual has consciousness Yet we follow them blindly Yes, we decide who belongs and who's acceptable To the child, color doesn't matter, gender doesn't matter Ethnicity doesn't matter, religion doesn't matter Education doesn't matter, wealth or poverty don't matter They only matter to conditioned, programmed, manipulated Indoctrinated and propagandized adults, like us, all of us We must matter to ourselves first, from the inside out Through the innate knowing of our hearts, souls, and spirits We all belong, we all matter, we are gifts to each other

George G
