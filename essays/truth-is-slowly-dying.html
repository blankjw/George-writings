TRUTH IS SLOWLY DYING

By George Gondron

TRUTH IS SLOWLY DYING As difficult as it may be to believe, there are those today Who believe the earth is flat, the Holocaust is not true We never sent men to the moon, science is not real The sun revolves around the earth, The earth is only 6,000 years old Slavery was a blessing for Negros Being gay or lesbian is a choice It’s no wonder these realities exist The illiteracy rate in the US exceeds 20% Over 54% of adults have literacy below 6th-grade level It is no wonder that the vast majority of wealth Is held in the hands of the educated, the privileged The wealthy create a false narrative in order to Maintain their control, manipulation works If you can appeal to a person’s emotions You win the battle of control, of dualistic thinking Ignorance is the enemy, not stupidity In a culture of ignorance, truth does not matter Where excessive wealth, power, and control exist There will always be division, us against them In addition, the basic constructs of the Christian religion Also have failed Replaced by dogma, doctrine, rites, and rituals The concepts of kindness, compassion, empathy, and love Of inclusion and human connection without exception Are also missing outside the believer’s immediate context In other words, no connection between head and heart It boils down to worship versus relationship Between religion and spirituality Truth is dying, ignorance and alternative facts are rising For your benefit, the benefit of humanity Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Through a meditative/contemplative practice Awaken your innate inner reference given at birth Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your truth, your true self, not just the ego self They bring balance inner and outer, they patiently wait

George G
