IF

By George Gondron

WHAT IF Just two small letters with amazing consequences What if A child could pursue their natural innate talents Imagine what the world might become If they could chase their dreams, reach their goals Before being categorized, labeled, to fit the 'norm' Forcing round pegs into square holes, and vice-versa What if We approached disease from a position of prevention Rather than a situation to be cured Where health care was more of a prerequisite Than an afterthought after the fact If we could get dollars out of the system What if There was equality in the job market Where people were more important than the bottom line Benefits were for all, not just those at the top Working together in collaboration Growing together toward a common goal What if Politicians really cared about the people People they supposedly represent Instead of the lobbyists who line their pockets Representing the corporate entities Who are the real lawmakers What if Religion, all religions followed the precepts that it professes Using the teachings of their original founders Rather than pointing fingers, blaming, and excluding Clinging to dogma, rules, and doctrines that leave one cold And eventually, only bring death to the soul What if Love was real and truly unselfish, unconditional Not just for those of my choosing But for all, all life everywhere I'll end with that question, only you can answer What if

George G
