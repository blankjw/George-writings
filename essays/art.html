ART

By George Gondron

ART Without art, we are lost Art has the power to express what words cannot Revealing the very essence of that which it attempts to portray If we but have the eyes, the heart, the soul, and the spirit to see A picture is worth a thousand words A piece of art is beyond all worth From the labor of the artist’s hands From the imagination of the artist’s mind From the very essence of the artist’s psyche Emerges a one-of-a-kind creation, never to be duplicated Only to be admired, embraced, taken in with all the senses Having the power to touch each single individual differently The question asked, ‘what do you experience?’ Is much more important than what you do you see? As you gaze upon a unique gift of creative ability The medium used for the creation is special to the artist Wood, metal, paint, clay, glass, stone, anything from nature Has its own purpose and form in the hands of the artist The image is already there It only needs to be revealed Let us not forget the dancers, singers, and musicians The actors, poets, and authors They also have the gift to touch our souls and lift our spirits We all have within us an artist, a creative energy Discover yours, and share it with the world, you are needed Support the humanities on every level From pre-K to graduate levels, they keep our history alive The survival of art, in all its many forms In all its possible forms is necessary for humanity And the planet to survive in a meaningful way As individuals, we too need change our perspective Our lives are almost entirely externally focused With no inner core or internal reference Choose to spend a few minutes each day in reflection Empty your mind and experience the silence, the stillness, With Presence – listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your truth, your true self – they patiently wait

George G
