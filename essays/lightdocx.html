LIGHT

By George Gondron

LIGHT

THAT WHICH MAKES VISION POSSIBLE – LIGHT CAN DARKNESS ALSO MAKE VISION POSSIBLE? WHEN I WAKE IN THE NIGHT WITH PIECES OF THE DREAM IN MY MEMORY DO I REFLECT ON THOSE PIECES, OR FALL BACK ASLEEP? WHEN I’M IN THE DARKNESS OF MY OWN DESPAIR, CAN I FIND ANY LIGHT? THERE IS A MESSAGE IN EVERY EXPERIENCE, ESPECIALLY THE ONES THAT SEEM TO HAVE NO MESSAGE AT ALL – THE ONES THAT CARRY PAIN LET YOURSELF SIT IN THE DARKNESS, IN THE DEPTHS OF STILLNESS, NOT GRASPING AT THE FEELINGS, JUST OBSERVING THEM WE ALL AT SOME TIME ARE ANOTHER, WILL HAVE A DARK NIGHT OF THE SOUL IT WILL BE A GIFT, IF WE CAN BUT BEFRIEND IT THE SHADOW NEEDS TO BE INTEGRATED, AND NOT RUN FROM BE KIND TO YOUR SHADOW, ACCEPTING OF YOUR FEAR, AND GENTLE WITH YOUR DOUBTS OUT OF THE DEPTH OF THE DARKNESS, THE CALM OF THE STILLNESS COMES THE HEALING OF THE SOUL BE STILL BE

GEORGE G
