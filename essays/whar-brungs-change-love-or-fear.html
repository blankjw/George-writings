WHAR BRUNGS CHANGE LOVE OR FEAR

By George Gondron

WHAT BRINGS CHANGE LOVE OR FEAR? You might want to reflect on that question Before you read any further In light of humanity as a whole Most people live in fear rather than love Including some within their immediate families A stark reality that we seem to ignore or not even allow To enter our consciousness, for, after all, we can’t fix it Whether we realize it or not, we make meaning Out of every thought, feeling, and action we have or do Consciously or unconsciously, meaning is always present Love is about being positive, fear is about being negative Which do you think is more rewarding, is healthier? For some, the question is not a fair one, for in truth Their entire lives have been about fear And for many others, the love they experienced was conditional They were loved when or if they ‘fell in line’ or Met the proper requirements deserving of love Without exception, genuine love is unconditional And unconditional love has the power to change So, for ourselves, we must love ourselves unconditionally Warts and all, our past and our present Forgiving ourselves for what we didn’t know at the time For being selfish versus selfless, beginning with ‘baby steps’ Rome was not built in a day and we will not change overnight All progress begins with the first step, take that step Life is not about dualism, black and white It is mostly gray, both/and, not either/or Most actions are situational, never always right or wrong Bring balance into your life, connect inner and outer Engage a meditative/contemplative practice each day For a few minutes experience silence, stillness, Presence We in the West are too polarized, dead from the neck down When asked ‘how do you feel?’, most respond with a thought So if you can replace ‘I feel’ with ‘I think’, you’re not feeling Empty your mind, focus on your breath, in and out Listen for the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your truth, your answers, your true self, they wait

George G
