VALUE VS QUALITY

By George Gondron

VALUE VS QUALITY Sadly, value does not equate to quality All life, all lives have value Through the proof that we exist, we are valuable A believer in a Creator, values existence All philosophies embrace this belief Our existence does not guarantee our quality of life We are relational, dependent beings Dependent upon one another How is it possible to insulate ourselves from the needs of the many, of other lives? Is it someone else's problem, not my concern? Do we fault them for being born into their situation? Does that excuse me from my connection to all of life? How can I profess to believe in a Supreme Being and not be concerned? How can I shut my eyes to the inequities, feel comfortable in my lack of need? I can't If I do, I'm a no more than a hypocrite We all have value, but millions do not even have the basic needs of life, very little quality Only an increase in consciousness, an increase in awareness can change the inequities Only a change of heart

George G
