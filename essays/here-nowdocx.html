HERE, NOW

By George Gondron

HERE, NOW How very much we miss in the here, now Thinking backward, deliberating forward The present unnoticed Is it possible to change yesterday? Can I predict tomorrow? If only If only, nothing more than a waste of the now If only, the ego's ploy As I 'if only', I miss what is The gifts of the present moment, unopened New awareness, increased consciousness manifest in the now Not yesterday, nor tomorrow, but now Living in the past, the hamster on the wheel Unsure of the future, disguised fear The greatest part of life goes unnoticed What I need is here, now What I seek lives only in the moment What I search for dwells ever in the present A whisper from the corner of my mind 'I Am" Not, I was, or I will be Only, 'I Am"

George G
