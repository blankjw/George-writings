MIND AND MATTER

By George Gondron

MIND AND MATTER Both of one reality best described as Mystery A belief that all of nature, the entire universe is conscious A hypothesis put forth by quantum physics that in a significant Way is more spiritual than that of most organized religion Buddhism excluded since Buddhism is not a religion Would that we all could give credence to that notion That everything, micro and macro has a degree of consciousness What a different world we would have Science can describe what matter does, but yet cannot define How matter becomes manifest, how does an electron become An electron, or a proton, neutron, quartz, etc., etc., etc. Evolution is the continual process of positive progressive change Mystery is always pulling us forward regardless the stubbornness Of the egoic mind, the selfishness of human behavior To become aware is to ‘awake’, clearly perceive, understand A defensive nature is the enemy of being aware, it has an agenda A selfish mind cares only about itself with feigned concern for others Everything we as human beings need has been provided It exists in the form of matter available in nature The fact that not all are able to participate in what nature provides Speaks to the lack of awareness and selfishness of the human mind There is no other reason, name me one Division, separation, exclusion, discrimination, bigotry, prejudice, Racism, homophobia, xenophobia, sexism, etc., etc., etc. All lie within the recesses of a selfish mind and a cold heart Lie within a mentality of ‘I’ve got mine and I want yours as well’ Aware or unaware, consciousness is the great ‘connector’ It levels the playing field for those who can see When we cease to learn, to grow, to become more, to wake up We are the problem, we are the ‘accusers’ and the uninformed But we never see ourselves as the issue, it’s always others And regardless the arena, blame is the name of the game If you’ve read this far, choose a meditative/contemplative practice Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Mystery Choose to become more fully human, more inclusive Empty your mind, listen to your inner guides, heart, soul, spirit They hold your true self, have your answers, they patiently wait

George G
