UNLIMITED DIVISION

By George Gondron

UNLIMITED DIVISION We are, without a doubt, a nation divided In our politics, our religions, our economics, our education The divisions are limitless In our neighborhoods, small communities, cities, and states Even within our own families We no longer talk with one another, we talk at one another And neither party really listens to the other Witness the chaos in the Republican Congress at this moment Rendering our government totally helpless to operate At a time when action is needed, partisan action The demagogues are winning, spreading their vitriol Rendering the ordinary citizen swimming in conspiracy theories Not a good time for our experiment in democracy Not a good time for justice and equality for all Not a good example for the young minds who witness Witness the dysfunction that has become so obvious It can’t be denied any longer, nor can it be defended There is a saying, ‘The Emperor has no clothes’ Used to describe a situation in which the general population Is unwilling to recognize or admit the obvious shortcomings Issues, or circumstances facing it in the moment We have reached that moment, it is very clear When voting rights are being circumvented and manipulated When alternative facts have become the norm When the lie is now the truth When minds are more closed than open When people accept willingly what normally is questioned When 20% of adults are illiterate And 54% have a literacy level of 6th grade or less At what point did we cease to think critically, to question, To doubt, to do our own research, to learn? We truly are, ‘our own worst enemy’ Challenge yourself, begin a meditative/contemplative practice Each day experience a few minutes of silence, stillness, In the presence of Presence, your image of a Creator Empty your mind and listen to your heart, soul, and spirit Reconnect to your true self, the truth sayer inside you, please

George G
