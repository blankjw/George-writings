MAY YOU

By George Gondron

MAY YOU What do you wish for others, hope for others All of life's experiences are teachers There is no life without pain, pain is a teacher In fact, some of life's most important lessons Are as a result of pain, emotional pain There is no life without failure, failure teaches Anything worth doing is worth doing wrong the first time Without failure we have little appreciation for success There is no life without loss Loss of relationships, loss of dreams, loss of control Loss teaches us to value and cherish what we have Experiences we have that we don't learn from Will be repeated, at some time And often, over, and over, and over again Nearly all of our grievous experiences are about relationships The more invested we were, the longer it takes to heal And some carry the pain to their grave, unable to forgive Unable to forget, punishing themselves needlessly Any experience, repeat, any experience We don't learn from, or we can't let go of Has a certain amount of control over us, always So, my hope for us all is to learn from our experiences Have enough joys in your life, to overcome your sorrows Enough hope to overcome your despair Enough kindness to forgive yourself, as well as others Enough patience to wait out the storms that will come May you look for the silver lining in every cloud May you never refuse to stop growing, to stop learning Remain humble in your successes Tolerant of your failures And tolerant of others in theirs May you find your unique gift and share it with others May you become aware of your many blessing May you find the peace within which is always present May you fall in love with your God who is closer to you Than you are to yourself A God that does love, unconditionally

George G
