DEMOCRACY

By George Gondron

DEMOCRACY The survival of democracy is grounded in the rule of law When the Constitution was written its premise was against Authoritative rule but based on freedom and equality When the law is not applied equally then neither is freedom Nor equality and democracy eventually fails History is very clear when the law fails, the results are Detrimental to the ‘have-nots’ and promising the ‘haves’ Beneficial to the wealthy and powerful Punishing to all others A few winners and many losers The protection and enforcement of the law begins with the Right to vote, electing officials who promise to uphold the law In our nation’s case, the upholding of the Constitution, The Bill of Rights and the following amendments Along with all laws promulgated by our government In recent decades we witness the failure of the law to be Administered equally to all, without exception Wealth, power, greed, and control have supplanted Equality under the law More and more elected officials on every level have become Untouchable Clearly demonstrating the failure of the law And subsequently, it follows, the failure of democracy We are, in every way, a nation divided Cooperation, conciliation, compromise, and bipartisanship Have all but vanished in the political environment They are willing to forsake the ideals upon which our nation Was founded for the sake of ‘political party’ For the sake of their own power and greed It has become too obvious and plain to witness A nation that was founded on a bright future Has grown very dark indeed, we are repeating history Our choices, change ourselves as individuals Nurture and find our inner core, our internal reference Choose a few minutes of silence, and stillness, with Presence Each day - listen to the whispers of our hearts, soul, and spirit And vote, in every election, in all elections, always

George G
