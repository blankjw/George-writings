TRIALS

By George Gondron

TRIALS Quite simply, life without trials is not life It is in the struggle that life emerges Just as the caterpillar struggles to leave the cocoon Its life is beauty as a result of the trial And what is the greatest of our trials? It is that of relationship There is nothing more tumultuous Yet nothing more satisfying As personal relationship It is a trial in which we play all the roles Judge, jury, prosecutor and advocate We sentence, decide, blame and support Sentencing unfairly Deciding without evidence Blaming through our bias Supporting through our ignorance Both ourselves and others The successful trial, results in the end as Clarity of perception and purity of thought Accuracy of response and exactness of reply Emerging from the silence and stillness within Trials engaged in conscious awareness Are more than worth the bumps in the road Awake, not asleep to the meaning of life A prayer of thanks for the gift of the trials A gratitude for hard won lasting relationships A knowing beyond the smallness of mind And behind it all there is only one truth It begins and ends with only one purpose The truth is Love, the purpose is Love It is always, always, always, Love

George G
