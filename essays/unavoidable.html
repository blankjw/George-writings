UNAVOIDABLE

By George Gondron

UNAVOIDABLE If we are in touch with our humanity we will Experience emotional suffering, it’s inevitable The problem is not the experience it’s how we deal with it Rather than understanding everything we experience Is a teacher, even emotional pain, we will do whatever is Necessary to not learn the lesson, to deny the teaching Medication, distractions, all the psychological defense mechanism The next shiny object, anything and everything that is shouting Own your pain, it’s telling you something about yourself But as we all know, the ‘go to’ reaction to almost all emotional Pain, is blame – we live in a blaming culture without a doubt Few there are that take ownership for what they may Think, feel, do or do not do – it’s not my fault It’s always him, her, or them who are at fault Here is a truth, blame never leads to a positive end Every negative emotion can be connected to some form of fear And as well every positive emotion is connected to love If it’s not obvious that our society is fear-based We are blind, we are asleep, we are a part of the problem Everything on social media directly or indirectly is associated with Two realities; fear and consumption, point fingers spend money With the current mindset of those in power, political and religious There is no chance of having a citizenry that is connected and at peace We are at extremes in both politics and religion without a doubt Individuals in our culture are relegated to functions of production And consumption – human doings not human beings We are nothing more than a means to an end when capitalism Reaches an extreme form of economics, the purpose of which is Profit, power, performance, and partisanship When a few have all the wealth, they control those that don’t Always wanting more at the expense of those who have little Please, embrace a meditative/contemplative practice Spend a few minutes each day in silence, be still, be present Learn the lessons of your emotional teachers, they are important Empty your mind of its incessant and repetitive thoughts Focus on a short mantra, breathe in, breathe out Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit, they wait

George G
