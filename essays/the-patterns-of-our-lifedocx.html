THE PATTERNS OF OUR LIFE

By George Gondron

THE PATTERNS OF OUR LIFE Once we establish our routines, our habits, our standards, our Practices, they become the patterns of our daily lives Our accustomed ways of thinking, feeling, and acting Become our familiars that are so ingrained that they operate Spontaneously, or one could say unconsciously without any Significant degree of reflection at all, ‘knee-jerk’ reactions As a result, this patterned behavior becomes our defense Against our own critical thinking, we no longer question or doubt Our own decisions or behavior, our patterns won’t allow it We forget the importance of the dynamic that states for every cause There is/are resulting effects, whether from thoughts, feelings or Actions, assumptions alone are not a valid determination of the Effects of any cause, they are the least likely to have any factual basis It is also obvious that most social media is about opinions and Assumptions, not factual information at all, but ‘alternative facts’ A lie by any other name is still a lie And if we believe the ‘liar’, the fault lies with each of us, not the liar For if we really were interested in the truth, we’d find it Our patterns have a way of strengthening our resistance to change For the ego does not like change, change is the enemy Here is the dichotomy, without an open mind the success Of an increase in awareness and consciousness is almost Nonexistent or impossible, it’s ‘my way or the highway’ Education in conjunction with personal experience and The willingness to understand our own psychological and Emotional pain is the journey of ‘becoming’, of being Fully human and fully alive, operating with all the innate Gifts we are given with our unique lives; mind, body, heart, Soul, and spirit working together in harmony We are not a reflective culture, our minds constantly filled With incessant and repetitive thoughts, we have no balance Add a meditative/contemplative practice to your patterns Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Focus on your breath or a short mantra, breathe in, breathe out The ego will resist the mind will wander, simple rinse and repeat Be consistent, practice is the key, committed practice Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit, they wait

George G
