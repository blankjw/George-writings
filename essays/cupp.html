CUPP

By George Gondron

CUPP Acronym – Comprehensive, utilization, potential, and Possibility - CUPP All four rely on critical thinking and reflection The necessity of a curious mind versus blind acceptance Reliable and factual Information in this modern age of social media is more the exception than the rule Almost all of social media content is meant to stir the emotions rather than challenge the intellect And usually those emotions revolve around fear, desire and attachment, selling us goods and services along with the latest conspiracy theories, all meant to distract To comprehend the possible, to recognize potential, and then to put it into practice, to utilize the results is the goal Kness-jerk reactions have no substantive merit Their only ‘claim to fame’ is to keep us stuck in our familiars, our habitual patterns, our routines that never change A closed mind uses emotional content for its defense not intelligence, a closed mind is incapable of critical thinking, they already have the answers to every imaginable problem, especially if it’s counter to their unfounded beliefs They are followers, not leaders, followers of what attracts their emotions and their arguments are almost always comparative, not about the real immediate problems The path of genuine education, of historical reality is not relying on social media or ‘the bully pulpit’ for truth The search for truth requires critical thinking, reflection, doubt, questions, and diligent research, not mere acceptance There is more truth to the phrase ‘trust but verify’ than meets the comprehension of ‘chosen ignorance’ Adopt a meditative/contemplative practice to awaken your own critical thinking, your innate discernment Take a few minutes each day for silence, stillness, and Isness Listen for the whispers of your inner guides, heart, soul, spirit

George G
