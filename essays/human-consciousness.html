HUMAN CONSCIOUSNESS

By George Gondron

HUMAN CONSCIOUSNESS A theory exists in quantum physics that everything has consciousness including the entire universe, micro to macro But until it is proven to be otherwise, only human consciousness has the ability to choose, has free will A rock cannot become a tree, or a tree become a raindrop Their purpose is established without choice, it has been established in their origin, in their beginning They will go through many changes, but not of their choosing So, what makes us humans choose that which is obviously harmful to our nature as opposed to that which is promising or beneficial? Simple answer, the three-letter word, ego, our minds When our minds are disconnected from our internal resources, when our thinking is polarized and dualistic, when life is either/or and never both/and we become the enemy we oppose And in addition to the aforementioned, we refuse to educate ourselves, refuse to do that which would bring greater consciousness Each new level of consciousness broadens and expands one’s interest and concern for that which is beyond themselves For as the potential of consciousness spirals upward so too our potential to reach non-duality as well The level of consciousness that knows life is inclusive not exclusive, is connected not separate A level of consciousness that embraces choice and free will responsibly without the necessity to blame and defend To become one with is vastly different than becoming one apart from The drive of the human psyche is to be connected, to be a part of the whole but we insist on being separate, which is really selfishness It’s surprising how so many “Christians’ rant and rave about undocumented immigrants, forgetting Joseph, Mary, and Jesus were refugees fleeing to Egypt for their survival Please, continue to educate yourself, expand your consciousness Become more aware and understanding, you are needed Adopt a meditative/contemplative practice Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Focus on your breath or a short mantra, heed your inner guides Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, have your answers, they patiently wait

George G
