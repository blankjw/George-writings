THE FIRST STEP

By George Gondron | Source: THE FIRST STEP.docx

THE FIRST STEP The first step toward solving a problem is to acknowledge there is a problem For many reasons we, in this nation, are unwilling to acknowledge there is a problem, acknowledge in an open honest manner The common narrative is to use defensive rhetoric to explain every and all disagreements, a rat chasing its tail Blame never works, let me repeat, blame never works The result is deeper divides and more closed minds, no solutions A mentality of ‘my way, or the highway’, how’s that working for us? There is nothing more dangerous to humanity than choosing ignorance and operating with closed minds, nothing Not only do we ‘thumb our noses’ at critical thinking and continued education, but we embrace our emotions over our intellect, limited though it may be The brain is like any other muscle in the body If unused it begins to atrophy and becomes useless We have been lulled into a stupor by using social media as our form of viable and accurate information The results are very apparent as our divides grow ever deeper And our differences ever more pronounced, none of which have any substantial support for their unfounded beliefs When we limit our experiences we also limit our understanding of truth, of facts, ‘we cannot see the forest for the trees’ The phrase ‘selective hearing’ has been used by all of us at one time or another, but currently it has become the norm for millions of people and if that doesn’t change we will never objectively identify the most important problems and consequently we as well will never solve them, we will continue to part of the problem and not part of the solution, I choose to listen, I hope you do as well To maintain balance in our lives we must connect inner and outer We must use the innate creativity given us at birth, our gifts Adopt a meditative/contemplative practice for balance Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Focus on your breath or a short mantra, breathe in, breathe out When the mind wanders, the ego resists, rinse and repeat Listen for the whispers of your inner guides, heart, soul, spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, they wait

George G
