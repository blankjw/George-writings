THE PATH OF LEAST RESISTANCE

By George Gondron

THE PATH OF LEAST RESISTANCE We humans, it seems, choose more often than not The safer, easier, more familiar path Stated another way The path that will never change us The path that will never challenge us The path of least resistance We are never able to quite grasp the fact That what we struggle against In our personal journey Is exactly what we need To become more fully human To become more fully alive We offer advice We criticize We attempt to change Everyone and everything except ourselves We have all the answers for the ills of the world While we fail to examine our own character It is so much easier to point a finger at others But never at ourselves It is not the world or others that need to change If we are honest, it is ourselves The only person I can control is me, not you Yet we spend a lot of wasted time and energy Attempting to control others to no avail There is a saying which defines insanity perfectly Doing the same things over and over Expecting to get different results, insane There is no easy, safe, or comfortable path Toward becoming a kind, compassionate, loving, Human being There is only the struggle It is our failures that lead to our successes It is our willingness to risk and be vulnerable That expands our awareness and consciousness Choose the path that manifests your creativity Avoid the path of least resistance, it’s a dead-end

George G
