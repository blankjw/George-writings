TO BELIEVE IS NOT TO KNOW

By George Gondron

TO BELIEVE IS NOT TO KNOW We, humans, will literally destroy relationships For what we believe yet do not know We know in one way only Through personal experience Experience discovered through our five senses Thoughts and feelings are a way of knowing after the fact After a direct experience I only know a person is racist, a bigot, a misogynist, Selfish, a liar, an abuser, a thief, a hypocrite, etc., etc., etc. As a result of personal experience From what I witnessed, not third-party information Most of what we say we know, especially about others Is not through personal experience, not even close It is through the judgment, opinions, half-truths, and lies Of others, regardless of the reasons The common parlance is called ‘gossip’ The primary function of which is to feel good about myself But the result is always damaging, always If we need to feel good about ourselves by putting others down The problem lies within us, not the other/others Projection is a psychological defense mechanism Used to criticize others for what we fail to see in ourselves And is used quite regularly We all have within us the best and the worst of human behavior ‘There but for the grace of God, go I’ Our weaknesses are nothing more than our lack of awareness Our ignorance, to put it bluntly Blame and denial are so much easier than self-actualization In our current societal development, we lack the evidence Of an inner core an internal reference We live in the mind, the ego, the intellect And unfortunately, intellect and intelligence are not the same We lack balance – inner and outer Spend a few minutes each day in a meditative/contemplative practice Empty the mind – be present to silence, stillness, and Presence Presence being your image of a creative Source, God if you may Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit, they wait

George G
