DISGUISES

By George Gondron

DISGUISES Condemnation disguised as righteousness Judgment disguised as understanding The ultra conservative with concern for only their beliefs and security The radical liberal denouncing disparity between wealth and poverty Complainers both, neither with a viable solution Protectors of their separate playgrounds As they spew their rhetoric at one another Those without are caught in the middle You want to complain, no problem Have a viable solution, for all Those in poverty, those without health care Those without means to provide their children with a decent education Those without enough to have a retirement plan Sorry, the answer from the right is not, well I got mine, let them get theirs From the left, it is not, take from the rich and give to the poor As long as the underlying problems are greed, control, lack of compassion, and the more subtle disguises Nothing changes, all remains the same Only another repeated verse, dysfunction continues In all the major religions there are three teachers Those that comfort the disturbed Those that disturb the comfortable Those that do both The first two, illusory disguises resulting in wealth to the teacher The third, the truth speaker, resulting in unity, in harmony, in compassion, for all Can we still hear the truth? Can we still hear the truth speaker? Can we? I sometimes wonder

George G
