LETTER TO HEATHER FRAZIER MAY 19 2016

By George Gondron

Melanie shared with me that you would like to speak with me or for me to email you to resolve this issue. The bottom line for me is the following: -Riley is not currently enrolled at the studio and has not been so since January. She has been back for four finale rehearsals, which is not considered a registered student. -The complimentary tickets that I give out are for registered families only. -It is stated in the studio policy that the recital deposit is non-refundable. -Melanie at first thought that you had a $12.00 credit on your account, however, there is a $58.00 costume balance. I have never allowed someone who is not a registered student to participate in recital before and after this experience, I will not in the future. I was doing Heather a favor allowing Riley to participate out of kindness. If you are not bringing Riley to the finale rehearsal this Sunday, please email me. If I do not hear from you I will assume she is not coming to recital and will re-choreograph as needed.

Respectfully, Ginger Gondron
