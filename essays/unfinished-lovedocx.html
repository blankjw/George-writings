UNFINISHED LOVE

By George Gondron

UNFINISHED LOVE She is the breath of my existence The steady beating of my heart The mirror of my soul, truly an eternal soul mate Connected in the past, perhaps eons past Connected in the present Connected forever An unfinished love, not quite found, but never lost Slipping into my thoughts, now and again Lingering memories in the recesses of my mind Moments of 'what if' played over the years But, 'what if's' can't redeem the past It is telling, as those years stretch out in time A reminder of the conditions we place on our connections Conditions that make no sense to us now Perhaps it was the immaturity of youth Or the fear of risk, or being vulnerable And maybe, it needs remain a mystery Unable to be explained, no answers needed An event meant to mature in this time For it is the now that matters Only the now is important Important not as a clinging, not with expectations But to be present to what has been found, that once was lost It is a feeling that is beyond all words Wordless, only known in the experience, in the living An aliveness that heightens the senses That affirms the essence of life Life in its relatedness to all of creation I hear her voice, and laugh with her laughter We share our stories and deepen our connection Our spirits are joined, she lives in my heart An unfinished love Now has been found And will never be lost For soul mates last forever She is a piece of my heart, a part of my soul, a light in my spirit She is my love

George G
