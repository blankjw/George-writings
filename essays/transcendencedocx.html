TRANSCENDENCE

By George Gondron

TRANSCENDENCE There is more than enough evidence throughout history to support the transcendent nature of Immanence through the yearning of the human psyche Up until recently, the problem has been an insistence on the separation of matter and spirit, but quantum physics, more than organized religion hypothesizes that the two are one, inseparable The work, study, and writing of two 2oth century men, Carl Jung and Teilhard de Chardin Jung, a famous Swiss psychiatrist and founder of analytical psychology and Chardin, a French Jesuit priest who was a paleontologist and archaeologist, both write about a matter/spirit reality It is paradoxical that quantum physics is more spiritual than religion But it’s also very understandable when the individual human is at odds within him/herself, relegating Immanence, Mystery, or God to an intellectual arena Contextual change allows for new experiences, refusing to add context that would elicit spiritual experiences is a form of ignorance The best definition of life is relationship, we are by nature relational beings, we don’t exist in a vacuum That relationship is not limited to just the material, the physical It also includes the spiritual All personal truth is beyond belief, it is experiential God, Mystery, Immanence doesn’t exist because of your belief or non-belief, only through your lived experience In our Western culture we have lost the connection to our inner core, to our internal resources, no longer connecting inner and outer Few of our beliefs are our experiences, they are given to us, we are followers, easily influenced by hyperbole, ‘gas-lighting’, propaganda Ignorance is the result of a lack of education, a willingness to accept without proof alternative facts, without critical thinking As Cark Jung said, ‘thinking is difficult, that’s why most people judge’ Please, add a meditative/contemplative practice to your life A new context, a few minutes of silence, stillness, and Presence daily Focus on your breath or a brief mantra, breathe in, breathe out Listen for the whispers of your inner guides, heart, soul, spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, they wait Discern, discover, embrace Mystery in your own life

George G
