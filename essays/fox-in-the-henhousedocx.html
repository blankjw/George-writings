FOX IN THE HENHOUSE

By George Gondron

FOX IN THE HENHOUSE When the fox oversees the henhouse, when the leader of the group has a vested interest, and when the lie becomes the truth, how do you imagine the story will end? What will be the results, who will suffer, and who will benefit? When those with power and control exhibit through their actions little or no concern for the consequences of their decisions what happens to those without power or control? What does it take for the mind to clearly perceive what is happening right before its eyes, to recognize there is no factual basis for the endless hyperbole, ‘gaslighting’, and propaganda proposed by those with all the wealth, power, and control We currently are witnessing, daily, the abandonment of the rule of law and the trampling of our Constitution and our rights therein as our democracy is swiftly turning into an oligarchy How is it possible that millions of people have been convinced that a consummate and habitual liar is the answer to their problems? That a man with 26 failed businesses, six bankruptcies, who stole from his own charity, an obvious racist and misogynist, married three times and unfaithful to each wife, paid ‘hush money’ to a porn star and a ‘Playboy Bunny’ is now our President The Wizard of Oz hiding behind a curtain, pulling levers and springs for his personal benefit and to our detriment Are we that ignorant, that illiterate, that uneducated Afterall, he said quiet often, ‘I love the poorly educated’ And it seems, we’re proven him correct, but maybe not all Our lack of awareness, comprehension, and consciousness is the result of what many now clearly perceive as a danger to our nation Where is the tipping point, the straw that breaks the camel’s back, rock bottom, or when will we ‘stop waiting in the wings’? Please become involved, educate yourself, use critical thinking Research, question, doubt, study, don’t just be a ‘sheep’ Adopt a daily meditative/contemplative practice Spend a few minutes in silence, stillness, with Presence, or your image of God, focus on your breath or a short mantra Breathe in, breathe out, when the ego resists simple rinse and repeat Listen to the whispers of your inner guides, heat, soul, spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, they wait

Geore G
