THE DEATH OF REASON

By George Gondron

THE DEATH OF REASON A line has been drawn in the sand Without the use of reason, unconsciously, unaware The specter of 'difference' has risen its' ugly head And thrives as never before, is alive and doing very well The unreflective mind gains more and more power Tell us who the 'bad' people are There's no need to question your motives If you're able to stir-up our emotions, we'll believe you If you're able to convince us through your meaningless rhetoric Through the so obvious overstated black and white wordiness That others are to blame for our condition We praise you, we accept you, we glorify you For we have lost the ability to reason We no longer really care what's true, what's false What's fact, what's fiction, what's a lie or fabrication We breath in your propaganda, spew out your venom Followers of the lies and half-truths Believers that we are right, and all others are wrong Yes, you play on our emotions, for we have no reason You play on our lack of logic, and critical thinking You have become our Goebbels, our Jim Jones, our dark savior Your success is driven through our own differences, not our sameness You use fear to motivate, anger to fuel the flames Of difference, of discrimination, of bigotry, and separateness You are the 'master' of stimulating the collective shadow Of 'stirring the pot' of dissension and blame Of supporting divisiveness, an 'us against them' mentality Your only visible deemed success, your inherited wealth Your supposed 'business acumen' of 'sticking it to others' Just the right message we wish to send to the world Just the right model we wish our youth to learn But behind the bluster and braggadocio lives an unconscious fool With no clue of who he really is, blinded by his ego The perfect manifestation of an obvious pure narcissist But for those who use reason, who use critical thinking He is the 'class clown', empty of real substance Let us hope reason has not died, for fear that we die with it

George G
