RADICALIZED CHRISTIANITY

By George Gondron

RADICALIZED CHRISTIANITY Organized religion has become as polarized as politics Christianity in particular has morphed into a faith of fear As it pushes its agenda into politics It has, in many instances, become unrecognizable From the teachings of the one, it professes to follow Gandhi, the famous Indian leader of non-violence Is quoted as saying, ‘I like your Christ, I do not like your Christians. Your Christians are so unlike your Christ.’ And he is not wrong It is no wonder that thousands are abandoning the Christian faith, at least as a form of organized religion I strongly suspect that if Jesus were to return today Christians would be the first to crucify him again In reading the four Gospels Everything he taught, everything he did Seems to be missing in the behavior of those who Call themselves Christian It becomes obvious they are Christian in name only Those that are the most dangerous They become one-issue voters To determine their political affiliation And interestingly, it’s an issue that Jesus never Mentions, never talks about, never sees as the real problem If I’m not mistaken, he does have a lot to say about Relationships, about love and compassion About charity and caring and sharing I know there are good people who honestly Live their lives in the manner that the human Jesus taught But they are few and far between We are on a slippery slope when politicians insist On incorporating religion into government Perhaps we’d be better off if they incorporated it Into their personal lives instead Render unto Caesar, render unto God Reflect, be a critical thinker, educate yourself Maintain balance – spend time in silence, stillness, With Presence – listen to your heart

George G
