BECOMING

By George Gondron

BECOMING All personal change is a process of becoming Where human creativity is unlimited Yet at some point in our lives, we cease to become We fall into the trap of simply accepting Accepting what is as it is, regardless of the circumstances Every dream, every hope, has within it the kernel of success But for most of us, the hope fades and the dream disappears As we become complacent and accepting of the status quo We allow our familiar to become an anchor, a ball and chain We encase ourselves in self-made prisons A p|rison to which we hold the key But we fail to search for the unopened lock Our release, our creativity has been buried beneath The murmurings of ‘shoulda, coulda, woulda’s’ We listen to the naysayers Fall into the trap of an egoic false self, focused on externals Never embracing the potential and possibilities of our true self Held within our hearts, soul, and spirit We lose the inherent wisdom we once had as a child For we are forced to conform to a society That values the material over the personal That cares more about production and consumption Than human relationships, human connections To suffer the loss of a conscious understanding Of an innate internal core, and inner reference Is also to suffer our capacities for kindness, empathy, and love For all humankind, regardless of race, color, or creed We are not human ‘doings’, we are human beings Meant to ‘become’, to perceive life as relationship Become again, never cease to dream, to hope, to be you Begin a meditative/contemplative practice Connect inner and outer, each day for a few minutes Experience silence and stillness, in the presence of Presence Your image of Universal Consciousness, of your God Empty your mind, breathe in, breathe out a short mantra Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your truth, they have your answers, they wait

George G
