HOW DO WE KNOW

By George Gondron

HOW DO WE KNOW? In relationships how do we really know the other? Is it what they say and what they do? Perhaps what they don't say or do Or do we guess at what we don't know? Possibly assume with no evidence And then there's all that 'stuff' that gets in the way That 'stuff' of course which is ours Our prejudices, pre-judgments, biases, etc. Forming opinions through only what we see Only what we hear Yet there is one thing for sure The first step to knowing another Is to know ourselves, first When we know ourselves we're open to others We see beyond the externals And listen with our hearts, our minds, our being Knowing ourselves is not simply the 'ego ideal' It is so much more, so very much more It is accepting the unacceptable It is owning those aspects of ourselves Which are the most difficult to admit For what we see in others, the bad and the good We also carry within ourselves When that is known, we're on the right path We then can know beyond the externals Beneath the surface, the persona, the facade For now we know the other is us, we are them Connected in our sameness Growing together in our uniqueness Gifts one to the other, to all of life Gifts of and from and a part of the Giver

George G
