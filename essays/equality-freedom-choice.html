EQUALITY, FREEDOM, CHOICE

By George Gondron

EQUALITY, FREEDOM, CHOICE All three are conditional, contingent, and limited In other words, they are not granted without restrictions And who imposes these restrictions? It depends on the subject and the circumstances One of the ways they are limited is through laws Laws passed by some governing body This means they are not unbiased They are manipulated through human interpretation And we all know how interpretation can be flawed There is clear evidence equality is not equal Freedom is not free for all And choices have their limitations Mandates which impact equality, freedom, and choice That are applied without any form of exception Are lacking in leniency and compassion Behind the saying “justice is blind” Lies the proposition that facts and evidence Are the determining factors of guilt and innocence Lawyers, juries, and judges are prohibited from Picking favorites are ruling for whomever they like And we all know impartiality, neutrality, and objectivity Don’t always rule Money, influence, and power often interfere Not to mention the effects of outside influences Fueled and inflamed by various social media platforms By propaganda, alternative facts, prejudice, conditioning, Programming, marketing, preconceptions, and Lack of comprehension or plain ignorance The refusal to think critically and be self-reflective Stir up the emotions and you then have control The one missing ingredient is a lack of internal reference Especially in the West, people no longer have a connection To their hearts, it’s all a matter of intellect and ego Polarized and dualistic thinking at its best Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, With Presence – listen to the whispers of your heart Discover the eternal wisdom and truth within

George G
