HAVE WE

By George Gondron | Source: HAVE WE.docx

HAVE WE? Are we at a tipping point, a place of no return? Have we taken the wrong fork in the road? At the crossroads in this journey, we call democracy, have we placed a man in charge of this nation who uses fear, retribution, coercion, retaliation, threat, and vengeance as a means of governance? Have we elected a monarch as opposed to a representative of all our citizens who refuses to abide by the Constitution and the rule of law, who ignores the separation of powers designed by our founding Fathers to keep us equal and free? Only two weeks in, and we can answer in the affirmative to all the above questions He demonstrates little or no concern at all for the subsequent damaging results of his ill-thought-out choices All that matters is what the would-be dictator and his insistence on being a ‘stable genius’ decide moment by moment And what sustains his ill-gotten power are the spineless cowards that turn a blind eye and bury their heads in the sand Kissing the King’s ring, and offering blind allegiance to the master It should be abundantly clear that what matters to those currently in power in this nation is wealth, power, and control So, the answer to the question, Have We? is a serious one Are we now on a slippery slope, one that we can’t stop? Is it too late for us to ‘wake-up’ and come to our senses? Are we on a trajectory that can’t be reversed? The answer of course is no, it’s not too late If we choose to use our critical thinking, our discernment If we sincerely do what’s necessary to educate ourselves as opposed to drinking the ‘Kool Aid’ and buying the ‘BS’ Don’t allow common sense to become so uncommon Add this new context to your life, adopt a meditative/contemplative practice, a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Bring balance to your life, connect inner and outer Focus on your breath or a short mantra, breathe in, breathe out When the mind wanders, simply rinse and repeat Listen to your inner guides, heart, soul, spirit They hold your true self, have your answers, they wait

George G
