IT'S NOT YESTERDAY - IT'S NOT TOMORROW

By George Gondron

IT'S NOT YESTERDAY - IT'S NOT TOMORROW

What's so difficult about being in the present moment? I dwell on yesterday because of anger and resentment I resist tomorrow out of fear and insecurity My yesterday is nothing more than a teacher for my today, if I but learn My tomorrow will be here soon enough, it cannot teach me today The past, the future, are not the present, the only moment that really exists How much I miss by not dwelling in the present Life does not lie behind me nor in front of me, but beside me in the now What significant moments pass me by in the now? I cannot change yesterday, nor predict tomorrow How many precious moments missed, how much energy wasted, living in any time but now I reflect on the present moment, now I become aware of the beating of my heart, the rhythm of my breath I become silent, still, aware of a yearning within that seeks the One Seeks a relationship of total acceptance Mercy, upon mercy, upon mercy Not yesterday, not tomorrow, always now

George G
