TERRORISTS NEGOTIATIONS

By George Gondron

TERRORIST NEGOTIATIONS For decades this nation has had a policy of not negotiating with terrorists, but obviously that policy under the current administration has changed as we entertain the future of Ukraine, a democracy, with Putin, a terrorist, and without Zelensky the President of Ukraine present – a transactional charade at best or the pacification of a thug at worst, a mind set of Trump of what’s in it for me The term ‘quid pro quo’ was brought to light in Trumps first term when it came to Ukraine If it’s not clear, it’s not obvious that everything about Trump’s mindset is transactional then we are the fools, everything is ‘quid pro quo’ And sadly, what is missing from the transactions are the effects endured by human beings, that doesn’t matter What does matter is how does it benefit Trump and his wealthy cronies And as far as Ukraine, they have rare metals that Trump wants and he doesn’t want to alienate Putin, the hell with Zelensky, so, let’s broker a peace and let the democracy of Ukraine suffer as long as Trump gets what he wants Within a few short weeks, what once was a ‘bright light shining on a hill’, for the rest of the world has become a nation that cannot be trusted, with a President that prefers the company of dictators and autocrats, ‘strong men’, than maintaining ties with our allies Trump it seems and his Republican buddies are willing to enter into any transaction that is beneficial to them, how it affects others is irrelevant, our willingness to choose ignorance will end badly In just a few weeks Trump and his wealthy buddies have become much, much richer, how about you, are you better off today? Do you care about the thousands that have been terminated by President Musk, any compassion or concern for their wellbeing? Musk is nothing but a ‘hatchet man’ supporting his already inflated ego, racking in billions of dollars from government contracts I hope you pay attention to what’s happening through the lens of critical thinking and not the ‘knee-jerk’ reaction of emotions aroused through hyperbole, gaslighting, and propaganda, I hope Spend some time each day in silence, stillness, with Isness Listen to your conscience, your inner guides They hold your true self, they have your answers, they wait

George G
