RELIGIOUS HYPOCRISY

By George Gondron

RELIGIOUS HYPOCRISY Any religious belief system whose focus is not about Compassion, empathy, inclusion, and most of all love Is a system of control, born out by the historical record Ironically Jews, Christians, and Muslims follow the same God But have been at odds with one another for centuries So much for love, compassion, and inclusion It seems we much prefer to follow a set of rules, dictates, Dogma, doctrine, and religious laws, than all else Well at least those who formulate, rule, and control The system, males, of course, are the ‘deciders’ The one we’re most familiar with is Christianity If we are really understanding of what Jesus taught About human connections, human relationships and Human responsibilities between and among ourselves And shine that knowledge on Christianity today The hypocrisy is as clear as the light of day Christians love to fight, argue, and disagreed among themselves When clearly what Jesus taught was about love About caring for one another, about connection and relationship Relationships with each other and with the One he called Father He was a Jew, not a Christian A Jew who more often than not was pointing to the dysfunction Of his own Jewish traditions, which also critique Christianity The escape for Christians, ‘Jesus came to die for our sins’ No where does Jesus say that, if that’s the message you receive From all that he taught and did, then you’ve missed the Message entirely, the message is not about saving your ‘soul’ It’s about becoming fully human, a kind, compassionate, Inclusive, loving, caring, person But if you insist, follow the rules, point fingers, judge others, Make excuses for your own behavior and continue to blame Then at the end of the day ask yourself, am I happy, Am I at peace, am I my brother’s keeper, I hope you are Bring a new perspective into your life, a contemplative practice A few minutes each day of silence, stillness, and Presence Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, they wait

George G
