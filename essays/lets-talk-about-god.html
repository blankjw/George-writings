LET'S TALK ABOUT GOD

By George Gondron

LET'S TALK ABOUT GOD If you've gotten beyond the title Stop, reflect on what attracted you for a moment Curiosity, interest, concern, questioning Or, just perhaps who is this fool to talk about God First, a few observations, scientific observations More and more, science is defining this universe as An intelligent mystery, an organized enigma Purposeful, directed, evolving, and benevolent But, like Dorothy in the Wizard of Oz Who or what is the wizard behind the curtain? And what is the purpose behind this gift? This life, free will, consciousness A gift, a true gift, has no string attached There is no, 'I give you this gift, if' The answer has to do with existence itself Existence through experience, through relationship I am not obliged to acknowledge the gift I can rest my convictions on intellect alone Convictions of believing or not believing Most form their ideas of God based on information Information given or acquired, not experienced Experience will reveal the gift is relational How could it not be The many dynamics of relationship can be taught But the encounter is solitary, is participation It's not words in the head, images in the mind It's the silence of the heart, stillness of the soul A felt sense that can't be denied A truth that can't be defined Arising from the nothingness, the emptiness Deep calling to deep, that is God

George G
