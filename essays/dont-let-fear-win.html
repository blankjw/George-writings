DON'T LET FEAR WIN

By George Gondron

DON'T LET FEAR WIN As the old saying goes 'These are troubling times' In my memory of over eighty-two years I've witnessed several 'troubling times' Korea, Macarthyism, Civil Rights, Viet Nam, Nuclear Proliferation, the Cold War, Iraq, The Drug War And the Great recession, to mention a few However, none were a result of words and actions Initiated, instigated, and perpetuated From the top down From the highest office in our wonderful nation Founded on the concepts of liberty and justice for all Not for the few, not for just the wealthy Not for just white people or the educated But for all, an inclusive not an exclusive word, all The fire has been ignited from the top And continually fanned and fueled from the top If we have any awareness at all, if we're concerned Concerned about now and the future Then we know the fear is real, almost palpable The fear of the obvious, the fear that no one it seems Has the courage within the 'party' now in power To say, this is enough, no more Anger and blame, 'the go to' positions so prevalent today Don't work, they never have, they never will Speaking truth to power will work when those in control Have the courage to do so, not until When fear wins, compassion and kindness lose When fear wins, discrimination and separation win When fear wins, love loses Being neutral when fear reins Is the cowards position Being neutral gives more power to the 'fearmonger' Even though love may be lost in fearful times Love will never die Have the courage to confront fear Have the courage to be a lover, it can bring needed change

George G
