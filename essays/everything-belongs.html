EVERYTHING BELONGS

By George Gondron

EVERYTHING BELONGS That which does not belong, does not exist Everything belongs Denial of what is amounts to blindness Acceptance of what is leads to change Everything belongs I cannot change what I do not understand I cannot understand what I do not experience I cannot experience what I refuse to accept Everything belongs To rebel against difference is non-acceptance To see our sameness is a step towards peace To share leads to communion Everything belongs When I see you, I see me When I help you, I heal me When I love my God, I love all Everything belongs What we call God; Spirit, Isness, Creator, Love Does not matter Labels; descriptions and distractions Everything belongs Within everything created there is Spirit Within every design there is Intelligence Within every conception there is Love Everything belongs You belong, we belong, they belong All belong

George G
