POEM

IT HURTS LIKE HELL

By George Gondron | Source: IT HURTS LIKE HELL.docx

IT HURTS LIKE HELL To pretend that the breakup of a significant relationship Doesn't hurt, is nothing short of self-deception It is lying to one's self It matters not the reason For the issue of right and wrong Belongs to both sides It matters not who leaves who It still hurts, and the denial makes it worse
