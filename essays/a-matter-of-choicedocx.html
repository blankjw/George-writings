A MATTER OF CHOICE

By George Gondron

A MATTER OF CHOICE If we’re fortunate, early on in our lives We will grasp the fact That what we think, what we feel, and what we do or do not do Is a matter of choice But for most, our lives will end Blaming, denying, justifying, rationalizing, etc. Or simply living in a state of tolerance Accepting the worn-out saying, ‘it is what it is’ Living in the past of ‘if only’ Or fearing the future of ‘just maybe’ Our repetitive thoughts, our usual emotions, and Our patterns of behavior become our self-made prison Become our familiar and only we have the key To unlock the jail we’ve built and set ourselves free The first step toward personal change is letting go Letting go of what is for what can be We all know those thoughts, feelings, and behavior That we cling to, that have become our unhealthy familiars In our addictive culture, they are easy to maintain After all, we are the way we are because of ______________ You can fill in the blank Once we understand we are choice-makers There is hope, no there is the possibility of change The saying, ‘no risk, no reward’ is very factual You and I are worth the risk, we deserve the reward The reward of becoming our ‘true self’, not a victim Not a passive bystander to our own existence Love only exists in relationship, beginning with loving ourselves For love is a reaching out, a giving, a sacrifice A necessary compliment to our lives is balance, inner and outer Adopt a meditative/contemplative practice Awaken your inner resources, your internal core Spend a few minutes each day in silence, with stillness Embrace Presence, Universal Consciousness, Isness, God Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit, they wait You matter, your choices matter, make them with awareness

George G
