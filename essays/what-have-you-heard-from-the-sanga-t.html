What have you heard from the sanga t

By George Gondron | Source: What have you heard from the sanga t.docx

1) What have you heard from the sangha that leads you to believe members are unhappy? 2) What have you heard from the sangha that leads you to believe members want longer periods of silence and less teaching? 3) How do you envision building community without sharing and teaching? 4) What do you see as the mission of the sangha? 5) If the goal of Buddhism is to help people awaken, become more aware, develop loving kindness, and let go of attachments, how do you expect that to happen by changing the format that now exists? 6) Is there resistance to a meeting for the purpose of Q & A for all members? 7) Is the sangha limited to Buddhist teaching only, or can other types of guided meditation be introduced occasionally? 8) If it's not broken, there's no need to fix it 9) When the needs of the few become more important than the needs of the many - it's called selfishness
