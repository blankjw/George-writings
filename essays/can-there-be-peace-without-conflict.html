CAN THERE BE PEACE WITHOUT CONFLICT

By George Gondron

CAN THERE BE PEACE WITHOUT CONFLICT? If we use recorded history as our reference and example Then the answer is no, there can’t And those conflicts are the result of fear Hiding behind different narratives All of which are ego-driven for selfish reasons, always Either those who perpetrate the horrors do so Because they are better than, stronger than, or Deserve what the other has, and they want it Whether it’s wealth, land, or natural resources Oh, and let’s not forget human resources, slaves Or they pass laws like Manifest Destiny on a secular level Or religiously send out their followers to convert the pagans Both used as a means to justify their own dysfunction To soothe their own blacken souls It has become very obvious to me in my twilight years That we live in a nation that prefers the lie over the truth Maybe that’s what’s behind those who insist on our Newest phenomenon Critical Race theory How very sad If you can’t teach the truth about your history You are doomed to repeat it again, and we do Why are we afraid of the truth, but promote the lie? One reason, those in control, those with the power Don’t want to lose either, their white numbers are shrinking Why do we not want to teach that in the process of Our westward progress we killed 80 to 100 million Native American Indians and then broke every treaty With those few that remained Why do we not teach about the history of the KKK Of the sweatshops, the union busters, or the 1921 Race massacre in Tulsa, Ok I have my suspicions, do you The human species has proven for centuries There will never be peace without conflict, horrible conflict To be a person of peace there must be a connection Between head, heart, soul, and spirit Get connected, be a person of peace, live and teach the truth

George G
