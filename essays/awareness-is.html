ESSAY

AWARENESS IS

By George Gondron

AWARENESS IS The most succinct definition of awareness; is to wake-up The individual who consciously seeks to know more Is one who chooses to wake-up, to become aware One who is reflective, a critical thinker, one who questions, And has doubts, uses due diligence to discover the truth To follow the facts, trust the experts, shift through the lies A clear indication of those who do not wish to become aware Those who are easily influenced by their emotions Neglecting their own intellect, following the hyperbole on Social media, the ‘alternative facts’, the ‘naysayers’ The conspiracy and ‘deep state’ narratives of the male ego One does not become a contributing member to a healthy Society through ‘luck’, it’s the result of work through effort A mindset of inclusion, conciliation, cooperation, and consensus A character type that promotes unity, togetherness, seeing Sameness before difference, knowing we are all more alike Than unalike, we are connected in more ways than one We all have common dreams, hopes, and wishes Believing in freedom and equal justice for all, not the few The freedom to worship as we please, to love as we please To explore and develop our own unique creativity with safety If social media is your source for learning, for becoming aware For ‘waking-up’, you are easily manipulated, easily controlled The sayings, ‘Koll Aid’ drinkers, following the ‘carrot on a stick’, The ‘blind leading the blind’, chasing the ‘pied piper’ Are not by accident, they are intentional, and they work The more divisive the rhetoric, the language, the more likely Its untruth, exaggerations meant to divide and control Language filled with hatred, accusation, blame, and fear Is not meant to unite, it is self-serving, not other serving In a real sense we have become lazy, clinging to our unawareness Preferring to be fed the ‘truth’ rather than seeking the ‘truth’ Please, be a seeker, spend a few minutes each day in silence, Stillness, with Presence, your image of Mystery, of God Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They are your inner guides, your ability to discern They hold your true self, have your answers, they wait

George G
