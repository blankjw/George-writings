WITHOUT A COUBT

By George Gondron

WITHOUT A DOUBT When people think they know the answers Their minds remain closed When they know they do not know Then they are willing to learn We are witnessing currently in our culture A ‘without a doubt’ phenomenon When millions of people accept, embrace, and believe Without a doubt They rely on social media and third-party information For their truth, without a doubt An attitude, which is very susceptible to conspiracy theories Very open to fear and negative influences For they are more easily influenced by their emotions Than through their intellect, their critical thinking Here’s the ‘fly in the ointment’ The ego will do everything and anything To avoid embarrassment and shame, avoid being vulnerable Including accepting without a doubt So, the ego will ‘stick to its guns’ rather than admit It is unwilling to accept proven facts or scientific data The mindset of a ‘creationist’ versus an ‘evolutionist’ It follows the narrative of ‘I may not be right but I’m never wrong’ When the underlying message we tell ourselves Is to avoid embarrassment, shame, vulnerability It requires a great deal of courage and bravery to admit Maybe I was wrong Maybe believing ‘without a doubt’ is my prison The first step toward personal transformation is to doubt One’s own unfounded beliefs, one’s own surety I trust the imperfect, the unsure, the uncertain Much more than those ‘without a doubt’ The path to avoiding the ‘without a doubt’ mentality Begins with the awakening of our own inner core The eternal truths we all possess with this gift we call life Begin with a few minutes each day in silence, with stillness, And with Presence – discover your true self, it patiently awaits Listen to the truth held within your own heart, soul, and spirit

George G
