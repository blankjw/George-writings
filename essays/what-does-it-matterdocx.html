WHAT DOES IT MATTER

By George Gondron

WHAT DOES IT MATTER? The forgotten phone call on a special day No apology for an unintended slip of the tongue The joke at the expense of the other The argument that I must win, because I'm right Lip service to those in poverty, the ones who struggle Lip service, but no action, no human contact What does matter? The nod, the smile, the handshake, the hug They matter Does it matter what I do, where I come from? Get to know me before you ask How quick we make assumptions about others How quick we form opinions with so little knowledge Opinions that lead to fear, violence, chaos, and death What does it matter? Look into my eyes Listen to my story, I will listen to yours Follow my journey, I will follow yours Then You will know that it matters It matters for you, for me, for the world, forever

George G
