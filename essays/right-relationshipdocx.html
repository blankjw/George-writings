RIGHT RELATIONSHIP

By George Gondron

RIGHT RELATIONSHIP It's not ownership - right relationship For ownership is about control It's not free from disappointment - right relationship Disappointment is a part of being human It's not about compulsion - right relationship Compulsion results in submission It's not desire - right relationship For desire's root is attachment It's not about rescue - right relationship You cannot save another from themselves It's not sex - right relationship Sex is treating the other as an object Right relationship is never linked to need Need of any description, any degree, any scope Right relationship is based on mutual choice Freely entered with no expectations Vulnerable attraction with unknown results A mystery to be explored, with no agenda Unique subjects one to the other, not objects It will involve a myriad of emotions All survivable with openness and vulnerability Nothing teaches like the cauldron of relationship Right relationship will survive any cauldron Grounded in freedom, kindness, and compassion Grounded in care, sacrifice, and risk Right relationship is forever In the end, it is very simple Right relationship is unconditional love Unconditional Love is right relationship

George G
