WHAT HAVE WE LOST

By George Gondron

WHAT HAVE WE LOST The feminine soul Through the birthing process, life survives, life continues And yet for centuries the feminine soul Has been held in check, has been shackled Has been controlled and manipulated By and through the lesser half, the masculine half Lesser only because of some ego need to be in control There is no other valid reason All male defensives are only that, defensives Defenses born from male weakness, from male fear For obviously that which is capable of giving birth Must be managed, restrained, and kept in check Must be held down It begs the question what has been lost As a result of this masculine behavior That to this very day repeats itself in myriad ways Hiding behind platitudes of equality Platitudes of course spoken only by males We can only imagine how history has been altered Altered in a negative sense, in a restrictive sense As a result of the exclusion of female energy Female intuition, intellect, and nurturance Women have literally had to fight for equality Fight to be considered more than just an object More than a means to an end, that end being Whatever meets the male agenda Whether that agenda was nothing more than a necessary Requirement for the continuance of the human race Or as sexual objects to satisfy the male sexual urgings It is difficult to discover or point to any major aspect of Existence in which the feminine spirit has played a major role A role of other than existing in the background, quietly Acquiescing to male authority, secular or sacred We need female energy, input, and intellect more than ever Women must resist the fragile male ego that needs to control At this point in our history, I trust women much more than men Without their inclusion, their wise input, the future looks bleak

George G
