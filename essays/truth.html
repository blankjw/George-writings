TRUTH

By George Gondron

TRUTH An inexhaustible word, is this word truth Only because, in the realm of human behavior Few actions are always true, as few also are always false For what is true for you, for me, for them, for us Arises from personal experience And by judging your actions, I assume I know your experiences But I don't, we always assume what we don't know We have the answers before the questions are asked And in most cases, being critical of you Is the failure to see the same in myself If your behavior followed my personal truth Then, it would probably be different As well of mine, if it followed your truth Yet there is a truth that most never see That when I see you, I also see me There is a truth that is true for all, and it is this Change is inevitable, sooner or later, change will happen While most resist and always will Symbolically kicking and screaming, being dragged along Others, welcome change and become the wiser They are the ones who learn from their failures The ones that know life is two steps forward, one step backward That know, the only consistency is change itself And, without change, there is no life, there's just existence There is another truth that will always be Life is relationship, from birth until death, and beyond It's not lived in a vacuum, or in our own little bubble Its substance and value is of our own making We can choose to change, especially our minds We can find new truths that enhance our lives And then, there remains the greatest truth It is an absolute truth, its name is Love Nothing compares, for Love is the energy, the essence For all that exists; for you, for me, for all Love has no beginning and Love will never end Truth is Love, and Love is truth Seek the One who is Love and you will know truth, your truth

George G
