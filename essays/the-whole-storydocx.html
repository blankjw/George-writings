THE WHOLE STORY

By George Gondron

THE WHOLE STORY We are living in times when the expectation of knowing The whole story is virtually impossible We rather, gravitate toward our media source of choice Which we invariably suspect has a bias, a prejudice built-in But that’s so much easier, channel surf Regardless of our preference, liberal, conservative, or independent We can be assured that we are getting a slanted view It’s almost as if we don’t want to know the truth We merely seek that which satisfies our underlying unspoken belief It’s so much easier to be ‘fed’ than critically think Or to question and doubt, to do our own research That’s entirely too much effort for too little results And that approach to ‘knowing’ is not knowing at all It’s called being manipulated and brain-washed Every link we click on, a channel we choose Involves a corresponding algorithm keeping a record of Our choices in all areas of our personal interest Entertainment, politics, religion, education, etc., etc., etc. There is no mystery to who we are, we are perfectly identified And being a culture based on consumption They can easily sell us what fits our character and personality We are hooked We seem to have forgotten or ignored the old saying ‘Don’t believe what you hear and only half of what you see’ It would seem that to ‘fact check’ is a waste of time And the marketers and advertisers know that all too well The name of the game, money, profit, control Get us to do, what in the clear light of day, we would never do Once we become emotionally attached, our discernment is gone Reason no longer matters, follow the ‘carrot on the stick’ Human wholeness is all-inclusive Not just our minds, our emotions, but also our heart, soul, and spirit To awaken those innate parts of our being do this Choose a meditative/contemplative daily practice Spend a few minutes in silence, be still, and embrace Presence Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit Discern, be intuitive, discover your true self – it waits

George G
