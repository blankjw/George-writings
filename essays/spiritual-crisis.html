SPIRITUAL CRISIS

By George Gondron | Source: SPIRITUAL CRISIS.docx

SPIRITUAL CRISIS Religion alone, regardless of the particular faith, will never succeed that has no spiritual basis In other words, that is empty of relationship with its particular deity named God, Allah, or Yahweh and focuses on worship and meritocracy alone In Christianity, Judaism, and Islam love is paramount and love by its very nature is relational, not as an emotion but as an action It is love that has no expectations and is selfless Recognizing obvious need and fulfilling that need to the best of our ability with no strings attached In our culture that is a dynamic that is an exception to the rule and not the rule at all Evidence to the contrary, we ae a nation spiritually and morally bankrupt, focused on the material, attached to ‘things’
