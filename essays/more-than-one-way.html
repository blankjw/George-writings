MORE THAN ONE WAY

By George Gondron

MORE THAN ONE WAY One could say the most important goals of an individual life are self-actualization, authenticity, individuation, and peak experiences Embracing not only the successes and accomplishments of one’s life but as well the failures, pain, and darkness The path one takes varies and it’s not a matter of the ‘right’ belief system, for the ultimate goal of them all is the same Understanding and relating to the Mystery that formed and continues to manifest this amazing universe Regardless of the path one takes, they all seem to have one common theme, embracing periodic moments of withdrawal A context that necessarily includes silence, stillness, and a nonverbal relationship with one’s belief in a creative Source, God if you wish All evidence to the contrary, in our current environment few there are that pursue this goal Rather, we halfheartedly join some ‘membership’ organization or choose to be influenced by whatever is popular on social media Being manipulated emotionally rather than intellectually Few there are that use critical thinking, question, doubt, research, or spend reflective time at all, we follow the ‘pied piper’ instead Astonishingly 54 % of adults in this nation have a literacy level of 6th grade or lower, that is shocking but it answers a lot of questions The brain is a muscle and needs exercise if it is to remain healthy We no longer read, we watch a screen for our information Information manipulated by algorithms to feed us what sells Propaganda sells, hypocrisy sells, and unfortunately much of organized religion sells, and it also kills We are all called to become the best we can be, to reach our full potential, to be gifts to one another, caring and compassionate There are many paths to the top of the mountain, choose one Adopt a meditative/contemplative pracrice Spend a few minutes each day in silence, with stillness and Presence Focus on your breath or a brief mantra, breathe in, breathe out The ego will definitely resist, the mind will wander Simply rinse and repeat, over and over Listen to the whispers of your inner guides, your inner core+ Your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, have your answers, they wait

George G
