COMPREHENSION^J UNDERSTANDING^J PERCEPTION

By George Gondron

COMPREHENSION, UNDERSTANDING, PERCEPTION Our lives, once we ‘settle’ into our familiar patterns, lack flexibility and spontaneity as we cease to learn and be curious Our thoughts, feelings, and actions become very predictable We limit the possibilities for our continued growth and awareness We restrict our comprehension, understanding, and perceptions because of our choices, or better stated, lack thereof Our most precious gift, free will, lies dormant locked in the prison of our own making because it’s easier to ‘settle’ than take a risk The acronym for comprehension, understanding, and perception is CUP, and when our cup is full, when we settle, we cease to learn Sadly, the potential for learning is unlimited, the CUP is never full We live in the past because of melancholy or anger, or we live in the future because of fear and insecurity All the while missing the most important moments of the ‘now’ Change and new learning always happens in the ‘now’, not in yesterday or tomorrow When our perceptions are filtered through established biases and prejudices, then the accuracy of our responses are very limited They tend to be very dualistic and polarized Resulting in black/white, either/or thinking with no possibility for a both/and potential to whatever the circumstance may be Fixed opinions and closed minds deal only in probabilities, not possibilities, they have an answer to every problem Progress results always in challenging the status quo CUP, comprehensive, understanding, and perception are unlimited They lead to reconciliation, cooperation, synthesis, and compromise CUP people are more interested in how we think than what we think They also understand that there is no limit to continuing education It is rather the lack of education which is our greatest problem Ignorance only exists because of limited education Add a daily meditative/contemplative practice to your life A time to empty your mind and experience the benefits of silence, stillness, and Presence, your understanding of God Focus on your breath or a brief mantra, breathe in, breathe out The mind will wander, the ego will resist, simply rinse and repeat Listen for the whispers of your inner guides, heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, have your answers, they patiently wait

George G
