FETISH`

By George Gondron

FETISH One definition An abnormally obsessive preoccupation Or attachment, a fixation Further, an unreasonable or excessive amount of Attention given to an object, thought, or feeling One of the dysfunctional fetishes in our culture Guns, guns, guns Especially among males of all ages This fetish is grounded in fear, not love Grounded in a ‘my way or the highway’ mentality As opposed to kindness and compassion The problems that exist in an environment of Extreme capitalism are not the result of selflessness But selfishness A culture that is morally and spiritually bankrupt And ironically much of it flows from and hides behind The guise of fundamental evangelical Christianity True spirituality never enters the equation In fact, spirituality, meditation, contemplation, Kindness, and compassion are not taught at all And hardly ever discussed in open conversations We have the most lax, negligent, incomprehensible Gun laws in the world When an eighteen-year-old can purchase an assault Weapon and large-capacity magazines with no background check But can’t buy a beer, cigarettes, a racy magazine Or a pistol until age twenty-one There is a serious problem Ninety percent of voting citizens support universal Background checks, but Republicans refuse to pass it No other country has ‘active shooter drills’ in their schools The drills I remember were ‘fire drills’ Mental health issues are universal Not limited to the United States of America But that’s the excuse used to avoid common sense guns laws We alone have this problem – no other nation in the world Stand up, speak out, be a part of the solution, not the problem

George G
