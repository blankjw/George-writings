A TRANSFORMATION OF CONSCIOUSNESS

By George Gondron

A TRANSFORMATION OF CONSCIOUSNESS Psychological and emotional growth, greater understanding, Expanded awareness, all indications of a transformation of Consciousness, a broadening of how we perceive ourselves And our world which connects us to our internal resources, Our innate inner core without which we can never become Objective, inclusive, and connected to all humanity We remain, in essence, unconscious clinging to our small Subjective beliefs most of which have no experiential basis Founded mostly on third party information, he/she/they said Or social media, which of course is always reliable and true Transformation of consciousness does not happen without our Concerted effort, our willingness to educate ourselves Beyond absorbing information like a sponge, the irresponsible And easy approach, and the result, ‘garbage in, garbage out’ One of the hallmarks of ignorance is defensiveness, ‘whataboutism’, Comparing the past to the present and predicting the future With belief, not with knowledge, not with personal experience When our lives are constantly ‘busy’ filled with the next distraction Thinking, feeling, and doing the same things day after day What chance for an increase in awareness and consciousness? Critical thinking, valid research, question and doubt, reflection, Are all parts of the journey to increased understanding We are fortunate to live in a democracy with untold available Resources to further educate ourselves, we prefer the easy way Just change the channel to the media source that stimulates our Emotions and encourages us not to think, but just believe To complain about what is without a valid and experiential basis Is to be part of the problem and it’s interesting to note that most ‘Complainers’ don’t have an educated solution but they do have A ‘knee-jerk’ reaction, like the Queen in Alice in Wonderland, ‘Off With his/her head’, that will solve the problem For certain we lack balance in our culture, inner and outer Add a meditative/contemplative practice to your daily life Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Isness Focus on your breath or a brief mantra, breathe in, breathe out The ego will resist, the mind will wander, rinse and repeat Listen to the whispers of your inner guides, heart, soul, spirit

George G
