ESSAY

EXISTENCE

By George Gondron

EXISTENCE What has been, what is now, what will be Has always existed, from the beginning There is nothing new, nothing has been added What arose out of emptiness, contains all There is only transformation, through impermanence There are no new elements, there is evolution Only discovery of what has always been Humans are not creators, we only happen upon what is It is the essence of new awareness It is the result of deeper consciousness That brings forth seeming newness of what is already present Impermanence brings change of what was, is, and will be Even consciousness emerges from what is But science cannot explain how consciousness happens That, in the cogitations of the mind, is yet a mystery The 'Is' that brings consciousness is known, but unknowable The 'Mystery' is known through experience, a connection A felt relationship beyond the senses This spirit within each of us The 'divine spark' within that calls forth understanding Also, has been from the beginning What we call 'I' or 'me' can't be known in depth It is only known through the narrow confines of the mind With the inclusion of the heart, soul, and spirit One begins to understand this 'I', this 'me' That springs forth from the mind of the One we call God We, each of us, are intentional Life is not accidental, life is deliberate, purposeful Life is about relationship, about connection And that life, our life, like all of nature, transforms Transforms through new awareness, deeper consciousness Transforms again at death, back to dust But the essence, the spirit, the soul, remains As it was in the beginning, and shall always be For what Love creates, never really dies It only transforms Existing forever

George G
