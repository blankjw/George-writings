FOR LOVE

By George Gondron

FOR LOVE It is impossible, beyond the conceivable To put into words what words cannot convey About the depth and meaning of unimagined love About what people through the ages Have literally given their lives for, willingly This boundless, limitless, eternal four-letter word Love To grasp and understand what cannot be understood Without the experience, is futile, is hopeless We cannot think our way into the experience Through our too many words, our minds alone It can only be found in the living In the bumping-up against all that life offers It is a living that encompasses Not just the sweetness, the excitement, the joy Of those certain relationships within our history That we 'fell in love' with, that may or may have endured Oh no, the love of which I speak is so much more It is a love that survives through the disappointments Through the crucible of life's pain A pain known only through meaningful relationships Whether it be family or friends In a true sense, love is not love That has not suffered, that has not experienced loss That has not known hurt, failure, and defeat That has not felt betrayed and forgotten That has not been crushed through disappointment, and yet Rises again, to love once more Stronger than before The bonds of this kind of love cannot be broken There are no weak links in this chain, this love All of existence, the entire universe Is held together, floating in this Divine Love It matters not whether you believe in a Creator For this kind of Love cannot not love, it is unconditional We are only asked to accept, to experience What is freely given and always available, this Love

George G
