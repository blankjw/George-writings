THE WAY

By George Gondron

THE WAY Is there a right way, a wrong way, or simply the way If we don't choose a way, the way will choose us The real question, the real important statement Is what we do, is the way that we follow Chosen through awareness, consciously as opposed to habitually Or the deeper question What motivates the choice, ignites and fires the way we follow The underlying incentive for most is ego driven Driven through selfish motives disguised with smiles and handshakes For a few it's driven by the heart, the soul, the spirit It's driven by selfless love, but only for a few Our culture, our society encourages one way The way of 'human doing', not 'human being' The way of the automaton, not one of awareness The way of forcing round pegs into square holes The way of individualism at the expense of individuation The way of extreme consumerism Of valuing what is, in action, accumulation, and status Forgetting the who of life, the otherness of life Our lifestyle is very telling, the platitudes can't hide the reality We care for those nearest us, the rest we barely noticed Lip service is our God We are adrift in a sea of common humanity Yet we have lost connection with others like ourselves We have lost connection with the Source of all that is Substituting membership for community Substituting worship for relationship Clinging to a few 'do's' and avoiding a few 'don't's' Thinking this is all that's required, this is all that's necessary To justify our lifestyle, to justify what we don't do The way can change, a different path can be taken There's always the matter of choice, a different choice A choice that includes incorporating our hearts, souls, and spirits Along with our minds into our awareness Of refocusing on the meaning of our lives Of including a little silence, stillness, and Presence each day Of taking a different path, a new way

George G
