WHEN DOES REALITY SHIFT

By George Gondron

WHEN DOES REALITY SHIFT What your reality is today is much different than when you were young Our reality changes as we become more aware, more conscious The less that awareness is the result of personal experience The more it becomes the result of someone else’s experience In other words, we are living their truth and not our own Our reality becomes one of a follower and not a seeker And the result, we do a disservice to our own gifts and capabilities Consequently, a shift in reality is not always a positive event Of course, being a follower is much easier Less effort, less stress. less reflection, less discernment, just follow And sadly, we seldom follow the one who challenges us intellectually Rather, we follow the one that stirs our emotions Negative emotions are grounded in fear Positive emotions are grounded in love Ask yourself, which do you follow? It’s much easier to resist change as a follower It’s more difficult if you are a seeker But the seeker knows the risk is worth the reward Unfortunately, the choice to change is the exception and not the rule The ego if anything, resists change Especially change that leads to a new reality grounded in experience Experience as a seeker, not a follower Playing it safe is not the way of a seeker Truth is never discovered playing it safe Moving from the familiar to the strange, being vulnerable, taking a risk All these lead to new awareness and consciousness, a shift A possibility of learning from and not living in the past A possibility of hoping for and not fearing the future The reality of the importance of living in the now Courage and bravery are required to make a shift in our reality That can’t be accomplished with the ego alone but must Incorporate the knowing of our inner core, our internal reference A meditative/contemplative practice awakens the potential We all innately already possess, the foundation of our existence For a few minutes each day be silent, be still, be in the presence of Presence, of your image of a creative Source, some call God Empty your mind of its incessant and repetitive thoughts With consistent practice, you will have a shift in awareness A shift in consciousness, a positive shift guaranteed Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit, they hold your truth They have your answers, they are your true self, they patiently wait

George G
