NORMALIZING THE UNACCEPTABLE

By George Gondron

NORMALIZING THE UNACCEPTABLE When the unacceptable becomes the normal When the lie becomes the truth When we are only concerned about one promise In a list of false promises What does that reveal about our level of consciousness Our concern for the least among the many The lack of awareness of our own dysfunction Speaks volumes on so many different levels There is an adage, 'you cannot know what you do not know' But there's also the saying, 'ignorance is no excuse' When no one is held accountable for the 'lie' No one is responsible for the 'deception' The abnormal becomes the normal Absolute power corrupts absolutely, history simply repeats itself How can gun violence become normalized, which it has? How can partisanship become more normal than conciliation? How can what I have become more important than what you have? How is it normal that the vast majority of wealth Is held in the hands of a few at the expense of the many? How is it normal that racism is still alive and well More than fifty years after the passing of the Civil Rights Act How can it be normal for more than 19 million children To go to bed at night hungry in the wealthiest nation on earth? We have become numb to the important issues of being human We are blind, deaf, and for the most part unconcerned For the millions that live in poverty in this country When a culture which has lost its heart, soul, and spirit At the expense of the mind, the intellect, and calls it normal How does it return to sanity, to compassion, to equality? Some would say we need to turn back to religion No, we don't, we need a change of heart Millions attend their chosen church, but the aforementioned Still remains, the dysfunction of what is has been normalized The ego refuses to experience a path that leads to heart knowledge A simple path of silence, of stillness, of Presence Leading to a normal life of connection and relationship A life given, existing, lasting, and evolving through and in Love

George G
