WHAT IS IGNORANCE

By George Gondron

WHAT IS IGNORANCE? Is it only mental incapacity, unintelligence, illiteracy? No, it is so much more, especially in an educated culture A society that has unlimited access to knowledge Through unlimited media resources, visual and aural It goes far beyond unawareness or the unfamiliar Beyond insensitivity, mental blindness, and superficiality This ignorance is that which is able to reject Reject the obvious truths that factually can't be denied Yet they are And that which is apparently indefensible, is still defended A fact that supports the sayings 'There are none so blind as those who will not see And none so deaf as those who will not hear' Simple, yet profound words that in truth Repeat themselves over and over and over again Just what is it within the human psyche That prevents the observable, the apparent, the conspicuous From being acknowledged? There is not just one answer, there are many But underlying them all is fear A fear that is so palatable that we are willing to accept the lie Rather than believing the truth And those that motivate through fear Are well aware of their influence, their power Well aware of how to control those who don't take the time The time to search for the truth, educate themselves Understand that there's seldom just an either/or solution More often than not, there's always a both/and resolution The answer to any problem that is approached through Compassion, concern, and equality for all people Is always different than the one that is dealt with From a fear based mentality, a mentality of difference, Rather than sameness, we are more alike than unalike The truths we need to know reside within ourselves When they become more important than our fears Then we will become compassionate, loving, caring people The truth will always win, for Love never fails

George G
