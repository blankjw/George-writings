LIES, THE NEW NORMAL

By George Gondron

LIES, THE NEW NORMAL Truth no longer matters, honesty is buried Hyperbole, exaggeration, distortion, overstatement And the favorite, alternative facts These matter, these are the new normal What divides matters, what separates matters Us against them matters Whoever us is, whoever they are And beneath all the above, dwells Power, control, greed, wealth, and righteousness And it helps to be white, that matters The externals matter, the haves matter The have-nots languish, in the shadow of those that have When did the lie become more important than the truth? How did it happen? How did the false narrative gain so much power? It has something to do with ignorance But not my ignorance, we all say to ourselves Then who's News flash - mine, yours, ours We all are guilty There are no innocent bystanders But there's lots of blamers Lots of finger pointers We all swallow the lie, hook, line, and sinker
