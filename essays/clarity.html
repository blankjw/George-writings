CLARITY

By George Gondron

CLARITY Clarity, objectivity, understanding, perception, transparency, Unambiguous, all words seeking to discover the real Those in the business of attempting to sell us something Don’t really care about what’s real They only care about control, manipulation, ego fulfillment, Power, reward, personal benefits, and profit Their audience is simply a means to an end, their end And the words they use do not challenge us to critically think, To reflect, to doubt, to question, to research, or to learn And if you pay attention, you will notice their words are meant To stir your emotions, to ‘fire you up’, not to encourage you to Discover the truth, the facts, the real, or even the obvious And their rhetoric is always negative, against, hostile, or blaming It’s much easier to convince through the emotions Than through the intellect That’s why the statement, ‘I love the uneducated’ Should be an eye-opener, very telling, and dangerous It’s saying clearly, ‘you are stupid, and I can manipulate you’ Once you accept the lie, the ‘alternative facts’, the conspiracy You have, in essence, given up your ability to discern, to know On any factual level for your thinking is now emotional And he, she, it, or they represent the ‘bad guys’ We are currently it seems, in an untenable situation And honestly, it is no surprise for we have willingly given up Two of our most powerful abilities, to think and to love We don’t care to ‘fact check’, reflect, question, doubt, or discover We don’t see ‘other’ as our sister or brother, they are the enemy We have lost the right to call ourselves rational human beings There is nothing rational about our behavior at all Unless of course, you consider being selfish rational Democracy won’t fail because of its precepts and ideals It will fail because of our inability to see clearly, lack of clarity A meditative/contemplative practice aids us with clarity A few minutes of silence, stillness, and Presence each day Quietens the mind of incessant repetitive thoughts Allows us to hear the whispers of our heart. soul, and spirit They hold our true self, have our answers, and our clarity

George G
