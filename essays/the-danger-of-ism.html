THE DANGER OF ISM

By George Gondron

THE DANGER OF ISM'S To be influenced by the voices of any ism Without reflection, without critical thinking Is to be the fool And there are more than a few, there are many All political ism's, religious ism's, national ism's Here's the conundrum Ism's don't have consciousness, only individuals Yet we become defended, guarded, and entrenched About what we say we believe in, blindly Unwilling to pull back the curtain of any ism To look beyond what seems obvious In order to understand the purpose, the basis Of any ism Relationship, compassion, kindness, peace, justice And love, have no appendage of an ism They are common descriptors of all humanity All races, all colors, all creeds
