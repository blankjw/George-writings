COMPLICITY

By George Gondron

COMPLICITY Guilt equals complicity, complicity equals guilt A few decades ago, perhaps twenty to thirty years Saying that we all are complicit through our silence Would not have been a fair statement Not everyone had the internet Not everyone had a cell phone Even not everyone had a TV But now, virtually everyone has the aforementioned We see and hear everything that is happening In this nation, in the world So, either you have picked a side That encompasses every aspect of your life Religious, political, economic, educational, etc. Or you have chosen to ‘stick your head in the sand’ Or chosen to wear ‘blinders’ Or simply remained silent Whatever your decision of not having an opinion Of remaining silent is an indication of your complicity If we’re not a part of the solution We are, through our silence, a part of the problem To refuse to become involved Even if it’s nothing more than deep reflection A little critical thinking, a little doubt, some questions Perhaps some fact checking We are guilty, we are complicit And as a result, whatever the consequences We have no right to complain We have no right to say ’well if I had known’ No excuse is good enough, none will suffice All that’s left for us to do, point fingers and blame And history shows, we are very good at that The ego never takes ownership for its mistakes, never If you happen to be in the complicit category At least do this Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, With Presence – empty your monkey mind Listen to the whispers of your heart, listen to Isness

George G
