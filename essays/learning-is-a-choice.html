LEARNING IS A CHOICE

By George Gondron

LEARNING IS A CHOICE You cannot teach anyone anything, even yourself Unless they and you are willing to learn Everything we do or do not do is a choice, always There are several paths to learning Academically and experientially are the primary two One is geared toward a formal educational setting Listening to lectures, reading books, studying to pass exams The other is more hands-on learning Perhaps testing out the beliefs we have about what we learned Direct personal experience, however, there is a third path Simply taking in what is offered without critical analysis Being a sponge and accepting what is as it is That which appeals to our current emotional state Appeals to our character and level of awareness The danger of this third method is the lack of validation Third-party information, gossip, and hearsay, without facts It can be described as the Kool-Aid of choice It becomes an emotional high, it separates and divides Us against them, low-level consciousness at best A consciousness when asked, ‘What is it you dislike About that person, that idea, that message.? And the reply, ‘I just don’t like he/she, them, it’ Here’s an interesting fact Psychologically there is nothing to like, dislike, or defend When one lives a congruent, fully human life A person who realizes it’s the questions we ask The doubts we have, our curious minds that are more Important than the accusations we make, our unfounded beliefs How we learn is just as important as our choice to learn Healthy learning is a life of balance, inner and outer Learning is cultivated not only from our minds, our ego But from our internal core, our inner reference innate with birth Become a discerning person, choose a meditative/contemplative Daily practice for just a few minutes of silence, with stillness Be in the presence of the one you may God, Creator, Source Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit Cherish their input, they hold your truth, your answers, they wait
