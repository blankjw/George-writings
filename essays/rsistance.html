RSISTANCE

By George Gondron

RESISTANCE Of all our personal strengths and weaknesses It is the capability of our ego to resist that is our greatest danger For without the ego’s approval, we will not readily accept change We will resist, defiantly, stubbornly, and consistently To be open to the unknown is to be open to mystery To be open to possibility, to opportunity, to greater awareness And deeper understanding as opposed to remaining stuck In our familiar, clinging to the passivity of our lives Spinning our wheels, having the same thoughts and feelings Day after day, doing the same things day after day Unable to step outside our comfort zone rejecting our resistance To broaden our horizons, increase our understanding, become More fully human is to risk and be vulnerable As the saying goes, ‘nothing ventured, nothing gained’ When we resist the unfamiliar we also restrict our freedom When we never question, doubt, or critically think We limit our own potential and possibility, we are less than Ignorance is always the enemy and ignorance can be remedied But it is a matter of choice We can use social media as our source of truth, or we can Seek reliable sources that can be fact checked for authenticity Fear is a great motivator, but so is love, choose love Peek behind the curtain, avoid the smoke and mirrors, learn Education is much more valuable than ‘gaslighting’ or Drinking the ‘Kool Aid’ flavor of the day A responsible life seeks clarity and understanding beyond Alternative facts, half-truths, and obvious lies Resistance is never the answer, it is the enemy Resistance, a closed mind, is simply the refusal to know, to learn To become informed with reliable information, to grow We have a gift called consciousness, use it well, use it wisely Add a meditative/contemplative practice to your life for balance It will aid in providing a clear understanding of your resistance Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Empty your mind, focus on your breath, breathe in, breathe out Listen to the whispers of your inner guides, your internal core They are your true self, they have your answers, they wait

George G
