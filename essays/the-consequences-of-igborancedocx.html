THE CONSEQUENCES OF IGBORANCE

By George Gondron

THE CONSEQUENCES OF IGNORANCE The lack of knowledge, information, or education results in Ignorance, forming opinions and beliefs without substance Without the use of our intellect mere acceptance of what is As it is, promotes and supports ignorance, easily Intelligence is our path to understanding The tool our mind uses to gather, analyze, and comprehend What input we receive from our five senses, our thoughts And our feelings – our internal computer so-to-speak And like the saying goes, ‘garbage in, garbage out’ To merely accept, without critical thinking is a fool’s errand Ignorance is not a condition, it is a choice When we accept what is offered, especially from social media Without fact checking, doubt, clear evidence to the contrary Not to mention our own ability to critically think We are only supporting our own continued ignorance We are buying into someone else’s false narrative Not meant to inform but only to promote their agenda And if you doubt or don’t believe we are being manipulated By social media, watch The Social Dilemma on Netflix, please Another clear result of ignorance is a closed mind A mind that deflects, distracts, changes the subject, Or uses words like ‘yeah but, what about, remember when’, They don’t entertain a discussion to learn, only to defend A meaningful discussion with ignorance is fruitless, walk away Continued and persistent ignorance also breeds fear, mistrust, And prejudice – and fuels conspiracy theories Ignorance separates and divides, focuses on the negative Finds fault everywhere except within itself Ignorance does not care to understand because it already knows A knowing not from personal experience but only because of The words of others, ignorance never leads to peace only division You know the saying, ‘my way or the highway’ Please use all your faculties, mind, heart, soul, and spirit A ‘better together’ approach – be discerning, a lost valuable talent Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Isness That inner voice, the mystery that says ‘I Am who am’ Your inner guides are your true self, hold your truth, they wait

George G
