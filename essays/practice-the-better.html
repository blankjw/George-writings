PRACTICE THE BETTER

By George Gondron

PRACTICE THE BETTER The practice of what’s better is more influential Than attempting to change what’s not working There is no way to ‘unlearn’ what’s already learned But there is a way to add to what’s better, more positive The power of education cannot be overstressed But education is not a matter of just accepting blindly What is offered without question, without critical thinking When our awareness and understanding grows and expands Our point of view changes and becomes more inclusive Maybe we connect with someone of a different race Ot a different religion or philosophical base And through that connection, through that experience and Interaction we lose our precious notions and ideas About racism and different belief systems We didn’t ‘unlearn’ we added to It’s a reminder that experience is always the best teacher The deepest learning, the most powerful experiences Are not the result of words but of personal experience Repetitive personal experience Failure almost always comes before success An aware, happy, healthy life, is a balanced life The connection between our inner core and our ego Between our innate internal resources and our mind When these are in balance we live with our better angels Empathy, kindness, compassion, inclusion, openness, A sense of connection and most importantly, love We understand that life is more about what we don’t know Than what we think we know, more about mystery We are less willing to allow our emotions to be our guides Learning that most of our negativity is the result of fear To practice the better, to have a balanced life, Adopt a meditative/contemplative daily practice For a few minutes each day engage silence, be still, be present Focus on a short mantra, slowly breathing in, breathing out Your mind will immediately wander, return to the beath Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, they wait

George G
