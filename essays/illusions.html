ILLUSIONS

By George Gondron

ILLUSIONS To see life as it is, beyond the illusions Is an exception rather than the rule Beyond a world full of deception and make believe Where truth seems to be a cheap commodity And personal advantage is more important than human need What we can't see clearly will continue to deceive us It always has, it always will, necessarily so How does an atmosphere of delusion continue With smoke and mirrors, feigned prosperity Subterfuge and lies disguised as truth Controlled by the 'haves', the wealthy, the well-to-do They control the media, in all its forms Revealing only what they deem necessary It is no longer 'we the people' It is we the wealthy, we the powerful, we who have the control Remember the 'Golden Rule'? Do unto others as you would have them do unto you It now has a new meaning, a new definition Those with the gold, the power, the control, make the rules There is no sector of life that is not tainted with the smell of power Education, politics, corporate business, justice, government, Health, medicine, food, media, and religion How did this happen, how can it change? It happens for only one reason When life, when people are less important than 'things' They're only three 'things' that matter; power, greed, control When these three prevail, people lose, people are last People are now just objects, no longer subjects Objects to do the bidding of power, greed, and control Objects called consumers to grease the wheels of profit Profit for the few, the elite, the privileged When objects become subjects, when there's a change of heart Or, at worst, when hearts are broken through turmoil and chaos There will be change, change cannot be stopped Let us hope for the former, let us awake from our illusions Put people first, relationships first, love first Let us all be merciful, kind, and just - let us

George G
