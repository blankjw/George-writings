OPERATIVE COMPASSION

By George Gondron

OPERATIVE COMPASSION Where compassion is real, there is action Sorrow for the sufferings of others Coupled with the urge to help Help in minor, small, and seemingly unimportant ways It is a going toward, not a retreat from the wounded Toward the needy in body, soul, and spirit Even though we witness compassion in many creatures Humans have the greatest capacity for kindness and empathy And yet, they also have the ability to turn a blind eye I do believe the essence of being human is to be compassionate As well, I do know why some are not compassionate Just as I do know that life is not sacred to all people What we each live by is our own value system Our own sense of ethics and integrity and morality Aware or unaware Some see life as a whole, others as a disconnect Some see sameness, others only difference
