DRIFTERS

By George Gondron

DRIFTERS What's happened to the nuclear family? Is it now the global village? But a global village that's disconnected? We drift apart One marriage, two, maybe three Blended families but no nexus The present generation misses so much Are there family meals any longer? Family picnics, game nights, reunions? Is the 'techie' age to blame? Are we in love with our gadgets? Are they now our relationships? Have we drifted that far apart? That things mean more than people It is worth reflecting upon, worth questioning When I'd rather text you, than talk to you Antiseptic interaction, no human to human Enamored with 'selfies' and instagrams Facebook, twitter, and the internet Can healthy relationships endure without human presence, human listening, human touch? The definition of community is changing Has changed I'm not so sure it's human Be careful of how far you drift Don't lose your connections They may not return

George G
