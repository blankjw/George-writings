THE LIE - Copy

By George Gondron

THE LIE Let’s begin with the truth Individuals, groups, all forms of entities social, religious, and political Lie for one reason, self-interest, including you and I If self-interest were not the reason, the truth would suffice The lie is always hiding something For example, in our current political environment When someone says, I don’t like him/her/them And when asked what it is they don’t like, and the reply is ‘I just don’t like them’, it’s a disguised lie with an underlying Prejudice, bias, judgment, fear, or projection For in truth, what we don’t like in others is also in us Just denied, hidden, or ignored, called projection Unless we use due diligence in our own search for truth We have no way of knowing if what we see/hear on social media Is true, factual, reliable, beneficial, or helpful It’s probably not, we’re being sold something for the self-interest Of others, unfortunately, the truth doesn’t sell, the lie does Truth has a way of forcing us to look at ourselves The lie, on the other hand, allows us to look at others You know, blame, point fingers, deny, judge, etc., et., etc. All of which in the end, makes us the problem, not the solution OH, and when we make excuses for what we think, feel, or do With no factual or experiential basis, we lie We defend, we cover up, motivated by fear absent of love The truth never has to be defended, the lie always does The most damaging of all lies are the ones we tell ourselves The liars are many, and the truth seekers are few Let’s you and I be truth seekers Let’s you and I become change-makers To remain silent or to support the lie out of fear Is to be part of the problem, part of the dysfunction We all have the power, and the ability to change for the better Choose a few minutes of silence, stillness, with Presence daily Empty your mind of its incessant and repetitive thoughts Connect inner and outer, bring balance to your life Focus on your breath or a short mantra, rinse and repeat Your heart, soul, and spirit hold your true self, have your answers

George G
