WASTED TIME AND ENERGY

By George Gondron

WASTED TIME AND ENERGY We spend untold amounts of time and energy In our attempts to change others in a direct way It doesn’t work and never will As a health professional, I can attest to the fact That seventy percent of new clients Sought counseling in order to change someone else Not themselves Ironically, if instead we sought to change ourselves Rather than change others, and that was our true focus Then the behavior of others would no longer be an issue For as we become more aware and conscious How we interact, react, and respond to others Is a matter of choice and eventually we realize Every thought, feeling, and action of ours Is about choice, not about ‘knee jerk’ reactions Unfortunately, we don’t live in a culture Where people take responsibility for their own lives Seldom are they accountable for their own consequences It is so obvious in every facet of this society The context doesn’t matter, it’s a ‘blame game’ If you purposely pursue your own personal growth Your relationships will change Not because of your new sense of self Your increase in awareness and consciousness But because people now perceive you as different And being different for many people is uncomfortable People prefer meaningless conversations, simple ‘chit chat’ To discussions of depth, to reasoned understanding Being drawn to your own individuation process You will eventually engage in some form of inner connection Easily accomplished through a meditative/contemplative practice A few minutes each day for silence, stillness, and Presence Presence being your image of a Creator, or if you prefer, God A time to empty your ever-busy mind of thoughts To become aware of the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit The innate truth of your true self given at birth To be connected, inner and outer

George G
