WITHIN

By George Gondron

WITHIN Within the whirlwind is the gentle breeze Within the great ocean is the single drop Within the storm there is always shelter Within the sadness lies a hidden joy Within the pain there is a lesson Within the sickness there is a cure There is always something more than is seen More than is heard More than is thought and felt The artist sees more in her painting than I can see The sculptor sees more in the clay than I observe Creation begins within the imagination The flash in consciousness, a new awareness The flickering of the flame in the darkness of doubt The pause after the deep sigh The silence between the thoughts Within the predawn awakening, there is more And then, there is the Great Within The living spark within each soul The joining of the human and divine Creation of the Creator, infused with God's DNA Separated only by the mind Never by the heart We all have our Within We cannot escape it, it's always there Waiting to be known Waiting to be present Waiting to love Always, waiting within

George G
