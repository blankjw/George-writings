IT'S HERE AGAIN

By George Gondron

IT’S HERE AGAIN Yes, it’s the Holiday Season Commercialized earlier and earlier as each year passes Of course, it is supposed to be a Christian celebration But honestly, it’s nothing more than a capitalistic endeavor For the rich and poor alike to spend money A designated day to celebrate the birth of a child Who is recognized as both human and divine The only time in recorded history when a creator God Chooses to become human I doubt He would approve of the current situation at all A time of the year when suicide spikes When people are stressed, filled with anxiety, not happiness Marriages are destroyed, families broken apart Credit card debt expands Maybe it would be a good idea to reflect on the benefits Of this Holiday Season and who really benefits And who suffers, who really pays the price The paradox – on TV the ads portray happy celebrations Families uniting, excitement over gift exchanges Walk thru your local mall or Wally World and Target Look at the faces, the body language, the palpable anxiety Maybe there are a few who sincerely enjoy the celebration Take the time to reflect on the actual event being recognized But we all know who the real winners are The corporate moguls of wealth and power Filling their coffers with ill-gotten riches as they prey On the disadvantaged, the poor, the disenfranchised But then again, we can look forward to another 364 days To struggle and pay off the debt we incurred for the celebration Rinse and repeat next year and the years that follow Not until those who have the power no longer control The narrative and the ultimate outcome, nothing will change Absolutely nothing, history is living proof of that observation We are role models for the young, what are they learning? Take time for reflection, a few minutes of silence, with stillness, With Presence, every day Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit – they wait

George G
