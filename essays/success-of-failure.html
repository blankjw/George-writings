SUCCESS OF FAILURE

By George Gondron

SUCCESS OF FAILURE Beware of those who have never failed There is no worthwhile success without failure There is no breakthrough without the struggle Our defeats carry more potential than our wins Our 'downfalls' are necessary for the 'ego' To keep us humble, not inflated, not prideful Pride of self is much different than pride of others Count your failures as blessings They are a reminder of the value of effort I would much rather be taught by a person A person who knows rupture, knows defeat One who has not inherited their purview Who's security has been hard won with compassion Not wealth gleaned from the sweat of others I can learn from those who have stumbled and fallen Those who don't look to be 'bailed out' Not those, Who sleep easily with no guilt for their actions No remorse of how their success was achieved Not those, they can teach me nothing Success gained through failure understands balance Understands fairness, equality, honesty, integrity We cherish various memories of our history Never forget your failures They are a part of our accomplishments They brought us a gift A gift that successes only cannot give They keep us in touch with our humanness With the mystery of our lives Appreciation for our struggles Cherish the failures preceding the successes

George G
