To Whom It May Concern September 2

By George Gondron

To Whom It May Concern September 2, 2014 Marcy Martinez, FNP-BC Supervisor of Billing

About a year or so ago, blood work ordered by your facility in regards to a six months checkup for myself, was partially denied by Medicare. After several discussions with Quest Diagnostics (the lab where the work was done), a Medicare representative, and Christy Lawrence, the issue was finally settled. I was not privy to all the discussions, so I do not know how the problem was solved. You will find enclosed another denial by Medicare on blood work performed at Quest this past July as a result of orders from your facility. I would sincerely appreciate someone investigating this denial. I believe it's not the responsibility of Quest (unless they filed the wrong 'coding'). I've not had problems with any of the lab work being paid by Medicare and my supplement carrier for many years. Please, have someone investigate this problem as soon as possible, so that it doesn't drag on for several months. I would appreciate a reply within two weeks. As mentioned above, you will find enclosed correspondence from Quest and Medicare regarding this issue. Thank you in advance for your assistance.

Sincerely, George Gondron
