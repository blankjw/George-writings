HYPOCXRISY IS ALIVE AND WELL

By George Gondron

HYPOCRISY IS ALIVE AND WELL Ignorance, the handmaid that fuels the astounding levels of Hypocrisy alive and well in our nation today The racism, homophobia, self-righteous indignation all Grounded in fear and the refusal to know One of the hallmarks of ignorance, it doesn’t want to know For knowing requires effort, learning, critical thinking Ignorance, on the other hand, only requires acceptance Stir my emotions, ignite my fear, give me the answers And I’ll believe, I’ll follow, I’ll accept the lie As long as I can be convinced that fault lies elsewhere But not in me, I can be manipulated and controlled I can say, ‘see, it’s their fault, not mine, they are the bad guys’ Unfortunately, those who seek power and control always Motivate through fear, through negativity, through conspiracy But never through kindness, compassion, acceptance, or love Their goal is to separate and divide, not unite and include Their character lacks integrity, morality, honesty, and decency They proudly demonstrate the nature of hypocrisy You know, one way today, the opposite way tomorrow They fan the flames of discord, concerned only with their thirst For dominance, to be ‘numero uno’ regardless the cost The law has no meaning for them, for they are the law Those that follow them are only ‘objects’ to be used for their pleasure They are nothing more than a means to an end, their end Ignorance is willing to embrace the lies while being stabbed In the back by the liar, for the liar has no allegiance to anyone Unless you follow them, without question, you are useless And you will suffer their degradation, their wrath Yes, hypocrisy is alive and well, as the saying goes, ‘we are willing To cut off our noses in spite of our faces’ Please seek balance in your life, don’t be a prisoner of your emotions Don’t allow the lies to become a false truth Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Isness Empty your mind, connect to your inner core your internal reference Focus on your breath or a short mantra, breathe in, breathe out Rinse and repeat, listen to your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, they wait

George G
