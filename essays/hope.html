HOPE

By George Gondron

HOPE There are many aspects to hope, many sources Hopes that arise from desperate need Food, clothing, shelter, safety and security Others that arise from excessive greed Money, power, wealth, domination and control From the selfless to the selfish From the 'givers and the takers' Both fall in the realm of hope But, both spring from different sources One, driven by love and compassion The other, by false security and superficiality Expectations are filled with hope Optimism is filled with hope Only the individual knows from whence springs their hope Is it hope for harmony, peace, justice, and mercy? Hope for acceptance, for a sense of belonging? For being a part of, not separate from? Hope is a gift, it comes with our life It bears the confidence, and the wish, of fulfillment It knows that when all else seems lost, there is still hope It never gives in to despair Hope remains Hope endures Hope is

George G
