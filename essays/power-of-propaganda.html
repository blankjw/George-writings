POWER OF PROPAGANDA

By George Gondron

POWER OF PROPAGANDA To not understand the power of propaganda Is to live life totally unaware And, the unaware life is not worth living It is simply an existence of one day following another Nothing more than repetitive routine, and unending distractions With maybe temporary respites of imagined gratification Which soon grows old, and we seek the next distraction Advertising and marketing are nothing more than propaganda Just called by another name, but the same process Purchase this, believe this, hate this, fear this, condemn this In other words, don't think, don't reflect, don't question Simply believe the words, whether true or false, just believe There are literally billions of dollars spent on propaganda And for what purpose? Because propaganda sells, propaganda controls, propaganda is power It is the ability to sell glasses to a blind person Ice to an Eskimo, and lies to the uninformed The ability to turn a nation into a mass of 'lemmings' Witness the reality of history, the reality of a Germany The reality of 'apartheid' and 'slavery', and 'ethnic cleansing' The reality of deaths of millions of Native Americans And you say, 'that can't happen now, can't happen here' Oh, but it can, and it's easily explained It does not take an Einstein to understand, it's quiet obvious We would rather have someone else think for us Than think for ourselves, critically think Which is nothing more than saying, 'let someone else do the work' 'I'll take my chances' Yes we will, and we'll also suffer the consequences To understand the seriousness of this problem Only requires a simple example When we are able to deny obvious confirmed facts We are our own worst enemy, we create the chaos that follows There is very strong evidence there is a 'dumbing down' in America And the powerful, the unscrupulous, the selfish take notice We must think, question, reflect, and stop denying Or we will suffer the results, we will, and those that follow

George G
