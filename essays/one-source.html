ONE SOURCE

By George Gondron

ONE SOURCE The beginnings of all human life some 200,000 years ago In southern Africa, connect us all genetically to each other Each one of us can trace our beginnings back to that time Our DNA is more than 99.9% alike in all human beings Our physical differences arising out of climate conditions Migration and survival methods and evolution We are truly all more alike than unalike And yet we focus on our infinitesimal differences On our small contrasts rather than our overwhelming sameness We arise from this one source physically, our alpha point Just as the universe itself is born from one Source Scientifically called the 'big bang', but with purpose Held together with a consciousness beyond human understanding Science is about cause and effect, proven with facts With tools of measurement, and with quantum physics Which now speaks of a universe of intelligent design Religion is a historical story, told in the language of its origin During the period of its origin and ongoing history All metaphysical philosophies, all religions, all beliefs Speak of an original Source, and out of their core beliefs Arises a common thread of love, compassion, mercy Peace, kindness, happiness, joy, and humility Stories of connection and relationship And again, we focus on the differences rather than the sameness Rather than the similar core beliefs Whatever God you believe in, that God must weep Weep over the manner in which we destroy one another Because of insignificant differences Differences in appearance, in culture, in all 'ism's' Differences in belief of the one correct true Source Which exists only in the mind, not in the heart To destroy one another over who's God is right Is to believe in a very small God indeed, very small What needs to change is the human heart, not the Source What needs to change is the dualistic mind, not God We need to learn from silence, from stillness, from Presence Then we will change, then we will know God, and each other

George G
