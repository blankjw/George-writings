RESIST

By George Gondron

RESIST From the head or the heart From the mind or the soul Resistance is our teacher, our spiritual guide There is always a meaning in the resistance Always a lesson to be learned From the mind, a position of assurance From the heart a challenge to change Resistance, can be the denial of what's immutable The permeability of Love Naysayers resist change, cling to the routine Their Gods are idols, not made of flesh and bone Analytical existence, blind prognosticators Unable to see the connections, to embrace the All Unable to venture beyond the ego's limits Unable to welcome the Mystery, include the esoteric Content with worship at a distance Satisfied with sameness, business as usual To ignore resistance, to not ask the questions Is to deny Life Content as solitary, unaware of community To miss the value of connection, relationship, difference Reflect on your resistance Examine your objections Question your unyielding positions They all are your teachers Positions for possible change Or chains that bind and impede Reflect

George G
