LET'S TALK ABOUT CHANGE

By George Gondron

LET’S TALK ABOUT CHANGE The mature adult will at some point realize that All important change is about changing themselves Keep in mind, change is a matter of choice, always Also keep in mind the ego will resist change at all costs So, what are those experiences that open us to the Possibility for personal change, that shatter our resistances One, the experience of deep emotional pain Two, the experience of unconditional love Three, a daily meditative/contemplative practice Any one of the three can initiate a breakthrough to change But here’s the ‘fly in the ointment’ Our culture affords us the opportunity to avoid any Or all three through medication and/or distraction Through the lack of a connection to our own inner core Or our refusal to educated ourselves, relying on social Media for our truth, our reality, our own dysfunction You are depressed? here take this pill My love frightens you? perhaps you don’t love yourself You avoid silence and stillness? Maybe you’re only a Human doing and have forgotten how to be a human being If the energy we humans waste attempting to change others Was used to change ourselves, the world would be A much better place by far As long as production and consumption is the name of The game, human relationships are secondary And as long as we resist necessary personal change We will continue to blame others for our own discomfort Please, adopt a meditative/contemplative practice Experience the teaching of silence, stillness, and Presence For just a few minutes each day empty your mind Be present to your image of Universal Consciousness, Isness Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, they wait

George G
