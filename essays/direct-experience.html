DIRECT EXPERIENCE

By George Gondron

DIRECT EXPERIENCE The most profound, numinous, and powerful experiences Of our individual lives Can only be known through direct experience Not intellectually or through our five senses Only through the ‘ah ha’ moments that occur spontaneously Even more mysteriously, something acts as a trigger For those experiences and we never know what that trigger may be Pain, joy, loneliness, silence, anxiety, etc., etc., etc. All have the potential to be a trigger, a stimulus When there is no connection between the mind, the ego And our inner core, our heart, soul, and spirit Then we are trapped in the belief that everything that can be known Can only be known through the mind, the five senses, the feelings To not have that inner connection, we in essence Are cut off from reality To further complicate our path of knowing We seem to forget the importance of critical learning We rather simply take in what’s offered and call it the truth And the result is dysfunction and chaos Witness the environment we live in today, on every level A dualistic environment that only knows oppositional thinking Right/wrong, good/bad, liberal/conservative, never both/and Dualism is the nemesis of our culture When everything in life is not about exclusion but inclusion Our actions reflect that we prefer to be separate Rather than together, anti-rather than pro The danger to all existence on this planet is one thing The human mind absent an inner connection, an inner core Without some mass paradigm shift in human consciousness Our next best path is to change ourselves, as individuals That we might have direct experience of the mystery, the numinous That path begins with a few minutes of silence, stillness, with Presence (one’s image of a Creator) each day Moments to empty our ‘monkey minds’ that dwell on the same Thoughts day after day – incessantly This contemplative practice brings awareness of the quiet whispers Of our heart, soul, and spirit – where our truth and healing abide

George G
