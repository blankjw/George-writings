THE FOOD OF THE MIND

By George Gondron

THE FOOD OF THE MIND Our physical bodies need nourishment that we may survive Our minds as well also need nourishment that we may survive But surviving and being well are entirely different What we ingest either physically or intellectually results in our well-being as well as our survival We can, and often do, accomplish one without the other Throughout history we can easily prove that the ego more often than not, chooses survival over well-being There are many reasons for this behavior but the biggest is we don’t see ourselves as being ‘one with’ but rather ‘one being apart from’ But alas there’s far too much for the few and far too little for the many One thing for sure, we’ve enough processed food and enough hyperbole, gaslighting, and alternate facts to be assured that neither our bodies or our minds will be nourished And the result leads to ignorance and then to stupidity The definition of stupidity is nothing more than hurting yourself while at the same time hurting others Most of our population seem to believe they can discover the truth. from simply watching their favorite media source But that source, rather than nourishing the mind, is in truth a poison What we willingly accept with no filter, is our ultimate downfall Our minds, like the slogan adopted by the advent of computers, become receptacles, garbage in, garbage out Once we discover an argument, we seem to believe we can defend, nothing else matters, not even obvious facts We minimize the gift we each have of free will, of individual choice Critical thinking, doubt, research, and relevant questions have no place in our current awareness or consciousness We are nothing more than sponges, accepting what we’re fed readily drinking the Kool Aid flavor of the day We have become as Pogo said, ‘our own worst enemy’ Please, spend a few minutes a day in silence, stillness, with Presence Focus on your breath or a brief mantra Listen to the whispers of your inner guides, heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, the patiently wait

George G
