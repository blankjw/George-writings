MISLABELED NEEDS

By George Gondron

MISLABELED NEEDS When the needs of the many Are usurped by the needs of the few Needs are mislabeled The word in the name of the few has a much different meaning It is disguised power It is control It is greed It is ego It is not need The arena doesn't matter, public or private The board room or the strategy room The council room or the war room The desires of the few become the false desires of the many The CEO represents the stockholders, not the workers The politician represents the lobbyist, not their constituents The religious leader represents the large donor, not the poor Not the disenfranchised, the needy, the marginalized The many, the masses, the majority In the end, have no voice to represent their need As long as the mislabeled needs of the few As long as the continued manipulation of the process Is controlled by a minority Nothing will change, it's only more of the same But what more can we expect when life is empty Made up of ego, mind, and intellect alone Void of heart, of soul, and of spirit When what I want, regardless of the venue Is more important than what you may need As sad as it may be Those who lead with their hearts, their soul, and their spirit As well as their minds Don't last very long, are not in demand And yet the paradox is, they are needed the most Don't give up, refuse to give in Stand against those who abuse through their false ideals Support those in need, whether the many or few They need our support, they really do

George G
