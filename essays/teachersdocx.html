TEACHERS

By George Gondron

TEACHERS In the realm of the spirit, the mystery we call life To believe that there's only one teacher Is a trap which leads to division, discord, and dissension Truth is never found in just one voice, one belief, one place It is found in the most unsuspecting places If we are able to see, able to hear It is found where we find compassion and mercy Peace and justice, and selfless unconditional love Connection, human connection is not about belief It is about relationship, it is about what is already present And unfortunately, what keeps us from seeing, from hearing What is already and has always been present More often than not is our need to cling to a belief Our reluctance to experience what already exists Our programming and conditioning by well meaning others That they have the answers, they know the way A true teacher is one that helps us find our own path Our own connection to the ultimate Source Not one that insists we believe what they believe But one that offers us tools to discover that Source Discover that connection that has always been If this amazing universe is not just happenstance Then there is a first cause, a meaning, a purpose and direction That first cause, that Source is known only through experience That is the purpose of relationship, of connection If we know the importance of human relationships How much more important to know, to experience The ultimate relationship, which has always been The energy of that Source abides within the entire universe And especially within you and I All the world's great teachers, prophets, sages, and nature Teach us about an inner resource we all carry Teach us about silence, stillness, connection, and relationship Their names don't matter, their basic truths do matter Not the distortions we've added to their teaching Especially those we've added to avoid their basic message Take time for silence, stillness, and Presence; find your truth

George G
