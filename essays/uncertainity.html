UNCERTAINITY

By George Gondron

UNCERTAINITY We currently live in times of great uncertainty Uncertainty only leads to fear Fear is the enemy of understanding Lack of understanding leads to faulty decisions Faulty decisions are the result of being influenced by our emotions To the detriment of our intellect and the outcome is ignorance Every major area of our existence is being thrown into uncertainty Our health, our safety, our financial wellbeing, our relationships Uncertainty has become the new normal, a feeling of insecurity A moment-to-moment hypervigilance of ’what’s next’ Our divisions within ourselves, within our own families, and others has become increasingly overstated and magnified Friends and acquaintances had fallen by the wayside because of minds that are closed, because of fear Uncertainty breeds contempt, even between the strongest of relationships, and the winner is ignorance, the refusal to learn Those in control, those who hold the power and the wealth maintain their positions by creating uncertainty utilizing the axiom, ‘divide and conquer’ And if we don’t follow the ‘pied piper’, we are the problem, the enemy, the fake news, the suckers and losers We are nothing more than a means to an end, their end At this juncture in our history, uncertainty reigns as we attempt to discern what’s behind the smoke and mirrors? What’s the ultimate goal of these millionaires and billionaires? I know for sure it’s not the wellbeing of you and I So how do we deal with the uncertainty? Is passivity the answer, is violence, there must be a middle way Only you can answer that question for yourself To awaken your own discernment and intuition, to connect to your internal resources, adopt a meditative/contemplative practice Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Focus on your breath or a short mantra, breathe in, breathe out The ego will resist, the mind will wander, simply rinse and repeat Listen to the whispers of your inner guides, heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers They bring balance to your life, inner and outer, they wait

George G
