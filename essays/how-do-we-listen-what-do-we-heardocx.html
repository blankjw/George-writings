HOW DO WE LISTEN, WHAT DO WE HEAR

By George Gondron

HOW DO WE LISTEN, WHAT DO WE HEAR What's hidden in the words we hear Beyond the obvious message Are we aware enough to hear what's not being spoken Most of us are not aware In fact, most of us don't hear at all Not out of disrespect, it's more subtle than that
