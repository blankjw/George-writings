UNCONDITIONAL LOVE

By George Gondron

UNCONDITIONAL LOVE I hear the words I see them written They cannot be grasped with my mind How is it possible? To love with no discrimination No distinction, no judgment No partiality or preference Without qualifications and not 'only ifs' There must be some logical reason For or against unconditional love Through the mind, there is none No rationalization This makes no sense There is no understanding of this platitude This throw away phrase used so carelessly And yet, it has an attraction, an allurement It somehow resounds with possibility This phrase, unconditional love I do know I know with my heart, my spirit, not my mind I know in the silence I know in the stillness I know in the Presence I know in the beginning, the middle, and the end All is love It is the alpha and the omega It is unconditional It is Love It Is

George G
