THE POWER OF ILLUSION

By George Gondron | Source: THE POWER OF ILLUSION.docx

THE POWER OF ILLUSION Magicians have always known the power of illusion Just as ministers of propaganda know the power of speech Our political system, our extreme capitalism Have learned both, very well The most proficient way to divide the masses Is to distract them, with negative rhetoric, verbal abuse It stirs the emotions, and makes the 'other guy' the culprit We fill our anxiety with empty gratifications Believing the charlatans as they say This or that will make us happy, bring us joy And the wheel grinds on, rolling to the next desire Lives pretending to be full, hollow and empty on the inside It matters not the political or religious affiliation We're all it seems dualistic thinkers Well, maybe 'thinking' is stretching it a bit We'd much rather blame, project, and deny Than think, at least critically Leave the thinking to the pundits, we'll just follow them And bask in the illusions they continue to feed us No doubts, no questions, as the blind lead the blind It's really understandable, you know All the 'ism's' have become more important than the individual Much more important than the ism called humanism Where does the human being fall on the continuum of importance Always after power, greed, and control, they are first And even if we're a close second, it's only as a consumer Making the powerful, the greedy, the controllers More and more secure, attached to their too many assets As we continue to believe the 'trickle down' rhetoric Even though it's proven to be a lie The 'trickle' stops at the top The CEO's continue to give themselves fat raises, fancy 'perks' As the workers wage remains stagnant, with fewer benefits Yes, the illusions hold us in check, unless we decide to wake up But, that seems doubtful, we're way too busy to awaken But if perhaps we do, let's hope it's not too late At least for our children and grandchildren

George G
