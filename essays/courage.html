COURAGE

By George Gondron

COURAGE To understand and yet resist, to turn away from what is wrong Is to watch and witness the vanishing virtue of courage Time slowly does make cowards of us all Courage is about choosing the hard way The easy way requires no courage at all, it’s the coward’s way To lack courage is to be defensive, biased, prejudiced, and exclusive It is to turn away from witnessed abuse, from discrimination Throughout our day we are making hundreds of choices, unaware For we are not preprogrammed Every thought, feeling, and action or inaction we have is chosen Every fork in the road is a choice point, right, left, or move forward Those are our options, oh wait, we can just remain stuck as well The courageous are willing to risk, to be vulnerable, to grow Being stuck to the courageous is not an option it is a prison In the face of obvious wrong, of false propaganda, to remain silent Is to lack courage and be complicit with the wrongful exaggerations A lie by any other name, is still a lie What we hear and see on social media is not reliable truth It is more about manipulation, alternative facts, and lies It’s about selling you something that’s not worth buying Asking you to accept a frame of reference that has only one option Believe what we are selling and nothing else, never question I would much rather listen to the person who is unsure about Matters of importance then the one who is closed to alternatives It takes courage to have courage, to discover one’s own truth I would much rather learn from those who share their failures Than those who brag about their successes, small minded people Success without failure is called luck, success after failure is called Work, we either choose fear over courage or courage over fear Conscience and courage are first cousins, to follow an informed Conscience is a courageous task, to ignore one’s conscience is Always the path of least resistance, allowing fear to rule Be courageous, adopt a meditative/contemplative practice Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Resist the ego’s desire to wander, stay focused Listen for the whispers of your inner guides, heart, soul, spirit Awaken your own discernment ability, be courageous

George G
