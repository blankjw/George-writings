MENTAL CONTAMINATION

By George Gondron

MENTAL CONTAMINATION Oh, how we do contaminate our minds Ruminating on the past, yesterday, last week Worry about tomorrow, the day after, next year Wasted moments, wasted energy, wasted effort The ego loves the past, future game It keeps us from living in the now What's real is only in the present moment How much we miss through gymnastics of the mind Occupied with fear and doubt, how can one be present? What do we not see or hear, in the now? What passes us by? What opportunities are overlooked? The mind uses selected focus to avoid the real Focus on past or future events The real is not yesterday, not tomorrow, only now The laughter of a child is different on each occasion The song of a bird is not repeated the same The hug not given is lost forever The, I love you, is not given in the past, not spoken in the future The laughter, the song, the hug, the love, happen in the now. The emotions of the past, the anxieties of the future Keep us from the sweetness of the now Don't miss the now Don't miss your life Don't miss you

George G
