IT'S A MATTER OF TIME

By George Gondron

IT'S A MATTER OF TIME Sorry, I don't have the time An interesting premise, when that's all we do have Easier to say, rather than it's not important to me Notice it's to me, not to us That's the world of me's, no us's The ego, the me, cares to the extent it's not bothered It's not inconvenienced It may be smiling on the outside, oh but inside Please, don't trouble me with matters that may create anxiety, or doubt I have my agenda, you can have yours I am comfortable, do not disturb me Don't confront me with my own dysfunction It's a waste of time I insulate and protect myself with my things I distance myself from my yearnings When there is no more time What then? Is it still simply a matter of time?

George G
