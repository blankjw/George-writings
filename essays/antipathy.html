ANTIPATHY

By George Gondron | Source: ANTIPATHY.docx

ANTIPATHY Hostility, opposition, aversion, abhorrence, disgust, animosity All definitions of antipathy supported by the underlying emotion Of fear, a fear that more often than not is the result of a closed mind And further, a closed mind is the hallmark of ignorance And ignorance is a choice, the refusal to learn The refusal to understand leads to fear Unaddressed fear leads to hatred, where hatred lives love is absent Where there is no love, death in all its many forms, wins To accept without evidence the results of antipathy Is to be a part of the problem, not the solution It is to be in lockstep with all the negativity of antipathy Fear by any other name is still fear Just as love by any other name is still love We know others because of their actions, not their promises Do their words separate and divide, or include and unite Do they wish to protect and defend all humanity Or do they wish to isolate and segregate A consciousness born from antipathy doesn’t care, please care Hatred creates its own results, as does kindness, compassion, and love Let us not fuel the tensions we feel within us and our society Through feeding our antipathy, rather let us ask what our tensions Are attempting to teach us about ourselves first, our culture second The saying, ‘united we stand, divided we fall’ is an obvious truth To reject out-of-hand what may be offered by another That might be valid information is to be a part of the problem It is also an indication of a closed mind, a mind that is fixed A mind that is willing to choose ignorance over truth, over facts If we no longer have a desire to learn, question, doubt And only accept, we are the fools, we are our own worst enemy There is no age limit to learning, becoming more aware and conscious There is only a lack of desire, a yearning to know Adopt a meditative/contemplative daily practice Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Isness Empty your mind, focus on your breath or a short mantra Listen for the whispers of your inner guides, heart, soul, spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers Connect to your inner resources, they patiently wait

George G
