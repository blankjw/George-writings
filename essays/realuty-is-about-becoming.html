REALUTY IS ABOUT BECOMING

By George Gondron

REALITY IS ABOUT BECOMING All of reality is about transformation, change, a continuous metamorphosis to the better, the more whole and congruent The nature of reality is not to remain static or fixed, unlike the ego which resists change and clings to the familiar regardless of the costs, regardless of the pain, regardless of facts to the contrary There is nothing more stubborn and self-assured as the human ego Millions, if not billions, have lost their lives as a result of the narrowmindedness and selfishness of the human mind disconnected from its own inner core and internal resources The real enemy throughout history has been and still is, the ignorance of a ‘closed mind’, a willingness to destroy for personal reasons We spend untold amounts of time, money, and energy attempting to change others and resist change ourselves, it never works Progress, positive progress, progress that benefits the many and not just the few is the nature of reality becoming toward whatever, if any, the final nexus may be It is impossible to stop positive change, progress, evolution, natural development, but the human ego will do its best to determine the outcome of ‘reality becoming’ in its egotistical favor And currently that outcome is in the hands of a few, a very few, that demonstrate very clearly their intentions, nothing short of total and absolute loyalty and obedience Just kiss the ring, bow, and reply, ‘yes Master’ Believe it or not, there is such a thing as, ‘a point of no return’ Please, don’t allow that to happen The planet will survive, but the human species is quite capable of of destroying itself, especially with a ‘stable genius’ at the helm The importance of educating ourselves, not through social media but through our own critical thinking, doubt, questions, research, and reflections cannot be overstated We alone are responsible for the choices we make, or do not make When we refuse to educate ourselves, the default is chosen ignorance Bring balance to your life, connect inner and outer Adopt a meditative/contemplative practice Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Isness Listen for the whispers of your inner guide, heart, soul, spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, they wait

George G
