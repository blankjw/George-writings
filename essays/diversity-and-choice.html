DIVERSITY AND CHOICE

By George Gondron

DIVERSITY AND CHOICE Without diversity and choice Creativity dies, progress dies, evolution dies Yet the human ego often rejects diversity And abhors choice which is not its own The result stagnation, atrophy, death A mentality of let us go back to the ‘good ole days’ Let us maintain our polarized and black-and-white thinking Let us continue the ‘tribal’ mentality that exists today A mentality that separates and divides Look carefully, objectively Perceive with clarity, see the forest and the trees Where does power abide, where does control abide? Where does wealth abide? In politics, in economics, in government, in religion How much real diversity exists in those areas? The human mind, in order to fully develop Needs an environment that is conducive to growth Not a preprogrammed agenda that is inflexible Just as all life flourishes in an environment based On its particular needs So too does the human mind, heart, soul, and spirit And each child is unique with its own creative nature Where diversity and choice are lacking The one to suffer is the child, always This means, in the end, the entire culture suffers as well An educational mentality of ‘don’t do as I do, do as I say do’ As a result, the measure of excellence in education Begins to fall, becoming a nation of ‘learned ignorance’ Becoming automatons as opposed to self-actualized Human beings, each with a creative and unique ability Social media has become our influencer, our teacher, Our go-to for any and everything With no need for critical thinking or reflection Just open the tap and fill the mind with your desired preference If people do not become more important than ‘things’ Our destination is bleak at best Support diversity and choice, it matters

George G
