BELIEF

By George Gondron

BELIEF The most reliable form of belief Is that which is the result of personal experience Otherwise, our belief has the possibility Of being totally false or only partially true Here is the danger As the digital age explodes the various forms Of social media, we are exposed more and more To those formats that target our emotions Not our rationality nor our intellect All forms of social media, including especially The gathering of large crowds to listen to a speaker Have the advantage of manipulating their listeners Through words that stir-up their emotions And once the emotions rule Real truth no longer matters And far too often the lie becomes the truth Once the emotions take over Discernment is thrown out the window Right and wrong no longer matter All the despots throughout history Knew and know the power of propaganda The power of attacking the emotions And avoid speaking to rationality and the intellect They are masters of division Of pitting us against each other And fomenting conspiracy theories ad nauseum Their true purpose is not to unite but divide Not to promote kindness and compassion But only fear and division Once we give up our ability to doubt To question, to think critically, to reflect We then become nothing more than sheep Following a false shepherd Even when we believe we’re exercising our freedom We are doing so through the lens of another Don’t be fooled, be the captain of your own ship Don’t let someone else determine your course

George G
