TRUTH NO LONGER MATTERS

By George Gondron | Source: TRUTH NO LONGER MATTERS.docx

TRUTH NO LONGER MATTERS When the lie becomes the norm When fiction becomes fact When power at the top in all domains is never questioned The result, the lie becomes the truth More division, more separation, more discrimination More religious persecution, more fundamentalism More corporate abuse, more political pandering More deception, more lies to cover up lies It's almost as if our drug of choice has become what's untrue Even in the face of proof to the contrary When facts contradict the narrative, don't support the story We still believe the lie Look, this is black - no, you say, it is white It has become that obvious, to those that see It has become that dangerous, to those that hear We follow the 'carrot on the stick', willingly If we ever had the ability to reason, it has been lost 'Knee jerk reaction' has taken the place of critical thinking We are sliding down a slippery slope A slope of untruths, misrepresentations, and distortions The bigger the 'whopper' the more we believe What excites us, what energizes us, what gives us a 'high' Is what we believe with no evidence at all, accept the lie It is our need to find fault in everyone, in everything Except ourselves The last place we look for the discomfort that we feel Is at our self, it's easier to blame the other or others We say to ourselves in a half-hearted manner 'How can I be the problem? I've done nothing wrong' Perhaps therein lies the answer Maybe we have done nothing wrong, deliberately But, what have we done right? Have we looked for the facts, do we question and doubt? Do we think for ourselves or let others think for us? We are responsible for what we think, feel, and do No one else, don't throw away your responsibility Don't be one of the blind, being led by the blind

George G
