THIS IS OUR MAKING

By George Gondron | Source: THIS IS OUR MAKING.docx

THIS IS OUR MAKING Our go-to position in times of discomfort here in the USA Is blame In truth, the reality of our discomfort is a matter of choice Maybe not a first choice But the accumulation of our previous choices That being true, it is only ourselves that can change our path No one else We alone or responsible for our misery and our happiness No one else To make the best of a bad situation is also a matter of choice It’s so much easier to use psychological defense mechanisms To deal with our dysfunctions than take ownership The gift of life Is free But that freedom has a responsibility, not only to itself But to all life We do not live in a vacuum, not islands unto ourselves We are stardust, connected to all things, original energy In our journey we make choices, daily, moment by moment Mostly unconsciously, seldom purposefully But rarely do we ever reflect on the consequences The consequences of those choices And most of those choices are based on want, not need Or put another way, based on desire and attachment And it’s that very desire and attachment that leads to Our own suffering, yes we are our own making For what we’re attached to, what we can’t let go of In thought, feeling or action has power over us Which is a way of realizing we don’t have power over Our own lives, our attachments do If your making has no internal reference, an inner source Life is all external, add this, a meditative/contemplative path Spend a few minutes each day in silence, in stillness Be present to Presence, your image of Isness, of God Touch the innate inner truth of your ‘true self’ As opposed to the egoic self, the ‘false self’ Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They have your answers and they patiently wait

George G
