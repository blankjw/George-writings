WHO FRAMES THE NARRATIVE OF HISTORY THE WINNERS

By George Gondron

WHO FRAMES THE NARRATIVE OF HISTORY THE WINNERS Much of recorded history is written by the winners Not the losers, at best, history is only half true At worst, it can be a total distortion The research and untiring efforts of those who seek the truth And attempt to correct the false narrative of history Are often ignored and ridiculed for their efforts The two areas of falsity and distortion are primarily religion And our human-to-human relationships, interpersonal actions Science for the most part can avoid this dilemma For science can demonstrably be proven through replication Of all species we humans are the most dangerous The most destructive, destructive through fear and greed Through our assumed rights one over another An obvious observation as we witness the carnage wrought By organized religion throughout the centuries And still being done today in less obvious ways And then there’s the ongoing issue of racism Simply defined as ‘you are different and don’t belong’ It’s not a matter necessarily of color, but of difference And what is different is to be feared and avoided Taken one step further, is to be controlled or eradicated Eradicated through genocide, in Germany 6 million Jews Ukraine 7 million, Bangladesh 3 million, Cambodia 3 million And last but not least in our own country We eradicated an estimated 80 to 100 million Native Americans But you won’t find that in our history books If you can see the forest in spite of the trees One doesn’t have to be the ‘sharpest tack in the box’ To understand that in Europe and in our nation White is right in all circumstances regardless of the arena Equality, inclusion, cooperation, conciliation, all should be Our narratives of truth, our winners, but they’re not When mind, heart, soul, and spirit are not connected We witness the dysfunction, clearly and plainly Connect all those parts in yourself, discover your true self Embrace Love, reject fear, become fully human, fully alive

George G
