WHITE MALE SUPREMACY

By George Gondron

WHITE MALE SUPREMACY We see the results, we witness the disintegration Of hundreds of years of white male supremacy They become angrier as they lose their grip Their grip on a culture that has been under their sway For many years, for many reasons, most selfish The absence of the feminine principle The control of religion without spirituality Dogmatic rhetoric of being the 'best', best what? Hidden in their greed, but framed as opportunity Relying on power, manipulation, and control as their guides Professing their own 'golden rule', because they have the gold Their fear is palpable as they cling to their attachments Their 'guns', their 'flag waving', their limited perceptions Their ideas are the right ideas, their way, the only way To be a white male is God's blessing Portraying Jesus as white with blonde hair, blue eyes The Protestant Jesus, far removed from reality A strange God that gives life to the vast majority of humans Who are not white males, did God make a mistake? Government controlled by white rich males Hidden in the shadows, behind the scenes Deciding what's best and for whom Buying politicians willing to prostitute themselves Rather than representing their constituents Represent all those who are not 'white males' Yes, it's beginning to fall apart As they scramble, throwing more money at the problem Buying their own brand of justice, their prescribed laws Written for their protection and their exception They march to the beat of their own 'drummer' Their only concern, for themselves, their power, their control Is the end of white male supremacy approaching? Is their 'house of cards' beginning to crumble? Let's hope so, let's hope this country can return Return to being land of the free, freedom for all A land of compassion, kindness, humility, and charity Where people become more important than 'things'

George G
