YEARNING

By George Gondron | Source: YEARNING.docx

YEARNING We seek without that which can only be found within A Divine spark, closer to us than we are to ourselves No tangible form, no sacred or profane ritual can fill that yearning It whispers to us in our quieter moments In the stillness of inactivity, a presence that has always been Tugging gently at the edges of our consciousness An undeniable yearning seeking union in the barely perceptible awareness of our minds Calling from the empty spaces between each thought A yearning unable to be expressed in words Barely acknowledged Forever present Yearning

George G
