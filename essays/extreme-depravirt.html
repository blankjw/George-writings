POEM

EXTREME DEPRAVIRT

By George Gondron

EXTREME DEPRAVITY The moral corruption of child abuse is second to none There is no greater act of degradation than that of a child Physically, emotionally, sexually, especially repeatedly There is no punishment too extreme for the perpetrator ‘Better to have a millstone tied around your neck And thrown into the sea than to abuse a little one’
