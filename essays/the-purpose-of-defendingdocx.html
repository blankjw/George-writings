THE PURPOSE OF DEFENDING

By George Gondron

THE PURPOSE OF DEFENDING We all do it We defend our behavior or another's behavior, or both Which, in and of itself is not necessarily the problem If there is a rational explanation for the defense But what rationale exists for obvious untruth? When the truth is as obvious as two plus two is four And yet we still defend the lie The problem lies within ourselves, not the truth In fact the lie can only continue to live if we defend it And upon examination, often the defense of the lie Has no logical or reasonable explanation, other than I just believe it, with no substantial evidence whatsoever The reason can't be from ignorance For ignorance is due to a lack of knowledge, unawareness The knowledge is there, it's obvious, it's palpable If we're able to see, hear, and think it's undeniable Maybe we defend the indefensible because of our egos We don't like to be wrong, made to appear a fool Or maybe we're just gullible Swallowing the bait 'hook, line, sinker' It does require a certain amount of humility or constraint To be able say, 'I was wrong, unwilling to check the facts' I fell for the rhetoric, the propaganda, the bluster, the lie' And perhaps the best excuse 'I chose the lesser of two evils' Well, I hope you enjoy living with that reality Major decisions necessarily require critical thinking Not 'knee jerk' reactions Not thoughts of 'I just don't like her/him' Good luck with that result also There is a saying, 'if it walks like a duck, quacks like a duck, And flies like a duck, it's a duck', not an eagle Some decisions in our lives require serious reflection For their results are extreme, substantial, and significant An uninformed decision is nothing more than a guess The ego guesses, the heart, soul, and spirit reflect Spend a little time in reflection, it's always the better path

George G
