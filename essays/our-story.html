OUR STORY

By George Gondron

OUR STORY Assuming we are humble enough to realize we are not perfect And believe me there are those who silently think they are Then the most important part of our personal growth Is a conscious decision to pursue our own transformation If we only believe we are nothing more than our thoughts Nothing more than our feelings and sensory perceptions Then the statement by the French philosopher and Jesuit priest Teilhard de Chardin, ‘We are not human beings having a spiritual experience. We are spiritual beings having a human experience.’ Makes no sense at all even if you consider yourself a religious person For personal transformation is not about religion, about being ‘saved’ It’s about a deeper relationship and connection to Isness, Mystery, Universal Consciousness, whatever name you prefer as God The search for meaning is universal and our personal story Hopefully includes that search, but culturally we are at a Disadvantage, being occupied with production and consumption Distracted by the next ‘shiny object’, living through our defenses All behavior, positive and negative, arises from a context An environment of love or fear, and the most horrible behavior When viewed in context can become logical, understandable Due to our frenzied lifestyles personal transformation is virtually Impossible, the context is not conducive to change We are too busy focused on the externals, the material, the BS We are too busy defending our own dysfunctional behavior We are unable to ‘see the forest, for the trees’ Unable to escape our self-made prisons to a healthier context Living, unknowingly, through the lens of fear and not love A full life, a human life, demands balance, inner and outer We obviously prefer one over the other, the outer Short of a miracle, we need the inner as well for transformation Add a meditative/contemplative practice to your life, new context Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Empty your ‘monkey mind’ of its incessant and repetitive thoughts Focus on your breath or a short mantra, breathe in, breathe out Your mind will wander, come back to the breath Listen for the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, have your answers, they wait

George G
