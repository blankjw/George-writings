THE GIFT OF MIND

By George Gondron

THE GIFT OF MIND The mind, connected to heart, soul, and spirit is a powerful resource, but without that connection often becomes very selfish, very self-centered, unconcerned for the safety and wellbeing of all humanity The power of consciousness, of thought, we take for granted The mind, with all its amazing power and potential we seldom, if ever, fully utilize through critical thought and reflection, we rather, simply take things for granted accepting without question even the most ridiculous circumstances There is an old saying, ‘the whole is greater than the sum of its parts’, our wholeness includes not only our minds, our consciousness, but the knowing held within our hearts, souls, and spirits A knowing that lies dormant when our focus is only on the externals of our existence, ignoring our internal resources innate with birth Without a connection to these resources, the mind can manifest the most terrible and horrible events human beings have ever committed All we need to do is review human history to witness the results When there is no balance between mind, heart, soul, and spirit there will always be suffering, and unfortunately, those that most often suffer are the innocent, those without power What is now happening within our own government is deliberate An unelected and un-vetted ‘hatchet’ man is destroying the lives of thousands of government employees without any comprehension of their importance This current administration is a perfect example of governance without heart, soul, and spirit, only an inflated narcissistic ego After all, if billionaires are running our government, we know what’s of value to them, power, control, and their wealth They have no concern about where their next meal comes from, or for that matter, their next anything, they can afford it To manifest balance within your own life, connect with your own internal resources, adopt a meditative/contemplative practice Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Focus on a short mantra or your breath, breathe in, breathe out The ego will resist, the mind will wander, rinse and repeat Listen to the whispers of your inner guides, heart, soul, spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, they wait

George G
