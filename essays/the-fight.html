THE FIGHT

By George Gondron

THE FIGHT If we are smart, if we are intelligent The only fight worth having in our lives Is the one we will and do have with ourselves Not others The greatest opponent we will ever face Looks back at us in the mirror We are both our greatest downfall And our noblest fulfillment It is only the few that reach this understanding To fight against anything other than the egoic self Is a fight that can never be won It is a fight against the wrong foe There is a famous quotation 'I have seen the enemy, and it is me' A very perceptive person arrived at this conclusion A lesson well worth learning, a valuable teaching When is the last time you heard of a fight for Compassion, empathy, loving kindness, or love? Almost all of our fights center around two concepts Power and control, with the icing being money and profit When is the last time you heard of someone fighting For their internal freedom, their interior transformation You don't and you won't, it doesn't make money The natural path of the human psyche is towards wholeness In every sense of the word That path has no set of directions, no pointers Saying this is the way, follow this teaching, read this book Go to this class, attend this workshop, gaze at your navel The words don't matter, they are not the way The way is found within each of us, individually The fight is ours alone and we must engage the battle If we are to become healthy human beings Our answers have always been, are now And always will be found within It is in the silence that we will learn In the stillness that we awaken And in the Presence that we find our life

George G
