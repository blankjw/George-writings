INFLUENCE

By George Gondron

INFLUENCE From the moment we are conceived Until the day we die We are constantly being influenced Through our senses, thoughts, and feelings Without filters, critical thinking, and self-analysis We are susceptible to influences that are At best life-enhancing, at worst life-debilitating There is one word that allows us To change course if those influences Have been damaging or harmful - choice Most everything we do or don’t do Is a matter of choice A life lived to the full is not easy And was never meant to be Failure is a necessary part of a full life It is part of the experience of being human Part of the path that leads to success It is one thing to remain shackled to harmful influences To accept the programming and conditioning We’ve all been exposed to It is quite another to have the courage to change If we do not pause Choose a path of objective self-reflection Critical self-analysis of what makes us ‘tick’ Of our thoughts, feelings, and actions We remain unconsciously tethered to patterns Which control our lives on every level More automatons than humans Living life with ‘knee-jerk’ reactions Pulled to and frow without conscious choice A fully human life is one grounded in Kindness, compassion, peace, mercy, and justice And most importantly forgiveness and love Forgiving ourselves the most for what we didn’t know And loving ourselves for choosing to change Everything we need to become fully human We already possess, It’s inside, waiting quietly

George G
