THE LOSS OF CRITICAL THINKING

By George Gondron

THE LOSS OF CRITICAL THINKING Just as easily the following could be titled The loss of continued open-minded education Either will fit the current reality The go-to source for controversial matters currently Is that which ignites our emotions And appeals to a sensibility that is dualistic Or polarized in its approach to almost any subject Perhaps better known as black and white thinking The type of thinking where facts don’t really matter And by the way, neither does truth It seems all the proof that is needed To support any position on any topic Is simply, because ‘so-and-so said’ And it really doesn’t matter about the credentials Of ‘so-and-so’ It requires very little reflection to ask What is this person or these persons gaining Through their attempts to convince us of anything? And in most cases, it’s a combination of financial gain And ego inflation, they now have followers They have all the necessary answers It works quite well in politics and religion Oh, and let us not forget the wonderful actions of Gossip and third-party information The underlying goal of which is to destroy relationships Without critical thinking Without devotion to realistic honest education We will continue on a perilous path Of losing our democracy, our republic It cannot improve under the current conditions It can only get worse Don’t follow ‘alternative facts’, don’t drink the ‘Kool-Aid’ Don’t swallow whole all that social media has to offer Question, doubt, reflect, be a critical thinker Continue to educate yourself Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, With Presence – empty your mind engage your heart

George G
