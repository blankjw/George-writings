LOVE DESERVED (1)

By George Gondron

LOVE DESERVED Is there such a concept? Love deserved? For many that seems to be true I will love you 'if' Or, I will love you 'when' Change, and I will love you If we put conditions on love, what then? Do we expect the same from others? They will only love us 'if' or 'when' Where did we learn that like and love are the same? I don't really have to like you to love you Your behavior perhaps, not your essence Conditional love creates distance not closeness To know, really know, unconditional love Is a gift beyond imagining It carries within it, the power to change To receive what is not deserved, not earned Notwithstanding my behavior, is a gift Love freely given goes far beyond merit It is, in essence, life Life is love Love is life The One whom we call Creator Loves everything into existence It would not be, if it were not loved There is no deserving There is only the receiving There is only Love

George G
