IF FACTS ARE NOT ENOUGH

By George Gondron

IF FACTS ARE NOT ENOUGH We appear to be at a treacherous crossroads A time like no other in our history Where facts really do not matter Two and two are four, but for too many The answer is five The need to be ‘right’ far exceeds The truth A clear and obvious truth When the state of consciousness The level of awareness is so dysfunctional In two significant aspects of our lives Namely politics and religion Made so very obvious as a result of dualism Life on all levels is encircled by a right/wrong, Black/white, good/bad, me against you, and Us against them attitude Then there is no hope for cooperation, conciliation, Or compromise An immature reality of, ‘my way or the highway’ Politics has become totally about power and money About control and manipulation Religion is nothing more than warming a church pew And putting money in a plate Also about power and control It’s not just Christian religion, the control is in all religions It has all become about worship, not action All about rites, rituals, dogma, and doctrine Not about spirituality, not about a serious prayer life Not a connection between the heart, soul, and spirit All cognitive, intellectual, and egoic Not about a relationship with a creator God whatsoever With the Isness of the universe We take our democracy, our Constitution for granted Those in Germany during the 1930s did as well That didn’t end well, Germany was a democracy Until Hitler gained control, it can happen here as well Be still, be silent, with Presence – connect with your heart

George G
