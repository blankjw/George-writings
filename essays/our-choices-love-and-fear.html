OUR CHOICES - LOVE AND FEAR

By George Gondron

OUR CHOICES – LOVE AND FEAR It can easily be defended that all positive emotions can be correlated with love and as well all negative emotions correlated with fear Whatever you may be experiencing emotionally, upon close examination can be connected to one or the other For example underlying the surface emotion of anger is an underlying fear In addition, love has the quality of being expansive, adding something positive to our essence, whereas fear is a reductive feeling, taking something away from our sense of self It’s in most cases an unconscious reaction that we seldom identify as fear based A level of consciousness that is more motivated by fear than love is obviously the dilemma we face today in our nation The purveyors of social media today focus their energies on two primary algorithms; one to sell us something, the other to arouse our fears Those who deal in propaganda, gaslighting, hyperbole, and alternative facts, do so primarily to arouse fear in one form or another Even those who equate what they’re attempting to sell us as something necessary and positive, the unspoken message still hints of fear if we don’t take the bait One of the major stumbling blocks in the dualism of love and fear Is our inability in a society based on production and consumption to learn and experience genuine ‘self-love’ Genuine self-love is an ‘inside’ job, a process of self-actualization Or individuation as described by Carl Jung, the Swiss psychoanalyst A matter of making unconscious content conscious All personal growth, the understanding of love and fear, the discovery of the true self as opposed to the egoic self is a lifelong journey Continuing education is key and necessary One addition is a meditative/contemplative daily practice A few minutes each day of silence, stillness, with Presence Presence being your image of a Creative Force, or God if you prefer Focus on your breath or a short mantra, breathe in, breathe out The ego will resist, the mind will wander, simply rinse and repeat Listen to the whispers of your inner guides, heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, have your answers, they patiently wait

George G
