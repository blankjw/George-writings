RELIGION FOUNDED ON LOVE

By George Gondron

RELIGION FOUNDED ON LOVE It is easier to say what love is not, than what love is For sure, we have relegated the word love to a single emotion When in fact, genuine love is hard, it is a sacrifice It is about giving, sharing, taking care of, not just receiving Often what we call love is better defined as gratification Personal satisfaction, I got what I wanted, even though it’s wrapped in the form of a ‘look what I did for you’ Love one another as I have loved you – Jesus But we don’t Hatred does not cease by hatred, but only by love – Buddha But we hate Love, never claims, it ever gives = Mahatma Gandhi But we want more He who has no attachments can really love others, for his/her love Is pure and divine – Bhavagad Gita But our desires are endless The ego really doesn’t want to know about the nature of genuine love For the ego struggles to see reality as a both/and concept In other words, if I love in the ways described above, unconditionally Then I can’t be critical of others, really? Everyone of those mentioned above used and understood critical thinking, and as well were unwilling to compromise their beliefs The nature of real love does not mean I can be used as a door mat ‘When the power of love overcomes the love of power the world will know peace – Jimi Hendrix, performed over 50 years ago There is an ever-widening abyss between belief and knowing To know love is to become loving, first of ourselves and then all others It is the first that seems to be the inescapable agent of our downfall We are unwilling to see the ugliness, the shadow, of our own nature Our defensive judgments, finger pointing, blame, denial, etc., etc., etc. The phrase, ‘physician heal thyself’, falls on deaf ears I know it is possible to love all of humanity and be critical as well I encourage you to adopt a meditative/contemplative practice Bring balance to your life, spend a few minutes each day in silence, In stillness, with Mystery – whatever your image of God Listen to the whispers of your inner guides, heart, soul, spirit They hold your true self, have your answers, they love, they wait

George G
