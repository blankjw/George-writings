REFLECTION

By George Gondron

REFLECTION If we are fortunate If we have the clarity to see Our paths will cross with those special ones Those who have the gift of perceiving our story Those in whose eyes we glimpse our reflection Those whose gaze mirrors the beauty of our soul Removing the mask, the persona, the facade Revealing a given-ness of communion A reality of connection beyond the smallness of mind A fleeting moment of mystical awareness It quickens the breath and warms the heart Like lifting a burden off of our being An understanding for the first time, a knowing Tears of quiet weep from our soul In tears of gratitude, a sense of found-ness A discovery of belonging to the Belonging They are there, those people Not just without but also within Reflecting back to us what we have failed to see Measured not in our goodness nor badness Measured in our humanness and our forgiveness Forgiveness which leads to our divinity Divinity which leads to our compassion Compassion which leads to our gratitude Seek them out They are there Seek

George G
