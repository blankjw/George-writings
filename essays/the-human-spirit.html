THE HUMAN SPIRIT

By George Gondron | Source: THE HUMAN SPIRIT.docx

THE HUMAN SPIRIT A flawless gem, an indestructible essence Is this human spirit that dwells within Infused with unimaginable strength and resilience Surviving under the most grievous circumstances Under horrendous struggles the spirit may bend But unlike the flesh, it will never break Formed in the fire of a Divine love Arising in the utterance of all creation It is never separated from its original Source Its light may flicker, and even grow dim But it is always and forever a part of Love's flame It has been in existence since the beginning of time Riding the crests of the waves of creation Dancing among the stars of the cosmos Housed within our human flesh Patiently waiting for our resistant awareness This eternal spirit that connects the universe Filling what appears to be void with its energy With its love, with its life, and with its essence As much a part of the Creator's spirit Is this human spirit within us all We are the dance, the song, the love Of That which dances, sings, and breathes all life This spirit within, this human spirit Will never end, never cease, only transform For what Love has created exists forever Time only changes the outer form Be present to the spirit that dwells within Be present to that which will always be Be present to the Self that lives forever Be present

George G
