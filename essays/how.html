ESSAY

HOW

By George Gondron

HOW Why, where, when, who, and how Of all the above, how is the most important How we see, how we hear, and most importantly, how we learn Our level of understanding, of awareness, of consciousness is correlated with the amount of effort we put into each, and it is the how of that effort that matters Critical thinking, deep reflection, doubt, questions, unwillingness to merely accept what’s offered are all necessary for the sincere pursuit of self-actualization, for radical change, for wholeness Western civilization in particular, no longer demonstrates a desire to learn beyond that which only stimulates their emotions That’s the easy way, but it’s also the way most likely to deceive for there is no substance to support the narrative, the BS There is only hyperbole to elicit fear and all that is ‘anti’ It is rhetoric intentionally meant to separate and divide, not unite It is the voice of those who seek power, control, and wealth beyond any rational need, enough is never enough So, if we merely accept what’s offered from the ‘emperor who has no clothes’, if we continue to drink the Kool Aid, then we are part of the problem and not part of the solution History is one of our greatest teachers, but when it’s denied or erased, it’s destined to be repeated over and over again We then are nothing more than sheep being led by wolves Acronyms such as CRT, DEI, and WOKE are important They are the enemies of those who wish to control and deceive Those who don’t want us to learn and remain poorly educated The enemy has been, is now, and will always be chosen ignorance For ignorance in a culture that offers unlimited knowledge, is chosen, and the refusal to learn is the enemy Watching social media and having our emotions aroused is not learning, it’s manipulation and conditioning We become nothing more than a means to an end An end that benefits the rich and powerful, not the poor Become a dedicated learner, not a ‘knee jerk’ reactor Connect to your inner core, your internal resources Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Isness Listen to the whispers of your inner guides, heart, soul, and spirit

George G
