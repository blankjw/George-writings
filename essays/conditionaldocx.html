CONDITIONAL

By George Gondron

CONDITIONAL Most of us, more or less, choose the conditions under Which we live Rarely do we consider the millions of human beings That do not have that option Trapped in circumstances not of their making nor their choosing Our understanding of their conditions is directly connected to Our open or closed mindedness, or put another way Our level of awareness, understanding, and intelligence The more closed minded we are the more judgmental we are The more critical and defensive we are And sadly, the more opposed we are to being willing To aid those in untenable life-threatening conditions Sorta like, out of sight, out of mind Or, please, don’t confuse me with the facts Which could be interpreted as a selfish or callous attitude Especially if we espouse a belief system that calls for Kindness, compassion, inclusion, and love Or, setting a formal belief system aside After all we are all more alike than unalike, all human beings All connected with similar hopes, wishes, and dreams And then there’s the argument we need to take care of our own First, the underprivileged in our nation, a valid statement But here’s the rub – there’s enough wealth to do both, easily There are more than enough resources to solve the problems What’s lacking is selflessness, a belief that ‘I am my brother’s Keeper’ – A clear understanding that a good definition Of love, is not a warm fussy feeling, but an act of sacrifice A willingness to do for others what at the moment they Cannot do for themselves – genuine love is not conditional, ever We have become so trapped in our own narrowness that We cannot see the forest for the trees, we wear ‘blinders’ Add a meditative/contemplative practice to your daily life A few minutes of silence, stillness, and Presence each day An emptying of the mind of incessant and repetitive thoughts Focus on your breathing, in and out, or a short mantra Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit, your true self They hold your truth, they have your answers, they patiently wait

George G
