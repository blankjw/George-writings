NON-DISCRIMINATORY - Copy

By George Gondron

NON-DISCRIMINATORY We separate ourselves through discrimination The areas of discrimination are literally unlimited, endless Race, color, creed, education, gender, economics, etc., etc. Discrimination never unifies it only separates Instead of recognizing that which is the same in our humanity We, it seems, prefer to point out what is different And it follows if our focus is on differences We will never unify, never recognize our commonality Never each a level of awareness or consciousness That is non-discriminatory The ego uses a process of compare, contrast, and compete To feel better or worse about itself, but never alike The phrase, ‘united we stand, divided we fall’ Loses its significance when we discriminate For as long as one person or group is set apart From all others, there will be discrimination For ‘wholeness’ to be a reality, on every level Every part must be included To experience alienation has become the norm Rather than the exception We as individuals waste way too much time Comparing ourselves to others needlessly What could possibly be gained in that agonizing process Nothing, but much is lost And the internal thought is always prefaced with ‘too’ Too much of this or too little of that We do it to ourselves as well as to others arbitrarily When our lives embrace balance, inner and outer When we choose a journey of self-discovery We will lose our tendencies to discriminate, to judge For it is the heart that holds necessary truth, not the ego Begin a meditative/contemplative practice A few minutes each day of silence, stillness, and Presence To cease the incessant and repetitive thoughts of the mind That go nowhere and accomplish no-thing Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit Discover your true non-discriminatory self, we need it

George G
