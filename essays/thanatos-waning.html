THANATOS WANING

By George Gondron

THANATOS WANING It will never succeed, this something called death This anomaly posed by the fragile mind An enigma of the ego to control through fear Fear, whose only purpose is to distract To distract us from life, from living, from being From being what only Love has created A piece of the Creator with freedom of will But the mind alone cannot grasp the continuum It is the heart, the soul, and the spirit They understand, they comprehend eternity The certitude that what begins cannot end For what emerges from the Infinite never dies Everything transforms, change is a given In every realm we evolve each moment A constant flow from the old to the new The mythical stories of death through the ages Speak of transition, not just an ending Two emotions rule all the others One is called fear, the precursor of death The other is love, the Giver of life Fear is about loss in one form are another Love is about gain in giving and receiving While existing on this planet that we call earth Fear's only purpose is to keep us from living By remaining asleep and chained to our doubts But Love's only purpose is to wake us up To take part in the journey with all of its wonder Death cannot win, it really never has For nothing is stronger than love Love is relationship and never loses Love is infinite and always wins

George G
