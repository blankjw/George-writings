THE WORLD OF CONFIRMATION BIAS

By George Gondron | Source: THE WORLD OF CONFIRMATION BIAS.docx

THE WORLD OF CONFIRMATION BIAS Through the explosion of social media It has become more and more obvious We seek that which confirms our own bias In the beginning, we may surf until something grabs our attention But that something is that which we are already attuned to It’s a built-in prejudice, attraction, proclivity, or bias Which may not even be conscious But it is there just beneath the surface of our awareness Like a moth drawn to a flame Alas, we are captured hook, line, and sinker This process happens simply because it’s easy No muss, no fuss, all our work has been done for us No need to critically think, to question, to doubt, to search Bam! – there it is, easy peasy He, she, they said, so it must be true There is no arena, no agenda, where these tactics aren’t used Marketers and advertisers know exactly how to Get, gain, and maintain our undivided attention For you see, if we refuse to do our own due diligence We are the fools, easily manipulated, led and controlled The reasons people believe what they believe are astounding They are shockingly unimaginable Objectifiable proven facts to the contrary And the snake-oil salesman says, ‘you gonna believe me Or those ole lying eyes?’ Be a part of the solution not a part of the problem ‘Don’t believe what you hear and only half of what you see’ Have a curious and open mind, and be resourceful Listen to input from all sides, discern what’s right for you A closed mind only knows how to defend, not to learn To be fully human, to be whole, we need balance Begin a meditative/contemplative practice for balance Spend a few minutes each day in silence, be still, embrace Isness Empty your mind of unnecessary and repetitive thoughts Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They reveal your true self, they hold your truth They have the answers you seek, they patiently wait

George G
