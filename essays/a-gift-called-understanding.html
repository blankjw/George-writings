A GIFT CALLED UNDERSTANDING

By George Gondron

A GIFT CALLED UNDERSTANDING Only through understanding do we have the choice to change Otherwise, we simply repeat day after day our ‘familiar’ Our same repetitive thoughts, same feelings, same actions A never-ending process of ‘rinse and repeat’ Except this process does not bring new consciousness or Greater awareness, we are today what we were all our Yesterdays, and will probably be all our tomorrows as well We grow older physically, but we don’t grow wiser intellectually We don’t expand our level of awareness and consciousness Our defenses remain intact, our resistances seldom change Our gift of understanding remains wrapped and unopened Understanding has a first cousin, called letting go They are partners, for if we reach some new understanding It requires that we let go of some routine, pattern, or habit That no longer has any benefit or continued merit if we are To move forward, to become more aware and conscious Understanding allows us to see a little more clearly Allows us to be a little more fully human, a little more inclusive A little more for and less against, a little more open than closed If we spent a little more energy attempting to understand ourselves And less energy attempting to change others There would definitely be a shift in our awareness and consciousness Our lack of tolerance, our tendency to judge others would be less As we begin to understand we are all more alike than unalike Our hopes, dreams, wishes, desires, and curiosities are similar We all have navels, we all bleed red, we all have beating hearts We are all the same color in the dark I have thought before, if we were all blind and deaf, requiring us to be Helpers to each other, what a different world this would be Change demands first and foremost, change yourself When you change, those around you will change as well We waste time and energy attempting to change others, don’t A suggestion: adopt a meditative/contemplative daily practice A few minutes each day be silent, be still, be present to Presence Focus on your breath, breathe in, breathe out Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, they bring understanding, they wait

George G
