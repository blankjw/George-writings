THE PROCESS OF UNDERSTANDING 2

By George Gondron

THE PROCESS OF UNDERSTANDING The purpose of understanding is not the search For someone or something that agrees with me But the search for the truth, no matter the result However, the preference of the ego is to seek out That which appeals to the ego’s needs and desires The ego has been conditioned for years through the Influences of life, day after day, moment by moment And all this influence is mostly unawares But it’s there and it is powerful Once those neural groves have been established in the brain They are very difficult to change For everything is seen through a lens that is colored To perceive and think in very limited manner And the result follows the saying “I may not be right, but I’m never wrong’ For in truth, the most difficult path for the ego to accept Is a path that reveals it was wrong The ego thrives in an environment of ‘rightness and wrongness’ Very dualistic and polarized in its thinking and limited perception Thinking that is so limited that it is very subjective With little objectivity at all The process of understanding is also a process of discovery Self-discovery, beyond the limited scope of the mind The mind, the ego are only a small part of who we are But for the person that has no reference to their inner core It is the only part, there are no others At best they only have an intellectual awareness of What is perceived through their senses, their thoughts, and feelings The ego takes center stage while the yearnings of the heart, soul, And spirit wait in the wings, quietly, patiently The importance of a meditative/contemplative practice Cannot be overstated – the gateway to our inner core Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Empty the ego, shut down the thoughts, that you might hear The whispers of the heart, soul, and spirit Your inner truth, your true self, your healing Our connections to the Real – they wait, patiently

George G
