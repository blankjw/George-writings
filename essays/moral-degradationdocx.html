MORAL DEGRADATION

By George Gondron

MORAL DEGRADATION Is the decay of morality in our society a threat A threat to democracy, to our Constitution Is morality like a cancer slowly destroying us from the inside out What is morality? How is it made manifest? Is it innate, a moral compass, or is it modeled and learned? Is it the character concerned with the difference between What’s good and what’s harmful, For sure, it is individual, justified by each person But here’s the real problem Morality of the mind is not morality of the heart, soul, and spirit When truth takes a back seat to power, control, and wealth When equality and justice do not apply to all people Morality simply does not exist It becomes nothing more than a mental construct Clung to by a few and ignored by most Including religious fundamentalist When there is no viable connection between inner and outer Between our mind and our inner core There is no rational argument pertaining to morality For in truth, it becomes situational Defended by each individual to fit their particular needs When it’s OK for over 50 million people in this country To live in food insecurity, children living with hunger Another 50 million are unable to afford health insurance Over 40 million living in poverty in the richest nation in the world How do these facts impact your moral compass? The words, ‘I am my brother’s keeper’ ring hollow Genuine love never turns away from obvious need The alternative is to hide from it, ignore it, justify it Take some time to examine your moral parameters Not from your rational or irrational mind but from your core From your internal reference the triad of heart, soul, and spirit Each day spend a few minutes in silence, stillness, with Presence Empty the egoic mind that you might hear the whispers Of your intuition, of an innate truth given at birth Your moral compass resides within as does your true self Listen carefully, they patiently wait

George G
