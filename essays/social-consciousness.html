SOCIAL CONSCIOUSNESS

By George Gondron

SOCIAL CONSCIOUSNESS The underpinning of social consciousness focused on Equality and justice for all is the living dynamic of love Love in the sense of action through connections Revealed in the behavior of inclusion, kindness, and compassion Words alone change nothing, they are only labels Our level of consciousness and awareness is shackled Only as a result of our personal choices We are open or closed to the possible through choice Our human potential for personal growth Is limited only as a result of our choices And to remain ignorant of our potential is a choice The saying, ‘we can’t see the forest for the trees’ Is very telling of our society and culture Our tribal nature cannot perceive that which is beyond Our tribal thinking Cannot perceive that social consciousness is inclusive Of all humanity, of all existence We operate from a dualistic mindset that by its very nature Is oppositional, clinging to a narrative of either/or As opposed to an inclusive consciousness of both/and Most cling to beliefs that have been given to them Beliefs that have not been the result of personal experience Translated, living through third-party information It’s revelatory of the saying ‘If most people are saying it’s the right thing to do It’s probably the wrong thing to do’ When our choices and decisions are grounded in the beliefs Of others, we forfeit our very lives Never discovering our own gifts, our own creativity Our own significant and individual journey We remain one of the crowd, colorless ghosts Pretending to be what we are not Discover a new path, a meditative/contemplative path Spend a few minutes each day in silence, with stillness In the presence of Presence, empty your mind Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They yearn for your attention, they patiently wait

George G
