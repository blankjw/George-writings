INEFFABLE

By George Gondron

INEFFABLE How does one know the unknowable? No doctrine, no dogma, can reveal the unknowable No belief can tell us 'this is what the unknowable is' It is easier to say what the unknowable is not than what the unknowable is Belief is an intellectual matter Knowing is a corporeal interior matter Arising from an experience with the Ineffable An experience too self-evident to be denied Too deep and profound to be explained Experience cannot be purchased, cannot be borrowed It happens through grace, only on an individual level If words could explain the experience, people would worship the words They would not seek the experience A unique trick by the Ineffable, requiring each of us, as individuals to seek our own experience It is available to all, the I - Thou relationship It can start with great pain, with great love It can start with joining in the silence, stillness, and presence each day Only for a few minutes Experience the Ineffable Know the unknowable

George G
