HUMBLE AND KIND

By George Gondron

HUMBLE AND KIND If given the choice To choose from all possible gifts Available to us human beings Choose to be humble and kind They will serve you much more than Power and pride We’ve all met both types But the kind and the humble Remain in our best memories While the prideful and powerful Soon lose their influence in our lives They seldom enter our thoughts, but when they do It’s not with fondness or respect The humble care less if they’re noticed The kind want no recognition for their selflessness While those of power seek recognition Their pride inflates their sense of importance Difficult to befriend and difficult to know The love of the humble and kind Is unconditional and asks for nothing But the love of the prideful and the powerful Demands fealty, obedience, and respect The former live life with an openness and curiosity The latter with closed minds focused on their desires The humble and kind act without asking ‘What’s in it for me’ They’re not concerned if the other person is worthy They only see a need that’s not been met For the powerful and the proud there’s Always a string attached to anything And everything they do The essence of the kind and the humble Swells with love and compassion Peace, mercy, justice, and forgiveness With the powerful and the proud It’s desire and attachment The choice is yours, please choose kindness and humility

George G
