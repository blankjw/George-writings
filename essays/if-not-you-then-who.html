IF NOT YOU^J THEN WHO

By George Gondron | Source: IF NOT YOU^J THEN WHO.docx

IF NOT YOU, THEN WHO Now in the twilight of my life, I have time for deep reflection Time for silence and stillness, time for remembering Remembering the wonderful human beings, I’ve encountered People filled with creativity and potential that the world desperately needs But for many reasons. too many to mention, they did not believe They were capable of making a difference, had no one telling them How special they were, in fact were more often than not told Don’t expect too much, you’ll be disappointed, stay in ‘your lane’ Messages filled with far too many, ‘you’re not enough’ But I promise you this, you’re never too old to begin Grandma Moses was seventy when she first began to paint, to be an artist In truth, we are the captain of our own ship, hoist your anchor Take a chance, listen to that deep yearning inside that wants to be expressed We are never too old to learn, never
