TIME

By George Gondron

TIME We live in a technological age of modern gadgets Making life easier, more convenient, savers of time Yet how often we hear the following; I don't have time Maybe later I can put my time to better use It's a waste of time Electronic marvels, the internet, the Iphone, Ipads They don't call it the web for nothing It has its place, and certainly its waste Texting, blogging, facebook, email What happened to face to face encounters, actually talking to one another? Strong connections fade into electronic messages Lesser connections are lost entirely Ah, but we don't have time Funny, that's really all we do have, time Time doesn't care, time does not wait Time does not waste itself, time flows on Maybe when there is no more time, we will wake up And maybe then, it will be too late Maybe

George G
