THE NECESSITY FOR DIVISION

By George Gondron

THE NECESSITY FOR DIVISION Underlying positive progress for all humanity is the disagreement and division held between and among all factions involved An educated mind is more than willing to examine all facts introduced in the back-and-forth process in order to arrive at the best possible outcome for all the lives touched by the decision Disagreement is never the enemy, the only enemy is a closed mind In fact, disagreement is a necessary catalyst needed to be assured that we have exhausted, as best as possible, any future pitfalls which may prevent a positive outcome for all people When a citizenry becomes so divided on a reasonable topic, or for that matter, just about any topic, the underlying evidence is not difficult to discern Polarized and dualistic thinking, low levels of literacy, ignorance, which is a lack of education, and the unwillingness to examine ourselves when it’s easier to point fingers and blame others are all defenses we use to maintain our ‘rightness’ Without division and disagreement, we may wake up one day and find ourselves in circumstances we never thought possible and realize it’s too late to alter the results Critical thinking, research, doubt, questions, and discussions with an open mind are all necessary components if we are to arrive at an objective and reasonable solution that enhances the lives of all concerned When educated adults cannot partake in an objective discussion about any topic, the problem is not the topic Closed minds are not interested in a point of view contrary to their own, their emotions overpower their intellect Education has been, is now, and will always be the key to objectivity Plato said, ‘The only real ill-doing is the deprivation of knowledge’ Education is a joy to the curious and necessary for the understanding of much that impacts our daily lives, never cease to learn For balance adopt a meditative/contemplative practice Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Presence Focus on your breath or a short mantra, the mind will wander, the ego will resist, simply rinse and repeat Listen to the whispers of your inner guides, heart, soul, and spirit They hold your true self, they have your answers, they wait

George G
