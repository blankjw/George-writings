THE ENEMY

By George Gondron | Source: THE ENEMY.docx

THE ENEMY It is frightening with clear objectivity To grasp who is considered the enemy in our nation Not outside our borders, but within our borders It is more obvious today than in the past If you are not a fundamentalist evangelical Christian You are the enemy If you are not a right-wing ultra-conservative You are the enemy If you are not in the club of the ultra-wealthy You are the enemy There is no discussion, no meaningful communication With these groups, they are always right And their view of right and wrong is very literal Very black and white, very opinionated In fact, their thinking is very dualistic The phrase situation ethics doesn’t exist Within their frame of reference If it’s wrong in their point of view It’s always wrong, no exception They can’t be bothered with feeding the hungry Visiting the prisoner, clothing the naked, Looking after the widow and the orphan But they can ‘preach it’ They are quick to judge, slow to forgive They could care less about liberation theology They reject the separation of church and state belief In fact, they reject the teaching of accurate history And as a result, history only repeats itself They are willing to be led like sheep Rather than utilizing the gift of free will The ability to think critically, to learn, to question, to doubt They are spiritually bankrupt; their religion is all cognitive Grounded in rules and regulations, but not relational Those of us who are the enemy, remember this It is only with the heart that one sees rightly It is only in silence, stillness, with Presence that we thrive It is where Truth abides

George G
