BENEATH THE SYRFACE

By George Gondron

BENEATH THE SURFACE In so many areas of our lives, what really matters Lies just beneath the surface One of the most prevalent emotions in our culture is anger What’s beneath the anger leads us to the real problem Anger is mostly a ‘knee-jerk’ reaction to a deeper issue Disappointment, frustration, loss, stress, broken trust, Assumptions, unmet expectations, failure, etc., etc., etc. And the basis of all the aforementioned is unrealized, Unspoken fear Ignorance is another area that flourishes as a result of A lack of education Believing that all we need do to educate ourselves Is to watch or listen to ‘social media’ That’s not education, it’s merely absorption Soaking up someone else’s narrative Usually as a result of stirred-up emotions, alternative facts, Half-truths or obvious lies – drinking the Kool Aid True education demands critical thinking, research, doubt, Study, questions, and personal experience Then there’s politics, the way politicians vote is readily available The accomplishments of each administration, readily available If your politics is governed by what politicians say, you are the fool And of course, let’s not forget religion, all religion Does your religion really challenge you to be kind, compassionate, Inclusive, understanding, charitable, available, and caring Or is it more about worship rather than relationship More about rites, rituals, dogma, doctrine, and meritocracy Than loving your neighbor, about human connection Life lived on the surface is not life, it’s mere existence What’s beneath the surface brings real life Bring unconscious behavior into the light of conscious understanding I urge you to adopt a meditative/contemplative practice An aid to discovering what’s beneath the surface Spend a few minutes each day in silence, be still, embrace Presence Breathe in, breathe out a short mantra, or focus on your breath Listen to the whispers of your heart, soul, and spirit They are your true self, they have your answers, they wait

George G
