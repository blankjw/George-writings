THE BURDEN OF CONSCIOUSNESS

By George Gondron

THE BURDEN OF CONSCIOUSNESS In some circles it is believed that all of creation has consciousness The degree of human consciousness is paramount For we alone have the ability of choice, of free will We can choose our thoughts, our feelings, and our actions But, unfortunately, most are ignorant of the fact That we do really choose all the above No one is holding a gun to our heads But, alas, it’s so much easier to blame than take ownership A truly conscious individual, an awakened individual Doesn’t blame, deny, or defend They are willing to reflect on their own thoughts and behavior In an open-minded and honest fashion Less willing to trust ‘third party’ information and social media More willing to critically think, question, and doubt They are not attracted to the next conspiracy theory The rhetoric who’s only purpose is separate and divide, not unite They’re not drinking the Kool-Aid or following the ‘pied piper’ They do realize the burden of being conscious, of free will They are aware of their devils and their angels Thewy know the importance of empathy, kindness, compassion, Of mercy, equality, and justice for all, not just the few They may not belong to a formal religion But they do believe in a Mystery that is an experience, is relational Education, the search for truth, personal experience is important Relying on others for honesty, integrity, and morality Is a ‘fools’ errand’, it is the abandonment of personal responsibility If we rely on others to make our choices, we suffer the consequences Use your gift of consciousness wisely There is more than enough evidence to the contrary Be kind, loving, and accepting of all your many strengths And don’t forget to include your weaknesses as well Do the same for all others, we are more alike than unalike Choose a meditative/contemplative practice for balance Spend a few minutes each day in silence, stillness, with Isness Empty your mind, focus on your breath in and out Listen for the whispers of your true self, your inner guides They have your answers, hold your truth, they wait

George G
